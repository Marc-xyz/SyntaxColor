<p><b>David Allen Welter</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Republican_Party_(United_States)" title="Republican Party (United States)">Republican</a> member of the <a href="/wiki/Illinois_House_of_Representatives" title="Illinois House of Representatives">Illinois House of Representatives</a> representing the 75th district since his appointment in July 2016. The 75th district includes all or parts of <a href="/wiki/Grundy_County,_Illinois" title="Grundy County, Illinois">Grundy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kendall_County,_Illinois" title="Kendall County, Illinois">Kendall</a>, <a href="/wiki/LaSalle_County,_Illinois" title="LaSalle County, Illinois">LaSalle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Will_County,_Illinois" title="Will County, Illinois">Will</a> counties.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-APPOINT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-APPOINT-1">[1]</a></sup> Prior to his appointment, he served as Chairman of the Grundy County Board.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-APPOINT_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-APPOINT-1">[1]</a></sup> During the 101st General Assembly, he serves on the following committees: Appropriations-Capital; Appropriations-Public Safety; Counties &amp; Townships; Public Utilities; State Government Administration; Transportation: Vehicles &amp; Safety. He is also the Republican spokesperson for the Committee on Energy &amp; Environment.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
