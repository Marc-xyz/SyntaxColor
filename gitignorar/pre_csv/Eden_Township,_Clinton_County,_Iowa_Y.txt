<p><b>Eden Township</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Civil_township" title="Civil township">township</a> in <a href="/wiki/Clinton_County,_Iowa" title="Clinton County, Iowa">Clinton County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iowa" title="Iowa">Iowa</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">USA</a>.  As of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Census,_2000" title="United States Census, 2000">2000 census</a>, its population was 856.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eden_Township,_Clinton_County,_Iowa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eden Township was organized in 1856.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eden_Township,_Clinton_County,_Iowa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eden Township covers an area of 35.63 square miles (92.3 km<sup>2</sup>) and contains one incorporated settlement, <a href="/wiki/Low_Moor,_Iowa" title="Low Moor, Iowa">Low Moor</a>.  According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Geological_Survey" title="United States Geological Survey">USGS</a>, it contains four cemeteries: Bowers-Dannatt-Hill, Cousin-Smith, Pehlam and Prairie Union.
</p><p>The streams of Brophy Creek and Cherry Creek run through this township.
</p>
