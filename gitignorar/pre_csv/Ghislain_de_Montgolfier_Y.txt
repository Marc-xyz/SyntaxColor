	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Ghislain de Montgolfier</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Ghislain de Montgolfier</b> (born 1943) is a French winemaker, and the former head of the <a href="/wiki/Bollinger" title="Bollinger">Bollinger</a> Champagne house, and the sixth generation to run the family business.
</p><p>Montgolfier was born in 1943,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and grew up in Paris.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wine-searcher.com_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-wine-searcher.com-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 1994, Montgolfier, great-great-grandson of founder <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Joseph_Bollinger&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Joseph Bollinger (page does not exist)">Joseph Bollinger</a> became head of Bollinger, succeeding <a href="/wiki/Christian_Bizot" title="Christian Bizot">Christian Bizot</a>, who had been president since 1978.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> In 2008, he was succeeded by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=J%C3%A9r%C3%B4me_Philipon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jérôme Philipon (page does not exist)">Jérôme Philipon</a>, the first non-family member to head Bollinger.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>Montgolfier is a former president of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Union_of_Champagne_Houses&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Union of Champagne Houses (page does not exist)">Union of Champagne Houses</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wine-searcher.com_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-wine-searcher.com-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
