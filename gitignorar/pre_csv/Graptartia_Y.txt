<p><i><b>Graptartia</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Africa" title="Africa">African</a> <a href="/wiki/Corinnidae" title="Corinnidae">corinnid sac spiders</a> first described by <a href="/wiki/Eug%C3%A8ne_Simon" title="Eugène Simon">Eugène Simon</a> in 1896.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Simo1896_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Simo1896-2">[2]</a></sup> As of April 2019<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Graptartia&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> it contains only two species.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NMBE_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-NMBE-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
