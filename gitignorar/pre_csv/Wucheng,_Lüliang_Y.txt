<p><b>Wucheng</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">吴城</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">吳城</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Wúchéng</span></i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Town_(China)" title="Town (China)">town</a> in  <a href="/wiki/Lishi_District" title="Lishi District">Lishi District</a>, <a href="/wiki/L%C3%BCliang" title="Lüliang">Lüliang</a>, in western <a href="/wiki/Shanxi" title="Shanxi">Shanxi</a> province, People's Republic of China.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is located 30 kilometres (19 mi) east-southeast of downtown Lüliang as the crow flies. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Taiyuan-Zhongwei-Yinchuan_Railway" title="Taiyuan-Zhongwei-Yinchuan Railway">Taiyuan-Zhongwei-Yinchuan Railway</a> runs through Wucheng, but there is apparently no passenger station here; in addition, <a href="/wiki/China_National_Highway_307" title="China National Highway 307">China National Highway 307</a> and <a href="/wiki/G20_Qingdao%E2%80%93Yinchuan_Expressway" title="G20 Qingdao–Yinchuan Expressway">G20 Qingdao–Yinchuan Expressway</a> pass through the town. As of 2011<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Wucheng,_L%C3%BCliang&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, it has 16 villages under its administration.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
