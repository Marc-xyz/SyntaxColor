<p><b>Luca Maniero</b> (born 11 July 1998) is an <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player who plays for <a href="/wiki/Imolese_Calcio_1919" title="Imolese Calcio 1919">Imolese</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/A.S._Cittadella" title="A.S. Cittadella">Cittadella</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Luca_Maniero_(footballer,_born_1998)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He made his professional debut in the <a href="/wiki/Serie_B" title="Serie B">Serie B</a> for <a href="/wiki/A.S._Cittadella" title="A.S. Cittadella">Cittadella</a> on 3 September 2016 in a game against <a href="/wiki/Ternana_Calcio" title="Ternana Calcio">Ternana</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 3 August 2019, he joined <a href="/wiki/Imolese_Calcio_1919" title="Imolese Calcio 1919">Imolese</a> on loan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
