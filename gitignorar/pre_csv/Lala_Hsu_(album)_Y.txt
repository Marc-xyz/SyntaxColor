<p><i><b>Lala Hsu</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">徐佳瑩</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Xú JiāYíng</span></i>) is <a href="/wiki/Taiwanese_people" title="Taiwanese people">Taiwanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Mandopop" title="Mandopop">Mandopop</a> artist <a href="/wiki/Lala_Hsu" title="Lala Hsu">Lala Hsu</a>'s (<a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">徐佳瑩</span>) self-titled debut Mandarin solo studio album. It was released by Asiamuse on 29 May 2009. The album features 11 self-composed tracks. The album was the bestseller in 2009.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p><p>With this album, Lala Hsu won "Best New Artist" at the <a href="/wiki/21st_Golden_Melody_Awards" title="21st Golden Melody Awards">21st Golden Melody Awards</a>.
</p>
