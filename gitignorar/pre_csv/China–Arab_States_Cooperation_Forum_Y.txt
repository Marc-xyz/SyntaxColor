	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">China–Arab States Cooperation Forum</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>China-Arab States Cooperation Forum</b> is a formal dialogue initiative between China and the <a href="/wiki/Arab_League" title="Arab League">Arab League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-brics_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-brics-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=China%E2%80%93Arab_States_Cooperation_Forum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The CASCF was established in 2004 by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/President_Hu" title="President Hu">President Hu</a> when he visited the Arab League headquarters in <a href="/wiki/Cairo" title="Cairo">Cairo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-brics_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-brics-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>At the 2010 meeting in Tianjin, strategic cooperation was discussed.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-brics_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-brics-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>At the 2016 meeting in Doha, the diamond anniversary of diplomatic relations between Communist China and an Arabic country was celebrated.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-brics_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-brics-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>At the 2018 meeting in Beijing, the <a href="/wiki/Belt_and_Road_Initiative" title="Belt and Road Initiative">Belt and Road Initiative</a> was strengthened.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-xhua_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-xhua-2">[2]</a></sup> The Arab world welcomed the Chinese "to play a greater role in regional affairs in the Middle East."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mfaprc_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-mfaprc-3">[3]</a></sup> A US$20 billion <a href="/wiki/China-Arab_states_special_loans" title="China-Arab states special loans">special line of credit</a> from China for "economic reconstruction" in post-conflict Arab countries was announced.
</p>
<ul><li>2004: 1st meeting in Cairo. Declaration of the China-Arab State Cooperation Forum.</li>
<li>2006: 2nd meeting in Beijing. Joint Communiqué on Environmental Protection.</li>
<li>2008: 3rd meeting in <a href="/wiki/Manama" title="Manama">Manama</a></li>
<li>2010: 4th meeting in <a href="/wiki/Tianjin" title="Tianjin">Tianjin</a></li>
<li>2012: 5th meeting in <a href="/wiki/Hammamet,_Tunisia" title="Hammamet, Tunisia">Hammamet</a></li>
<li>2014: 6th meeting in Beijing</li>
<li>2016: 7th meeting in <a href="/wiki/Doha" title="Doha">Doha</a></li>
<li>2018: 8th meeting in <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a></li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Statistics">Statistics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=China%E2%80%93Arab_States_Cooperation_Forum&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Statistics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Arabs have become China's greatest supplier of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crude_oil" title="Crude oil">crude oil</a>, and if grouped together, are China's seventh biggest trading partner.
