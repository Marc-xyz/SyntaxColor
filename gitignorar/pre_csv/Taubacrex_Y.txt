<p><i><b>Taubacrex</b></i> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Extinct" title="Extinct">extinct</a> <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of birds of the family <a href="/wiki/Quercymegapodiidae" title="Quercymegapodiidae">Quercymegapodiidae</a> from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Late_Oligocene" title="Late Oligocene">Late Oligocene</a> to <a href="/wiki/Early_Miocene" title="Early Miocene">Early Miocene</a> (<a href="/wiki/Deseadan" title="Deseadan">Deseadan</a>) <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Trememb%C3%A9_Formation&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Tremembé Formation (page does not exist)">Tremembé Formation</a> of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Taubat%C3%A9_Basin&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Taubaté Basin (page does not exist)">Taubaté Basin</a> in the state of <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo_(state)" title="São Paulo (state)">São Paulo</a>, Brazil. The type species is <i>Taubacrex granivora</i>. In the original description, <a href="/wiki/Herculano_Marcos_Ferraz_de_Alvarenga" title="Herculano Marcos Ferraz de Alvarenga">Herculano Alvarenga</a> classified it as a member of family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rallidae" title="Rallidae">Rallidae</a>. It provides the earliest fossil record of <a href="/wiki/Gastrolith" title="Gastrolith">gastroliths</a> in birds of the order <a href="/wiki/Galliformes" title="Galliformes">Galliformes</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mayr2009_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mayr2009-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
