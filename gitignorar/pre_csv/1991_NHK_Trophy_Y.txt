<p>The <b>1991 <a href="/wiki/NHK_Trophy" title="NHK Trophy">NHK Trophy</a></b> was held in <a href="/wiki/Hiroshima" title="Hiroshima">Hiroshima</a>. Medals were awarded in the disciplines of <a href="/wiki/Single_skating" title="Single skating">men's singles, ladies' singles</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pair_skating" title="Pair skating">pair skating</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ice_dancing" title="Ice dancing">ice dancing</a>.
</p>
