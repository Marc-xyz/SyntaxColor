<p><b>Joonas Tamm</b> (born 2 February 1992) is an Estonian professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Centre-back" title="Defender (association football)">centre back</a> for <a href="/wiki/Eliteserien" title="Eliteserien">Eliteserien</a> club <a href="/wiki/Lillestr%C3%B8m_SK" title="Lillestrøm SK">Lillestrøm</a>, on loan from <a href="/wiki/FC_Flora" title="FC Flora">Flora</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_football_team" title="Estonia national football team">Estonia national team</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tulevik">Tulevik</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Tulevik">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tamm started out playing for hometown team <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/JK_Tulevik_Viljandi" title="JK Tulevik Viljandi">Tulevik</a>. He made his debut in the <a href="/wiki/Meistriliiga" title="Meistriliiga">Meistriliiga</a> on 29 March 2008, in a 1–1 home draw against <a href="/wiki/JK_Sillam%C3%A4e_Kalev" title="JK Sillamäe Kalev">Sillamäe Kalev</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Flora">Flora</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Flora">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In February 2009, Tamm moved to <a href="/wiki/FC_Flora" title="FC Flora">Flora</a>. He scored his first Meistriliiga goal on 9 May 2009, in a 4–0 away victory over <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/JK_Tammeka_Tartu" title="JK Tammeka Tartu">Tammeka</a>.
</p><p>In August 2009, Tamm joined Italian club <a href="/wiki/U.C._Sampdoria" title="U.C. Sampdoria">Sampdoria</a> on a season-long loan, where he played for the club's <i>Primavera</i> side.
</p><p>Tamm returned to Flora in August 2010. He helped the team win the Meistriliiga in <a href="/wiki/2010_Meistriliiga" title="2010 Meistriliiga">2010</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="IFK_Norrk.C3.B6ping"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="IFK_Norrköping">IFK Norrköping</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: IFK Norrköping">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 27 December 2010, Tamm signed a three-year contract with <a href="/wiki/Allsvenskan" title="Allsvenskan">Allsvenskan</a> club <a href="/wiki/IFK_Norrk%C3%B6ping" title="IFK Norrköping">IFK Norrköping</a>, with the option to extend the contract for another year.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In July 2011, he moved to <a href="/wiki/Division_1_(Swedish_football)" title="Division 1 (Swedish football)">Division 1</a> side <a href="/wiki/IF_Sylvia" title="IF Sylvia">IF Sylvia</a> on loan. Tamm scored on his debut for the club on 9 July 2011, in a 2–1 away victory over <a href="/wiki/Motala_AIF" title="Motala AIF">Motala AIF</a>. He made his debut in the Allsvenskan on 25 August 2011, in a 2–1 home victory over <a href="/wiki/Djurg%C3%A5rdens_IF_Fotboll" title="Djurgårdens IF Fotboll">Djurgårdens IF</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Trelleborgs_FF">Trelleborgs FF</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Trelleborgs FF">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 2 December 2013, Tamm signed a one-year contract with Division 1 club <a href="/wiki/Trelleborgs_FF" title="Trelleborgs FF">Trelleborgs FF</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Tulevik">Return to Tulevik</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Return to Tulevik">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 9 February 2015, Tamm returned to his boyhood club, Tulevik, on a one-year contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> Positioned as a centre-forward, he went on to score 9 goals in 15 league appearances.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Return_to_Flora">Return to Flora</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Return to Flora">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 22 June 2015, Tamm joined his former club Flora for an undisclosed fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> He went on to help Flora win Meistriliiga titles in the <a href="/wiki/2015_Meistriliiga" title="2015 Meistriliiga">2015</a> and <a href="/wiki/2017_Meistriliiga" title="2017 Meistriliiga">2017</a> seasons.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="Sarpsborg_08_.28loan.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Sarpsborg_08_(loan)">Sarpsborg 08 (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Sarpsborg 08 (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 31 January 2018, Tamm joined <a href="/wiki/Eliteserien" title="Eliteserien">Eliteserien</a> club <a href="/wiki/Sarpsborg_08_FF" title="Sarpsborg 08 FF">Sarpsborg 08</a> on a season-long loan.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Starting from the first qualifying round, he helped the team reach the <a href="/wiki/2018%E2%80%9319_UEFA_Europa_League" title="2018–19 UEFA Europa League">UEFA Europa League</a> group stage. Tamm played in all six group stage matches as Sarpsborg 08 finished last in their group.
</p>
<h4><span id="Korona_Kielce_.28loan.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Korona_Kielce_(loan)">Korona Kielce (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Korona Kielce (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 8 February 2019 Tamm joined <a href="/wiki/Ekstraklasa" title="Ekstraklasa">Ekstraklasa</a> club <a href="/wiki/Korona_Kielce" title="Korona Kielce">Korona Kielce</a> on a six-month loan deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span id="Lillestr.C3.B8m_.28loan.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Lillestrøm_(loan)">Lillestrøm (loan)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Lillestrøm (loan)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On 27 June 2019, Tamm moved to Eliteserien club <a href="/wiki/Lillestr%C3%B8m_SK" title="Lillestrøm SK">Lillestrøm</a> on loan until the end of the <a href="/wiki/Sarpsborg_08_FF" title="Sarpsborg 08 FF">2018–19 season</a>, with the option to make the deal permanent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Joonas_Tamm&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Tamm has represented Estonia at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-16_football_team" title="Estonia national under-16 football team">under-16</a>, <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-17_football_team" title="Estonia national under-17 football team">under-17</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-18_football_team" title="Estonia national under-18 football team">under-18</a>, <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-19_football_team" title="Estonia national under-19 football team">under-19</a>, <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-21_football_team" title="Estonia national under-21 football team">under-21</a> and <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_under-23_football_team" title="Estonia national under-23 football team">under-23</a> levels. On 10 June 2011, Tamm was named by manager <a href="/wiki/Tarmo_R%C3%BC%C3%BCtli" title="Tarmo Rüütli">Tarmo Rüütli</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Estonia_national_football_team" title="Estonia national football team">Estonia</a> squad to face <a href="/wiki/Chile_national_football_team" title="Chile national football team">Chile</a> and <a href="/wiki/Uruguay_national_football_team" title="Uruguay national football team">Uruguay</a> in <a href="/wiki/Exhibition_game" title="Exhibition game">friendly</a> matches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> He made his debut for the senior national team on 19 June 2011, in a 0–4 away loss to Chile.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup> Tamm scored his first international goals on 7 October 2017, when he found the net three times in the second half of a 6–0 away victory in a <a href="/wiki/2018_FIFA_World_Cup_qualification_(UEFA)" title="2018 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA)">2018 FIFA World Cup qualification</a> match against <a href="/wiki/Gibraltar_national_football_team" title="Gibraltar national football team">Gibraltar</a>, becoming the first player to score a hat-trick for Estonia since 1996.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup>
</p>
