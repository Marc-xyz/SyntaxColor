<p>The <b>men's parallel bars</b> competition at the <a href="/wiki/1936_Summer_Olympics" title="1936 Summer Olympics">1936 Summer Olympics</a> was held at the <a href="/wiki/Waldb%C3%BChne" title="Waldbühne">Waldbühne</a> on 10 and 11 August. It was the sixth appearance of the event.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sports-reference_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-sports-reference-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-OfficialReport_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-OfficialReport-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Competition_format">Competition format</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gymnastics_at_the_1936_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_parallel_bars&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Competition format">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The gymnastics format returned to the aggregation format used in 1928 but not in 1932. Each nation entered a team of eight gymnasts (Bulgaria had only 7). All entrants in the gymnastics competitions (Neri of Italy did not compete in the parallel bars) performed both a compulsory exercise and a voluntary exercise, with the scores summed to give a final total. The scores in the parallel bars were added to the other apparatus scores to give individual all-around scores; the top six individual scores on each team were summed to give a team all-around score. No separate finals were contested.
</p>
