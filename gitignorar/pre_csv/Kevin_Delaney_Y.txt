	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Kevin Delaney</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><table class="infobox biography vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold"><div class="fn" style="display:inline">Kevin Delaney</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Residence</th><td class="label"><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>, U.S.</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Nationality</th><td class="category">American</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Occupation</th><td class="role">Voice actor</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Years active</th><td>1995–present</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Kevin Delaney</b> is an <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Voice_acting" title="Voice acting">voice actor</a>, who is best known for voicing Edna Mode in <i><a href="/wiki/Disney_Infinity_(video_game)" title="Disney Infinity (video game)">Disney Infinity</a></i>, Captain Marvel in <i><a href="/wiki/Mortal_Kombat_vs._DC_Universe" title="Mortal Kombat vs. DC Universe">Mortal Kombat vs. DC Universe</a></i>, Ricardo and Couier in <i><a href="/wiki/Time_and_Eternity" title="Time and Eternity">Time and Eternity</a></i> and Ryuji Keikain in <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nura:_Rise_of_the_Yokai_Clan:_Demon_Capital" title="Nura: Rise of the Yokai Clan: Demon Capital">Nura: Rise of the Yokai Clan: Demon Capital</a></i>. He also provided additional voices for <i>Looney Tunes: Stranger than Fiction</i>, <i>Looney Tunes: Reality Check</i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Lightning_Returns:_Final_Fantasy_XIII" title="Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy XIII">Lightning Returns: Final Fantasy XIII</a></i>.
</p>
