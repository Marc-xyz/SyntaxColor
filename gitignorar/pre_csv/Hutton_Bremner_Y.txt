<p><b>Thomas Hutton Bremner</b> (18 July 1912 – 12 September 1969) was a Scottish <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who played in the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Football_League" title="Scottish Football League">Scottish League</a> for <a href="/wiki/Queen%27s_Park_F.C." title="Queen's Park F.C.">Queen's Park</a>, <a href="/wiki/Motherwell_F.C." title="Motherwell F.C.">Motherwell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hamilton_Academical_F.C." title="Hamilton Academical F.C.">Hamilton Academical</a> as an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Inside_forward" title="Inside forward">inside forward</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-QPFC_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-QPFC-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-afcheritage_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-afcheritage-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Litster_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Litster-5">[5]</a></sup> He represented Scotland at amateur level and made one appearance for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scottish_League_XI" title="Scottish League XI">Scottish League XI</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
