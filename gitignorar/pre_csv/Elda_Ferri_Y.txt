<p><b>Elda Ferri</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Italians" title="Italians">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Film_producer" title="Film producer">film producer</a>.
</p><p>She is the administrator of the company <i>Jean Vigo Italia S.r.l.</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She co-produced the comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Life_Is_Beautiful" title="Life Is Beautiful">Life Is Beautiful</a></i> (1997) with <a href="/wiki/Gianluigi_Braschi" title="Gianluigi Braschi">Gianluigi Braschi</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> for which they both received an Academy Award nomination for <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Picture" title="Academy Award for Best Picture">Best Picture</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The film also earned them a <a href="/wiki/David_di_Donatello_for_Best_Producer" title="David di Donatello for Best Producer">David di Donatello for Best Producer</a> as well as a <a href="/wiki/European_Film_Awards#Best_European_Film_.28Film_of_the_year.29" title="European Film Awards">European Film Award for Best Film</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2005 she was awarded the prize of the "European producer of the year".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
