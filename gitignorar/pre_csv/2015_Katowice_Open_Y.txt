<p>The <b>2015 Katowice Open</b> was a women's <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> tournament played on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hard_court" title="Hard court">indoor hard courts</a>. It was the 3rd edition of the <a href="/wiki/Katowice_Open" title="Katowice Open">Katowice Open</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/WTA_International_tournaments" title="WTA International tournaments">International category</a> of the <a href="/wiki/2015_WTA_Tour" title="2015 WTA Tour">2015 WTA Tour</a>. It took place at <a href="/wiki/Spodek" title="Spodek">Spodek</a> arena in <a href="/wiki/Katowice" title="Katowice">Katowice</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a>, from 6 April through 12 April 2015.
</p>
