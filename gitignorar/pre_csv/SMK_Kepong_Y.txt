<p><b>Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Kepong</b> (English: Kepong National Secondary School), is a <a href="/wiki/State_school" title="State school">public</a> <a href="/wiki/Secondary_school" title="Secondary school">secondary school</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Selangor" title="Selangor">Selangor</a>. It was established in 1965.
</p>
