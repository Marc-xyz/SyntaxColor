	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Edgeworth (surname)</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Edgeworth</b> is an English <a href="/wiki/Toponymic_surname" title="Toponymic surname">toponymic surname</a>. It probably derives from <a href="/wiki/Edgeworth,_Gloucestershire" title="Edgeworth, Gloucestershire">Edgeworth</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire" title="Gloucestershire">Gloucestershire</a>, but the name is long established in Ireland, where it is claimed that the family settled in <a href="/wiki/County_Longford" title="County Longford">County Longford</a> in 1583.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>   
</p><p>Notable people with the surname include:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Richard_Lovell_Edgeworth" title="Richard Lovell Edgeworth">Richard Lovell Edgeworth</a> (1744–1817), Anglo-Irish scientist, inventor, writer and educator, father of Maria Edgeworth and Michael Pakenham Edgeworth
<ul><li>His son <a href="/wiki/Michael_Pakenham_Edgeworth" title="Michael Pakenham Edgeworth">Michael Pakenham Edgeworth</a> (1812–1881), botanist</li>
<li>His daughter <a href="/wiki/Maria_Edgeworth" title="Maria Edgeworth">Maria Edgeworth</a> (1767–1849), novelist
<ul><li>His grandson <a href="/wiki/Francis_Ysidro_Edgeworth" title="Francis Ysidro Edgeworth">Francis Ysidro Edgeworth</a> (1845–1926), statistician and economist</li></ul></li></ul></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Henry_Essex_Edgeworth" title="Henry Essex Edgeworth">Henry Essex Edgeworth</a> (1745–1807), <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Irish_Catholic" title="Irish Catholic">Irish Catholic</a> priest and confessor of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Louis_XVI" title="Louis XVI">Louis XVI</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Kenneth_Edgeworth" title="Kenneth Edgeworth">Kenneth Edgeworth</a> (1880–1972), Irish astronomer, economist and engineer</li></ul>
<p><b>Fictional characters:</b>
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Miles_Edgeworth" title="Miles Edgeworth">Miles Edgeworth</a>, character from the <i>Ace Attorney</i> video game series</li></ul>
