<p><b>Glattfelden railway station</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Railway_station" title="Railway station">railway station</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Swiss</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canton_of_Zurich" title="Canton of Zurich">canton of Zurich</a>. The station is actually situated in the <a href="/wiki/B%C3%BClach" title="Bülach">municipality of Bülach</a> but takes its name from the immediately adjacent <a href="/wiki/Glattfelden" title="Glattfelden">municipality of Glattfelden</a>. It is located on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Winterthur%E2%80%93B%C3%BClach%E2%80%93Koblenz_railway_line" title="Winterthur–Bülach–Koblenz railway line">Winterthur to Koblenz line</a>, and is served by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zurich_S-Bahn" title="Zurich S-Bahn">Zurich S-Bahn</a> line <a href="/wiki/S9_(ZVV)" title="S9 (ZVV)">S9</a> between <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zurich" title="Zurich">Zurich</a> and <a href="/wiki/Schaffhausen" title="Schaffhausen">Schaffhausen</a>. A <a href="/wiki/PostBus_Switzerland" title="PostBus Switzerland">PostBus</a> service (ZVV 540) connects the station with the village.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-zvv2_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-zvv2-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-zvv_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-zvv-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-saw_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-saw-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
