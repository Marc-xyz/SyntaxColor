<p>The <b>2016 Seattle Mariners season</b> was the 40th season in franchise history. The Mariners played their 17th full season (18th overall) at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Safeco_Field" title="Safeco Field">Safeco Field</a>. Despite finishing with a winning record of 86–76, they failed to make the playoffs, finishing second place in the American League West Division.
</p>
