<p><i><b>Gogana bornormalis</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Drepanidae" title="Drepanidae">Drepanidae</a> first described by Jeremy Daniel Holloway in 1998.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is found on <a href="/wiki/Borneo" title="Borneo">Borneo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
