<p><i><b>Idealist</b></i> is a 1976 Yugoslav <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Igor_Pretnar" title="Igor Pretnar">Igor Pretnar</a>. It was entered into the <a href="/wiki/10th_Moscow_International_Film_Festival" title="10th Moscow International Film Festival">10th Moscow International Film Festival</a> where <a href="/wiki/Radko_Poli%C4%8D" title="Radko Polič">Radko Polič</a> won the award for Best Actor.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Moscow1977_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Moscow1977-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
