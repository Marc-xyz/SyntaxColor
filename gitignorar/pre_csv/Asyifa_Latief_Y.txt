<p><b>Asyifa Syafiningdyah Putrambami Latief</b> (born 20 September 1988), better known as <b>Asyifa Latief</b>, is an Indonesian model who was <a href="/wiki/Miss_Indonesia_2010" title="Miss Indonesia 2010">Miss Indonesia 2010</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She was the first Arab-Indonesian to be crowned as Miss Indonesia.
</p><p>Latief represented <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Miss_World_2010" title="Miss World 2010">Miss World 2010</a> competition, which was held in November 2010.
</p><p>Latief was also crowned as Miss Best Body.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She is a model and a student at <a href="/wiki/Parahyangan_Catholic_University" title="Parahyangan Catholic University">Parahyangan Catholic University</a> at <a href="/wiki/Bandung" title="Bandung">Bandung</a>, majoring in accounting.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span id="Miss_Indonesia.27s_CROWN"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Miss_Indonesia's_CROWN">Miss Indonesia's CROWN</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Asyifa_Latief&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Miss Indonesia's CROWN">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Asyifa Latief is successor incumbent at 1 June 2010 - 3 June 2011 (only 1 year hold the crown) and her crown is given to <a href="/wiki/Miss_Indonesia_2011" title="Miss Indonesia 2011">Miss Indonesia 2011</a>.
</p>
