<p><b>Jonathon Gary Margetts</b> (born 28 September 1993) is an English professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">striker</a> for <a href="/wiki/Matlock_Town_F.C." title="Matlock Town F.C.">Matlock Town</a>, on loan from
<a href="/wiki/Boston_United_F.C." title="Boston United F.C.">Boston United</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Hull_City">Hull City</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Hull City">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Doncaster" title="Doncaster">Doncaster</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Yorkshire" title="South Yorkshire">South Yorkshire</a>, Margetts was a <a href="/wiki/Hull_City_A.F.C." title="Hull City A.F.C.">Hull City</a> youth graduate.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Jonathan_Margetts_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Jonathan_Margetts-2">[2]</a></sup> In July 2012, he progressed through the youth ranks into the first team and signed his first professional contract.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> On 27 February 2014, he joined <a href="/wiki/Gainsborough_Trinity_F.C." title="Gainsborough Trinity F.C.">Gainsborough Trinity</a> in a one-month <a href="/wiki/Loan_(sports)" title="Loan (sports)">loan</a> deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>Margetts scored 10 goals in only 13 league appearances for Gainsborough, including a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hat-trick_(association_football)" title="Hat-trick (association football)">hat-trick</a> in a 6–0 home win over <a href="/wiki/Oxford_City_F.C." title="Oxford City F.C.">Oxford City</a> on 12 April.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> He returned to Hull in the summer, but was loaned to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Harrogate_Town_F.C." title="Harrogate Town F.C.">Harrogate Town</a> on 18 August.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 23 December 2014, Margetts returned to Gainsborough, also on a loan deal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> On 13 March 2015, he joined <a href="/wiki/Cambridge_United_F.C." title="Cambridge United F.C.">Cambridge United</a> on loan until the end of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> Margetts made his professional debut on the following day, coming on as a second-half <a href="/wiki/Substitute_(association_football)" title="Substitute (association football)">substitute</a> in a 1–1 <a href="/wiki/EFL_League_Two" title="EFL League Two">League Two</a> home draw against <a href="/wiki/Stevenage_F.C." title="Stevenage F.C.">Stevenage</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tranmere_Rovers">Tranmere Rovers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tranmere Rovers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following a successful pre-season trial and scoring five goals in three games, Margetts signed a one-year deal with <a href="/wiki/National_League_(division)" title="National League (division)">National League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Tranmere_Rovers_F.C." title="Tranmere Rovers F.C.">Tranmere Rovers</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> He joined <a href="/wiki/National_League_North" title="National League North">National League North</a> club <a href="/wiki/Stockport_County_F.C." title="Stockport County F.C.">Stockport County</a> on loan on 27 November 2015,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> where he scored four goals from seven appearances.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-J._Margetts_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-J._Margetts-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FAT_14-0"><a href="#cite_note-FAT-14">[14]</a></sup> He joined National League side <a href="/wiki/Altrincham_F.C." title="Altrincham F.C.">Altrincham</a> on a one-month loan on 23 January 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> On 22 March 2016, Margetts joined National League team <a href="/wiki/Southport_F.C." title="Southport F.C.">Southport</a> on loan for the rest of the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_National_League#National_League" title="2015–16 National League">2015–16 season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Lincoln_City">Lincoln City</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Lincoln City">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 14 July 2016, Margetts joined National League club <a href="/wiki/Lincoln_City_F.C." title="Lincoln City F.C.">Lincoln City</a> on a one-year contract, following a successful trial.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Scunthorpe_United">Scunthorpe United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Scunthorpe United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After scoring five goals in seven matches for Lincoln, Margetts signed for <a href="/wiki/EFL_League_One" title="EFL League One">League One</a> club <a href="/wiki/Scunthorpe_United_F.C." title="Scunthorpe United F.C.">Scunthorpe United</a> on 31 August 2016 on a two-year contract for an undisclosed fee.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup> He scored his first goal for Scunthorpe on his debut in an <a href="/wiki/EFL_Trophy" title="EFL Trophy">EFL Trophy</a> tie against <a href="/wiki/Shrewsbury_Town_F.C." title="Shrewsbury Town F.C.">Shrewsbury Town</a> on 4 October 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-19"><a href="#cite_note-19">[19]</a></sup> A loan move to <a href="/wiki/Crewe_Alexandra_F.C." title="Crewe Alexandra F.C.">Crewe Alexandra</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-20"><a href="#cite_note-20">[20]</a></sup> fell through in early February 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-21"><a href="#cite_note-21">[21]</a></sup> On 1 March 2017, Margetts rejoined Lincoln City on loan until the end of 2016–17.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-22"><a href="#cite_note-22">[22]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Boston_United">Boston United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jonny_Margetts&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Boston United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 2 July 2018 Margetts joined <a href="/wiki/Boston_United_F.C." title="Boston United F.C.">Boston United</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-23"><a href="#cite_note-23">[23]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 23 October 2018 he returned to <a href="/wiki/Gainsborough_Trinity_F.C." title="Gainsborough Trinity F.C.">Gainsborough Trinity</a> on loan for three months.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-24"><a href="#cite_note-24">[24]</a></sup> He returned to Boston United in mid January 2019, but was loaned out again on 30 January, this time to <a href="/wiki/Matlock_Town_F.C." title="Matlock Town F.C.">Matlock Town</a> for one month.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup> In the beginning of March 2019, Margetts joined the same club on loan once again, this time until the end of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-26"><a href="#cite_note-26">[26]</a></sup>
</p>
