<p><b>Mokkavatnet</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Lake" title="Lake">lake</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ogndal" title="Ogndal">Ogndal</a> area of the municipality of <a href="/wiki/Steinkjer" title="Steinkjer">Steinkjer</a> in <a href="/wiki/Tr%C3%B8ndelag" title="Trøndelag">Trøndelag</a> county, <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a>.  The 17.17-square-kilometre (6.63 sq mi) lake is located about 30 kilometres (19 mi) southeast of the <a href="/wiki/Steinkjer_(town)" title="Steinkjer (town)">town of Steinkjer</a>.  The lake is one of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Headwaters" title="Headwaters">headwaters</a> of the river <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ogna_(river)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ogna (river) (page does not exist)">Ogna</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-snl_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-snl-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
