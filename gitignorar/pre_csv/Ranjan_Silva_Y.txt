He is a right-handed batsman and a right-arm off-break bowler who plays for <a href="/wiki/Singha_Sports_Club" title="Singha Sports Club">Singha Sports Club</a>. He was born in <a href="/wiki/Kalutara" title="Kalutara">Kalutara</a>.
</p><p>Silva made his first-class debut in November 2008 against <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seeduwa_Raddoluwa_Sports_Club" title="Seeduwa Raddoluwa Sports Club">Seeduwa Raddoluwa</a>. In both innings where he batted, he scored a duck. From two overs of bowling, he took figures of 1-13.
</p>
