<p>"<b>The Final Hour</b>" is a single by Canadian country music artist <a href="/wiki/Hank_Smith_(singer)" title="Hank Smith (singer)">Hank Smith</a>. The song debuted at number 45 on the <i><a href="/wiki/RPM_(magazine)" title="RPM (magazine)">RPM</a></i> Country Tracks chart on March 20, 1971.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It peaked at number 1 on June 5, 1971.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
