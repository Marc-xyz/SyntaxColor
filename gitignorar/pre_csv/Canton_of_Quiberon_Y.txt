		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b><a href="/wiki/Cantons_of_France" title="Cantons of France">canton</a> of Quiberon</b> is an administrative division of the <a href="/wiki/Morbihan" title="Morbihan">Morbihan</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a>, northwestern <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. Its borders were modified at the French canton reorganisation which came into effect in March 2015. Its seat is in <a href="/wiki/Quiberon" title="Quiberon">Quiberon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-decret_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-decret-1">[1]</a></sup>
