<p>The <b>8th  <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Golden_Globe_Award" title="Golden Globe Award">Golden Globe Awards</a></b>, honoring the best in <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">film</a> for <a href="/wiki/1950_in_film" title="1950 in film">1950</a> films, were held on February 28, 1951, in the <a href="/wiki/Ciro%27s" title="Ciro's">Ciro's</a> nightclub in  <a href="/wiki/West_Hollywood,_California" title="West Hollywood, California">West Hollywood, California</a>, at 8433 <a href="/wiki/Sunset_Boulevard" title="Sunset Boulevard">Sunset Boulevard</a>, on the <a href="/wiki/Sunset_Strip" title="Sunset Strip">Sunset Strip</a>.
</p>
