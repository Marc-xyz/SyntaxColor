<p>The <b>2006 Duke Blue Devils football team</b> represented the <a href="/wiki/Duke_University" title="Duke University">Duke University</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2006_NCAA_Division_I_FBS_football_season" title="2006 NCAA Division I FBS football season">2006 NCAA Division I FBS football season</a>. The team was led by head coach <a href="/wiki/Ted_Roof" title="Ted Roof">Ted Roof</a>. They played their home games at <a href="/wiki/Wallace_Wade_Stadium" title="Wallace Wade Stadium">Wallace Wade Stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/Durham,_North_Carolina" title="Durham, North Carolina">Durham, North Carolina</a>.
</p>
