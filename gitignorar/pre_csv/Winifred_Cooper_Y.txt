	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Winifred Cooper</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">British film editor</div>
<table class="infobox biography vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold"><div class="fn" style="display:inline">Winifred Cooper</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Occupation</th><td class="role">Film editor</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Years active</th><td>1931–1944</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Winifred Cooper</b> was a British film editor known for her work on films like <i><a href="/wiki/They_Met_in_the_Dark" title="They Met in the Dark">They Met in the Dark</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Alfred_Hitchcock" title="Alfred Hitchcock">Hitchcock</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Rich_and_Strange" title="Rich and Strange">Rich and Strange</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
