<p><i><b>Acleris yasutoshii</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> of the family <a href="/wiki/Tortricidae" title="Tortricidae">Tortricidae</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Taiwan" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>.
</p>
