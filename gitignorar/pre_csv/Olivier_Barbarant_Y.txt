<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life">Life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Olivier_Barbarant&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He lived in <a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a>. Since 1994, he has been living in <a href="/wiki/Saint-Quentin,_Aisne" title="Saint-Quentin, Aisne">Saint-Quentin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Picardy" title="Picardy">Picardy</a>, where he teaches school, currently at the Lycée Lakanal in Sceaux.
</p><p>In 1995, he met Veronique Elzière called Berenice in his books. He adopted Cosette in July 1995.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
