<p><b>Trzebca</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[ˈtʂɛpt͡sa]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Nowa_Brze%C5%BAnica" title="Gmina Nowa Brzeźnica">Gmina Nowa Brzeźnica</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Paj%C4%99czno_County" title="Pajęczno County">Pajęczno County</a>, <a href="/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA_Voivodeship" title="Łódź Voivodeship">Łódź Voivodeship</a>, in central Poland. It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of <a href="/wiki/Nowa_Brze%C5%BAnica" title="Nowa Brzeźnica">Nowa Brzeźnica</a>, 14 km (9 mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Paj%C4%99czno" title="Pajęczno">Pajęczno</a>, and 88 km (55 mi) south of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA" title="Łódź">Łódź</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The village has a population of 65.
</p>
