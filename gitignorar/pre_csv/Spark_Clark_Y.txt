<p><b>Michael Keith "Spark" Clark</b> is a former <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> running back who played for one season for the <a href="/wiki/Pittsburgh_Steelers" title="Pittsburgh Steelers">Pittsburgh Steelers</a> 
of the <a href="/wiki/National_Football_League" title="National Football League">National Football League</a> (NFL).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Clark played <a href="/wiki/College_football" title="College football">college football</a> at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Akron_University" title="Akron University">Akron University</a> for <a href="/wiki/Akron_Zips_football" title="Akron Zips football">Akron Zips football</a> team.
</p>
