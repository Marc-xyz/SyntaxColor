<p><b>Natural cytotoxicity triggering receptor 3</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Protein" title="Protein">protein</a> that in humans is encoded by the <i>NCR3</i> <a href="/wiki/Gene" title="Gene">gene</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid8824804_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-pmid8824804-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid11782277_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-pmid11782277-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-entrez_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-entrez-5">[5]</a></sup> NCR3 has also been designated as <b>CD337</b> (<a href="/wiki/Cluster_of_differentiation" title="Cluster of differentiation">cluster of differentiation</a> 337) and as <b>NKp30</b>.
</p>
