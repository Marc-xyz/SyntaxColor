<p><b>Qaziabad</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">قاضي اباد</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Qāz̤īābād</b>) is a village in <a href="/wiki/Qaleh-ye_Khvajeh_Rural_District" title="Qaleh-ye Khvajeh Rural District">Qaleh-ye Khvajeh Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Andika_County)" title="Central District (Andika County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Andika_County" title="Andika County">Andika County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Khuzestan_Province" title="Khuzestan Province">Khuzestan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 106, in 14 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
