<p><b>Chí Linh</b> is a <a href="/wiki/District-level_town_(Vietnam)" title="District-level town (Vietnam)">city</a> in <a href="/wiki/H%E1%BA%A3i_D%C6%B0%C6%A1ng_Province" title="Hải Dương Province">Hải Dương Province</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Red_River_Delta" title="Red River Delta">Red River Delta</a> region of <a href="/wiki/Vietnam" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>. In 2010, Chí Linh <a href="/wiki/District_(Vietnam)" title="District (Vietnam)">District</a> was upgraded to a <a href="/wiki/District-level_town_(Vietnam)" title="District-level town (Vietnam)">district-level town</a> and in 2019, Chí Linh town became the second <a href="/wiki/Provincial_city_(Vietnam)" title="Provincial city (Vietnam)">provincial city</a> of Hải Dương Province. Chí Linh is known as a very beautiful country with six rivers converging, with hills, mountains, forests ... The capital of the city is Sao Đỏ (Red Star).
</p><p>There are many beautiful temples and pagodas in Chi Linh. Many of them concerning with Vietnam's National Heroes: <a href="/wiki/Tr%E1%BA%A7n_H%C6%B0ng_%C4%90%E1%BA%A1o" title="Trần Hưng Đạo">Trần Hưng Đạo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nguy%E1%BB%85n_Tr%C3%A3i" title="Nguyễn Trãi">Nguyễn Trãi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chu_V%C4%83n_An" title="Chu Văn An">Chu Văn An</a>. Côn Sơn and Kiếp Bạc pagodas are famous all over Vietnam.
</p><p>One of the best golf courses in North Vietnam, Star Golf, was opened in Sao Đỏ in 2003.
</p><p>Pha Lai, the biggest thermal power plant in North Vietnam is located in this city.
</p><p>As of 2019 the city had a population of 220,421.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Statoids_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Statoids-1">[1]</a></sup> The city covers an area of 282.91 km². The city capital lies at Sao Đỏ ward.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Statoids_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Statoids-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
