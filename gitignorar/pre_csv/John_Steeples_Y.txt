<p><b>John Steeples</b> (28 April 1959 – 20 March 2019) was an <a href="/wiki/English_people" title="English people">English</a> former professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> His death was announced on 21 March 2019.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
