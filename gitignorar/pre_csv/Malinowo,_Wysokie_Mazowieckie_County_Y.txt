<p><b>Malinowo</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[maliˈnɔvɔ]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Klukowo" title="Gmina Klukowo">Gmina Klukowo</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Wysokie_Mazowieckie_County" title="Wysokie Mazowieckie County">Wysokie Mazowieckie County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Podlaskie_Voivodeship" title="Podlaskie Voivodeship">Podlaskie Voivodeship</a>, in north-eastern Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Klukowo,_Wysokie_Mazowieckie_County" title="Klukowo, Wysokie Mazowieckie County">Klukowo</a>, 22 km (14 mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Wysokie_Mazowieckie" title="Wysokie Mazowieckie">Wysokie Mazowieckie</a>, and 63 km (39 mi) south-west of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Bia%C5%82ystok" title="Białystok">Białystok</a>.
</p>
