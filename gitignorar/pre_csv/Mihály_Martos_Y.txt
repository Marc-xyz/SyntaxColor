<p><b>Mihály Martos</b> (born 1 February 1945) is a Hungarian <a href="/wiki/Speed_skating" title="Speed skating">speed skater</a>. He competed at the <a href="/wiki/1964_Winter_Olympics" title="1964 Winter Olympics">1964 Winter Olympics</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1968_Winter_Olympics" title="1968 Winter Olympics">1968 Winter Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
