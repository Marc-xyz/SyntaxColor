<p>The <b>St Andrews Angels</b>, also known as the <b>University of St Andrews Angel Network</b>, or the <b>University of St Andrews Alumni Angel Network</b> is an angel investment network managed by alumni from the <a href="/wiki/University_of_St_Andrews" title="University of St Andrews">University of St Andrews</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The network is led by a group of alumni entrepreneurs, financiers and faculty within the United States and United Kingdom who promote Scottish ventures and facilitate deal flow within its network, as well as provide investment capital, strategic advice and mentoring  to early-stage ventures.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Organisation">Organisation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Andrews_Angels&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Organisation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The St Andrews Angels is led by two independent boards, located in both London and New York, each composed of business executives, financiers and entrepreneurs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> Each board performs deal discovery, whilst members independently facilitate any equity investments. The group seeks to promote Scottish ventures and technology outside of the country &amp; supports events centred upon fostering greater linkages between London, New York &amp; Scotland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Andrews_Angels&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The group was founded in 2013 by University of St Andrews students and alumni.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Membership">Membership</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Andrews_Angels&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Membership">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Membership to the St Andrews Angels is open to all University of St Andrews alumni, faculty and affiliated individuals. Non-affiliated individuals may be permitted to join if sponsored by a current member.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> To join as an investor however, individuals must qualify as having accredited investor status under Regulation D of the Securities and Exchange Commission in the US. Investor membership in the UK requires certified <a href="/wiki/High-net-worth_individual" title="High-net-worth individual">high-net-worth individual</a> status or self-certified sophisticated investor status as detailed by the Financial Conduct Authority. Investor members must participate in one investment annually.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Education">Education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=St_Andrews_Angels&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The St Andrews Angels provides educational seminars, concerning entrepreneurship and the capital raising process, to students at the University.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> The group also organises regular opportunities for University students to attend pitch events in both St Andrews and further afield through their sister organisations and partners. The group also runs a series of lectures and keynote speeches centred around economics and entrepreneurship. Former notable speakers include <a href="/wiki/David_S._Rose" title="David S. Rose">David S. Rose</a>.
</p>
