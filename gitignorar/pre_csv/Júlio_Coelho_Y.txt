<p><b>Júlio Manuel Pires Coelho</b> (born 18 July 1984) is a Portuguese <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays for <a href="/wiki/U.D._Oliveirense" title="U.D. Oliveirense">U.D. Oliveirense</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Goalkeeper_(association_football)" title="Goalkeeper (association football)">goalkeeper</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Football_career">Football career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=J%C3%BAlio_Coelho&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Football career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Pa%C3%A7os_de_Ferreira" title="Paços de Ferreira">Paços de Ferreira</a>, Coelho spent the vast majority of his career with hometown club <a href="/wiki/F.C._Pa%C3%A7os_de_Ferreira" title="F.C. Paços de Ferreira">F.C. Paços de Ferreira</a>. After playing through the youth ranks since the age of 11, he made the first team in 2003.
</p><p>After two years and only one <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Segunda_Liga" title="Segunda Liga">Segunda Liga</a> appearance,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Coelho was loaned to lowly <a href="/wiki/S.L._Nelas" title="S.L. Nelas">S.L. Nelas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rebordosa_A.C." title="Rebordosa A.C.">Rebordosa AC</a>. After some good performances with the former he was recalled by Paços, but again only served as a backup, consecutively to <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Correia_(footballer,_born_1974)" title="Pedro Correia (footballer, born 1974)">Pedro</a> – another youth product born in the city – and Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Peterson_Pe%C3%A7anha" title="Peterson Peçanha">Peterson Peçanha</a>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> the first of his 20 <a href="/wiki/Primeira_Liga" title="Primeira Liga">Primeira Liga</a> matches during his spell at the <i><a href="/wiki/Est%C3%A1dio_da_Mata_Real" title="Estádio da Mata Real">Estádio da Mata Real</a></i> took place on 6 January 2008, in a 2–1 home win against <a href="/wiki/U.D._Leiria" title="U.D. Leiria">U.D. Leiria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>Released in the summer of 2012 after <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_Liga_de_Honra" title="2011–12 Liga de Honra">one season</a> in the second division with <a href="/wiki/C.F._Os_Belenenses" title="C.F. Os Belenenses">C.F. Os Belenenses</a>, Coelho resumed his career in that level, with <a href="/wiki/F.C._Penafiel" title="F.C. Penafiel">F.C. Penafiel</a>. He only missed one game from 42 in <a href="/wiki/2013%E2%80%9314_Segunda_Liga" title="2013–14 Segunda Liga">2013–14</a>, as the latter returned to the top flight after an eight-year absence; in January 2014 and June 2016, he agreed to contract extensions.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
