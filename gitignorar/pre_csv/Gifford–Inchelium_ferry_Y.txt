	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Gifford–Inchelium ferry</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>

<p>The <b>Gifford–Inchelium ferry</b>, also known as <b>GIF</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Ferry" title="Ferry">ferry</a> across the <a href="/wiki/Columbia_River" title="Columbia River">Columbia River</a> in <a href="/wiki/Washington_(state)" title="Washington (state)">Washington state</a>.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Confederated_Tribes_of_the_Colville_Reservation" title="Confederated Tribes of the Colville Reservation">Colville Confederated Tribes</a> operate this ferry across <a href="/wiki/Franklin_D._Roosevelt_Lake" title="Franklin D. Roosevelt Lake">Roosevelt Lake</a> on the upper Columbia.  It connects <a href="/wiki/Inchelium,_Washington" title="Inchelium, Washington">Inchelium, Washington</a>, to <a href="/wiki/Washington_State_Route_25" title="Washington State Route 25">State Route 25</a> across the river. The fare to ride is free.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-free_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-free-1">[1]</a></sup> The weight limit is 40 tons.
</p>
