<p><b>Walther</b> is a masculine given name and a surname. It is a <a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a> form of <a href="/wiki/Walter_(name)" title="Walter (name)">Walter</a>, which is derived from the <a href="/wiki/Old_High_German" title="Old High German">Old High German</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Walthari" title="Walthari">Walthari</a></i>, containing the elements <i>wald</i> "rule", "brightness" and <i>hari</i>—"army, warrior".
</p>
