	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Mali national cricket team</h1>

<p>The <b>Malian national cricket team</b> is the team that represents the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Mali" title="Republic of Mali">Republic of Mali</a> in <a href="/wiki/International_cricket" title="International cricket">international cricket</a>. They are an associate member<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Assoc_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Assoc-1">[1]</a></sup> of the <a href="/wiki/International_Cricket_Council" title="International Cricket Council">International Cricket Council</a> (ICC), and will eventually represent the country of <a href="/wiki/Mali" title="Mali">Mali</a> in international <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricket</a> matches. They are yet to play any such matches although they have played several matches. They became a member of the ICC in 2005.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2018, the ICC decided to grant full <a href="/wiki/Twenty20_International" title="Twenty20 International">Twenty20 International</a> (T20I) status to all its members. Therefore, all <a href="/wiki/Twenty20" title="Twenty20">Twenty20</a> matches played between Mali and other <a href="/wiki/List_of_International_Cricket_Council_members" title="List of International Cricket Council members">ICC members</a> after 1 January 2019 will be a full T20I.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
