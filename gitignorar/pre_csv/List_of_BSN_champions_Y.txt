	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of BSN champions</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>List of BSN champions</b> are the champions of the <a href="/wiki/Baloncesto_Superior_Nacional" title="Baloncesto Superior Nacional">Baloncesto Superior Nacional</a> (BSN) Championship Finals, which is the championship series for the BSN and the conclusion of the professional basketball's league tournament in <a href="/wiki/Puerto_Rico" title="Puerto Rico">Puerto Rico</a>. Most Finals have been played in a <a href="/wiki/Playoff_format#Best-of-seven_playoff" title="Playoff format">best-of-seven format</a>. The winning team of the series receives the championship trophy.
</p><p>Three teams are tied with the most championships won at 14. These teams are the <a href="/wiki/Atl%C3%A9ticos_de_San_Germ%C3%A1n" title="Atléticos de San Germán">Atléticos de San Germán</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leones_de_Ponce_(basketball)" title="Leones de Ponce (basketball)">Leones de Ponce</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Vaqueros_de_Bayam%C3%B3n" title="Vaqueros de Bayamón">Vaqueros de Bayamón</a>. The three teams have won a combined 42 of 87 championships.
</p>
