	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of cultural property of national significance in Switzerland: Obwalden</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This list contains all <a href="/wiki/Cultural_property" title="Cultural property">cultural property</a> of national significance (class A) in the <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_Obwalden" title="Canton of Obwalden">canton of Obwalden</a> from the 2009 <a href="/wiki/Swiss_Inventory_of_Cultural_Property_of_National_and_Regional_Significance" title="Swiss Inventory of Cultural Property of National and Regional Significance">Swiss Inventory of Cultural Property of National and Regional Significance</a>. It is sorted by <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_Switzerland" title="Municipalities of Switzerland">municipality</a> and contains 22 individual buildings, 6 collections, 3 archaeological finds and 1 special site or object.
</p><p>The geographic coordinates provided are in the <a href="/wiki/Swiss_coordinate_system" title="Swiss coordinate system">Swiss coordinate system</a> as given in the Inventory.
