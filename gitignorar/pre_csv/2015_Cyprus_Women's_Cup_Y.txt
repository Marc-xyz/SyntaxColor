<p>The <b>2015 Cyprus Women's Cup</b> was the eighth edition of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cyprus_Women%27s_Cup" title="Cyprus Women's Cup">Cyprus Women's Cup</a>, an invitational <a href="/wiki/Women%27s_association_football" title="Women's association football">women's football</a> tournament held annually in <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a>. It took place from 4–11 March 2015.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Group_stage">Group stage</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Cyprus_Women%27s_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Group stage">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Teams were placed into three groups of four.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_women%27s_national_football_team" title="New Zealand women's national football team">New Zealand</a> were originally drawn in Group C, but later withdrew and were replaced by <a href="/wiki/South_Africa_women%27s_national_football_team" title="South Africa women's national football team">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tie-breaking_criteria">Tie-breaking criteria</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Cyprus_Women%27s_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tie-breaking criteria">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>For the group stage of this tournament, where two or more teams in a group <a href="/wiki/Tiebreaker" title="Tiebreaker">tied on an equal number of points</a>, the finishing positions will be determined by the following tie-breaking criteria in the following order:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<ol><li>number of points obtained in the matches among the teams in question</li>
<li>goal difference in all the group matches</li>
<li>number of goals scored in all the group matches</li>
<li>drawing of lots</li></ol>
