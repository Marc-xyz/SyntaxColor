<p><b>Melody</b> is the name of a residential high-rise completed in May 2016 in the <a href="/wiki/Arts_%26_Entertainment_District" title="Arts &amp; Entertainment District">Arts &amp; Entertainment District</a> neighborhood of <a href="/wiki/Miami" title="Miami">Miami</a>, <a href="/wiki/Florida" title="Florida">Florida</a>, U.S.A. The building rises about 467 feet (142 m)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-emporis_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-emporis-1">[1]</a></sup> with 36 floors and contains nearly 500 rental units.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-exmiami_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-exmiami-2">[2]</a></sup> It is located about one block from the <a href="/wiki/Adrienne_Arsht_Center_station" title="Adrienne Arsht Center station">Adrienne Arsht Center/Omni</a> <a href="/wiki/Metromover" title="Metromover">Metromover</a> and <a href="/wiki/Metrobus_(Miami-Dade_County)" title="Metrobus (Miami-Dade County)">Metrobus</a> hub. 
</p>
