<p>The <b>Donaldson Smith's sparrow-weaver</b> (<i>Plocepasser donaldsoni</i>) is a species of <a href="/wiki/Bird" title="Bird">bird</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Ploceidae" title="Ploceidae">Ploceidae</a>. It is found in Africa from southern Ethiopia to central Kenya and southern Somalia.
</p>
