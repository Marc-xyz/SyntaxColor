<p>The <b>2011 World Sambo Championships</b> were held in <a href="/wiki/Vilnius" title="Vilnius">Vilnius</a>, Lithuania on November 11 to 13 for men's and women's sport <a href="/wiki/Sambo_(martial_art)" title="Sambo (martial art)">Sambo</a> and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Combat_Sambo" title="Combat Sambo">Combat Sambo</a> championships was held in <a href="/wiki/Siemens_Arena" title="Siemens Arena">Siemens Arena</a>.
</p>
