<p><b>Shorncliffe railway station</b> is the terminus station of the <a href="/wiki/Shorncliffe_railway_line" title="Shorncliffe railway line">Shorncliffe line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Queensland" title="Queensland">Queensland</a>, Australia. It serves the <a href="/wiki/Brisbane" title="Brisbane">Brisbane</a> suburb of <a href="/wiki/Shorncliffe,_Queensland" title="Shorncliffe, Queensland">Shorncliffe</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shorncliffe_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The railway line from Brisbane to Sandgate opened in 1882, and the terminal station, located near Curlew Street, was named Sandgate. <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The line was extended to Shorncliffe in 1897, and the name Sandgate was transferred to the new terminal station.  <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In 1938 it was renamed Shorncliffe.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>The line allowed Brisbane residents to travel to the water of <a href="/wiki/Moreton_Bay" title="Moreton Bay">Moreton Bay</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Shorncliffe,_Queensland" title="Shorncliffe, Queensland">Shorncliffe</a> shoreline.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Beyond the station lie two sidings used to stable trains overnight.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Services">Services</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shorncliffe_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Services">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shorncliffe is the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminus_station" title="Terminus station">terminus station</a> for <a href="/wiki/Shorncliffe_railway_line" title="Shorncliffe railway line">Shorncliffe line</a> services to and from <a href="/wiki/Roma_Street_railway_station" title="Roma Street railway station">Roma Street</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cannon_Hill_railway_station" title="Cannon Hill railway station">Cannon Hill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Manly_railway_station" title="Manly railway station">Manly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cleveland_railway_station" title="Cleveland railway station">Cleveland</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
