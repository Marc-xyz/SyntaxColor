<p><b>Killay South</b> (<a href="/wiki/Welsh_language" title="Welsh language">Welsh</a>: <i lang="cy">Cilâ - De</i>) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electoral_ward" title="Electoral ward">electoral ward</a> in the City and County of <a href="/wiki/Swansea" title="Swansea">Swansea</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wales" title="Wales">Wales</a>.  The ward is mainly rural and consists of some or all of the following areas: <a href="/wiki/Dunvant" title="Dunvant">Dunvant</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ilston" title="Ilston">Ilston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Killay,_Swansea" title="Killay, Swansea">Killay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sketty" title="Sketty">Sketty</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Upper_Killay" title="Upper Killay">Upper Killay</a> in the parliamentary constituency of <a href="/wiki/Swansea_West_(UK_Parliament_constituency)" title="Swansea West (UK Parliament constituency)">Swansea West</a>.
</p><p>It is bounded by the wards of <a href="/wiki/Dunvant_(electoral_ward)" title="Dunvant (electoral ward)">Dunvant</a> and <a href="/wiki/Killay_North" title="Killay North">Killay North</a> to the north; <a href="/wiki/Sketty_(electoral_ward)" title="Sketty (electoral ward)">Sketty</a> to the east; <a href="/wiki/Mayals_(electoral_ward)" title="Mayals (electoral ward)">Mayals</a> to the south; and <a href="/wiki/Fairwood,_Swansea" title="Fairwood, Swansea">Fairwood</a> to the west.
