	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Jarqavieh Sofla District</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Not to be confused with <a href="/wiki/Jarqavieh_Sofla_Rural_District" title="Jarqavieh Sofla Rural District">Jarqavieh Sofla Rural District</a>.</div>
<p><b>Jarqooyeh Sofla District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">بخش جرقویه سفلی</span>‎; Lower Jarqavieh District) is a district (<a href="/wiki/Bakhsh" title="Bakhsh">bakhsh</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Isfahan_County" title="Isfahan County">Isfahan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Isfahan_Province" title="Isfahan Province">Isfahan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 20,970, in 5,473 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  The District has three cities: <a href="/wiki/Nikabad" title="Nikabad">Nikabad</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nasrabad,_Isfahan" title="Nasrabad, Isfahan">Nasrabad</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Mohammadabad,_Isfahan" title="Mohammadabad, Isfahan">Mohammadabad</a>. The District has two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rural_Districts_of_Iran" title="Rural Districts of Iran">rural districts</a> (<i>dehestan</i>): <a href="/wiki/Jarqavieh_Sofla_Rural_District" title="Jarqavieh Sofla Rural District">Jarqavieh Sofla Rural District</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jarqavieh_Vosta_Rural_District" title="Jarqavieh Vosta Rural District">Jarqavieh Vosta Rural District</a>.
</p><p>Nikabad is the most famous and well-known city in this district.
</p>
