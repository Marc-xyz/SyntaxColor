<p><b>Chandarlapadu</b> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Krishna_district" title="Krishna district">Krishna district</a> of the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> state of <a href="/wiki/Andhra_Pradesh" title="Andhra Pradesh">Andhra Pradesh</a>. It is located in <a href="/wiki/Chandarlapadu_mandal" title="Chandarlapadu mandal">Chandarlapadu mandal</a> of <a href="/wiki/Vijayawada_revenue_division" title="Vijayawada revenue division">Vijayawada revenue division</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> It is one of the villages in the mandal to be a part of <a href="/wiki/Andhra_Pradesh_Capital_Region" title="Andhra Pradesh Capital Region">Andhra Pradesh Capital Region</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
