		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Maddin Creek</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Stream" title="Stream">stream</a> in <a href="/wiki/Washington_County,_Missouri" title="Washington County, Missouri">Washington County</a> in the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is a tributary of the <a href="/wiki/Big_River_(Missouri)" title="Big River (Missouri)">Big River</a>.
</p><p>Maddin Creek, historically spelled "Madden Branch", has the name of the local Madden family.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> 
</p>
