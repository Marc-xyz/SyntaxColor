<p><b>Popovac</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_Serbia" title="Municipalities of Serbia">municipality</a> of <a href="/wiki/Veliko_Gradi%C5%A1te" title="Veliko Gradište">Veliko Gradište</a>, <a href="/wiki/Serbia" title="Serbia">Serbia</a>. According to the 2002 census, the village has a population of 190 people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
