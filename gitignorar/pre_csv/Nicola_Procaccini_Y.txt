<p><b>Nicola Procaccini</b> (born 21 January 1976 in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>) is an Italian politician and <a href="/wiki/Member_of_the_European_Parliament" title="Member of the European Parliament">Member of the European Parliament</a> since <a href="/wiki/2019_European_Parliament_election_in_Italy" title="2019 European Parliament election in Italy">2019</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>He served as Mayor of <a href="/wiki/Terracina" title="Terracina">Terracina</a> to 30 May 2011 to 6 May 2015. On 21 June 2016 he was again re-elected Mayor of Terracina.
</p>
