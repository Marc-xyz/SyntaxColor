<p>In <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Enzymology" title="Enzymology">enzymology</a>, a <b><a href="/wiki/Cellodextrin" title="Cellodextrin">cellodextrin</a> phosphorylase</b> (<a href="/wiki/Enzyme_Commission_number" title="Enzyme Commission number">EC</a> <a class="external text" href="https://enzyme.expasy.org/EC/2.4.1.49" rel="nofollow">2.4.1.49</a>) is an <a href="/wiki/Enzyme" title="Enzyme">enzyme</a> that <a href="/wiki/Catalysis" title="Catalysis">catalyzes</a> the <a href="/wiki/Chemical_reaction" title="Chemical reaction">chemical reaction</a>
</p>
<dl><dd>(1,4-beta-D-<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Glucosyl" title="Glucosyl">glucosyl</a>)n + phosphate <span class="mwe-math-element"><span class="mwe-math-mathml-inline mwe-math-mathml-a11y" style="display: none;"><math alttext="{\displaystyle \rightleftharpoons }" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1998/Math/MathML">
  <semantics>
    <mrow class="MJX-TeXAtom-ORD">
      <mstyle displaystyle="true" scriptlevel="0">
        <mo class="MJX-variant" stretchy="false">⇌<!-- ⇌ --></mo>
      </mstyle>
    </mrow>
    <annotation encoding="application/x-tex">{\displaystyle \rightleftharpoons }</annotation>
  </semantics>
</math></span><img alt="\rightleftharpoons " aria-hidden="true" class="mwe-math-fallback-image-inline" src="https://wikimedia.org/api/rest_v1/media/math/render/svg/1c37b981df851b9e54e489e017b1481e37d418f3" style="vertical-align: -0.338ex; width:2.324ex; height:1.843ex;"/></span> (1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n-1 + alpha-D-glucose 1-phosphate</dd></dl>
<p>Thus, the two <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Substrate_(biochemistry)" title="Substrate (biochemistry)">substrates</a> of this enzyme are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n" title="(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n">(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n</a> and <a href="/wiki/Phosphate" title="Phosphate">phosphate</a>, whereas its two <a href="/wiki/Product_(chemistry)" title="Product (chemistry)">products</a> are <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n-1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n-1 (page does not exist)">(1,4-beta-D-glucosyl)n-1</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Alpha-D-glucose_1-phosphate&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Alpha-D-glucose 1-phosphate (page does not exist)">alpha-D-glucose 1-phosphate</a>.
</p><p>This enzyme belongs to GH (glycoside hydrolases) family 94.  The <a href="/wiki/List_of_enzymes" title="List of enzymes">systematic name</a> of this enzyme class is <b>1,4-beta-D-oligo-D-glucan:phosphate alpha-D-glucosyltransferase</b>. This enzyme is also called <b>beta-1,4-oligoglucan:orthophosphate glucosyltransferase</b>.
</p>
