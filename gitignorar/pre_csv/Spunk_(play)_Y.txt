<p><i><b>Spunk</b></i> is a play by <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Playwright" title="Playwright">playwright</a> <a href="/wiki/George_C._Wolfe" title="George C. Wolfe">George C. Wolfe</a> and is an adaptation of three stories by <a href="/wiki/Zora_Neale_Hurston" title="Zora Neale Hurston">Zora Neale Hurston</a>.  Wolfe won a 1989 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Obie_award" title="Obie award">Obie award</a> for best off-Broadway director for Spunk.
</p>
