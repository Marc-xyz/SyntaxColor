<p><i><b>Kalimagone</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysian</a> <a href="/wiki/Linyphiidae" title="Linyphiidae">dwarf spiders</a> first described by A. V. Tanasevitch in 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tana2017_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Tana2017-2">[2]</a></sup> As of April 2019<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Kalimagone&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> it contains only two species from <a href="/wiki/Borneo" title="Borneo">Borneo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NMBE_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-NMBE-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
