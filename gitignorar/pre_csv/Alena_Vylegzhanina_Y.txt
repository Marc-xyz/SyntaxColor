<p><b>Alyona Viktorovna Vylegzhanina</b> (Алёна Викторовна Вылегжанина, born 14 August 1987)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IOC_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-1">[1]</a></sup> is a Russian <a href="/wiki/Water_polo" title="Water polo">water polo</a> player. She was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Russia_women%27s_national_water_polo_team" title="Russia women's national water polo team">Russia women's national water polo team</a>, playing as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Water_polo_positions" title="Water polo positions">centre back</a>. She was a part of the team at the <a href="/wiki/Water_polo_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament" title="Water polo at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament">2008 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IOC_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On club level she played for <a href="/wiki/Uralochka_Zlatoust" title="Uralochka Zlatoust">Uralochka Zlatoust</a> in Russia.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
