<p>The <b>women's 200 metre <a href="/wiki/Breaststroke" title="Breaststroke">breaststroke</a></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Swimming_(sport)" title="Swimming (sport)">swimming</a> event held as part of the <a href="/wiki/Swimming_at_the_1928_Summer_Olympics" title="Swimming at the 1928 Summer Olympics">swimming at the 1928 Summer Olympics</a> programme.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup> It was the second appearance of the event, which was established in 1924. The competition was held from Tuesday to Thursday, 7 to 9 August 1928.
</p><p>Twenty-one swimmers from twelve nations competed.
</p>
