<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Goldhawks&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Goldhawks's name is taken from Goldhawk Road near Shepherd's Bush in West <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The vocalist and guitarist are brothers.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Frontman Bobby Cook had initially started a career as a solo singer/songwriter,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> releasing the singles "Gone So Far", "Deja Vu" and "Homesick" in 2007 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dance_To_The_Radio" title="Dance To The Radio">Dance To The Radio</a> record label<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and playing with <a href="/wiki/Laura_Marling" title="Laura Marling">Laura Marling</a>, <a href="/wiki/Noah_and_the_Whale" title="Noah and the Whale">Noah and the Whale</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jamie_T" title="Jamie T">Jamie T</a> before forming the band.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The band's debut, <i>Trick of Light</i>, arrived on <a href="/wiki/Mercury_Records" title="Mercury Records">Mercury Records</a> late in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The group spent part of Autumn 2010 touring <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> and in January 2011 announced they would be releasing their debut album in March.  Having released Trick Of The Light in March 2011 the band released Keep The Fire EP through iTunes 3 April 2011 consisting of 5 mixes of the track. There have been no further releases; the band's Facebook page has had no updates since 2011 and their website is defunct.
</p>
