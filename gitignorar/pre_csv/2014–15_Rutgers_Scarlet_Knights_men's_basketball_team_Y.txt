<p>The <b>2014–15 Rutgers Scarlet Knights men's basketball team</b> represented <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rutgers-New_Brunswick" title="Rutgers-New Brunswick">Rutgers University</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2014%E2%80%9315_NCAA_Division_I_men%27s_basketball_season" title="2014–15 NCAA Division I men's basketball season">2014–15 NCAA Division I men's basketball season</a>. The Scarlet Knights, led by second year head coach <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Jordan_(basketball)" title="Eddie Jordan (basketball)">Eddie Jordan</a>, played their home games at the <a href="/wiki/Louis_Brown_Athletic_Center" title="Louis Brown Athletic Center">Louis Brown Athletic Center</a>, better known as <i>The RAC</i>, as first year members of the <a href="/wiki/Big_Ten_Conference" title="Big Ten Conference">Big Ten Conference</a>.
</p>

<p>They finished the season 12–21, 5–13 in AAC play to finish in seventh place. They advanced to the quarterfinals of the <a href="/wiki/2014_American_Athletic_Conference_Men%27s_Basketball_Tournament" title="2014 American Athletic Conference Men's Basketball Tournament">AAC Tournament</a> where they lost to Louisville.
