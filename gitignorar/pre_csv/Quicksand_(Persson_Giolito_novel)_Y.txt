<p><i><b>Quicksand</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>: <i><b>Störst av allt</b></i>) is a novel by <a href="/wiki/Malin_Persson_Giolito" title="Malin Persson Giolito">Malin Persson Giolito</a>. It was originally published on 7 July, 2016 by <a href="/wiki/Wahlstr%C3%B6m_%26_Widstrand" title="Wahlström &amp; Widstrand">Wahlström &amp; Widstrand</a> in <a href="/wiki/Swedish_language" title="Swedish language">Swedish</a>. An <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a> translation was published on 7 March, 2017 by <a href="/wiki/Other_Press" title="Other Press">Other Press</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Quicksand_(Persson_Giolito_novel)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The book received the 2016 <a href="/wiki/Best_Swedish_Crime_Novel_Award" title="Best Swedish Crime Novel Award">Best Swedish Crime Novel Award</a> and the 2017 <a href="/wiki/Glass_Key_award" title="Glass Key award">Glass Key award</a>. <i><a href="/wiki/Kirkus_Reviews" title="Kirkus Reviews">Kirkus Reviews</a></i> said that "Giolito gives us the unsettling monologue of a teenage girl as she works her way through her role in murder. It is a splendid work of fiction."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Adaptation">Adaptation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Quicksand_(Persson_Giolito_novel)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Adaptation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In September 2017, it was announced that <a href="/wiki/Netflix" title="Netflix">Netflix</a> had ordered an adaption of the novel to be developed into an eponymous series, <i><a href="/wiki/Quicksand_(TV_series)" title="Quicksand (TV series)">Quicksand</a></i>, and would partner with <a href="/wiki/FLX" title="FLX">FLX</a> to produce the series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-deadline_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-deadline-2">[2]</a></sup> The series premiered on April 5, 2019.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
