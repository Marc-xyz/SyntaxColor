<p><b>Nate Allen Sallie</b> (born October 28, 1976) is an American singer-songwriter, recording artist, multi-instrumentalist, and concert performer signed with <a href="/wiki/Curb_Records" title="Curb Records">Curb Records</a>. He is best known for his pop melodies, transparent lyrics, numerous radio hits as a solo artist, and frontman for Grammy Nominated band <a href="/wiki/NewSong" title="NewSong">NewSong</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nate_Sallie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Recording artist, concert performer and singer/songwriter Nate Sallie has won numerous music awards, penned multiple radio hits, served as lead singer for Grammy nominated band Newsong, toured the globe, earned a bachelor's degree in Music and continues to study and expand his knowledge of music and the music business.
</p><p>Nate launched his career in college coffee houses as Nate Sallie and the <i>Melt Like Sugar Orchestra</i>. He was offered three record deals before landing with Curb Records and recording his first project Inside Out (2003), which yielded numerous radio hits. In addition to having songs on WoW and Absolute Smash Hit Compilations, Nate has contributed songs to numerous best-selling recording artists.
</p>
