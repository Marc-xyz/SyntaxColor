<p><b>Moses Chavula</b> (born 8 August 1985) is a <a href="/wiki/Malawi" title="Malawi">Malawian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">footballer</a> who plays as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defender_(football)" title="Defender (football)">defender</a> for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MTL_Wanderers" title="MTL Wanderers">MTL Wanderers</a>.
</p><p>A longtime member of the <a href="/wiki/Malawi_national_football_team" title="Malawi national football team">Malawi national football team</a>, Chavula has competed for his national team since 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Chavula is a left-footed player and plays as left-back for his team and Malawi.
</p>
