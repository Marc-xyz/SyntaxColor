<p><b>Kodam VI/Mulawarman</b> (<i>6th Military Regional Command/Mulawarman</i>) is a military territorial command (<a href="/wiki/Military_district" title="Military district">military district</a>) of the <a href="/wiki/Indonesian_Army" title="Indonesian Army">Indonesian Army</a>. It has been in active service as the local division for the provinces of <a href="/wiki/North_Kalimantan" title="North Kalimantan">North Kalimantan</a>, <a href="/wiki/East_Kalimantan" title="East Kalimantan">East Kalimantan</a> and <a href="/wiki/South_Kalimantan" title="South Kalimantan">South Kalimantan</a> (from 1958-1985 and from 2010 to present).
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Brief_history">Brief history</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kodam_VI/Mulawarman&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Brief history">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Kodam_VI_Mulawarman,_Balikpapan.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1080" data-file-width="1920" decoding="async" height="141" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e3/Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg/250px-Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e3/Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg/375px-Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e3/Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg/500px-Kodam_VI_Mulawarman%2C_Balikpapan.jpg 2x" width="250"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Kodam_VI_Mulawarman,_Balikpapan.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>VI Mulawarman Military district command HQ, situated in <a href="/wiki/Balikpapan" title="Balikpapan">Balikpapan</a>, <a href="/wiki/East_Kalimantan" title="East Kalimantan">East Kalimantan</a></div></div></div>
<p>The 6th MRC, then as the <b>10th Military Region Command</b> (<i>Komando Daerah Militer X</i>)  holding the <i>Lambung Mangkurat</i> honorific was activated in 1958 with the headquarters in <a href="/wiki/Banjarmasin" title="Banjarmasin">Banjarmasin</a> and serving South and Central Kalimantan, while the 9th MRC (<i>Komando Daerah Militer IX</i>), holding the <i>Mulawarman</i> honorific, was based in <a href="/wiki/Samarinda" title="Samarinda">Samarinda</a> and with East Kalimantan as its AOR. Both formations were raised in response to the threat faced by the <a href="/wiki/Permesta" title="Permesta">Permesta</a> revolt the same year. This format lasted until the 1984 reorganization of military regions by the Commander of the National Armed Forces, when the 6th MRC was granted the <i>Tanjungpura</i> honorific when it merged with the 10th and 9th MRCs with a new garrison in <a href="/wiki/Balikpapan" title="Balikpapan">Balikpapan</a>. In 2010, the INAF split its military region command for Indonesian parts of <a href="/wiki/Borneo" title="Borneo">Borneo</a> into two<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>, thus the 6th MRC, now with a smaller AOR including North, East and South Kalimantan and with its garrison still in Balikpapan, was granted the <i>Mulawaman</i> honorific title.
</p>
