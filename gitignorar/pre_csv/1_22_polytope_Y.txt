<p>In 6-dimensional <a href="/wiki/Geometry" title="Geometry">geometry</a>, the <b>1<sub>22</sub></b> polytope is a <a href="/wiki/Uniform_polytope" title="Uniform polytope">uniform polytope</a>, constructed from the <a href="/wiki/E6_(mathematics)" title="E6 (mathematics)">E<sub>6</sub></a> group. It was first published in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/E._L._Elte" title="E. L. Elte">E. L. Elte</a>'s 1912 listing of semiregular polytopes, named as V<sub>72</sub> (for its 72 vertices).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter_symbol" title="Coxeter symbol">Coxeter symbol</a> is <b>1<sub>22</sub></b>, describing its bifurcating <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter-Dynkin_diagram" title="Coxeter-Dynkin diagram">Coxeter-Dynkin diagram</a>, with a single ring on the end of the 1-node sequence. There are two rectifications of the 1<sub>22</sub>, construcated by positions points on the elements of 1<sub>22</sub>. The <b>rectified 1<sub>22</sub></b> is constructed by points at the mid-edges of the <b>1<sub>22</sub></b>. The <b>birectified 1<sub>22</sub></b> is constructed by points at the triangle face centers of the <b>1<sub>22</sub></b>.
</p><p>These polytopes are from a family of 39 convex <a href="/wiki/Uniform_6-polytope" title="Uniform 6-polytope">uniform polytopes in 6-dimensions</a>, made of <a href="/wiki/Uniform_5-polytope" title="Uniform 5-polytope">uniform polytope</a> facets and <a href="/wiki/Vertex_figure" title="Vertex figure">vertex figures</a>, defined by all permutations of rings in this <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter-Dynkin_diagram" title="Coxeter-Dynkin diagram">Coxeter-Dynkin diagram</a>: <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" <p>The 1_22 polytope contains 72 vertices, and 54 <a href="/wiki/5-demicube" title="5-demicube">5-demicubic</a> facets. It has a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Birectified_5-simplex" title="Birectified 5-simplex">birectified 5-simplex</a> <a href="/wiki/Vertex_figure" title="Vertex figure">vertex figure</a>. Its 72 vertices represent the root vectors of the <a href="/wiki/Simple_Lie_group" title="Simple Lie group">simple Lie group</a> <a href="/wiki/E6_(mathematics)" title="E6 (mathematics)">E<sub>6</sub></a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Construction">Construction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1_22_polytope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Construction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>It is created by a <a href="/wiki/Wythoff_construction" title="Wythoff construction">Wythoff construction</a> upon a set of 6 <a href="/wiki/Hyperplane" title="Hyperplane">hyperplane</a> mirrors in 6-dimensional space.
</p><p>The facet information can be extracted from its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter-Dynkin_diagram" title="Coxeter-Dynkin diagram">Coxeter-Dynkin diagram</a>, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel branch 01lr.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/df/CDel_branch_01lr.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/></span>.
</p><p>Removing the node on either of 2-length branches leaves the <a href="/wiki/5-demicube" title="5-demicube">5-demicube</a>, 1<sub>31</sub>, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel branch 01lr.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/df/CDel_branch_01lr.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/></span>.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Vertex_figure" title="Vertex figure">vertex figure</a> is determined by removing the ringed node and ringing the neighboring node. This makes the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Birectified_5-simplex" title="Birectified 5-simplex">birectified 5-simplex</a>, 0<sub>22</sub>, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node 1.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bd/CDel_node_1.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/></span>.

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Geometric_folding">Geometric folding</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1_22_polytope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Geometric folding">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The <b>1<sub>22</sub></b> is related to the <a href="/wiki/24-cell" title="24-cell">24-cell</a> by a geometric <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Folding_(Dynkin_diagram)" title="Folding (Dynkin diagram)">folding</a> E6 → F4 of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter-Dynkin_diagram" title="Coxeter-Dynkin diagram">Coxeter-Dynkin diagrams</a>, E6 corresponding to <b>1<sub>22</sub></b> in 6 dimensions, F4 to the 24-cell in 4 dimensions. This can be seen in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter_plane" title="Coxeter plane">Coxeter plane</a> projections. The 24 vertices of the 24-cell are projected in the same two rings as seen in the 1<sub>22</sub>.

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Construction_2">Construction</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1_22_polytope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Construction">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Its construction is based on the <a href="/wiki/E6_(mathematics)" title="E6 (mathematics)">E<sub>6</sub></a> group and information can be extracted from the ringed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coxeter-Dynkin_diagram" title="Coxeter-Dynkin diagram">Coxeter-Dynkin diagram</a> representing this polytope: <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel branch 10.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/97/CDel_branch_10.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/></span>.
</p><p>Removing the ring on the short branch leaves the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Birectified_5-simplex" title="Birectified 5-simplex">birectified 5-simplex</a>, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea 1.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/aa/CDel_nodea_1.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/></span>.
</p><p>Removing the ring on the either 2-length branch leaves the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Birectified_5-orthoplex" title="Birectified 5-orthoplex">birectified 5-orthoplex</a> in its alternated form: <b>t<sub>2</sub>(2<sub>11</sub>)</b>, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel branch 10.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/97/CDel_branch_10.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3a.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/56/CDel_3a.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel nodea.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/CDel_nodea.png" width="5"/></span>.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Vertex_figure" title="Vertex figure">vertex figure</a> is determined by removing the ringed node and ringing the neighboring ring. This makes <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/3-3_duoprism_prism" title="3-3 duoprism prism">3-3 duoprism prism</a>, {3}×{3}×{}, <span style="display:inline-block;"><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node 1.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bd/CDel_node_1.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 2.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/16/CDel_2.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node 1.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bd/CDel_node_1.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 2.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/16/CDel_2.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node 1.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="9" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/b/bd/CDel_node_1.png" width="9"/><img alt="CDel 3.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="6" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c3/CDel_3.png" width="6"/><img alt="CDel node.png" data-file-height="23" data-file-width="5" decoding="async" height="23" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5e/CDel_node.png" width="5"/></span>.
