<p><b>Jackner Louis</b> (born 15 January 1993) in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bahamas" title="Bahamas">Bahamas</a> is a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Striker_(association_football)" title="Striker (association football)">Forward</a>. He currently plays for <a href="/wiki/BFA_Senior_League" title="BFA Senior League">BFA Senior League</a> club Cubs and the <a href="/wiki/Bahamas_national_football_team" title="Bahamas national football team">Bahamas national football team</a>.
</p>
