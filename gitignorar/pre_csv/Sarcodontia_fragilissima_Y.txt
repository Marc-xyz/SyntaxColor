<p><i><b>Sarcodontia fragilissima</b></i> is a species of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hydnoid_fungus" title="Hydnoid fungus">toothed</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Crust_fungus" title="Crust fungus">crust fungus</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Meruliaceae" title="Meruliaceae">Meruliaceae</a>. The fungus was originally <a href="/wiki/Species_description" title="Species description">described</a> as <i>Hydnum fragilissimum</i> by <a href="/wiki/Miles_Joseph_Berkeley" title="Miles Joseph Berkeley">Miles Joseph Berkeley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Moses_Ashley_Curtis" title="Moses Ashley Curtis">Moses Ashley Curtis</a> in 1873.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Berkeley_1873_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Berkeley_1873-2">[2]</a></sup> It was transferred to the genus <i><a href="/wiki/Sarcodontia" title="Sarcodontia">Sarcodontia</a></i> by T.L. Nikolajeva in 1961.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Nikolajeva_1961_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Nikolajeva_1961-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
