<p><b>Rokytne</b> (<a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk">Рокитне</span>, <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Ракитное</span>) is an <a href="/wiki/Urban-type_settlement" title="Urban-type settlement">urban-type settlement</a> located on the <a href="/wiki/Ros_River" title="Ros River">Ros River</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kiev_Oblast" title="Kiev Oblast">Kiev Oblast</a> (<a href="/wiki/Oblast" title="Oblast">region</a>) of <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>. It is the administrative center of <a href="/wiki/Rokytne_Raion,_Kiev_Oblast" title="Rokytne Raion, Kiev Oblast">Rokytnianskyi Raion</a>. Population: <span class="nowrap">11,395 (2013 est.)</span><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ua2013estimate_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-ua2013estimate-1">[1]</a></sup>. In 2001, population was 13,790.
</p>
