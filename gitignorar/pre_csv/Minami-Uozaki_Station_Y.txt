<p><b>Minami-Uozaki Station</b><span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">南魚崎駅</span><span style="display:none">,</span> <i>Minami-Uozaki eki</i>)</span> is a railway station on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rokko_Island_Line" title="Rokko Island Line">Rokko Island Line</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kobe" title="Kobe">Kobe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hy%C5%8Dgo_Prefecture" title="Hyōgo Prefecture">Hyōgo Prefecture</a>, Japan, operated by <a href="/wiki/Kobe_New_Transit" title="Kobe New Transit">Kobe New Transit</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Lines">Lines</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Minami-Uozaki_Station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Lines">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Minami-Uozaki Station is served by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rokko_Island_Line" title="Rokko Island Line">Rokko Island Line</a> automated guideway transit, and is located 2.0 kilometers from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Terminal_station" title="Terminal station">terminus</a> of the line at <a href="/wiki/Sumiyoshi_Station_(JR_West)" title="Sumiyoshi Station (JR West)">Sumiyoshi Station</a>.
</p>

<p>Minami-Uozaki Station has a single <a href="/wiki/Island_platform" title="Island platform">island platform</a>.
