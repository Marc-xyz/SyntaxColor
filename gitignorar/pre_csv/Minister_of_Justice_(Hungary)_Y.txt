	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Minister of Justice (Hungary)</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Minister of Justice of Hungary</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>: <i lang="hu">Magyarország igazságügyi minisztere</i>) is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Government_of_Hungary" title="Government of Hungary">Hungarian cabinet</a> and the head of the Ministry of Justice. The current justice minister is <a href="/wiki/L%C3%A1szl%C3%B3_Tr%C3%B3cs%C3%A1nyi" title="László Trócsányi">László Trócsányi</a>.
</p><p>The position was called <b>People's Commissar of Justice</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>: <i lang="hu">igazságügyi népbiztos</i>) during the <a href="/wiki/Hungarian_Soviet_Republic" title="Hungarian Soviet Republic">Hungarian Soviet Republic</a> in 1919, <b>Minister of Justice and Law Enforcement</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>: <i lang="hu">igazságügyi és rendészeti miniszter</i>) from 2006 to 2010 and <b>Minister of Public Administration and Justice</b> (<a href="/wiki/Hungarian_language" title="Hungarian language">Hungarian</a>: <i lang="hu">közigazgatási és igazságügyi miniszter</i>) between 2010 and 2014.
</p><p>This page is a list of Ministers of Justice of Hungary.
</p>
