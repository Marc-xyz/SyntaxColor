<p><b>Samseong Station</b> is a station on <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Subway_Line_2" title="Seoul Subway Line 2">Seoul Subway Line 2</a>. It serves the eastern area of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Teheranno" title="Teheranno">Teheranno</a>. Some of the more famous buildings near the station include <a href="/wiki/World_Trade_Center_Seoul" title="World Trade Center Seoul">World Trade Center Seoul</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/COEX_Mall" title="COEX Mall">COEX Mall</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_Electric_Power" title="Korea Electric Power">Korea Electric Power (KEPCO)</a> headquarters, Korea Air City Terminal (buses run from here to <a href="/wiki/Incheon_International_Airport" title="Incheon International Airport">Incheon</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gimpo_Airport" title="Gimpo Airport">Gimpo</a> Airports, and vice versa), and Gangnam main police and fire stations. Due to security concerns, the station was closed during the <a href="/wiki/2010_G20_Seoul_summit" title="2010 G20 Seoul summit">G20 summit</a> and the <a href="/wiki/2012_Nuclear_Security_Summit" title="2012 Nuclear Security Summit">2012 Nuclear Security Summit</a>, as this station is directly connected with <a href="/wiki/COEX_Convention_%26_Exhibition_Center" title="COEX Convention &amp; Exhibition Center">COEX</a>.
</p><p>The ridership of this station is very high, consistently ranking among the five most heavily used subway stations in Korea. 
The table below shows the average daily ridership between 2010 and 2012.

<p>Although the name of this station shares its pronunciation with the company <a href="/wiki/Samsung" title="Samsung">Samsung</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hanja" title="Hanja">Hanja</a> for the company (三星) and the station (三成) are different, so there is no relation between the two.
</p><p>The 836-meter (914-yard) section of sidewalk along Yeongdong Boulevard from exit No.5 of this station, outside <a href="/wiki/COEX_Convention_%26_Exhibition_Center" title="COEX Convention &amp; Exhibition Center">COEX Convention &amp; Exhibition Center</a> and <a href="/wiki/ASEM_Tower" title="ASEM Tower">ASEM Tower</a> is designated as a smoke-free zone by the <a href="/wiki/Seoul_Metropolitan_Government" title="Seoul Metropolitan Government">Seoul Metropolitan Government</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
