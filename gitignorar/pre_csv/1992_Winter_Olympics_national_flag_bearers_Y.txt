<p>During the Parade of Nations section of the <a href="/wiki/1992_Winter_Olympics" title="1992 Winter Olympics">1992 Winter Olympics</a> opening ceremony, athletes from the participating countries marched into the arena. Each delegation was led by a flag bearer and a sign with the name of the country on it. The Parade of Nations was organized in French, the official languages of <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. As tradition dictates, <a href="/wiki/Greece_at_the_1988_Winter_Olympics" title="Greece at the 1988 Winter Olympics">Greece</a> led the parade and <a href="/wiki/France_at_the_1992_Winter_Olympics" title="France at the 1992 Winter Olympics">France</a> was the last to march to the stadium as the host nation.
</p>
