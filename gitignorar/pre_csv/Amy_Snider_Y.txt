<p><b>Marion Amy Snider</b> (born 26 May 1942) is a Canadian <a href="/wiki/Hurdling" title="Hurdling">hurdler</a>. She competed in the <a href="/wiki/Athletics_at_the_1964_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_80_metres_hurdles" title="Athletics at the 1964 Summer Olympics – Women's 80 metres hurdles">women's 80 metres hurdles</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1964_Summer_Olympics" title="1964 Summer Olympics">1964 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
