<p><i><b>Party Mix!</b></i> is a 1981 <a href="/wiki/Remix" title="Remix">remix</a> album by <a href="/wiki/The_B-52%27s" title="The B-52's">The B-52's</a>, released in between their second album <i><a href="/wiki/Wild_Planet" title="Wild Planet">Wild Planet</a></i> and the <i><a href="/wiki/Mesopotamia_(EP)" title="Mesopotamia (EP)">Mesopotamia</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">EP</a>. On the original vinyl, this six-song collection contained songs from their first two albums remixed and sequenced to form two long tracks, one on each side. On the CD version, however, all the songs feature their own tracks.
</p><p>In the US, <i>Party Mix!</i> and a 1990 remix version of <i>Mesopotamia</i> were combined onto one CD, released in 1991. In Europe, both albums were made available on separate CDs, with <i>Mesopotamia</i> retaining the original US mix. However, <i>Party Mix!</i> is no longer in print in Europe.
</p>
