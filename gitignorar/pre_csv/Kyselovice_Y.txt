<p><b>Kyselovice</b> is a village and municipality (<i><a href="/wiki/Obec" title="Obec">obec</a></i>) in <a href="/wiki/Krom%C4%9B%C5%99%C3%AD%C5%BE_District" title="Kroměříž District">Kroměříž District</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Zl%C3%ADn_Region" title="Zlín Region">Zlín Region</a> of the Czech Republic.
</p><p>The municipality covers an area of 6.75 square kilometres (2.61 sq mi), and has a population of 493 (as at 28 August 2006).
</p><p>Kyselovice lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Krom%C4%9B%C5%99%C3%AD%C5%BE" title="Kroměříž">Kroměříž</a>, 25 km (16 mi) north-west of <a href="/wiki/Zl%C3%ADn" title="Zlín">Zlín</a>, and 229 km (142 mi) east of <a href="/wiki/Prague" title="Prague">Prague</a>.
</p>
