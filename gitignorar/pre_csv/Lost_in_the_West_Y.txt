<p><i><b>Lost in the West</b></i> is a three-part <a href="/wiki/Comedy" title="Comedy">comedy</a>-<a href="/wiki/Western_(genre)" title="Western (genre)">western</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-LAT-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Miniseries" title="Miniseries">miniseries</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RGJ_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-RGJ-2">[2]</a></sup> that aired on <a href="/wiki/Nickelodeon" title="Nickelodeon">Nickelodeon</a> from May 28–30, 2016. It was directed by <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Gonz%C3%A1lez_(cinematographer)" title="Carlos González (cinematographer)">Carlos González</a> and produced by Galdo Media.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
