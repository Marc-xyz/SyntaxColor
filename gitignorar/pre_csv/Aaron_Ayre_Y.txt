<p><b>Aaron Ayre</b> (born 6 August 1992) is an Australian <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Wicket-keeper" title="Wicket-keeper">wicket-keeper</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cricinfo_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cricinfo-1">[1]</a></sup> He made his <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">first-class</a> debut for <a href="/wiki/Victoria_cricket_team" title="Victoria cricket team">Victoria</a> on 14 November 2015 in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Sheffield_Shield_season" title="2015–16 Sheffield Shield season">2015–16 Sheffield Shield</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FC_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-FC-2">[2]</a></sup> On 9 January 2016 he made his <a href="/wiki/Twenty20" title="Twenty20">Twenty20</a> debut for the <a href="/wiki/Melbourne_Renegades" title="Melbourne Renegades">Melbourne Renegades</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Big_Bash_League_season" title="2015–16 Big Bash League season">2015–16 Big Bash League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BBL_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-BBL-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
