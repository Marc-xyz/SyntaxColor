<p>The <b>South Shields, Marsden, and Whitburn Colliery Railway</b> was a Whitburn Coal Company built twin track branch railway line that ran along the North Sea coast in <a href="/wiki/County_Durham" title="County Durham">County Durham</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, from <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Westoe_Lane_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Westoe Lane railway station (page does not exist)">Westoe Lane</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Shields" title="South Shields">South Shields</a> to <a href="/wiki/Whitburn_Colliery" title="Whitburn Colliery">Whitburn Colliery</a> at <a href="/wiki/Marsden,_Tyne_and_Wear" title="Marsden, Tyne and Wear">Marsden</a> via two intermediate stations, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Whitburn_Colliery_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Whitburn Colliery railway station (page does not exist)">Whitburn Colliery</a> station (renamed Marsden in 1926), and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marsden,_County_Durham" title="Marsden, County Durham">Marsden</a> (which closed in 1926).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-subbrit1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-subbrit1-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_Shields,_Marsden,_and_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Opening">Opening</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_Shields,_Marsden,_and_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Opening">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The line was built in the late 1870s without an Act of Parliament to serve the newly constructed <a href="/wiki/Whitburn_Colliery" title="Whitburn Colliery">Whitburn Colliery</a> and was opened as a private railway in May 1879. Apart from the colliery and those working there the line served the Lighthouse limestone quarry, a paper manufactory, and local farms. The line opened to the public on 19 March 1888.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Nationalisation">Nationalisation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_Shields,_Marsden,_and_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Nationalisation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 1 January 1947 the coal company was vested in the <a href="/wiki/National_Coal_Board" title="National Coal Board">National Coal Board</a> so the railway became the first nationalised passenger line in Britain.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-subbrit1_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-subbrit1-1">[1]</a></sup> (Most of the railways in Britain were nationalised on 1 January 1948 under the terms of the Transport Act 1947).
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Closure">Closure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=South_Shields,_Marsden,_and_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Closure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Westoe_Lane_station,_South_Shields,_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day,_1953_(geograph_5215651).jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1319" data-file-width="2347" decoding="async" height="124" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg/220px-Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg/330px-Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg/440px-Westoe_Lane_station%2C_South_Shields%2C_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day%2C_1953_%28geograph_5215651%29.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Westoe_Lane_station,_South_Shields,_Marsden_%26_Whitburn_Colliery_Railway_on_Last_Day,_1953_(geograph_5215651).jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Westoe Lane station, South Shields, Marsden &amp; Whitburn Colliery Railway on Last Day, 1953</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Westoe_Lane_(South_Shields)_Station_2043788.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="540" data-file-width="960" decoding="async" height="124" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg/220px-Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg/330px-Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg/440px-Westoe_Lane_%28South_Shields%29_Station_2043788.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Westoe_Lane_(South_Shields)_Station_2043788.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Westoe Lane (South Shields) Station in 1953</div></div></div>
<p>Passenger service was officially withdrawn on 14 November 1953, but trains ran until 23 November. The majority of the line closed to freight with the <a href="/wiki/Whitburn_Colliery" title="Whitburn Colliery">Whitburn Colliery</a> on 8 June 1968 although the section running through <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Westoe_Lane_railway_station&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Westoe Lane railway station (page does not exist)">Westoe Lane</a> to <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Westoe_Colliery&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Westoe Colliery (page does not exist)">Westoe Colliery</a> remained open until 1993.
</p>
