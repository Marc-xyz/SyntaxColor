<p><b>Warnik</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[ˈvarnik]</a></span> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i lang="de">Warningshof</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Ko%C5%82baskowo" title="Gmina Kołbaskowo">Gmina Kołbaskowo</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Police_County" title="Police County">Police County</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Pomeranian_Voivodeship" title="West Pomeranian Voivodeship">West Pomeranian Voivodeship</a>, in north-western Poland, close to the <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> border.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
