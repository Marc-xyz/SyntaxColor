<p><b>Godziszewo</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[ɡɔd͡ʑiˈʂɛvɔ]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Siedlec" title="Gmina Siedlec">Gmina Siedlec</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Wolsztyn_County" title="Wolsztyn County">Wolsztyn County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Poland_Voivodeship" title="Greater Poland Voivodeship">Greater Poland Voivodeship</a>, in west-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) north-west of <a href="/wiki/Wolsztyn" title="Wolsztyn">Wolsztyn</a> and 69 km (43 mi) west of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84" title="Poznań">Poznań</a>.
</p><p>The village has a population of 300.
</p>
