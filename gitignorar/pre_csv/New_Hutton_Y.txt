	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">New Hutton</h1>

<p><b>New Hutton</b> is a village and <a href="/wiki/Civil_parish" title="Civil parish">civil parish</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Lakeland" title="South Lakeland">South Lakeland</a>, Cumbria, England. At the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_Census_2011" title="United Kingdom Census 2011">UK census 2011</a> the parish had a population of 348.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census-2011_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-census-2011-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The parish lies to the east of <a href="/wiki/Kendal" title="Kendal">Kendal</a>, and to the north of <a href="/wiki/Old_Hutton" title="Old Hutton">Old Hutton</a>. The <a href="/wiki/M6_motorway" title="M6 motorway">M6 motorway</a> runs through the eastern edge of the parish, and the <a href="/wiki/A684_road" title="A684 road">A684 road</a> runs across the parish roughly east–west.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census-2011_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-census-2011-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The village lies to the south of the A684. The church of St Stephen is in the centre of the village.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-diocese_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-diocese-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>There is a <a href="/wiki/Parish_councils_in_England" title="Parish councils in England">parish council</a>, the lowest tier of local government.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cumbria_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-cumbria-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The parish contains 12 <a href="/wiki/Listed_building" title="Listed building">listed buildings</a>, all at grade II, including the church.
