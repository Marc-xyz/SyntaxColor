<p><b>THIS IS</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Dance_music" title="Dance music">dance music</a> curation brand.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It is one of the biggest music curation brands in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UK" title="UK">UK</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The brand releases dance music <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Compilation_albums" title="Compilation albums">compilation albums</a>, curates dance music <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Playlists" title="Playlists">playlists</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and puts on live events.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> They were the first to stream an event live into Spotify.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playlists">Playlists</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=THIS_IS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Playlists">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>THIS IS manages a series of dynamic playlists on <a href="/wiki/Spotify" title="Spotify">Spotify</a>, <a href="/wiki/Apple_Music" title="Apple Music">Apple Music</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Deezer" title="Deezer">Deezer</a>, <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vevo" title="Vevo">Vevo</a>, <a href="/wiki/SoundCloud" title="SoundCloud">SoundCloud</a>, and other streaming services. As of December 2015 the playlists were generating more than 6 million streams per month.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> The playlists include THIS IS House,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> THIS IS Dancefloor,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> and THIS IS Chill. THIS IS House and THIS IS Dancefloor are two of the most listened-to playlists in the UK.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Compilation_albums">Compilation albums</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=THIS_IS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Compilation albums">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dance music compilations released by the brand include THIS IS Dance 2014 in December 2013, THIS IS House in May 2014, THIS IS Chill in July 2014, House Every Weekend in July 2015, and THIS IS Dance 2016 in October 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Live_events">Live events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=THIS_IS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Live events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On 31 October 2015 the brand launched THIS IS Live, a live event at 93 Feet East in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> The event was headlined by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drum%E2%80%99n%E2%80%99bass" title="Drum’n’bass">drum’n’bass</a> duo <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sigma_(duo)" title="Sigma (duo)">Sigma</a>, and featured DJs <a href="/wiki/David_Zowie" title="David Zowie">David Zowie</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Scales_(disc_jockey)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Scales (disc jockey) (page does not exist)">Scales</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup> Footage of the whole event was streamed live into Spotify, a world first, as well as on to <a href="/wiki/YouTube" title="YouTube">YouTube</a>, <a href="/wiki/Facebook" title="Facebook">Facebook</a> and <a href="/wiki/Twitter" title="Twitter">Twitter</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-17"><a href="#cite_note-17">[17]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-18"><a href="#cite_note-18">[18]</a></sup>
</p>
