	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Coitsville Center, Ohio</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Coitsville Center</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unincorporated_community" title="Unincorporated community">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Mahoning_County,_Ohio" title="Mahoning County, Ohio">Mahoning County</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ohio" title="Ohio">Ohio</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Coitsville_Center,_Ohio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A variant name was Coitsville.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> A post office called Coitsville was established in 1826, and remained in operation until 1900.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> The community was named for its location near the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Geographical_center" title="Geographical center">geographical center</a> of Coitsville Township.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
