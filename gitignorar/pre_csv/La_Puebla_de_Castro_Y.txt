<p><b>La Puebla de Castro</b> (<small>Spanish: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Spanish" title="Help:IPA/Spanish">[la ˈpweβla ðe ˈkastɾo]</a></span>), is a municipality located in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Huesca_(province)" title="Huesca (province)">province of Huesca</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aragon" title="Aragon">Aragon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. According to the 2004 <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a> (<a href="/wiki/Instituto_Nacional_de_Estad%C3%ADstica_(Spain)" title="Instituto Nacional de Estadística (Spain)">INE</a>), the municipality has a population of 372 inhabitants.
