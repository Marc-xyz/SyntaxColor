<p><b>Kurt-Yelga</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Курт-Елга</span>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">khutor</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Kugarchinsky_District" title="Kugarchinsky District">Kugarchinsky District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bashkortostan" title="Bashkortostan">Bashkortostan</a>, Russia. The population was 40 as of 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> There are 15 streets.
</p>
