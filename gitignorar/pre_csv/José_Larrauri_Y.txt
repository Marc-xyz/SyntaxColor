<p><b>José Ramón Martínez Larrauri</b> (born 12 January 1940) is a Spanish retired <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who played as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Football_career">Football career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jos%C3%A9_Larrauri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Football career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Bilbao" title="Bilbao">Bilbao</a>, <a href="/wiki/Biscay" title="Biscay">Biscay</a>, Larrauri only played for two clubs during his 14-year senior career. He started out at <a href="/wiki/SD_Indautxu" title="SD Indautxu">SD Indautxu</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Segunda_Divisi%C3%B3n" title="Segunda División">Segunda División</a>.
</p><p>In 1965, Larrauri was bought by <a href="/wiki/Athletic_Bilbao" title="Athletic Bilbao">Athletic Bilbao</a>. He made his debut in <a href="/wiki/La_Liga" title="La Liga">La Liga</a> on 19 September, in a 1–0 home win against <a href="/wiki/Valencia_CF" title="Valencia CF">Valencia CF</a>; during his nine-year spell at the <a href="/wiki/San_Mam%C3%A9s_Stadium_(1913)" title="San Mamés Stadium (1913)">San Mamés Stadium</a> he played 277 games overall, including 24 in <a href="/wiki/UEFA" title="UEFA">European competition</a>.
</p>
