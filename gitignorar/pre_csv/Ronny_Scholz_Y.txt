<p><b>Ronny Scholz</b> (born 24 April 1978 in <a href="/wiki/Forst_(Lausitz)" title="Forst (Lausitz)">Forst</a>) is a former professional <a href="/wiki/Road_bicycle_racing" title="Road bicycle racing">road racing</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cyclist" title="Cyclist">cyclist</a> from <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> who retired after the 2009 season.
</p>
