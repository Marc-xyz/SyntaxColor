<p><b>The Haven</b> is a rural <a href="/wiki/Hamlet_(place)" title="Hamlet (place)">hamlet</a> in <a href="/wiki/West_Sussex" title="West Sussex">West Sussex</a>, south east <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a> located about 7 miles to the west of <a href="/wiki/Horsham" title="Horsham">Horsham</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The Haven is primarily an agricultural area with 75 dwellings scattered across it.
</p><p>One of its best known landmarks is the <a href="/wiki/Pub" title="Pub">pub</a>, <i>The Blue Ship</i>. The pub itself has a main bar that dates back to the 15th century although the exterior of the pub is <a href="/wiki/Victorian_era" title="Victorian era">Victorian</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hall_and_Woodhouse" title="Hall and Woodhouse">Hall and Woodhouse</a> beer is served from two hatches straight from the barrel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
