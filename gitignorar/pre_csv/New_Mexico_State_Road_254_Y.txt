<p><b>State Road 254</b> (<b>NM 254</b>) is a 4.378-mile-long (7.046 km) <a href="/wiki/State_highway" title="State highway">state highway</a> in the US state of <a href="/wiki/New_Mexico" title="New Mexico">New Mexico</a>. NM 254's northern terminus is at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Route_380_in_New_Mexico" title="U.S. Route 380 in New Mexico">U.S. Route 380</a> (US 380) in <a href="/wiki/Roswell,_New_Mexico" title="Roswell, New Mexico">Roswell</a>, and the southern terminus is at <a href="/wiki/New_Mexico_State_Road_256" title="New Mexico State Road 256">NM 256</a> in <a href="/wiki/Roswell,_New_Mexico" title="Roswell, New Mexico">Roswell</a>.
</p>
