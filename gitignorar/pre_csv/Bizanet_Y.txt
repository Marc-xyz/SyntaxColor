<p><b> Bizanet  </b> (<a href="/wiki/Languedocien_dialect" title="Languedocien dialect">Languedocien</a>: <i>Bisanet</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_the_Aude_department" title="Communes of the Aude department">commune</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Aude" title="Aude">Aude</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a> in southern <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. <a href="/wiki/Ch%C3%A2teau_de_Gaussan" title="Château de Gaussan">Château de Gaussan</a> is located in the commune.
</p>
