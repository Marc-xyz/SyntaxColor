<p><i><b>Leidi</b></i> is a 2014 Colombian short <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Sim%C3%B3n_Mesa_Soto" title="Simón Mesa Soto">Simón Mesa Soto</a>. It won the <a href="/wiki/Short_Film_Palme_d%27Or" title="Short Film Palme d'Or">Short Film Palme d'Or</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Cannes_Film_Festival" title="2014 Cannes Film Festival">2014 Cannes Film Festival</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Leidi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Leidi lives with her mom and her baby. Her boyfriend, Alexis, hasn't shown up in days. That sunny morning, after she's bathed her baby, Leidi is sent to buy plantains. Outside, a guy tells her he has seen Alexis with another girl. Leidi won’t return home until she finds the father of her child.
</p>
