<p>The <b>SIG GL 5040/5140</b> is a 40 mm <a href="/wiki/Grenade_launcher" title="Grenade launcher">grenade launcher</a>, which can be mounted under all <a href="/wiki/SIG_SG_550" title="SIG SG 550">SIG SG 550/551</a> <a href="/wiki/Assault_rifle" title="Assault rifle">assault rifle</a> models and is operated in single-shot mode.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-multiref1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-multiref1-1">[1]</a></sup>
For compact SG 552/553, the smaller GL 5340 grenade launcher can be used.
</p>
