<p><b>Dağeteği</b> is a village in the <a href="/wiki/Yusufeli" title="Yusufeli">District of Yusufeli</a>, <a href="/wiki/Artvin_Province" title="Artvin Province">Artvin Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>. As of 2010, it had a population of 140 people.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
