<p><b>Sarah Guillemard</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Canadians" title="Canadians">Canadian</a> provincial politician, who was elected as the Member of the <a href="/wiki/Legislative_Assembly_of_Manitoba" title="Legislative Assembly of Manitoba">Legislative Assembly of Manitoba</a> for the riding of <a href="/wiki/Fort_Richmond" title="Fort Richmond">Fort Richmond</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2016_Manitoba_general_election" title="2016 Manitoba general election">2016 election</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She is a member of the Progressive Conservative party, and defeated NDP incumbent <a href="/wiki/Kerri_Irvin-Ross" title="Kerri Irvin-Ross">Kerri Irvin-Ross</a> in the election.
</p>
