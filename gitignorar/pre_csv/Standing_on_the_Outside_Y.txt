<p><i><b>Standing on the Outside: The Songs of Cold Chisel</b></i> is a tribute album dedicated to the songs of <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australian</a> band <a href="/wiki/Cold_Chisel" title="Cold Chisel">Cold Chisel</a>. The album features 18 songs recorded by Australian and <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a> artists <a href="/wiki/The_Living_End" title="The Living End">The Living End</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Crane" title="Dallas Crane">Dallas Crane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pete_Murray_(Australian_singer-songwriter)" title="Pete Murray (Australian singer-songwriter)">Pete Murray</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ben_Lee" title="Ben Lee">Ben Lee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thirsty_Merc" title="Thirsty Merc">Thirsty Merc</a>, <a href="/wiki/Evermore_(band)" title="Evermore (band)">Evermore</a>, <a href="/wiki/Paul_Kelly_(Australian_musician)" title="Paul Kelly (Australian musician)">Paul Kelly</a>, <a href="/wiki/Troy_Cassar-Daley" title="Troy Cassar-Daley">Troy Cassar-Daley</a>, <a href="/wiki/Grinspoon" title="Grinspoon">Grinspoon</a>, <a href="/wiki/You_Am_I" title="You Am I">You Am I</a>, <a href="/wiki/Katie_Noonan" title="Katie Noonan">Katie Noonan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Something_for_Kate" title="Something for Kate">Something for Kate</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alex_Lloyd" title="Alex Lloyd">Alex Lloyd</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shane_Nicholson_(singer)" title="Shane Nicholson (singer)">Shane Nicholson</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Waifs" title="The Waifs">The Waifs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sarah_Blasko" title="Sarah Blasko">Sarah Blasko</a>, <a href="/wiki/Augie_March" title="Augie March">Augie March</a> and <a href="/wiki/The_Flairz" title="The Flairz">The Flairz</a>. 
</p><p><i>Standing on the Outside</i> was conceived by Ed St John of <a href="/wiki/Warner_Music_Group" title="Warner Music Group">Warner Australia</a> as a tribute to the label's best-selling Australian act.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The album featured liner notes by <a href="/wiki/Glenn_A._Baker" title="Glenn A. Baker">Glenn A. Baker</a>. A special edition featured a second CD containing the original 18 Cold Chisel songs. The album peaked at number 2 on the ARIA Charts and was certified platinum.
</p>
