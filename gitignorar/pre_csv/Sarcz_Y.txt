<p><b>Sarcz</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[sart͡ʂ]</a></span> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i lang="de">Zaskerhütte</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Trzcianka" title="Gmina Trzcianka">Gmina Trzcianka</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Czarnk%C3%B3w-Trzcianka_County" title="Czarnków-Trzcianka County">Czarnków-Trzcianka County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Poland_Voivodeship" title="Greater Poland Voivodeship">Greater Poland Voivodeship</a>, in west-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
