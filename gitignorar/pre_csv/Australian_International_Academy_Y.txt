<p><b>Australian International Academy</b> is an Islamic school group in various locations in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>. The school was Australia's first Muslim college and was then known as King Khalid. Its primary school was set up in 1999 with a grant from Saudi Arabia, that being the only foreign money the school has received.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The school has six campuses that offer the Australian National Curriculum and the <a href="/wiki/International_Baccalaureate" title="International Baccalaureate">International Baccalaureate</a>. The academy sports a Primary Campus (providing grades Prep to Grade 5) in <a href="/wiki/Coburg" title="Coburg">Coburg</a>, and a Secondary Campus (Grade 6 to Year 12) in Merlynston, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/North_Coburg" title="North Coburg">North Coburg</a>. A new campus, based in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Caroline_Springs" title="Caroline Springs">Caroline Springs</a>, commenced operation at the beginning of 2014.
</p><p>Over the past decade, the Academy has expanded into <a href="/wiki/New_South_Wales" title="New South Wales">New South Wales</a> by purchasing a school in <a href="/wiki/Strathfield_South,_New_South_Wales" title="Strathfield South, New South Wales">South Strathfield</a>  (previously known as Noor Al Houda Islamic College) and an additional campus in <a href="/wiki/Kellyville,_New_South_Wales" title="Kellyville, New South Wales">Kellyville</a>.
</p><p>The Academy also has a campus in <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi" title="Abu Dhabi">Abu Dhabi</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.A.E" title="U.A.E">U.A.E</a> which in 2013 became a fully accredited <a href="/wiki/International_Baccalaureate" title="International Baccalaureate">International Baccalaureate</a> (IB).
</p><p>The Australian International Academy is also accredited with being the first and only Muslim school to offer all three IB programmes in the southern hemisphere.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
