<p><b>Splicing factor, suppressor of white-apricot homolog</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Protein" title="Protein">protein</a> in humans that is encoded by the SFSWAP <a href="/wiki/Gene" title="Gene">gene</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-entrez_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-entrez-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>This gene encodes a human homolog of <i><a href="/wiki/Drosophila" title="Drosophila">Drosophila</a></i> splicing regulatory protein. This gene autoregulates its expression by control of splicing of its first two <a href="/wiki/Intron" title="Intron">introns</a>. In addition, it also regulates the splicing of <a href="/wiki/Fibronectin" title="Fibronectin">fibronectin</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/CD45" title="CD45">CD45</a> genes. Two transcript variants encoding different isoforms have been identified.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-entrez_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-entrez-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
