<p>The <b>2002 Fed Cup</b> was the 40th edition of the most important competition between national teams in women's <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a>.
</p><p>The final took place at the <a href="/wiki/Palacio_de_Congresos_de_Maspalomas" title="Palacio de Congresos de Maspalomas">Palacio de Congresos de Maspalomas</a> in <a href="/wiki/Gran_Canaria" title="Gran Canaria">Gran Canaria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a> on 2–3 November. <a href="/wiki/Slovakia_Fed_Cup_team" title="Slovakia Fed Cup team">Slovakia</a> defeated <a href="/wiki/Spain_Fed_Cup_team" title="Spain Fed Cup team">Spain</a>, giving Slovakia their first title.
</p>
