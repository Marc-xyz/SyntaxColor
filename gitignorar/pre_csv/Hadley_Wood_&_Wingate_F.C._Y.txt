<p><b>Hadley Wood &amp; Wingate Football Club</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in the <a href="/wiki/Finchley" title="Finchley">Finchley</a> area of <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. They played at <a href="/wiki/Wingate_%26_Finchley_F.C." title="Wingate &amp; Finchley F.C.">Wingate &amp; Finchley</a>'s Harry Abrahams Stadium.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hadley_Wood_%26_Wingate_F.C.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The club was established as a youth team in 2003, and was a feeder club for Wingate &amp; Finchley.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In 2015 an adult team was formed and joined the Premier Division of the <a href="/wiki/Hertfordshire_Senior_County_League" title="Hertfordshire Senior County League">Hertfordshire Senior County League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FCHD_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-FCHD-3">[3]</a></sup> They finished fourth in their first season,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FCHD_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-FCHD-3">[3]</a></sup> and were promoted to Division One of the <a href="/wiki/Spartan_South_Midlands_Football_League" title="Spartan South Midlands Football League">Spartan South Midlands League</a>. They left the league and disbanded at the end of the <a href="/wiki/2016%E2%80%9317_Spartan_South_Midlands_Football_League" title="2016–17 Spartan South Midlands Football League">2016–17 season</a>.
</p>
