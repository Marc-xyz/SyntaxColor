<p><b>Mohd Amirul Izwan Yaacob</b> (born 25 April 1986) is a <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Referee_(association_football)" title="Referee (association football)">referee</a>. He has been a full international for <a href="/wiki/FIFA" title="FIFA">FIFA</a> since 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Mohd_Amirul_Izwan_Yaacob&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Amirul Izwan Yaacob started the professional refereeing in 2011 and was awarded his FIFA badge in 2012.
</p><p>He refereed some matches in <a href="/wiki/AFC_Champions_League" title="AFC Champions League">AFC Champions League</a> and <a href="/wiki/AFC_Cup" title="AFC Cup">AFC Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2015, he was selected for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U-17_World_Cup" title="U-17 World Cup">U-17 World Cup</a> in Chile.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
