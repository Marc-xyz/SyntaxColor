<p><b>St. Paul's R.C. Academy</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Catholic_Church" title="Catholic Church">Roman Catholic</a> <a href="/wiki/Secondary_school" title="Secondary school">secondary school</a> in <a href="/wiki/Dundee" title="Dundee">Dundee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. The school was established in 2009 as a merger between <a href="/wiki/Lawside_Academy" title="Lawside Academy">Lawside Academy</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St_Saviour%27s_RC_High_School" title="St Saviour's RC High School">St. Saviours High School</a>. The combined school occupies a new site on Gillburn Road in the Kirkton area of <a href="/wiki/Dundee" title="Dundee">Dundee</a>, with the two previous sites left empty. As it is north of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St_John%27s_RC_High_School" title="St John's RC High School">St. John's</a>, the other Catholic secondary school in Dundee, St. Paul's Academy succeeds <a href="/wiki/Lawside_Academy" title="Lawside Academy">Lawside</a> in holding the position as the northernmost state Catholic secondary school in <a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>. The academy is named after <a href="/wiki/Paul_the_Apostle" title="Paul the Apostle">Paul the Apostle</a>, Paul being the middle name of the Right Reverend <a href="/wiki/Vincent_Logan" title="Vincent Logan">Vincent Logan</a>, the ninth <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bishop_(Catholic_Church)" title="Bishop (Catholic Church)">bishop</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Diocese_of_Dunkeld" title="Roman Catholic Diocese of Dunkeld">Diocese of Dunkeld</a>. The school has 4 houses named after <a href="/wiki/Saint" title="Saint">saints</a>: <a href="/wiki/Andrew_the_Apostle" title="Andrew the Apostle">St. Andrew</a>, <a href="/wiki/Columba" title="Columba">St. Columba</a>, <a href="/wiki/Margaret_the_Virgin" title="Margaret the Virgin">St. Margaret</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Ninian" title="Saint Ninian">St. Ninian</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Saint_Patrick" title="Saint Patrick">St. Patrick</a>.
</p><p>As of 2019 the school has an enrollment of 1420 students and although the school is Catholic, it welcomes all religious backgrounds. St Paul's offers a wide range of subjects in its curriculum, whilst following the <a href="/wiki/Curriculum_for_Excellence" title="Curriculum for Excellence">CfE</a>. At St Paul's, courses are available at a wide range of levels including the new National levels 3, 4 and 5 as well as Higher and Advanced Higher depending on the subject.
</p>
