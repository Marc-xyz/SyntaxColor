<p><b>Inda Zib'i</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Reservoir" title="Reservoir">reservoir</a> located in the <a href="/wiki/Enderta_(woreda)" title="Enderta (woreda)">Inderta</a> <i>woreda</i> of the <a href="/wiki/Tigray_Region" title="Tigray Region">Tigray Region</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a>. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Earthen_dam" title="Earthen dam">earthen dam</a> that holds the reservoir was built in 1999 by <a href="/wiki/SAERT" title="SAERT">SAERT</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-joke_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-joke-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Dam_characteristics">Dam characteristics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inda_Zib%27i&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Dam characteristics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Dam height: 12.34 metres</li>
<li>Dam crest length: 227 metres</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Spillway" title="Spillway">Spillway</a> width: 2 metres</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Capacity">Capacity</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inda_Zib%27i&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Capacity">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Original capacity: 182 593 m³</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Dead_storage&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Dead storage (page does not exist)">Dead storage</a>: 20 000 m³</li>
<li>Reservoir area: 4.05 ha</li></ul>
<p>In 2002, the life expectancy of the reservoir (the duration before it is filled with sediment) was estimated at 25 years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-joke_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-joke-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Irrigation">Irrigation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inda_Zib%27i&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Irrigation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Designed irrigated area: 13 ha</li>
<li>Actual irrigated area in 2002: 0 ha</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Environment">Environment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Inda_Zib%27i&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Environment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catchment" title="Catchment">catchment</a> of the reservoir is 1.49 km² large, with a perimeter of 4.77 km and a length of 1320 metres. The reservoir suffers from rapid <a href="/wiki/Siltation" title="Siltation">siltation</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The lithology of the catchment is Agula Shale.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-joke_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-joke-1">[1]</a></sup> Part of the water that could be used for irrigation is lost through <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Seepage" title="Seepage">seepage</a>; the positive side-effect is that this contributes to <a href="/wiki/Groundwater_recharge" title="Groundwater recharge">groundwater recharge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
