<p><b>Gohyeon-dong</b> is the central dong within the city of Geoje-si.  The City Hall, stadium, Intercity bus terminal, and many businesses and homes are located there.  When Geoje City is mentioned, it usually refers to Gohyeon-dong or <a href="/wiki/Jangpyeong-dong" title="Jangpyeong-dong">Jangpyeong-dong</a>.
