<p><b>Gora</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Гора</span>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">village</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Vinogradovsky_District" title="Vinogradovsky District">Vinogradovsky District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arkhangelsk_Oblast" title="Arkhangelsk Oblast">Arkhangelsk Oblast</a>, Russia. The population was 35 as of 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
