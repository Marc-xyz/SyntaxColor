<p><b>David Richard "Huddy" Hudson</b> (born December 28, 1949) is a retired professional <a href="/wiki/Ice_hockey" title="Ice hockey">ice hockey</a> player who played 409 games in the <a href="/wiki/National_Hockey_League" title="National Hockey League">National Hockey League</a> in the 1970s. In his career, he scored 59 goals 124 assists and 183 points.  He played for the <a href="/wiki/New_York_Islanders" title="New York Islanders">New York Islanders</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kansas_City_Scouts" title="Kansas City Scouts">Kansas City Scouts</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Colorado_Rockies_(NHL)" title="Colorado Rockies (NHL)">Colorado Rockies</a>.
</p>
