<p><b><a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Tom%C3%A9_and_Pr%C3%ADncipe" title="São Tomé and Príncipe">São Tomé and Príncipe</a></b> first competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/Paralympic_Games" title="Paralympic Games">Paralympic Games</a></b> in 2016, at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Summer_Paralympics" title="2016 Summer Paralympics">Summer Games</a> in <a href="/wiki/Rio_de_Janeiro" title="Rio de Janeiro">Rio de Janeiro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>. One wheelchair athlete was sent to compete in <a href="/wiki/Track_and_field" title="Track and field">track and field</a> events.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IPC_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-IPC-1">[1]</a></sup>
São Tomé and Príncipe has never taken part in the <a href="/wiki/Winter_Paralympic_Games" title="Winter Paralympic Games">Winter Paralympic Games</a>, and no athlete from the country has ever won a Paralympic medal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IPC_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-IPC-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
