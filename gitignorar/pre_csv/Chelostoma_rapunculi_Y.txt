<p><i><b>Chelostoma rapunculi</b></i> is a species of bee in the family <a href="/wiki/Megachilidae" title="Megachilidae">Megachilidae</a>. It is found in Europe &amp; Northern Asia (excluding China) and North America.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-itis_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-itis-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gbif_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-gbif-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-buglink_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-buglink-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
