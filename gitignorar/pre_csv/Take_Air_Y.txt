<p><b>Take Air</b> is an all-you-can-fly <a href="/wiki/Airline" title="Airline">airline</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-veconomist_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-veconomist-1">[1]</a></sup> based in <a href="/wiki/Martinique" title="Martinique">Martinique</a>. It operates scheduled services in the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Take_Air&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In December 2006 a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/L-410" title="L-410">Let L-410 UVP-E20</a> was delivered to Take Air and by March 2007 had logged well over 300 flight hours. A second aircraft was delivered on 18 March 2007 and will join Take Air’s fleet immediately after going through the necessary formalities.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LE_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-LE-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Destinations">Destinations</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Take_Air&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Destinations">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Take Air operates scheduled charter flights connecting Martinique with <a href="/wiki/Saint_Martin" title="Saint Martin">Saint Martin</a>, <a href="/wiki/Antigua" title="Antigua">Antigua</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guadeloupe" title="Guadeloupe">Guadeloupe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dominica" title="Dominica">Dominica</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saint_Lucia" title="Saint Lucia">Saint Lucia</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Vincent_(island)" title="Saint Vincent (island)">St Vincent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canouan" title="Canouan">Canouan</a> and <a href="/wiki/Union_Island" title="Union Island">Union Island</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LE_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-LE-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
