<p><b>Anastasija Al'bertovna Bavykina</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Анастасия Альбертовна Бавыкина</span>; born <span style="display:none">(<span class="bday">1992-07-06</span>)</span>6 July 1992) is a Russian female <a href="/wiki/Volleyball" title="Volleyball">volleyball</a> player, playing as an opposite. She is part of the <a href="/wiki/Russia_women%27s_national_volleyball_team" title="Russia women's national volleyball team">Russia women's national volleyball team</a>. 
</p><p>She competed at the <a href="/wiki/Volleyball_at_the_2015_European_Games_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament" title="Volleyball at the 2015 European Games – Women's tournament">2015 European Games</a> in Baku. On club level she played for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zarechie_Odintsovo" title="Zarechie Odintsovo">Zarechie Odintsovo</a> in 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
