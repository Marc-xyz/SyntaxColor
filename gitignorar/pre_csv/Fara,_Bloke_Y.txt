<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fara,_Bloke&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The settlement was formerly called <i>Male Bloke</i> (literally, 'little Bloke'), distinguishing it from <i><a href="/wiki/Velike_Bloke" title="Velike Bloke">Velike Bloke</a></i> (literally, 'big Bloke'). The Bloke <a href="/wiki/Parish_church" title="Parish church">parish church</a> was built in the settlement and the name <i>Fara</i> (literally, '<a href="/wiki/Parish" title="Parish">parish</a>') became the name of the settlement.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bloke_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Bloke-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Church">Church</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Fara,_Bloke&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Church">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The parish church in the village is dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Michael_(archangel)" title="Michael (archangel)">Saint Michael</a> and belongs to the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Ljubljana" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Ljubljana">Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Ljubljana</a>. It has been an independent parish since at least the 14th century.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
