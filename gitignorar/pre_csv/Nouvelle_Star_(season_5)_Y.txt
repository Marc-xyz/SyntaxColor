<p>The fifth season of <b><a href="/wiki/Nouvelle_Star" title="Nouvelle Star">Nouvelle Star</a></b> began on February 28, 2007. Once again all four judges returned and <a href="/wiki/Virginie_Efira" title="Virginie Efira">Virginie Efira</a> was hosting her second season. 
Auditions were held in the following cities:
</p>
<dl><dd><a href="/wiki/Rennes" title="Rennes">Rennes</a></dd>
<dd><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marseilles" title="Marseilles">Marseilles</a></dd>
<dd><a href="/wiki/Lyon" title="Lyon">Lyon</a></dd>
<dd><a href="/wiki/Bordeaux" title="Bordeaux">Bordeaux</a></dd>
<dd><a href="/wiki/Lille" title="Lille">Lille</a></dd>
<dd><a href="/wiki/Paris" title="Paris">Paris</a></dd></dl>
<p>The winner of the season was <a href="/wiki/Julien_Dor%C3%A9" title="Julien Doré">Julien Doré</a> over Tigane Drammeh.
</p>
