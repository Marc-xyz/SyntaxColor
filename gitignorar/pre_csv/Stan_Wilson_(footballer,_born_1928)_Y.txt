<p><b>Stan Wilson</b> (23 January 1928 – 24 January 2002) was  a former <a href="/wiki/Australian_rules_football" title="Australian rules football">Australian rules footballer</a> who played with <a href="/wiki/Richmond_Football_Club" title="Richmond Football Club">Richmond</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Australian_Football_League" title="Australian Football League">Victorian Football League</a> (VFL). <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-encyc_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-encyc-1">[1]</a></sup>		
</p><p><br/>		
</p>
