<p><i><b>Moments in a Lifetime</b></i> is a biographical/autiobiographical book about the Irish band <a href="/wiki/Clannad" title="Clannad">Clannad</a>, written by Barbara Bennett with band member <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Noel_Duggan" title="Noel Duggan">Noel Duggan</a>. The book was published to coincide with Clannad's 2008 tour of Britain and Ireland. The book contains exclusive photographs and accounts of touring and recording as a band.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NorthernSkyline_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-NorthernSkyline-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
