<p><i><b>Las verdes praderas</b></i> (English: <span lang="en">The Green Meadows</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/1979_in_film" title="1979 in film">1979</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Spanish_film" title="Spanish film">Spanish film</a> written and directed by <a href="/wiki/Jos%C3%A9_Luis_Garci" title="José Luis Garci">José Luis Garci</a>, starring <a href="/wiki/Alfredo_Landa" title="Alfredo Landa">Alfredo Landa</a>. The film is a reflection of the years of prosperity lived in Spain after the transition to democracy that followed the death of <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Franco" title="Francisco Franco">Francisco Franco</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Las_verdes_praderas&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>José Rebolledo has a good job at an advertising company and sincerely loves his wife, Elena. The couple has two children and they all seem the perfect family. Without financial problems they can afford some quirks and have acquired a chalet in the Sierra de Madrid where they spend weekends in the company of family and friends, playing sports and having barbecues. However,for José, within this apparent happiness lies a deep sense of frustration with the lifestyle he has chosen. José finds himself out of place in this bourgeois life and he decides  to break with it.
</p>
