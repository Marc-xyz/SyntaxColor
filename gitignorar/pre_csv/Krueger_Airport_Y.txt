<p><b>Krueger Airport</b> (<a href="/wiki/Federal_Aviation_Administration" title="Federal Aviation Administration">FAA</a> <a href="/wiki/Location_identifier" title="Location identifier">LID</a>: <b>OR72</b>) is a private <a href="/wiki/Airport" title="Airport">airport</a> located 3 miles (5 km) northeast of <a href="/wiki/Boring,_Oregon" title="Boring, Oregon">Boring</a> in <a href="/wiki/Clackamas_County,_Oregon" title="Clackamas County, Oregon">Clackamas County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oregon" title="Oregon">Oregon</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. Jeff Linscott purchased the property in 2007 and runs JL Aviation from the airport.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-katu_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-katu-1">[1]</a></sup> The airport faced complaints from neighbors in 2013 over noise from an un-permitted helipad on the property.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-katu_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-katu-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
