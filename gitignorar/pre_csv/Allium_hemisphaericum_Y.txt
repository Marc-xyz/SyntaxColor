<p><i><b>Allium hemisphaericum</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of onion <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Endemic" title="Endemic">endemic</a> to the Island of <a href="/wiki/Sicily" title="Sicily">Sicily</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mediterranean" title="Mediterranean">Mediterranean</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hisako_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-hisako-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>Little is known about <i>Allium hemisphaericum</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hisako_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-hisako-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
