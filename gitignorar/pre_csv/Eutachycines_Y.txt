<p><i><b>Eutachycines</b></i> is a genus of <a href="/wiki/Rhaphidophoridae" title="Rhaphidophoridae">cave or camel crickets</a> in the subfamily <a href="/wiki/Aemodogryllinae" title="Aemodogryllinae">Aemodogryllinae</a> and tribe Aemodogryllini.  Originating in Asia, species have been found in Sri Lanka, southern China, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indo-China" title="Indo-China">Indo-China</a> and west <a href="/wiki/Malesia" title="Malesia">Malesia</a>.
</p>
