<p><b>Shaun Barry Haig</b> (born 19 March 1982) in <a href="/wiki/Auckland" title="Auckland">Auckland</a> is a <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand" title="New Zealand">New Zealand</a> former <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a> who played <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">First-class</a> and <a href="/wiki/List_A_cricket" title="List A cricket">List A cricket</a> for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Otago_Volts" title="Otago Volts">Otago Volts</a>. A right-handed batsman and occasional wicket-keeper, Haig was contracted to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Otago_Volts" title="Otago Volts">Volts</a> since making his debut in early 2006.
</p><p>He is now an <a href="/wiki/Umpire_(cricket)" title="Umpire (cricket)">umpire</a> and stood in matches in the <a href="/wiki/2015%E2%80%9316_Plunket_Shield_season" title="2015–16 Plunket Shield season">2015–16 Plunket Shield season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-umpire_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-umpire-1">[1]</a></sup> In June 2016 he was added to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/International_Panel_of_Umpires_and_Referees" title="International Panel of Umpires and Referees">International Panel of Umpires and Referees</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NZ_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-NZ-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 3 January 2017 he stood in his first <a href="/wiki/Twenty20_International" title="Twenty20 International">Twenty20 International</a> (T20I) match, between <a href="/wiki/Bangladeshi_cricket_team_in_New_Zealand_in_2016%E2%80%9317" title="Bangladeshi cricket team in New Zealand in 2016–17">New Zealand and Bangladesh</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-T20I_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-T20I-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>In January 2018, he was named as one of the seventeen on-field umpires for the <a href="/wiki/2018_Under-19_Cricket_World_Cup" title="2018 Under-19 Cricket World Cup">2018 Under-19 Cricket World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2018U19_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-2018U19-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
