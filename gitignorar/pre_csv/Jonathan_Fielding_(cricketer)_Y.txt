<p><b>Jonathan Mark Fielding</b> (born 13 March 1973) is an English <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a>. Fielding is a right-handed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Batsman_(cricket)" title="Batsman (cricket)">batsman</a> who bowls <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Slow_left-arm_orthodox" title="Slow left-arm orthodox">slow left-arm orthodox</a>. He was born in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bury,_Lancashire" title="Bury, Lancashire">Bury, Lancashire</a>.
</p><p>Fielding played a single <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">first-class</a> match for <a href="/wiki/Lancashire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Lancashire County Cricket Club">Lancashire</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1994_English_cricket_season" title="1994 English cricket season">1994 season</a> against <a href="/wiki/Cambridge_University_Cricket_Club" title="Cambridge University Cricket Club">Cambridge University</a> at <a href="/wiki/Fenner%27s" title="Fenner's">Fenner's</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cambridge" title="Cambridge">Cambridge</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In his only career first-class match, he scored an <a href="/wiki/Not_out" title="Not out">unbeaten</a> 27 runs in Lancashire's second innings. With the ball he took 2 wickets, those of <a href="/wiki/John_Carroll_(cricketer)" title="John Carroll (cricketer)">John Carroll</a> in Cambridge's first-innings and <a href="/wiki/John_Ratledge" title="John Ratledge">John Ratledge</a> in their second-innings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> This was his only senior appearance for Lancashire.
</p><p>In 1996, he made his <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minor_Counties_Championship" title="Minor Counties Championship">Minor Counties Championship</a> debut for <a href="/wiki/Cumberland_County_Cricket_Club" title="Cumberland County Cricket Club">Cumberland</a> against <a href="/wiki/Buckinghamshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Buckinghamshire County Cricket Club">Buckinghamshire</a>. Fielding played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minor_counties_of_English_and_Welsh_cricket" title="Minor counties of English and Welsh cricket">Minor counties</a> cricket for Cumberland from 1996 to 2006, including 28 Minor Counties Championship matches<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and 16 <a href="/wiki/MCCA_Knockout_Trophy" title="MCCA Knockout Trophy">MCCA Knockout Trophy</a> matches.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> During his time with Cumberland, Fielding played <a href="/wiki/List_A_cricket" title="List A cricket">List A cricket</a>, making his debut in that format against <a href="/wiki/Middlesex_County_Cricket_Club" title="Middlesex County Cricket Club">Middlesex</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1996_NatWest_Trophy" title="1996 NatWest Trophy">1996 NatWest Trophy</a>. He represented Cumberland in eleven List A matches, with his final one coming against the <a href="/wiki/Warwickshire_Cricket_Board" title="Warwickshire Cricket Board">Warwickshire Cricket Board</a> in the 1st round of the <a href="/wiki/2002_Cheltenham_%26_Gloucester_Trophy" title="2002 Cheltenham &amp; Gloucester Trophy">2002 Cheltenham &amp; Gloucester Trophy</a> which was played in 2001.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAM_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-LAM-5">[5]</a></sup> He also played for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minor_Counties_cricket_team" title="Minor Counties cricket team">Minor Counties cricket team</a> in the 1997 and 1998 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Benson_and_Hedges_Cup" title="Benson and Hedges Cup">Benson and Hedges Cup</a>'s, making six appearances.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LAM_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-LAM-5">[5]</a></sup> In total, Fielding played 11 List A matches, scoring 110 runs at a <a href="/wiki/Batting_average_(cricket)" title="Batting average (cricket)">batting average</a> of 6.87, with a best score of 26. With the ball he took 16 wickets at a <a href="/wiki/Bowling_average" title="Bowling average">bowling average</a> of 37.18, with best figures of 3/47 against <a href="/wiki/Cornwall_County_Cricket_Club" title="Cornwall County Cricket Club">Cornwall</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1999_NatWest_Trophy" title="1999 NatWest Trophy">1999 NatWest Trophy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>Fielding played for the Lancashire Second XI, but also played for the <a href="/wiki/Gloucestershire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Gloucestershire County Cricket Club">Gloucestershire Second XI</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Derbyshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Derbyshire County Cricket Club">Derbyshire Second XI</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
