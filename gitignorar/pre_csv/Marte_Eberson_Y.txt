<p><b>Marte Maaland Eberson</b> (born 12 December 1987) is a Norwegian keyboardist and composer, known from bands including Machine Birds, <a href="/wiki/Morning_Has_Occurred" title="Morning Has Occurred">Morning Has Occurred</a> and <a href="/wiki/Highasakite" title="Highasakite">Highasakite</a>. She is the daughter of the guitarist <a href="/wiki/Jon_Eberson" title="Jon Eberson">Jon Eberson</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marte_Eberson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:262px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13,_2014.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1456" data-file-width="2380" decoding="async" height="159" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg/260px-Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg/390px-Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9f/Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg/520px-Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13%2C_2014.jpg 2x" width="260"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Marte_Eberson_at_Vossajazz_April_13,_2014.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Marte Eberson at Voss 2014.</div></div></div>
<p>Eberson was born in <a href="/wiki/Oslo" title="Oslo">Oslo</a>, and obtained her formal musical education from the Music program at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Foss_videreg%C3%A5ende_skole" title="Foss videregÃ¥ende skole">Foss videregÃ¥ende skole</a>, "Sund FolkehÃ¸gskole", followed by a bachelor's degree from the Jazz program at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Griegakademiet" title="Griegakademiet">Griegakademiet</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UiB" title="UiB">UiB</a>) in Bergen.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-uib_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-uib-1">[1]</a></sup> She is involved in many different bands and various musical contexts, most recently with the band "Eberson Funk Ensemble", a band including her guitarist father Jon Eberson, <a href="/wiki/P%C3%A5l_Thowsen" title="PÃ¥l Thowsen">PÃ¥l Thowsen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sigurd_Hole" title="Sigurd Hole">Sigurd Hole</a> and Kim Erik Pedersen at Victoria, the Norwegian National Jazz Scene in Oslo 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nasjonaljazzscene_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-nasjonaljazzscene-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In spite of her young age, Eberson has an impressive record. She won the "Jazz i sikte" with the band "Lugom Trio", second place in "UrÃ¸rt" final 2011 with the electronica duo "Machine Birds", and one of her new projects, <a href="/wiki/Morning_Has_Occurred" title="Morning Has Occurred">Morning Has Occurred</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Natalie_Sandtorv" title="Natalie Sandtorv">Natalie Sandtorv</a>, won the "Jazz i sikte" 2012. In 2012, she was rewarded with the <a href="/wiki/Sildajazzprisen" title="Sildajazzprisen">Sildajazzprisen</a>. She is also a member of the critically acclaimed indie band <a href="/wiki/Highasakite" title="Highasakite">Highasakite</a>, and has been playing with "Sjur Miljeteig Group" since 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sildajazz_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-sildajazz-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
