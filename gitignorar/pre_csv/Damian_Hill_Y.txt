<p><b>Damian Hill</b> (~1976 - 2018) was an Australian actor.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He was the writer of and an actor in <i><a href="/wiki/Pawno" title="Pawno">Pawno</a></i> for which he received two 2016 AACTA Award nominations, <a href="/wiki/AACTA_Award_for_Best_Actor_in_a_Leading_Role" title="AACTA Award for Best Actor in a Leading Role">Best Actor in a Leading Role</a> and <a href="/wiki/AACTA_Award_for_Best_Original_Screenplay" title="AACTA Award for Best Original Screenplay">Best Original Screenplay</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He was also nominated for Best Actor in 2018 for <i><a href="/wiki/West_of_Sunshine" title="West of Sunshine">West of Sunshine</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
