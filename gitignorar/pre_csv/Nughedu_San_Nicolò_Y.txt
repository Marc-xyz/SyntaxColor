<p><b>Nughedu San Nicolò</b> (<a href="/wiki/Sardinian_language" title="Sardinian language">Sardinian</a>: <i lang="sc">Nughedu Santu Nigola</i>) is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Sassari" title="Province of Sassari">Province of Sassari</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Sardinia" title="Sardinia">Sardinia</a>, located about 180 kilometres (112 miles) northwest of <a href="/wiki/Cagliari" title="Cagliari">Cagliari</a> and about 5 kilometres (3 miles) northwest of <a href="/wiki/Sassari" title="Sassari">Sassari</a>. 
</p><p>Nughedu San Nicolò borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Anela" title="Anela">Anela</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bono,_Italy" title="Bono, Italy">Bono</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bonorva" title="Bonorva">Bonorva</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bultei" title="Bultei">Bultei</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ittireddu" title="Ittireddu">Ittireddu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ozieri" title="Ozieri">Ozieri</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pattada" title="Pattada">Pattada</a>.
</p>
