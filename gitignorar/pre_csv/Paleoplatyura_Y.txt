<p><i><b>Paleoplatyura</b></i> is a genus of predatory fungus gnats in the family <a href="/wiki/Keroplatidae" title="Keroplatidae">Keroplatidae</a>. There are about seven described species in <i>Paleoplatyura</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-itis_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-itis-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gbif_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-gbif-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-buglink_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-buglink-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Greenwalt2019_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Greenwalt2019-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Species">Species</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Paleoplatyura&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Species">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>These seven species belong to the genus <i>Paleoplatyura</i>:
</p>
<ul><li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Paleoplatyura_aldrichii&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Paleoplatyura aldrichii (page does not exist)">P. aldrichii</a></i> <small>Johannsen, 1909</small></li>
<li><i><a href="/wiki/Paleoplatyura_johnsoni" title="Paleoplatyura johnsoni">P. johnsoni</a></i> <small>Johannsen, 1910</small></li>
<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Paleoplatyura_melanderi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Paleoplatyura melanderi (page does not exist)">P. melanderi</a></i> <small>Fisher, 1941</small></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Extinction" title="Extinction">†</a> <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mycetobia_macrocera&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mycetobia macrocera (page does not exist)">M. macrocera</a></i> <small>(Meunier, 1899)</small></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Extinction" title="Extinction">†</a> <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Palaeoplatyura_eocenica&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Palaeoplatyura eocenica (page does not exist)">P. eocenica</a></i> <small>Cockerell, 1921</small></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Extinction" title="Extinction">†</a> <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Palaeoplatyura_loewi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Palaeoplatyura loewi (page does not exist)">P. loewi</a></i> <small>Meunier, 1922</small></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Extinction" title="Extinction">†</a> <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Paleoplatyura_macrocera&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Paleoplatyura macrocera (page does not exist)">P. macrocera</a></i> <small>Meunier, 1899</small></li></ul>
