	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Mount Whiting</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Mount Whiting</b> (<span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Mount_Whiting&amp;params=71_40_S_62_37_W_"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">71°40′S</span> <span class="longitude">62°37′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">71.667°S 62.617°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-71.667; -62.617</span></span></span></a></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Mount_Whiting&amp;params=71_40_S_62_37_W_"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">71°40′S</span> <span class="longitude">62°37′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">71.667°S 62.617°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-71.667; -62.617</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>) is a pyramidal <a href="/wiki/Mountain" title="Mountain">mountain</a>, largely ice free and steep cliffed on the south side, standing at the southwest side of <a href="/wiki/Rankin_Glacier" title="Rankin Glacier">Rankin Glacier</a> near the east coast of <a href="/wiki/Palmer_Land" title="Palmer Land">Palmer Land</a>. Mapped by <a href="/wiki/United_States_Geological_Survey" title="United States Geological Survey">United States Geological Survey</a> (USGS) in 1974. Named by <a href="/wiki/Advisory_Committee_on_Antarctic_Names" title="Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names">Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names</a> (US-ACAN) for topographic engineer <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ronald_F._Whiting&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ronald F. Whiting (page does not exist)">Ronald F. Whiting</a>, a member of the USGS geological and mapping party to the <a href="/wiki/Lassiter_Coast" title="Lassiter Coast">Lassiter Coast</a> area, 1970-71.
