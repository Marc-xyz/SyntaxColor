<p><b>Lorcha</b> (<small>Spanish: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Spanish" title="Help:IPA/Spanish">[ˈloɾtʃa]</a></span>) or <b>L'Orxa</b> (<small>Valencian: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Catalan" title="Help:IPA/Catalan">[ˈloɾtʃa]</a></span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_Spain" title="Municipalities of Spain">municipality</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Comarques_of_the_Valencian_community" title="Comarques of the Valencian community"><i>comarca</i></a> of <a href="/wiki/Comtat" title="Comtat">Comtat</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Valencian_Community" title="Valencian Community">Valencian Community</a>, Spain.
