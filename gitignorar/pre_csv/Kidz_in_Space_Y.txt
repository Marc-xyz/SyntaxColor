<p><b>Kidz in Space</b> is a New Zealand <a href="/wiki/Pop_music" title="Pop music">pop music</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip-hop</a> band, consisting of Josh Fountain, Ashley Hughes. Their costumed media appearances have been likened to those of band, <a href="/wiki/Split_Enz" title="Split Enz">Split Enz</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
