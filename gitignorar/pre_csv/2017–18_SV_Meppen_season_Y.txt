<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/2017%E2%80%9318_in_German_football" title="2017–18 in German football">2017–18</a> <a href="/wiki/SV_Meppen" title="SV Meppen">SV Meppen</a> season</b> is the 106th season in the <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club's history and 1st season in the <a href="/wiki/3._Liga" title="3. Liga">3. Liga</a>, the third tier of <a href="/wiki/German_football_league_system" title="German football league system">German football</a>, having been promoted from the <a href="/wiki/Regionalliga_Nord" title="Regionalliga Nord">Regionalliga Nord</a> in <a href="/wiki/2016%E2%80%9317_Regionalliga" title="2016–17 Regionalliga">2017</a>. In addition to the domestic league, SV Meppen also participated in this season's edition of the <a href="/wiki/Lower_Saxony_Cup" title="Lower Saxony Cup">Lower Saxony Cup</a>, the regional cup for teams in Lower Saxony. Meppen play their matches at the <a href="/wiki/H%C3%A4nsch-Arena" title="Hänsch-Arena">Hänsch-Arena</a>, located in <a href="/wiki/Meppen" title="Meppen">Meppen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Emsland" title="Emsland">Emsland</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lower_Saxony" title="Lower Saxony">Lower Saxony</a>, Germany. The season covers a period from 1 July 2017 to 30 June 2018.
</p>
