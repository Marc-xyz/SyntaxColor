<p><b>Ovidiu Melniciuc</b> (born 16 January 1994) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanian_people" title="Romanian people">Romanian</a> <a href="/wiki/Rugby_union" title="Rugby union">rugby union</a> player. He plays in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fly-half_(rugby_union)" title="Fly-half (rugby union)">fly-half</a> position for amateur <a href="/wiki/SuperLiga_(rugby)" title="SuperLiga (rugby)">SuperLiga</a> club <a href="/wiki/CS_Universitatea_Cluj-Napoca_(rugby)" title="CS Universitatea Cluj-Napoca (rugby)">Cluj</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Ovidiu_Melniciuc_SuperLiga_Profile_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Ovidiu_Melniciuc_SuperLiga_Profile-1">[1]</a></sup> He also plays for Romania's national team the <a href="/wiki/Romania_national_rugby_union_team" title="Romania national rugby union team">Oaks</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
