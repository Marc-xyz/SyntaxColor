<p><b>Servair</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Subsidiary" title="Subsidiary">subsidiary</a> of the Swiss catering company <a href="/wiki/Gategroup" title="Gategroup">gategroup</a>, specialised in airline <a href="/wiki/Catering" title="Catering">catering</a>. Servair has its head office in the <a href="/wiki/Continental_Square" title="Continental Square">Continental Square</a> complex in Roissypôle, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris-Charles_de_Gaulle_Airport" title="Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport">Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport</a> and in <a href="/wiki/Tremblay-en-France" title="Tremblay-en-France">Tremblay-en-France</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Servair is a French company with around 10,000 employees, providing restaurant services, equipping and cleaning. It has 130 customer companies. Formerly a subsidiary of <a href="/wiki/Air_France" title="Air France">Air France</a>, Servair and its subsidiaries had a turnover of more than 761.5 million euros in 2010-2011.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>The Servair network, composed of Servair, its partners (Alpha, Flying Food Group, Servair Air Chef and Eurest Servair) and its technical support, has more than 100 units throughout the world.
</p>

<p>Servair’s has 4 main activities:
</p>
<ul><li>Catering, i.e. in-flight food services.</li>
<li>Handling, i.e. equipping and logistics, which is the loading/unloading of aircraft (pillows, blankets, trolleys, etc.) and the management and storage of hotel products (preparation of drinks, food, tax free products,…).</li>
<li>Ramp, i.e. airport assistance, going from runway assistance to terminal assistance..</li>
<li>Cleaning, i.e. cabin service, to prepare a clean and comfortable aircraft.</li>
<li>9,150 employees</li>
<li>755 million euros in turnover</li>
<li>7.8 million euros of net consolidated result</li>
<li>More than 100 units around the world, with its partners</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Servair&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>1971: Creation of Servair.</li>
<li>1974: Servair has 520 employees.</li>
<li>1975: Creation of Servair Lyon</li>
<li>1986: Servair provides restaurant services for Presidential journeys.</li>
<li>1990: Creation of ACNA (Armement Cabine et Nettoyage Avion), a company specialising in cleaning and equipping aircraft.</li>
<li>1990: 20 million trays served per year.</li>
<li>1990: Servair continues its establishment in Africa with the creation of DAKAR Catering</li>
<li>1991: Opening of Jet Chef at Le Bourget</li>
<li>1992: Opening of the units Servair 2, Orly Air Traiteur (OAT) and CPA at Roissy</li>
<li>1995: Servair is present in 14 countries.</li>
<li>1999: Creation of CAP (Culin’Air Paris) with Star Airlines and Bruneau Pégorier Catering</li>
<li>2000: Servair serves 40 million meals per year</li>
<li>2001: Opening of Special Meals Catering, a kosher production unit in Paris</li>
<li>2002: Opening of Mali Catering in Bamako Airport</li>
<li>2003: Opening of the new Servair laboratory</li>
<li>2004: Inauguration of a new kitchen in Mauritania</li>
<li>2005: CPA has transformed itself into a catering centre specialized in long-haul activity</li></ul>
