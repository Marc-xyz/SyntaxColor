<p><b>Albert Richard West</b> (7 November 1920 – 8 June 1985) was an English <a href="/wiki/Cricket" title="Cricket">cricketer</a>. West was a left-handed <a href="/wiki/Batting_(cricket)" title="Batting (cricket)">batsman</a> who bowled <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Slow_left-arm_orthodox" title="Slow left-arm orthodox">slow left-arm orthodox</a>.  He was born at <a href="/wiki/Earl_Shilton" title="Earl Shilton">Earl Shilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leicestershire" title="Leicestershire">Leicestershire</a>.
</p><p>West made his <a href="/wiki/First-class_cricket" title="First-class cricket">first-class</a> debut for <a href="/wiki/Leicestershire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Leicestershire County Cricket Club">Leicestershire</a> against <a href="/wiki/Oxford_University_Cricket_Club" title="Oxford University Cricket Club">Oxford University</a> in 1939 at the <a href="/wiki/University_Parks" title="University Parks">University Parks</a>.  He made two further first-class appearances for Leicestershire in the <a href="/wiki/1939_County_Championship" title="1939 County Championship">1939 County Championship</a>, against <a href="/wiki/Hampshire_County_Cricket_Club" title="Hampshire County Cricket Club">Hampshire</a> at the <a href="/wiki/County_Ground,_Southampton" title="County Ground, Southampton">County Ground, Southampton</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sussex_County_Cricket_Club" title="Sussex County Cricket Club">Sussex</a> at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Central_Recreation_Ground,_Hastings" title="Central Recreation Ground, Hastings">Central Recreation Ground, Hastings</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  He scored 50 runs in his three first-class appearances, which came at an <a href="/wiki/Batting_average_(cricket)" title="Batting average (cricket)">average</a> of 10.00, with a high score of 22.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>  With the ball, he took 2 wickets at an expensive <a href="/wiki/Bowling_average" title="Bowling average">bowling average</a> of 102.50, with best figures of 2/46.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>He died at the place of his birth on 8 June 1985.
</p>
