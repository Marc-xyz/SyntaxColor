<p><b>Turowo</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[tuˈrɔvɔ]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Czernice_Borowe" title="Gmina Czernice Borowe">Gmina Czernice Borowe</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Przasnysz_County" title="Przasnysz County">Przasnysz County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Masovian_Voivodeship" title="Masovian Voivodeship">Masovian Voivodeship</a>, in east-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of <a href="/wiki/Czernice_Borowe" title="Czernice Borowe">Czernice Borowe</a>, 6 km (4 mi) south-west of <a href="/wiki/Przasnysz" title="Przasnysz">Przasnysz</a>, and 86 km (53 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Warsaw" title="Warsaw">Warsaw</a>.
</p>
