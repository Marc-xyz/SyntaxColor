<p><b>Albert Ramírez</b> (born 7 May 1992) is a Venezuelan <a href="/wiki/Boxing" title="Boxing">boxer</a>. He competed in the <a href="/wiki/Boxing_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_light_heavyweight" title="Boxing at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Men's light heavyweight">light heavyweight</a> event at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Summer_Olympics" title="2016 Summer Olympics">2016 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Bio_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Bio-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
