<p><i><b>Turbonilla hofmani</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sea_snail" title="Sea snail">sea snail</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marine_(ocean)" title="Marine (ocean)">marine</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusk" title="Mollusk">mollusk</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Pyramidellidae" title="Pyramidellidae">Pyramidellidae</a>, the pyrams and their allies.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoRMS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-WoRMS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
