<p><i><b>Bromance: My Brother's Romance</b></i> is a 2013 <a href="/wiki/Cinema_of_the_Philippines" title="Cinema of the Philippines">Filipino</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Screwball_comedy_film" title="Screwball comedy film">screwball comedy</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Satirical_film" title="Satirical film">satire film</a> starring <a href="/wiki/Zanjoe_Marudo" title="Zanjoe Marudo">Zanjoe Marudo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cristine_Reyes" title="Cristine Reyes">Cristine Reyes</a>. It is the first foray of <a href="/wiki/Skylight_Films" title="Skylight Films">Skylight Films</a> into the comedy genre<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and is directed by <a href="/wiki/Wenn_V._Deramas" title="Wenn V. Deramas">Wenn V. Deramas</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The film is based on a true story<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> and premiered on May 15, 2013.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bromance:_My_Brother%27s_Romance&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A young man is forced to pretend to be his estranged gay brother in order to keep a major business from falling through. Though he has always hated his brother, the young man gains a new appreciation as he lives his brother's life.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Rating">Rating</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bromance:_My_Brother%27s_Romance&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Rating">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Box_Office">Box Office</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bromance:_My_Brother%27s_Romance&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Box Office">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><b>Bromance: My Brother's Romance</b> landed on No. 2 on its opening weekend beaten by <a href="/wiki/Star_Trek_Into_Darkness" title="Star Trek Into Darkness">Star Trek Into Darkness</a> on a small margin.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> <i>Bromance</i> earned P32,334,475 on its first 5 days of showing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> After 3 weeks of showing, it grossed P74,511,438.
</p>
