<p><i><b>Morpheis cognata</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Cossidae" title="Cossidae">Cossidae</a>. It was described by <a href="/wiki/Francis_Walker_(entomologist)" title="Francis Walker (entomologist)">Francis Walker</a> in 1856. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> and <a href="/wiki/Honduras" title="Honduras">Honduras</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
