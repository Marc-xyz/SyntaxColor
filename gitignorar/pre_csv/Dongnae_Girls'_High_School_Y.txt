<p><b>Dongnae Girls' High School</b> (<a href="/wiki/Korean_language" title="Korean language">Korean</a>: <span lang="ko">동래여자고등학교</span>) is a private girls high school located in <a href="/wiki/Bugok-dong,_Busan" title="Bugok-dong, Busan">Bugok-3-dong</a>, <a href="/wiki/Geumjeong_District" title="Geumjeong District">Geumjeong-gu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Busan" title="Busan">Busan</a>, <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dongnae_Girls%27_High_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dongnae Girls' High School was founded on October 15, 1895 under the name Ilshin Girls' School (일신여학교). In June 1925, the school moved to Boksan-dong and changed its name to Dongnae Ilshin Girls' School (동래일신여학교). The school was then split into a separate middle school and high school in September 1951, and Dongnae Girls' High School was established as a private school on July 14, 2009. The current principal Moon Seong-gyu was appointed on September 1, 2015 as the school's 23rd principal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-url_rel학교연혁_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-url_rel학교연혁-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
