<p><b>Querétaro Fútbol Club Femenil</b> is a Mexican women's <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Quer%C3%A9taro_City" title="Querétaro City">Querétaro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quer%C3%A9taro" title="Querétaro">Querétaro</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>. The club has been the female section of <a href="/wiki/Quer%C3%A9taro_F.C." title="Querétaro F.C.">Querétaro F.C. </a> since 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-LigaFemenil_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-LigaFemenil-2">[2]</a></sup> The team currently plays in the <a href="/wiki/Liga_MX_Femenil" title="Liga MX Femenil">Liga MX Femenil</a> which started in July 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
