<p>The <b>Beach Boulevard</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Single_carriageway" title="Single carriageway">single carriageway</a> road in the city of <a href="/wiki/Aberdeen" title="Aberdeen">Aberdeen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>. It is also locally known as the <i>Beach Bouley</i>, the <i>Bouley</i> and the <i>Boulevard</i>. At the east end, it has a junction with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beach_Esplanade,_Aberdeen" title="Beach Esplanade, Aberdeen">Beach Esplanade</a>. <a href="/wiki/Codonas_Amusement_Park" title="Codonas Amusement Park">Codonas Amusement Park</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Satrosphere_Science_Centre" title="Satrosphere Science Centre">Satrosphere Science Centre</a> are located here.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-McIver_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-McIver-2">[2]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Chinese_State_Circus" title="Chinese State Circus">Chinese State Circus</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Moscow_State_Circus" title="Moscow State Circus">Moscow State Circus</a> are regularly hosted at the site during the summer months.
</p>
