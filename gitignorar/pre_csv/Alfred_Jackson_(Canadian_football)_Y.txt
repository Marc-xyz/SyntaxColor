<p><b>Alfred Melvin Jackson, Jr.</b> (born July 7, 1967) is a former professional <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> <a href="/wiki/Wide_receiver" title="Wide receiver">wide receiver</a> who played nine seasons in the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Football_League" title="Canadian Football League">Canadian Football League</a> for three teams. He also played six seasons in the <a href="/wiki/National_Football_League" title="National Football League">National Football League</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Cornerback" title="Cornerback">cornerback</a> for three teams.
</p>
