<p><b>Leigh Rogers</b> (born 1 August 1989 in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>) is an <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ice_dancer" title="Ice dancer">ice dancer</a>. With former partner <a href="/wiki/Lloyd_Jones_(figure_skater)" title="Lloyd Jones (figure skater)">Lloyd Jones</a>, she is the 2006 &amp; 2007 British junior national champion and competed twice at the <a href="/wiki/World_Junior_Figure_Skating_Championships" title="World Junior Figure Skating Championships">World Junior Figure Skating Championships</a>.
</p>
