<p><b>Lac de Bettaniella</b> (<a href="/wiki/Corsican_language" title="Corsican language">Corsican</a>: <i lang="co">Lavu à a Vetta Niella</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Lake" title="Lake">lake</a> in <a href="/wiki/Corsica" title="Corsica">Corsica</a>, <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>. At an elevation of 2321 m, its surface area is 0.075 km². In French, it is sometimes alternatively (and abusively) known as <i>Lac de Bellebone</i> or <i>Lac du Rotondo</i>.
</p>
