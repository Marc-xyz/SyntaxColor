<p><i><b>Diploschema mandibulare</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Beetle" title="Beetle">beetle</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cerambycidae" title="Cerambycidae">Cerambycidae</a>. It was described by Ernst Fuchs in 1964.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
