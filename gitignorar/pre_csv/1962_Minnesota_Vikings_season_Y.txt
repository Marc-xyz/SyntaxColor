<p>The <a href="/wiki/1962_NFL_season" title="1962 NFL season">1962 season</a> was the <a href="/wiki/Minnesota_Vikings" title="Minnesota Vikings">Minnesota Vikings</a>' second in the <a href="/wiki/National_Football_League" title="National Football League">National Football League</a>. Under head coach <a href="/wiki/Norm_Van_Brocklin" title="Norm Van Brocklin">Norm Van Brocklin</a>, the team finished with a 2–11–1 record that still stands as the franchise's worst season record in terms of winning percentage, both by today's standards (.179) and at the time (.154), when ties were not counted as games played. The Vikings have won at least three games in every season since.
</p>
