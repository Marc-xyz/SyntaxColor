<p>The <b>1991–92 UMass Minutemen basketball team</b> represented the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Massachusetts_Amherst" title="University of Massachusetts Amherst">University of Massachusetts Amherst</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%9392_NCAA_Division_I_men%27s_basketball_season" title="1991–92 NCAA Division I men's basketball season">1991–92 NCAA Division I men's basketball season</a>. The Minutemen, led by fourth year head coach <a href="/wiki/John_Calipari" title="John Calipari">John Calipari</a>, played their home games at <a href="/wiki/Curry_Hicks_Cage" title="Curry Hicks Cage">Curry Hicks Cage</a> and were members of the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_10_Conference" title="Atlantic 10 Conference">Atlantic 10 Conference</a>. They finished the season 30–5, 13–3 in A-10 play to finish in first place. The Minutemen won the A-10 Conference Tournament by beating <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia_Mountaineers_men%27s_basketball" title="West Virginia Mountaineers men's basketball">West Virginia</a> in the finals. They were awarded a #1 seed in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1992_NCAA_Men%27s_Division_I_Basketball_Tournament" title="1992 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Tournament">NCAA Tournament</a>. The Minutemen advanced to the Sweet Sixteen, losing to <a href="/wiki/1991%E2%80%9392_Kentucky_Wildcats_men%27s_basketball_team" title="1991–92 Kentucky Wildcats men's basketball team">Kentucky</a> in the East Regional semifinal.
</p>
