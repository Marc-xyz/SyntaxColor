<p><b>Andrew Harold Gibson</b> was the <a href="/wiki/Commissioner_of_Yukon" title="Commissioner of Yukon">commissioner of Yukon</a> from 1950 to 1951.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> He was preceded by John Edward Gibben and succeeded by Frederick Fraser. He died in 1971 in Whitehouse Territory, Yukon, Canada .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
