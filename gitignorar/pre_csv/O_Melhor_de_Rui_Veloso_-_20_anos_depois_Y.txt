<p><i><b>O Melhor de Rui Veloso - 20 anos depois</b></i> (The Greatest of Rui Veloso - 20 Years Later) was <a href="/wiki/Rui_Veloso" title="Rui Veloso">Rui Veloso</a>'s compilation album released on EMI Portugal in 2000.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Rate_Your_Music_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Rate_Your_Music-1">[1]</a></sup>  Unrecorded tracks are not includes, the singles "Beira" and "Rock da Liberdade" ("Free Rock") are not included in this collector's album.
</p>
