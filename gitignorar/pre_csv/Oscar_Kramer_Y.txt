<p><b>Oscar Kramer</b> (25 May 1935 – 7 April 2010) was an <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentine</a> <a href="/wiki/Film_producer" title="Film producer">film producer</a>. He was born in <a href="/wiki/Buenos_Aires" title="Buenos Aires">Buenos Aires</a>. He worked in the <a href="/wiki/Cinema_of_Argentina" title="Cinema of Argentina">cinema of Argentina</a> when he was young. In the 1970s he lived in <a href="/wiki/Antwerp" title="Antwerp">Antwerp</a>, Belgium, where he worked in the diamond industry. After a few decades, in the 1980s he returned to <a href="/wiki/Argentina" title="Argentina">Argentina</a> with his wife and son to direct films.
</p>
