<p><b>WXKO</b> (1150 <a href="/wiki/AM_broadcasting" title="AM broadcasting">AM</a>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Radio_station" title="Radio station">radio station</a> broadcasting a <a href="/wiki/Sports_radio" title="Sports radio">sports</a> format. Licensed to <a href="/wiki/Fort_Valley,_Georgia" title="Fort Valley, Georgia">Fort Valley, Georgia</a>, United States, the station is owned by Shanks Broadcasting.
</p>
