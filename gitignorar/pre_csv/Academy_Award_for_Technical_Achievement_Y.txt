	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Academy Award for Technical Achievement</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Technical Achievement Award</b> is one of three <a href="/wiki/Academy_Scientific_and_Technical_Award" title="Academy Scientific and Technical Award">Scientific and Technical Awards</a> given from time to time by the <a href="/wiki/Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences" title="Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences">Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences</a>. (The other two awards are the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Scientific_and_Engineering_Award" title="Scientific and Engineering Award">Scientific and Engineering Award</a> and the Academy Award of Merit.)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SciTech_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SciTech-1">[1]</a></sup> The Technical Achievement Award is an honorary award that is given annually to those whose particular technical accomplishments have contributed to the progress of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Motion_picture_industry" title="Motion picture industry">motion picture industry</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TechAchieveAward_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-TechAchieveAward-2">[2]</a></sup> The award is a certificate, which describes the achievement and lists the names of this being honored for the particular contribution.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TechAchieveAward_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-TechAchieveAward-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TechAch_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-TechAch-3">[3]</a></sup> These awards are usually given at a dinner ceremony held weeks prior to the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Academy Awards</a> broadcast and a brief excerpt is shown in the Oscars telecast. 
</p>
