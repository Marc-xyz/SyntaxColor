<p><b>Keyun Lu Station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">科韵路站</span>; literally: 'Keyun Road Station') is a station on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Line_5,_Guangzhou_Metro" title="Line 5, Guangzhou Metro">Line 5</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Guangzhou_Metro" title="Guangzhou Metro">Guangzhou Metro</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is located at the under the junction of Huangpu Avenue Middle (<span lang="zh-Hans">黄埔大道中</span>) and Keyun Road (<span lang="zh-Hans">科韵路</span>) in the <a href="/wiki/Tianhe_District" title="Tianhe District">Tianhe District</a>, near Tianhe Science and Technology Park (<span lang="zh-Hans">天河科技园</span>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> It opened on 28<span class="nowrap"> </span>December 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
