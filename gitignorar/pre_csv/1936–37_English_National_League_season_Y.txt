<p>The <b>1936–37 English National League season</b> was the second season of the <a href="/wiki/English_National_League" title="English National League">English National League</a>, the top level ice hockey league in <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. 11 teams participated in the league, and the <a href="/wiki/Wembley_Lions" title="Wembley Lions">Wembley Lions</a> won the championship.
</p>
