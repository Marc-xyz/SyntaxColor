<p>The <b>Basilicata regional election of 1975</b> took place on 15 June 1975.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Christian_Democracy_(Italy)" title="Christian Democracy (Italy)">Christian Democracy</a> was by far the largest party, largely ahead of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Communist_Party" title="Italian Communist Party">Italian Communist Party</a>, which came distantly second. After the election <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Vincenzo_Verrastro&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Vincenzo Verrastro (page does not exist)">Vincenzo Verrastro</a>, the incumbent Christian Democratic President, was re-elected President.
</p>
