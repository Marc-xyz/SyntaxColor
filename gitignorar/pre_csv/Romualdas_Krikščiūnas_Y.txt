		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Romualdas Krikščiūnas</b> (June 18, 1930 in Kaunas, Lithuania – November 2, 2010) was the apostolic administrator of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Diocese_of_Panev%C4%97%C5%BEys" title="Roman Catholic Diocese of Panevėžys">Roman Catholic Diocese of Panevėžys</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lithuania" title="Lithuania">Lithuania</a>. He was ordained in 1954 and became bishop in 1966.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
