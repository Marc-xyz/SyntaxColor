<p><b>Jordan Lenín Rezabala Anzules</b> (born 29 February 2000) is an <a href="/wiki/Ecuador" title="Ecuador">Ecuadorian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who currently plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Club_Tijuana" title="Club Tijuana">Club Tijuana</a>.
</p>
