<p><i><b>Shirin in Love</b></i> is an Iranian-American <a href="/wiki/Romantic_comedy" title="Romantic comedy">romantic comedy</a> film directed by <a href="/wiki/Ramin_Niami" title="Ramin Niami">Ramin Niami</a> and starring <a href="/wiki/Nazanin_Boniadi" title="Nazanin Boniadi">Nazanin Boniadi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Riley_Smith" title="Riley Smith">Riley Smith</a>, <a href="/wiki/Maz_Jobrani" title="Maz Jobrani">Maz Jobrani</a> and <a href="/wiki/Anahita_Khalatbari" title="Anahita Khalatbari">Anahita Khalatbari</a>. It was released in March 2014 via AMC Independent.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Shirin_in_Love&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Shirin is an absent-minded, yet sophisticated, young Iranian-American woman who lives in "<a href="/wiki/Tehrangeles" title="Tehrangeles">Tehrangeles</a>", the large <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/History_of_the_Iranians_in_Los_Angeles" title="History of the Iranians in Los Angeles">Iranian community of Los Angeles</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> While Shirin has been engaged for years to a successful Iranian plastic surgeon in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills" title="Beverly Hills">Beverly Hills</a>, she lives with her overbearing mother and empathetic father. When she falls in love with a mysterious young man who lives in a lighthouse in Northern California, a secret unravels and cultures clash, challenging all the tradition Shirin grew up with - and she re-discovers herself in the process.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
