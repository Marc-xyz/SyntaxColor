<p><b>Sheikh</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urdu_language" title="Urdu language">Urdu</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ur">شيخ</span>‎), is an Arabic word meaning elder of a tribe, lord, revered old man, or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ulema" title="Ulema">Islamic scholar</a>. The Shaikh of <a href="/wiki/Sindh" title="Sindh">Sindh</a> are one of the larger communities of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sindhi_people" title="Sindhi people">Sindhi Muslims</a> ethnic group. They speak the various dialects of <a href="/wiki/Sindhi_language" title="Sindhi language">Sindhi</a>, depending on their place of residence. The Shaikh are largely urban community, residing in the towns and cities of <a href="/wiki/Sindh" title="Sindh">Sindh</a>. Shaikhs have different community in them, namely baghdadi, sanjogi, nangani, deewan and chatani.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" title="Wikipedia:Citing sources"><span title="This citation requires a reference to the specific page or range of pages in which the material appears. (February 2019)">page needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Origin">Origin</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sindhi_Shaikh&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Origin">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/South_Asia" title="South Asia">South Asia</a>, it is used as an ethnic title generally attributed to Muslim trading families descended from the higher clans and are generally very rich and powerful in the local administration and economy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citing_sources" title="Wikipedia:Citing sources"><span title="This citation requires a reference to the specific page or range of pages in which the material appears. (February 2019)">page needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p>
