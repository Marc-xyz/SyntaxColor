<p><i><b>¡Ando Bien Pedo!</b></i> (<i>I Am Very Drunk</i>) is the title of a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">studio album</a> released by Mexican group <a href="/wiki/Banda_Los_Recoditos" title="Banda Los Recoditos">Banda Los Recoditos</a> in 2010. The album became the group's first number-one title in the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Top_Latin_Albums" title="Billboard Top Latin Albums"><i>Billboard</i> Top Latin Albums chart</a>. The lead single of the <a href="/wiki/Ando_Bien_Pedo" title="Ando Bien Pedo">same name</a> peaked at number-one in the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Latin_Songs" title="Hot Latin Songs">Hot Latin Songs</a> and Regional Mexican Songs charts in the United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-encabeza_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-encabeza-1">[1]</a></sup> <i>Ando Bien Pedo</i> received a nomination for Best Banda Album at the 11th Latin Grammy Awards. The album has sold more than 550,000 copies worldwide
</p>
