<p><b>High Park</b> is a neighbourhood in west <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alberta" title="Alberta">Alberta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.  The neighbourhood became a part of Edmonton when the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jasper_Place,_Alberta" title="Jasper Place, Alberta">Town of Jasper Place</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amalgamation_(politics)" title="Amalgamation (politics)">amalgamated</a> with Edmonton in 1964.
</p><p>The neighbourhood is bounded on the north by 111 Avenue, on the south by 107 Avenue, on the east by 149 Street, and on the west by 156 Street.
</p><p>Most of the residential construction in the neighbourhood occurred between the end of <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a> and <a href="/wiki/1970_in_Canada" title="1970 in Canada">1970</a>.  It was during this period that about 85% of the residences were built.  According to the 2001 Federal Census, approximately four out of five residences in the neighbourhood are owner occupied with only one out of five being <a href="/wiki/Renting" title="Renting">rented</a>.  The residences in the neighbourhood are almost exclusively <a href="/wiki/Single-family_detached_home" title="Single-family detached home">single-family dwellings</a>, which make up approximately 96% of the residences.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>There is a single school in the neighbourhood, High Park Elementary School.
</p><p>The community is represented by the High Park <a href="/wiki/Neighborhood_council" title="Neighborhood council">Community League</a>, established in 1959, which maintains a <a href="/wiki/Community_centre" title="Community centre">community hall</a> and outdoor rink located at 154 Street and 110 Avenue.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=High_Park,_Edmonton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the City of Edmonton's 2012 municipal census, High Park had a population of <span data-sort-value="7003138900000000000♠">1,389</span> living in <span data-sort-value="7002646000000000000♠">646</span> dwellings,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2012population_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-2012population-7">[7]</a></sup> a -8% change from its 2009 population of <span data-sort-value="7003151000000000000♠">1,510</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2009population_11-0"><a href="#cite_note-2009population-11">[11]</a></sup> With a land area of 0.72 km<sup>2</sup> (0.28 sq mi),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nbhds_6-1"><a href="#cite_note-nbhds-6">[6]</a></sup> it had a population density of <span data-sort-value="7003192920000000000♠">1,929.2</span> people/km<sup>2</sup> in 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nbhds_6-2"><a href="#cite_note-nbhds-6">[6]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2012population_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-2012population-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Surrounding_neighbourhoods">Surrounding neighbourhoods</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=High_Park,_Edmonton&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Surrounding neighbourhoods">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The neighbourhood is surrounded by a mixture of residential and industrial neighbourhoods.  Residential neighbourhoods include <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Britannia_Youngstown" title="Britannia Youngstown">Britannia Youngstown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canora,_Edmonton" title="Canora, Edmonton">Canora</a>, Grovenor, <a href="/wiki/Mayfield,_Edmonton" title="Mayfield, Edmonton">Mayfield</a>, and <a href="/wiki/McQueen,_Edmonton" title="McQueen, Edmonton">McQueen</a>.  Industrial neighbourhoods include High Park Industrial, Huff Bremner Estate Industrial, and Sheffield Industrial.
</p>
