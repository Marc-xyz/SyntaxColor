<p><b>Westfield Arkadia</b> in <a href="/wiki/Warsaw" title="Warsaw">Warsaw</a>, <a href="/wiki/Poland" title="Poland">Poland</a> is the largest shopping complex in <a href="/wiki/Central_Europe" title="Central Europe">Central Europe</a> (as of 2007).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In total it has 287,000 square metres (3,100,000 sq ft) of space, 230 shops, 25 restaurants, and a movie theater.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-warsawvoice_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-warsawvoice-2">[2]</a></sup> It is owned and operated by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Unibail-Rodamco" title="Unibail-Rodamco">Unibail-Rodamco</a>.
</p><p>It includes the following stores: <a href="/wiki/Carrefour" title="Carrefour">Carrefour</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/EMPiK" title="EMPiK">EMPiK</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leroy_Merlin" title="Leroy Merlin">Leroy Merlin</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saturn_(store)" title="Saturn (store)">Saturn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cinema_City_Poland" title="Cinema City Poland">Cinema City</a>, Euro RTV AGD, <a href="/wiki/H%26M" title="H&amp;M">H&amp;M</a>, and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Zara_(clothing)" title="Zara (clothing)">Zara</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-warsawvoice_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-warsawvoice-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Arkadia opened on 20 October 2004 and was developed by BEG Ingenierie Polska and Cefic Polska.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-warsawvoice_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-warsawvoice-2">[2]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/International_Council_of_Shopping_Centers" title="International Council of Shopping Centers">International Council of Shopping Centers</a> awarded it the best shopping mall project in Poland, Shopping Center of the Year in 2004, and the European Shopping Center Award for best mall in Europe in 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> It was declared Warsaw's most disabled-friendly building by the Warsaw Without Barriers programme and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NGO" title="NGO">NGO</a> Association of Friends of Integration (Stowarzyszenie Przyjaciół Integracji).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 25 September, 2019 Arkadia has changed its name to Westfield Arkadia<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>.
</p>
