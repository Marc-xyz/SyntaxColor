<p><b>Chueca</b> is a municipality located in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toledo_(province)" title="Toledo (province)">province of Toledo</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Castile-La_Mancha" title="Castile-La Mancha">Castile-La Mancha</a>, Spain. According to the 2014 <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> the municipality has a population of 271 inhabitants.
</p>
