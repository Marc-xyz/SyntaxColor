<p><b>Satkhira Government High School</b> (<b>SGHS</b>) (<a href="/wiki/Bengali_language" title="Bengali language">Bengali</a>: <span lang="bn">সাতক্ষীরা সরকারি উচ্চ বিদ্যালয়</span>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Public_school_(government_funded)" title="Public school (government funded)">public</a> <a href="/wiki/Education_in_Bangladesh" title="Education in Bangladesh">high school</a> situated at the centre of <a href="/wiki/Satkhira" title="Satkhira">Satkhira</a> town in the <a href="/wiki/Satkhira_District" title="Satkhira District">Satkhira district</a>, part of the <a href="/wiki/Khulna_Division" title="Khulna Division">Khulna division</a>, in south-western <a href="/wiki/Bangladesh" title="Bangladesh">Bangladesh</a>. It is housed in a two-story 'L' shaped building.
</p><p>The school celebrated 50 years foundation day in 2012.
</p>

<li>White socks</li></ul>
