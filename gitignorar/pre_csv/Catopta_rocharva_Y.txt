<p><i><b>Catopta rocharva</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Cossidae" title="Cossidae">Cossidae</a>. It was described by <a href="/wiki/Leo_Sheljuzhko" title="Leo Sheljuzhko">Leo Sheljuzhko</a> in 1943. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Tajikistan" title="Tajikistan">Tajikistan</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gissar" title="Gissar">Gissar</a>, western <a href="/wiki/Pamir_Mountains" title="Pamir Mountains">Pamir</a>) and north-eastern <a href="/wiki/Afghanistan" title="Afghanistan">Afghanistan</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
