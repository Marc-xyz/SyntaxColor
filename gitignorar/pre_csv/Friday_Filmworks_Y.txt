<p><b>Friday Filmworks</b> is an <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> <a href="/wiki/Film" title="Film">motion picture</a> <a href="/wiki/Production_company" title="Production company">production</a> company founded by <a href="/wiki/Neeraj_Pandey" title="Neeraj Pandey">Neeraj Pandey</a> and Shital Bhatia in 2008.
</p><p>Set up in 2008, Friday Filmworks has produced trend-setting films that are original in content and entertaining to the audience. <a href="/wiki/A_Wednesday!" title="A Wednesday!">A Wednesday</a> was the first movie under the banner which won many awards in different categories. The other films under the Friday Filmworks include <a href="/wiki/Special_26" title="Special 26">Special 26</a>, Baby, <a href="/wiki/Total_Siyapaa" title="Total Siyapaa">Total Siyapaa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Saat_Uchakkey" title="Saat Uchakkey">Saat Uchakkey</a>, <a href="/wiki/M.S._Dhoni:_The_Untold_Story" title="M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story">M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story</a>, <a href="/wiki/Naam_Shabana" title="Naam Shabana">Naam Shabana</a>.
</p><p>Upcoming Movies are <a href="/wiki/Toilet:_Ek_Prem_Katha" title="Toilet: Ek Prem Katha">Toilet: Ek Prem Katha</a> which is set to release on 11 August 2017 and <a href="/wiki/Aiyaary" title="Aiyaary">Aiyaary</a> in 2018.
</p><p>It also produced a Marathi film <a href="/wiki/Taryanche_Bait" title="Taryanche Bait">Taryanche Bait</a> in 2011 and a Bengali film <a href="/wiki/The_Royal_Bengal_Tiger_(film)" title="The Royal Bengal Tiger (film)">The Royal Bengal Tiger</a> in 2014.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Neeraj_Pandey" title="Neeraj Pandey">Neeraj Pandey</a>'s powerful short film 'Ouch' was shown for the first time ever at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mumbai_Film_Festival" title="Mumbai Film Festival">Mumbai Film Festival</a>, well known for showcasing the best of films in front of a demanding audience.
</p><p>In 2016, Friday Filmworks and <a href="/wiki/Reliance_Entertainment" title="Reliance Entertainment">Reliance Entertainment</a> came together to form a new venture named <a href="/wiki/Plan_C_Studios" title="Plan C Studios">Plan C Studios</a> to produce films. The movie Rustom which was released in 2016 was the first film under this venture.<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Toilet_-_Ek_Prem_Katha_(film)" title="Toilet - Ek Prem Katha (film)">Toilet: Ek Prem Katha</a> and <a href="/wiki/Naam_Shabana" title="Naam Shabana">Naam Shabana</a>  are planned to be released worldwide in 2017 under Plan C Studios.
</p>
