	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">List of international cricket centuries by Martin Crowe</h1>

<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="545" data-file-width="564" decoding="async" height="213" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg/220px-Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg/330px-Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg/440px-Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Martin_Crowe_2011.jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Martin Crowe has scored 21 centuries in <a href="/wiki/Test_cricket" title="Test cricket">Test cricket</a> and <a href="/wiki/One_Day_International" title="One Day International">One Day Internationals</a>.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Martin_Crowe" title="Martin Crowe">Martin Crowe</a> was a <a href="/wiki/Cricket_in_New_Zealand" title="Cricket in New Zealand">New Zealand cricketer</a>. A right-handed <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Batsman" title="Batsman">batsman</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-cricinfo1_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-cricinfo1-1">[1]</a></sup> he played for <a href="/wiki/New_Zealand_national_cricket_team" title="New Zealand national cricket team">New Zealand</a> between 1982 and 1995, including captaining them for 16 Tests and 44 ODIs between 1990 and 1993,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and made 21 <a href="/wiki/Century_(cricket)" title="Century (cricket)">centuries</a> in international cricket, 17 in Tests and 4 in One Day Internationals. As of March 2019<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_international_cricket_centuries_by_Martin_Crowe&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup> he sits joint sixty-fourth in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_cricketers_by_number_of_international_centuries_scored" title="List of cricketers by number of international centuries scored">list of century-makers in international cricket</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[a]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-mostcenturies_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-mostcenturies-5">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>Crowe made his <a href="/wiki/Test_cricket" title="Test cricket">Test</a> debut against <a href="/wiki/Australia_national_cricket_team" title="Australia national cricket team">Australia</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Basin_Reserve" title="Basin Reserve">Basin Reserve</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wellington" title="Wellington">Wellington</a> on 26 February 1982.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[5]</a></sup> He scored his first Test century against <a href="/wiki/England_cricket_team" title="England cricket team">England</a>, also at the Basin Reserve on 20 January 1984, scoring 100.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1st_Test_100_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-1st_Test_100-7">[6]</a></sup> He scored his final Test century, also against England at <a href="/wiki/Old_Trafford_Cricket_Ground" title="Old Trafford Cricket Ground">Old Trafford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Manchester" title="Manchester">Manchester</a> on 30 June 1994,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-last_Test_100_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-last_Test_100-8">[7]</a></sup> scoring 115. He played his final Test match against <a href="/wiki/India_national_cricket_team" title="India national cricket team">India</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Barabati_Stadium" title="Barabati Stadium">Barabati Stadium</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cuttack" title="Cuttack">Cuttack</a> on 8 November 1995,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[8]</a></sup> having played 77 matches. His highest score in Tests is 299, scored against <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka_national_cricket_team" title="Sri Lanka national cricket team">Sri Lanka</a> at the Basin Reserve on 31 January 1991.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Test_high_score_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-Test_high_score-10">[9]</a></sup>
</p><p>Crowe made his <a href="/wiki/One_Day_International" title="One Day International">One Day International</a> (ODI) debut against <a href="/wiki/Australia_national_cricket_team" title="Australia national cricket team">Australia</a> at <a href="/wiki/Eden_Park" title="Eden Park">Eden Park</a>, <a href="/wiki/Auckland" title="Auckland">Auckland</a> on 13 February 1982.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[10]</a></sup> He scored his first ODI century against <a href="/wiki/England_cricket_team" title="England cricket team">England</a>, also at Eden Park, scoring 105 <a href="/wiki/Not_out" title="Not out">not out</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1st_ODI_100_12-0"><a href="#cite_note-1st_ODI_100-12">[11]</a></sup> He scored his final ODI century against <a href="/wiki/India_national_cricket_team" title="India national cricket team">India</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Keenan_Stadium" title="Keenan Stadium">Keenan Stadium</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jamshedpur" title="Jamshedpur">Jamshedpur</a> on 15 November 1995,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-last_ODI_100_13-0"><a href="#cite_note-last_ODI_100-13">[12]</a></sup> scoring 107 <a href="/wiki/Not_out" title="Not out">not out</a>, his highest score in ODIs. He played his final ODI in the final game of the same series against India at the <a href="/wiki/Vidarbha_Cricket_Association_Ground" title="Vidarbha Cricket Association Ground">Vidarbha Cricket Association Ground</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nagpur" title="Nagpur">Nagpur</a> on 26 November 1995,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[13]</a></sup> having played 143 matches.
</p>
