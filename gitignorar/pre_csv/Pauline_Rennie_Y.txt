	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Pauline Rennie</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">For the fictional character, see <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pauline_Rennie_(Under_the_Dome)" title="Pauline Rennie (Under the Dome)">Pauline Rennie (Under the Dome)</a>.</div>
<p><b>Pauline Rennie</b> (born January 4, 1945) is a <a href="/wiki/Canadians" title="Canadians">Canadian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voice_actress" title="Voice actress">voice actress</a> whose career included voice roles on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Care_Bears_(TV_series)" title="The Care Bears (TV series)"><i>The Care Bears</i></a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Rennie, who hails from <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>,  provided the voice of Fizgerald Fieldmouse on <i><a href="/wiki/Maggie_Muggins" title="Maggie Muggins">Maggie Muggins</a></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> as well as voices on <a href="/wiki/Mary_Grannan" title="Mary Grannan">Mary Grannan</a>'s follow-up 1960 series for the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Canadian Broadcasting Corporation">Canadian Broadcasting Corporation</a>, <i>Just Mary</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Queen's_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Queen's-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
