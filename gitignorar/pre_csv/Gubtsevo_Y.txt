<p><b>Gubtsevo</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Губцево</span>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">selo</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Gus-Khrustalny_District" title="Gus-Khrustalny District">Gus-Khrustalny District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vladimir_Oblast" title="Vladimir Oblast">Vladimir Oblast</a>, Russia. The population was 53 as of 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> There are 6 streets.
</p>
