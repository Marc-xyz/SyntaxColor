<p><b>Tolombeh-ye Nabvi Mohammadabad</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">تلمبه نبوي محمداباد</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Tolombeh-ye Nabvī Moḩammadābād</b>) is a village in <a href="/wiki/Golestan_Rural_District_(Kerman_Province)" title="Golestan Rural District (Kerman Province)">Golestan Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Sirjan_County)" title="Central District (Sirjan County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sirjan_County" title="Sirjan County">Sirjan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kerman_Province" title="Kerman Province">Kerman Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 87, in 22 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
