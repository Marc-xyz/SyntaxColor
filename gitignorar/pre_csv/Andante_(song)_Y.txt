<p>"<b>アンダンテ<span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">ANDANTE</span>)</span></b>" is a song by <a href="/wiki/Hitomi_Yaida" title="Hitomi Yaida">Hitomi Yaida</a>, released as her seventh Japanese single, and the second from the album <i><a href="/wiki/I/flancy" title="I/flancy">i/flancy</a></i>.
A limited edition version was released also, including a different coloured case and a CD-Extra track which could be used to access a special website to book tickets to an Hitomi Yaida performance.
</p><p>The B-side track "<a href="/wiki/Fast_Car" title="Fast Car">Fast Car</a>" is a cover version of the <a href="/wiki/Tracy_Chapman" title="Tracy Chapman">Tracy Chapman</a> song, Yaida continues to perform this track live and it appeared on her <a href="/wiki/MTV_Unplugged" title="MTV Unplugged">MTV Unplugged</a> performance.
</p><p>It reached number 4 in the charts on July 20 2002.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
