<p><b>Kamýk nad Vltavou</b> is a village and municipality in <a href="/wiki/P%C5%99%C3%ADbram_District" title="Příbram District">Příbram District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Central_Bohemian_Region" title="Central Bohemian Region">Central Bohemian Region</a>, <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic" title="Czech Republic">Czech Republic</a>. It lies on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vltava_River" title="Vltava River">Vltava River</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
