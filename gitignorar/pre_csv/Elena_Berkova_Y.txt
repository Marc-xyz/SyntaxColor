<p><b>Elena Sergeyevna Berkova</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Елена Серге́евна Беркова</span>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk">Оле́на Сергі́ївна Бе́ркова</span>, <b>Olena Serhiyvna Berkova</b>, born March 11, 1985) is a <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukrainian</a>-<a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russian</a> ex-<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Model_(profession)" title="Model (profession)">model</a>, <a href="/wiki/Television_presenter" title="Television presenter">television presenter</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singer</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Actress" title="Actress">actress</a> (including <a href="/wiki/Hardcore_pornography" title="Hardcore pornography">hardcore</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pornographic_actress" title="Pornographic actress">pornography acting</a>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2004, she joined the popular show <a href="/wiki/Dom-2" title="Dom-2">Dom-2</a> on TV channel <a href="/wiki/TNT_(Russian_TV_channel)" title="TNT (Russian TV channel)">TNT</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> In 2006, Elena appeared as a singer in with the <a href="/wiki/Pop_music" title="Pop music">pop music</a> <a href="/wiki/Girl_group" title="Girl group">girl group</a> Min net!.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>At age 24, Berkova gave birth to a son. The child's father is her third ex-husband, Ivan Belkov.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p><p>In March 2009, Berkova, being seven months pregnant, announced her intention to participate in the <a class="extiw" href="https://ru.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%92%D1%8B%D0%B1%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%8B_%D0%BC%D1%8D%D1%80%D0%B0_%D0%A1%D0%BE%D1%87%D0%B8_(2009)" title="ru:Выборы мэра Сочи (2009)">elections</a> of the mayor of <a href="/wiki/Sochi" title="Sochi">Sochi</a>. Her campaign video appeared on the Internet.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rr_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-rr-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup> Later Berkova refused to participate in the elections.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p><p>On November 2, 2017, Berkova announced her candidacy for the <a href="/wiki/2018_Russian_presidential_election" title="2018 Russian presidential election">2018 Russian presidential election</a> and even recorded a video with the  election program.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup>
</p>
