<p>The <b>Taizhou Wu</b> (台州片) is a Southern <a href="/wiki/Wu_Chinese" title="Wu Chinese">Wu Chinese</a> language spoken  in and around <a href="/wiki/Taizhou,_Zhejiang" title="Taizhou, Zhejiang">Taizhou</a> in <a href="/wiki/Zhejiang" title="Zhejiang">Zhejiang</a> province. It is to some extent mutually intelligible with <a href="/wiki/Taihu_Wu" title="Taihu Wu">Taihu Wu</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (April 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Accuracy_dispute#Disputed_statement" title="Wikipedia:Accuracy dispute"><span title="The material near this tag is possibly inaccurate or nonfactual. (April 2016)">dubious</span></a> <span class="metadata"> – <a href="/wiki/Talk:Taizhou_Wu#Dubious" title="Talk:Taizhou Wu">discuss</a></span></i>]</sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Avoid_weasel_words" title="Wikipedia:Avoid weasel words"><span title="The material near this tag possibly uses too vague attribution or weasel words. (April 2016)">which?</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p>
