		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Nanchang Foreign Language School</b> (南昌外国语学校) is a middle and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/High_school" title="High school">high school</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Nanchang" title="Nanchang">Nanchang</a>, the capital city of <a href="/wiki/Jiangxi" title="Jiangxi">Jiangxi</a> province, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%E2%80%99s_Republic_of_China" title="People’s Republic of China">China</a>. Designated by Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China as one of the only 16 high schools in Mainland China that possess "direct admission” quotas(保送生) to send students directly to universities in China while waiving the <b>National Higher Education Entrance Examination,</b>  It is the first council <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Foreign_language_school" title="Foreign language school">foreign language school</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Jiangxi" title="Jiangxi">Jiangxi</a> province and it is also a key school in <a href="/wiki/Nanchang" title="Nanchang">Nanchang</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History_and_location">History and location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanchang_Foreign_Language_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History and location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school was established in the 1994, and was originally called Nanchang No.22 School. It was situated in 126 Tao Yuan Zhong Road, <a href="/wiki/Nanchang" title="Nanchang">Nanchang</a>. The school occupies approximately 60 mu.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Since 1994, the president of Nanchang Foreign Language School has been Ms. Wang Xiuying.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Students">Students</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanchang_Foreign_Language_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Students">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Pupils from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Primary_schools" title="Primary schools">primary schools</a> around the city have to pass a special exam to register at the Nanchang Foreign Language School. Nanchang Foreign Language School is one of the 16 foreign language schools which can recommend <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/High_school" title="High school">high school</a> students for admission to college. It was about 20 per cent student have chance to take the walk exams in the 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> Nanchang Foreign Language School is made up of three-year <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/High_school" title="High school">high school</a> and three-year <a href="/wiki/Middle_school" title="Middle school">middle school</a>. There are 54 <a href="/wiki/Class_(education)" title="Class (education)">classes</a> in the school with 3,260 students. There are 24 classes in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/High_school" title="High school">high school</a> and 30 classes in the <a href="/wiki/Middle_school" title="Middle school">middle school</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Faculties">Faculties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanchang_Foreign_Language_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Faculties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2011, the staff and teaching faculty of Nanchang Foreign Language School include 213 people among whom are 26 teachers with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Master_degree" title="Master degree">Master degree</a> and many senior teachers. There are also several foreign teachers from Japan, Spain, the United States, the UK and other countries. And the teachers also have chance to be sent abroad to study like the United States, the UK, Japan and Australia and New Zealand.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Facilities">Facilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanchang_Foreign_Language_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Facilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Nanchang foreign language school is composed of two campuses including junior and senior. The school offers modern <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Classrooms" title="Classrooms">classrooms</a>, <a href="/wiki/Planetarium" title="Planetarium">planetarium</a>, ecological hall, computer rooms, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Libraries" title="Libraries">libraries</a>, <a href="/wiki/Laboratory" title="Laboratory">labs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lecture_hall" title="Lecture hall">lecture hall</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dining_rooms" title="Dining rooms">dining rooms</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gyms" title="Gyms">gyms</a> and other first class facilities to all the students and teachers.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Motto">Motto</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nanchang_Foreign_Language_School&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Motto">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Motto" title="Motto">motto</a> of Nanchang Foreign Language School is morality and dedication, integrity and education （Chinese:明德敬业，正己立人）. And school believes in the philosophy of "development for all, all are excellent".
</p>
