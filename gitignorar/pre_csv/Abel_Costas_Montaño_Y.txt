<p><b>Abel Costas Montaño</b> (May 25, 1920 – February 11, 2015) was a <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivian</a> <a href="/wiki/Prelate" title="Prelate">prelate</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Church" title="Roman Catholic Church">Roman Catholic Church</a>.
</p><p>Costas Montaño was born in Pocona, Bolivia and was ordained a priest on September 22, 1945. He was appointed auxiliary archbishop of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Cochabamba" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Cochabamba">Archdiocese of Cochabamba</a> on November 11, 1968, as well as titular bishop of Novi, and was ordained bishop on January 6, 1969. Montaño was appointed Bishop of the <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Diocese_of_Tarija" title="Roman Catholic Diocese of Tarija">Diocese of Tarija</a> on December 11, 1974, where he would serve until his retirement on October 20, 1995.
</p>
