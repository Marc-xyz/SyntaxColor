<p><b>John Blake</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/Make-up_artist" title="Make-up artist">make-up artist</a>. He has been nominated for one <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Academy_Award" title="Academy Award">Academy Award</a>, for the film <a href="/wiki/Hoffa" title="Hoffa">Hoffa</a>. He shared the nomination with <a href="/wiki/Ve_Neill" title="Ve Neill">Ve Neill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Greg_Cannom" title="Greg Cannom">Greg Cannom</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> He has worked on films for directors like <a href="/wiki/Oliver_Stone" title="Oliver Stone">Oliver Stone</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Coen_brothers" title="Coen brothers">Coen brothers</a> as key make-up artist on <a href="/wiki/Nixon_(film)" title="Nixon (film)">Nixon</a>, <a href="/wiki/U_Turn_(1997_film)" title="U Turn (1997 film)">U Turn</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Fargo_(film)" title="Fargo (film)">Fargo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> In more recent years, he has worked on <a href="/wiki/The_Avengers_(2012_film)" title="The Avengers (2012 film)">The Avengers</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Iron_Man_(2008_film)" title="Iron Man (2008 film)">Iron Man</a> trilogy, <a href="/wiki/There_Will_Be_Blood" title="There Will Be Blood">There Will Be Blood</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tropic_Thunder" title="Tropic Thunder">Tropic Thunder</a>, and many other films over a 30-year career.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
