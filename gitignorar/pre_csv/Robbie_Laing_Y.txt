<p><b>Robert Alan Laing</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (born March 5, 1958) is an American <a href="/wiki/College_basketball" title="College basketball">college basketball</a> coach, currently an assistant at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Central_Florida" title="University of Central Florida">University of Central Florida</a>. He had been the head men's basketball coach at <a href="/wiki/Campbell_University" title="Campbell University">Campbell University</a> from 2003 to 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
