<p><i><b>No Letting Go</b></i> is a 2015 American drama film directed by Jonathan D. Bucari.  It is Bucari's directorial debut and is based on his 2013 short film <i>Illness</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  It is also based on the true story of the producer and co-screenwriter Randi Silverman.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
