<p>The <a href="/wiki/Discography" title="Discography">discography</a> of <a href="/wiki/INXS" title="INXS">INXS</a>, an Australian <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> band, consists of 12 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album">studio albums</a>, 62 <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">singles</a>, 12 <a href="/wiki/Compilation_album" title="Compilation album">compilation albums</a>, 4 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Live_album" title="Live album">live albums</a> and 5 <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">extended plays</a>.
</p>

<p><b>Notes</b>
</p>
<ul><li><b>*</b> <span class="citation wikicite" id="endnote_*none"><a href="#ref_*none"><b><sup>^</sup></b></a></span>  When "Never Tear Us Apart" was first released in 1988, it originally peaked at number 14 in Australia. After the airing of the 2014 miniseries <i><a href="/wiki/INXS:_Never_Tear_Us_Apart" title="INXS: Never Tear Us Apart">INXS: Never Tear Us Apart</a></i>, the single charted again in Australia and ended up surpassing its original peak, peaking at number 11.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Featured">Featured</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=INXS_discography&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Featured">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2002 in America, <a href="/wiki/McDonald%27s" title="McDonald's">McDonald's</a> and <a href="/wiki/Toyota" title="Toyota">Toyota</a> used "<a href="/wiki/New_Sensation" title="New Sensation">New Sensation</a>" for a promo campaign; the song was also featured in the 2003 film <i><a href="/wiki/Shattered_Glass_(film)" title="Shattered Glass (film)">Shattered Glass</a></i>. "What You Need" featured in a Chevy trucks advertising campaign. In Australia, "<a href="/wiki/By_My_Side_(INXS_song)" title="By My Side (INXS song)">By My Side</a>" was used by insurance company <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NRMA" title="NRMA">NRMA</a> on and off for eight years. "<a href="/wiki/The_One_Thing_(song)" title="The One Thing (song)">The One Thing</a>" is featured in <i><a href="/wiki/Grand_Theft_Auto:_Vice_City_Stories" title="Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories">Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories</a></i> under the fictional radio station <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Flash_FM" title="Flash FM">Flash FM</a>.
