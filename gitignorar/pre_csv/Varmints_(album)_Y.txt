<p><i><b>Varmints</b></i> is the debut studio album by the British composer and <a href="/wiki/Electronica" title="Electronica">electronica</a> musician <a href="/wiki/Anna_Meredith" title="Anna Meredith">Anna Meredith</a>, released on 4 March 2016 on <a href="/wiki/Moshi_Moshi_Records" title="Moshi Moshi Records">Moshi Moshi</a>. The album won <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Album_of_the_Year_Award" title="Scottish Album of the Year Award">Scottish Album of the Year Award</a> in 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BBCAwardNews_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-BBCAwardNews-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In a highly positive review for <i><a href="/wiki/Pitchfork_(website)" title="Pitchfork (website)">Pitchfork</a></i>, Laura Snapes awarded <i>Varmints</i> with the website's "Best New Music" label, writing: "[Anna Meredith's] first wholly independent project reveals her to be one of the most innovative minds in modern British music: She wears her obvious theoretical grounding lightly and never lets it obstruct her ecstatic quest for new ideas and deranged stimuli. And <i>Varmints</i> is a knockout, the kind that makes you see cartoon stars."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-PitchReview_8-1"><a href="#cite_note-PitchReview-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
