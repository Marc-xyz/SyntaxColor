	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Unterwalden frank</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Frank</b> was the currency of the <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Swiss</a> <a href="/wiki/Cantons_of_Switzerland" title="Cantons of Switzerland">canton</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canton_of_Unterwalden" title="Canton of Unterwalden">Unterwalden</a> between 1798 and 1850. It was subdivided into 10 <i>Batzen</i>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Unterwalden_frank&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Swiss_franc#History" title="Swiss franc">Frank</a> was the currency of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Helvetian_Republic" title="Helvetian Republic">Helvetian Republic</a> from 1798. The Helvetian Republic ceased issuing coins in 1803. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nidwalden" title="Nidwalden">Nidwalden</a> issued coins in 1811 and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Obwalden" title="Obwalden">Obwalden</a> issued coins in 1812. In 1850, the <a href="/wiki/Swiss_franc" title="Swiss franc">Swiss franc</a> was introduced, with ​<span class="frac nowrap">1<span class="visualhide"> </span><sup>1</sup>⁄<sub>2</sub></span> Swiss francs = 1 Unterwalden Frank.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Coins">Coins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Unterwalden_frank&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Coins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Both half cantons issued billon coins in denominations of ​<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>⁄<sub>2</sub></span> and 1 Batzen, together with silver coins for 5 Batzen.
</p>
