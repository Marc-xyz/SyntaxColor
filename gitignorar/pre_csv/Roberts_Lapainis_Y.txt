<p><b>Roberts Lapainis</b> (September 10, 1913 – September 30, 1974) was a <a href="/wiki/Latvia" title="Latvia">Latvian</a> ice hockey <a href="/wiki/Goaltender" title="Goaltender">goaltender</a>. He played with the <a href="/wiki/Latvia_men%27s_national_ice_hockey_team" title="Latvia men's national ice hockey team">Latvia men's national ice hockey team</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Latvia_at_the_1936_Winter_Olympics" title="Latvia at the 1936 Winter Olympics">1936 Winter Olympics</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Garmisch-Partenkirchen" title="Garmisch-Partenkirchen">Garmisch-Partenkirchen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
