<p>The <b>1964 CONMEBOL Pre-Olympic Tournament</b> took place during May and June 1964. It was the 2nd <a href="/wiki/CONMEBOL_Pre-Olympic_Tournament" title="CONMEBOL Pre-Olympic Tournament">CONMEBOL Pre-Olympic Tournament</a>.
</p><p>Bolivia, Paraguay and Venezuela did not participate. Argentina and Brazil qualified for the <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_1964_Summer_Olympics" title="Football at the 1964 Summer Olympics">1964 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-rsssf_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-rsssf-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>It is estimated that <a href="/wiki/Estadio_Nacional_disaster" title="Estadio Nacional disaster">328 people died in a stampede</a> during the match between Argentina and Peru on 24 May 1964.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
