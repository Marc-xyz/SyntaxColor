<p><b>Robert Levenhagen</b> (October 2, 1924 – September 1973) was a <a href="/wiki/Canadian_football" title="Canadian football">Canadian football</a> player who played for the <a href="/wiki/BC_Lions" title="BC Lions">BC Lions</a> and <a href="/wiki/Calgary_Stampeders" title="Calgary Stampeders">Calgary Stampeders</a>. He played college football at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Washington" title="University of Washington">University of Washington</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
