<p>The <b>Ardeu</b> is a right <a href="/wiki/Tributary" title="Tributary">tributary</a> of the river <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B%C4%83c%C3%A2ia_(river)" title="Băcâia (river)">Băcâia</a> in <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RO_Annex7409_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-RO_Annex7409-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RO_Atlas176IV.1.111.6.2_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-RO_Atlas176IV.1.111.6.2-2">[2]</a></sup> It flows into the Băcâia in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boze%C8%99" title="Bozeș">Bozeș</a>. Its length is 25 km (16 mi) and its basin size is 48 km<sup>2</sup> (19 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RO_Atlas176IV.1.111.6.2_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-RO_Atlas176IV.1.111.6.2-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
