<p><b>Trubizh</b> (<a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk">Трубі́ж</span>, <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Трубе́ж</span>) is a river entirely located in <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Left_tributary" title="Left tributary">left tributary</a> of <a href="/wiki/Dnieper" title="Dnieper">Dnieper</a>. It falls into the Dnieper's <a href="/wiki/Kaniv_Reservoir" title="Kaniv Reservoir">Kaniv Reservoir</a> (named after <a href="/wiki/Kaniv" title="Kaniv">Kaniv</a>).
</p><p>It is not to be confused with the Russian <a href="/wiki/Trubezh_River" title="Trubezh River">Trubezh River</a> (both of them are called Трубеж in Russian).
</p><p>Major cities: <a href="/wiki/Pereiaslav" title="Pereiaslav">Pereiaslav</a>.
</p>
