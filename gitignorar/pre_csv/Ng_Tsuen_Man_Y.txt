		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Ng Tsuen Man</b> (born June 11, 1948) is a <a href="/wiki/Hong_Kong" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canoe_racing" title="Canoe racing">sprint canoer</a> who competed from the mid-1970s to the mid-1980s. At the <a href="/wiki/1976_Summer_Olympics" title="1976 Summer Olympics">1976 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, he was eliminated in the repechages of both the <a href="/wiki/Canoeing_at_the_1976_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_K-1_500_metres" title="Canoeing at the 1976 Summer Olympics – Men's K-1 500 metres">K-1 500 m</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Canoeing_at_the_1976_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_K-4_1000_metres" title="Canoeing at the 1976 Summer Olympics – Men's K-4 1000 metres">K-4 1000 m</a> events. <a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">Eight years later</a> in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, Ng was eliminated in the repechages of the <a href="/wiki/Canoeing_at_the_1984_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_K-4_1000_metres" title="Canoeing at the 1984 Summer Olympics – Men's K-4 1000 metres">K-4 1000 m</a> event.
</p>
