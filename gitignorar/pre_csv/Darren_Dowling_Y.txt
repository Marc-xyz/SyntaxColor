<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Darren_Dowling&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="British_Touring_Car_Championship">British Touring Car Championship</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Darren_Dowling&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: British Touring Car Championship">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Dowling drove the final six rounds of the <a href="/wiki/British_Touring_Car_Championship" title="British Touring Car Championship">British Touring Car Championship</a> in 2006. He raced in a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lexus_IS200" title="Lexus IS200">Lexus IS200</a> for the newly formed <a href="/wiki/BTC_Racing" title="BTC Racing">BTC Racing</a> team alongside team-mate <a href="/wiki/Chris_Stockton" title="Chris Stockton">Chris Stockton</a>. His best ever finish was a fourteenth position at <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone</a>, ending the season with no points. The team continued in 2007, but he was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Nick_Leason" title="Nick Leason">Nick Leason</a>.
</p>
