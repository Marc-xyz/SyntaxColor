<p><i><b>Manay Na Ye Dil</b></i> (lit: The heart does not accept) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pakistani" title="Pakistani">Pakistani</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Television_series" title="Television series">television series</a> that aired on <a href="/wiki/Hum_TV" title="Hum TV">Hum TV</a> and is directed by Babar Jawad.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The story is based on the emotional conflicts of four people who feel powerless to control their feelings.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot">Plot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Manay_Na_Ye_Dil&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Plot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zainab (Deepti Gupta), an orphan who has been raised by her aunt, has since childhood been in love with her aunt's son, Sheheryar (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Faisal_Qureshi_(actor)" title="Faisal Qureshi (actor)">Faisal Qureshi</a>). Sheheryar ends up in an arranged marriage with Rubab (<a href="/wiki/Nadia_Hussain" title="Nadia Hussain">Nadia Hussain</a>), a selfish woman and the complete opposite of what Sheheryar expects in a wife. When Rubab aborts their child, Sheheryar loses his mind and in deep depression, he becomes involved with Roshni (<a href="/wiki/Ayesha_Khan" title="Ayesha Khan">Ayesha Khan</a>), a prostitute. Sheheryar falls in love with Roshni, but his mother manipulates the situation in such a way that Zainab and Sheheryar get married. Sheheryar, however, does not forget his love and goes in search of Roshni. He finds that Roshni is happily married to another man (<a href="/wiki/Faisal_Rehman" title="Faisal Rehman">Faisal Rehman</a>) in (<a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>) with a son. Sheheryar does not know that he is in fact  the father of the child. Yet Sheheryar due to his obsession, tries to reconcile his relation with Roshni, and they succeed. The drama ends with both leaving together.
</p>
