<p><b>Rogalinek</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[rɔɡaˈlinɛk]</a></span> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i lang="de">Warthebrück</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Mosina" title="Gmina Mosina">Gmina Mosina</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84_County" title="Poznań County">Poznań County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Poland_Voivodeship" title="Greater Poland Voivodeship">Greater Poland Voivodeship</a>, in west-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) east of <a href="/wiki/Mosina" title="Mosina">Mosina</a> and 17 km (11 mi) south of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84" title="Poznań">Poznań</a>.
</p><p>The village has an approximate population of 1,501.
</p>
