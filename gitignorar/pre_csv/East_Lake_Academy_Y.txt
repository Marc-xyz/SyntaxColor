<p><b>East Lake Academy</b> is an independent Roman Catholic school in <a href="/wiki/Lake_Forest,_Illinois" title="Lake Forest, Illinois">Lake Forest, Illinois</a> for children from pre-kindergarten through eighth grade. It is located in East Lake Forest. Lake Forest, IL, 60045 is about 45-60 minutes away from downtown Chicago. 
</p>
