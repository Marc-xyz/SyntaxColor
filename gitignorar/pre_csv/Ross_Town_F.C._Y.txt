<p><b>Ross Town F.C.</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Ross-on-Wye" title="Ross-on-Wye">Ross-on-Wye</a>, <a href="/wiki/Herefordshire" title="Herefordshire">Herefordshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>. The club was formed in the summer of 1993 following the amalgamation of the town's two senior sides, Ross United and Woodville, and joined the Herefordshire League Premier Division. They joined the <a href="/wiki/Hellenic_Football_League" title="Hellenic Football League">Hellenic Football League</a> Division One in 1996 but dropped back down to the <a href="/wiki/Herefordshire_Football_League" title="Herefordshire Football League">Herefordshire League</a> in 2007. In late 2009 Ross Town resigned from the Herefordshire League and folded.
</p><p>Their ground was on a loop of the <a href="/wiki/River_Wye" title="River Wye">River Wye</a>. The club was affiliated to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Herefordshire_County_FA" title="Herefordshire County FA">Herefordshire County FA</a>.
