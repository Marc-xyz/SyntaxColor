<p><b>Andreas Garyfallos</b> (born 1931) is a Greek <a href="/wiki/Water_polo" title="Water polo">water polo</a> player. He competed in the <a href="/wiki/Water_polo_at_the_1968_Summer_Olympics" title="Water polo at the 1968 Summer Olympics">men's tournament</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1968_Summer_Olympics" title="1968 Summer Olympics">1968 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
