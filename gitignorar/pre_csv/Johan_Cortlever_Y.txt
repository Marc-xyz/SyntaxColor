<table class="infobox biography vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold"><div class="fn" style="display:inline">Johan George Cortlever</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Born</th><td>4 August 1885<br/><div class="birthplace" style="display:inline"><a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a></div></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Died</th><td>14 April 1972<span style="display:none"> (<span class="dtend dday deathdate">1972-04-15</span>)</span> (aged 86)<br/><div class="deathplace" style="display:inline">Amsterdam</div></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Nationality</th><td class="category"><a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Dutch</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Occupation</th><td class="role">swimmer</td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Known for</th><td>1908, 1920 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Summer_Olympics" title="Summer Olympics">Summer Olympics</a></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Johan George Cortlever</b> (4 August 1885 in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a> – 14 April 1972 in Amsterdam) was a <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Dutch</a> <a href="/wiki/Backstroke" title="Backstroke">backstroke</a> swimmer and <a href="/wiki/Water_polo" title="Water polo">water polo</a> player who competed in the <a href="/wiki/1908_Summer_Olympics" title="1908 Summer Olympics">1908 Summer Olympics</a> and in the <a href="/wiki/1920_Summer_Olympics" title="1920 Summer Olympics">1920 Summer Olympics</a>.
</p><p>In 1908, he competed in the <a href="/wiki/Swimming_at_the_1908_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_100_metre_backstroke" title="Swimming at the 1908 Summer Olympics – Men's 100 metre backstroke">100 metre backstroke</a> competition, but he was eliminated in the first round.
</p><p>He was part of the Dutch water polo team, which finished fourth in the <a href="/wiki/Water_polo_at_the_1908_Summer_Olympics" title="Water polo at the 1908 Summer Olympics">1908 tournament</a>.
</p><p>Twelve years later he was a member of the Dutch water polo team, which finished sixth in the <a href="/wiki/Water_polo_at_the_1920_Summer_Olympics" title="Water polo at the 1920 Summer Olympics">1920 tournament</a>.
</p>
