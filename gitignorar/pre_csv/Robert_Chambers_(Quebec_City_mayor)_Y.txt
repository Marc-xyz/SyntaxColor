<p><b>Robert Chambers</b> (1834–1886)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> was a Canadian politician, serving as Mayor of <a href="/wiki/Quebec_City" title="Quebec City">Quebec City</a> from May 1878 to May 1880.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
