<p><b><a href="/wiki/Brighton" title="Brighton">Brighton</a> Sithebe</b> (born 15 August 1985 in <a href="/wiki/Tembisa" title="Tembisa">Tembisa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gauteng" title="Gauteng">Gauteng</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South African</a> former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_(soccer)" title="Football (soccer)">football (soccer)</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Striker_(association_football)" title="Striker (association football)">striker</a> and <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for <a href="/wiki/Premier_Soccer_League" title="Premier Soccer League">Premier Soccer League</a> club <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chippa_United" title="Chippa United">Chippa United</a> and <a href="/wiki/South_Africa_national_football_team" title="South Africa national football team">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International">International</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lerato_Chabangu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: International">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He made his debut in a <a href="/wiki/COSAFA_Cup" title="COSAFA Cup">COSAFA Cup</a> match against the <a href="/wiki/Seychelles_national_football_team" title="Seychelles national football team">Seychelles</a> on 26 February 2005 and was part of Bafana Bafana's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2008_African_Nations_Cup" title="2008 African Nations Cup">2008 African Nations Cup</a> squad and the <a href="/wiki/2013_Africa_Cup_of_Nations" title="2013 Africa Cup of Nations">2013 Africa Cup of Nations</a> squad.
</p><p>He was called back after two years by <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Igesund" title="Gordon Igesund">Gordon Igesund</a> for <a href="/wiki/2014_African_Nations_Championship" title="2014 African Nations Championship">CHAN 2014</a> hosted in <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a> and he played a full match in final group stage against <a href="/wiki/Nigeria_national_football_team" title="Nigeria national football team">Nigeria</a> in 3–1 defeat. That was his final game for Bafana and he was personal criticize together with <a href="/wiki/Moeneeb_Josephs" title="Moeneeb Josephs">Moeneeb Josephs</a> for a loss against Nigeria by then <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minister_of_Sport_and_Recreation_(South_Africa)" title="Minister of Sport and Recreation (South Africa)">Minister of Sport and Recreation</a> <a href="/wiki/Fikile_Mbalula" title="Fikile Mbalula">Fikile Mbalula</a>, when he said "<b>Bafana Bafana are week, useless and they are the bunch of losers individual</b>."
</p>
