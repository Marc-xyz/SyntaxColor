<p><b>North Page</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in <a href="/wiki/Fayette_County,_West_Virginia" title="Fayette County, West Virginia">Fayette County</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia" title="West Virginia">West Virginia</a>, United States. North Page is 7.5 miles (12.1 km) southwest of <a href="/wiki/Gauley_Bridge,_West_Virginia" title="Gauley Bridge, West Virginia">Gauley Bridge</a>.
</p>
