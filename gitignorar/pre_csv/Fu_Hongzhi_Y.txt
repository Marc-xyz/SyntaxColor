<p><b>Fu Hongzhi</b> is a Chinese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paralympic" title="Paralympic">paralympic</a> <a href="/wiki/Archery" title="Archery">archer</a>. She won the silver medal at the <a href="/wiki/Archery_at_the_2008_Summer_Paralympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_individual_recurve" title="Archery at the 2008 Summer Paralympics – Women's individual recurve">Women's individual recurve - W1/W2</a> event at the <a href="/wiki/2008_Summer_Paralympics" title="2008 Summer Paralympics">2008 Summer Paralympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Beijing" title="Beijing">Beijing</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
