<p>The <b>2013 Southern Jaguars football team</b> represented <a href="/wiki/Southern_University" title="Southern University">Southern University</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2013_NCAA_Division_I_FCS_football_season" title="2013 NCAA Division I FCS football season">2013 NCAA Division I FCS football season</a>. The Jaguars were led by first year head coach <a href="/wiki/Dawson_Odums" title="Dawson Odums">Dawson Odums</a>, who was made the new head coach he served serving as interim head coach for the final nine games of the previous season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The Jaguars played their home games at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ace_W._Mumford_Stadium" title="Ace W. Mumford Stadium">Ace W. Mumford Stadium</a> and were a member of the West Division of the <a href="/wiki/Southwestern_Athletic_Conference" title="Southwestern Athletic Conference">Southwestern Athletic Conference</a> (SWAC). The Jaguars finished the season with a <abbr title="9 wins, 4 losses">9–4</abbr> record, as West Division Champions and with a victory over <a href="/wiki/2013_Jackson_State_Tigers_football_team" title="2013 Jackson State Tigers football team">Jackson State</a> in the <a href="/wiki/SWAC_Championship_Game" title="SWAC Championship Game">SWAC Championship Game</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Champ_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Champ-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
