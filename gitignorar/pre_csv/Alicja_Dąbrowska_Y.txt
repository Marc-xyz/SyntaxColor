<p><b>Alicja Dąbrowska</b> (born 1956) is a Polish politician and doctor. She held a seat in the <a href="/wiki/Sejm" title="Sejm">Sejm</a>, being elected in 2007, 2011, and 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In 2019 she ran but was not elected.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
