<p>The <b>1992 Individual Long Track World Championship</b> was the 22nd edition of the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_Motocyclisme" title="Fédération Internationale de Motocyclisme">FIM</a> <a href="/wiki/Motorcycle_speedway" title="Motorcycle speedway">speedway</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Individual_Long_Track_World_Championship" title="Individual Long Track World Championship">Individual Long Track World Championship</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The event was held on 13 September 1992 in <a href="/wiki/Pfarrkirchen" title="Pfarrkirchen">Pfarrkirchen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>The world title was won by <a href="/wiki/Marcel_Gerhard" title="Marcel Gerhard">Marcel Gerhard</a> of Switzerland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
