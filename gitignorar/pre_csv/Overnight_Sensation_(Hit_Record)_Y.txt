<p>"<b>Overnight Sensation (Hit Record)</b>" is a hit single by <a href="/wiki/Raspberries_(band)" title="Raspberries (band)">Raspberries</a>, released in September 1974.  It was written by band leader <a href="/wiki/Eric_Carmen" title="Eric Carmen">Eric Carmen</a>, who also provided the lead vocals. It was the first single release from their fourth and final LP, <i><a href="/wiki/Starting_Over_(Raspberries_album)" title="Starting Over (Raspberries album)">Starting Over</a></i>.
</p><p>The song reached the Top 40 on three principal US charts, number 18 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>, number 24 on <i><a href="/wiki/Cashbox_(magazine)" title="Cashbox (magazine)">Cashbox</a></i>, and number 26 on <i><a href="/wiki/Record_World" title="Record World">Record World</a></i>. It was also a number 22 hit in Canada. It spent 12 weeks on <i>Billboard</i>, and was their final charting single release.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/John_Lennon" title="John Lennon">John Lennon</a>, a Raspberries fan, particularly liked "Overnight Sensation" and was present for part of the recording of the <i>Starting Over</i> album.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> Although uncredited, he is said to have assisted with the production of the song.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Later_uses">Later uses</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Overnight_Sensation_(Hit_Record)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Later uses">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Overnight Sensation" was included on the <i>Raspberries Pop Art Live</i> CD set from their reunion concert recording, November 26, 2004, at the House of Blues in Cleveland, Ohio, released August 18, 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Cover_versions">Cover versions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Overnight_Sensation_(Hit_Record)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Cover versions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1980, <a href="/wiki/Cherie_Currie" title="Cherie Currie">Cherie</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/Marie_Currie" title="Marie Currie">Marie Currie</a> recorded "Overnight Sensation". It was included on their album, <i><a href="/wiki/Messin%27_with_the_Boys" title="Messin' with the Boys">Messin' with the Boys</a></i>.
</p><p>The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kevin_McDermott_Orchestra" title="Kevin McDermott Orchestra">Kevin McDermott Orchestra</a> recorded "Overnight Sensation" for their 1994 album, "The Last Supper".
</p>
