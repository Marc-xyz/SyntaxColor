<p><b>Bagherpara</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bengali_language" title="Bengali language">Bengali</a>: <span lang="bn">বাঘারপাড়া</span>) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Upazila" title="Upazila">Upazila</a> of <a href="/wiki/Jessore_District" title="Jessore District">Jessore District</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Banglapedia_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Banglapedia-1">[1]</a></sup> in the Division of <a href="/wiki/Khulna_Division" title="Khulna Division">Khulna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bangladesh" title="Bangladesh">Bangladesh</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bagherpara_Upazila&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bagherpara is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Bagherpara_Upazila&amp;params=23.2167_N_89.3500_E_"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">23°13′00″N</span> <span class="longitude">89°21′00″E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">23.2167°N 89.3500°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">23.2167; 89.3500</span></span></span></a></span>. It has a total area of 308.29 km².
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bagherpara_Upazila&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/2011_Bangladesh_census" title="2011 Bangladesh census">2011 Bangladesh census</a>, Bagherpara had a population of 216,897. Males constituted 49.59% of the population and females 50.41%. Muslims formed 83.60% of the population, Hindus 16.28%, Christians 0.01% and others 0.11%. Bagherpara had a literacy rate of 52.79% for the population 7 years and above.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>As of the <a href="/wiki/1991_Bangladesh_census" title="1991 Bangladesh census">1991 Bangladesh census</a>, Bagherpara has a population of 168,938. Males constituted 51.21% of the population, and females 48.79%; this Upazila's eighteen-up population was 84303. Bagherpara has an average literacy rate of 34.5% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-census-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
