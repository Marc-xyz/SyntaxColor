<p><b>Manuel Garcia</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Census-designated_place" title="Census-designated place">census-designated place</a> (CDP) in <a href="/wiki/Starr_County,_Texas" title="Starr County, Texas">Starr County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a>, United States. It is a new CDP formed from part of the former <a href="/wiki/La_Casita-Garciasville,_Texas" title="La Casita-Garciasville, Texas">La Casita-Garciasville</a> CDP prior to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Census_2010" title="United States Census 2010">2010 census</a> with a population of 203.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
