<p><i><b>Changping of the War</b></i>, also known as <i><b>The Battle of Changping</b></i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a Chinese television series based on the events surrounding the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Changping" title="Battle of Changping">Battle of Changping</a>, fought between the states of <a href="/wiki/Qin_(state)" title="Qin (state)">Qin</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zhao_(state)" title="Zhao (state)">Zhao</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Warring_States_period" title="Warring States period">Warring States period</a>. The series was produced in 2004 but has yet to be aired on television stations in China.
</p>
