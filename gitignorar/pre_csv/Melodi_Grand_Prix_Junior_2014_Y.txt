	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Melodi Grand Prix Junior 2014</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Melodi Grand Prix Junior 2014</b> was Norway's thirteenth national <a href="/wiki/Melodi_Grand_Prix_Junior" title="Melodi Grand Prix Junior">Melodi Grand Prix Junior</a> for young <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Singer" title="Singer">singers</a> aged 8 to 15. It was held in <a href="/wiki/Oslo_Spektrum" title="Oslo Spektrum">Oslo Spektrum</a>, <a href="/wiki/Oslo" title="Oslo">Oslo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Norway" title="Norway">Norway</a> and broadcast live by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Norwegian_Broadcasting_Corporation" title="Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation">Norwegian Broadcasting Corporation</a> (NRK). It was hosted by <a href="/wiki/Margrethe_R%C3%B8ed" title="Margrethe Røed">Margrethe Røed</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tooji" title="Tooji">Tooji</a>.
</p><p>The winner was 13-year-old <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mathea-Mari&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mathea-Mari (page does not exist)">Mathea-Mari</a> with the song "#Online".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
