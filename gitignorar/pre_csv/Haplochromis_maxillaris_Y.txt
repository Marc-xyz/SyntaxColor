<p><i><b>Haplochromis maxillaris</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Cichlid" title="Cichlid">cichlid</a> <a href="/wiki/Endemism" title="Endemism">endemic</a> to <a href="/wiki/Lake_Victoria" title="Lake Victoria">Lake Victoria</a>.  This species can reach a length of 16 centimetres (6.3 in) <a href="/wiki/Fish_measurement" title="Fish measurement">SL</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
