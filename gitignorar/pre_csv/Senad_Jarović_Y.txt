<p><b>Senad Jarović</b> (born 20 January 1998) is a Bosnian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">forward</a> for <a href="/wiki/FC_ViOn_Zlat%C3%A9_Moravce" title="FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce">FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce</a> on loan from <a href="/wiki/S%C3%B8nderjyskE_Fodbold" title="SønderjyskE Fodbold">SønderjyskE</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Senad_Jarovi%C4%87&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He made his professional debut in the <a href="/wiki/Slovenian_PrvaLiga" title="Slovenian PrvaLiga">Slovenian PrvaLiga</a> for <a href="/wiki/NK_Dom%C5%BEale" title="NK Domžale">NK Domžale</a> on 28 February 2016 in a game against <a href="/wiki/NK_Krka" title="NK Krka">NK Krka</a> and scored on his debut.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 10 February 2019, Jarović signed with <a href="/wiki/S%C3%B8nderjyskE_Fodbold" title="SønderjyskE Fodbold">SønderjyskE</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Danish_Superliga" title="Danish Superliga">Danish Superliga</a> until the summer 2022.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> On 21 August 2019, he was loaned out to <a href="/wiki/FC_ViOn_Zlat%C3%A9_Moravce" title="FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce">FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce</a> for the rest of 2019.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
