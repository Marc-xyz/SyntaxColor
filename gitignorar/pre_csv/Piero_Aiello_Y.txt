<p><b>Piero Aiello</b> (born 30 June 1956) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Italian_people" title="Italian people">Italian</a> politician from the <a href="/wiki/New_Centre-Right" title="New Centre-Right">New Centre-Right</a>. Since 2013 he serves as Senator of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Italy" title="Parliament of Italy">Parliament of Italy</a> representing <a href="/wiki/Calabria" title="Calabria">Calabria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
