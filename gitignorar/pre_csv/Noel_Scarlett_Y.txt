<p><b>Noel Scarlett</b> (born January 21, 1974) is a former <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> <a href="/wiki/Defensive_tackle" title="Defensive tackle">defensive tackle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/National_Football_League" title="National Football League">National Football League</a> for the <a href="/wiki/New_England_Patriots" title="New England Patriots">New England Patriots</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kansas_City_Chiefs" title="Kansas City Chiefs">Kansas City Chiefs</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Cowboys" title="Dallas Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</a>. He also was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Claymores" title="Scottish Claymores">Scottish Claymores</a> in <a href="/wiki/NFL_Europe" title="NFL Europe">NFL Europe</a>. He played <a href="/wiki/College_football" title="College football">college football</a> at <a href="/wiki/Langston_University" title="Langston University">Langston University</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scarlett attended <a href="/wiki/Elbert_County_High_School" title="Elbert County High School">Elbert County High School</a>, where he was a two-way lineman. As a junior, he received All-area and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Atlanta_Journal-Constitution" title="Atlanta Journal-Constitution">Atlanta Journal-Constitution</a> Super Senior honors. As a senior, he didn't play football because of trouble with the law.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-afl_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-afl-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>He worked for 2 years in a produce plant making his way up to a supervisor's role, before accepting a football scholarship from <a href="/wiki/National_Association_of_Intercollegiate_Athletics" title="National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics">NAIA</a> <a href="/wiki/Langston_University" title="Langston University">Langston University</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-afl_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-afl-1">[1]</a></sup> As a sophomore he was named a starter at <a href="/wiki/Defensive_tackle" title="Defensive tackle">defensive tackle</a>, tallying 40 tackles (6 for loss) and 2 sacks. As a junior, he posted 40 tackles and 6 sacks. 
</p><p>As a senior, he led the team with a career-high 9.5 sacks and 9 tackles for loss. He had 3 sacks against <a href="/wiki/Oklahoma_Panhandle_State_University" title="Oklahoma Panhandle State University">Oklahoma Panhandle State University</a>. He finished his college career with 120 tackles (93 solo), 19 tackles for loss and 17.5 sacks. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional_career">Professional career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Professional career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="Minnesota_Vikings_.28first_stint.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Minnesota_Vikings_(first_stint)">Minnesota Vikings (first stint)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Minnesota Vikings (first stint)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Scarlett was selected by the <a href="/wiki/Minnesota_Vikings" title="Minnesota Vikings">Minnesota Vikings</a> in the seventh round (236th overall) of the <a href="/wiki/1999_NFL_Draft" title="1999 NFL Draft">1999 NFL Draft</a>. He was waived on September 5.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> 
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="New_England_Patriots">New England Patriots</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: New England Patriots">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On December 22, <a href="/wiki/1999_NFL_season" title="1999 NFL season">1999</a>, he was signed by the <a href="/wiki/New_England_Patriots" title="New England Patriots">New England Patriots</a> to their <a href="/wiki/Practice_squad" title="Practice squad">practice squad</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> He was released on December 29. He was re-signed on January 5, <a href="/wiki/2000_NFL_season" title="2000 NFL season">2000</a>. 
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/2000_NFL_season" title="2000 NFL season">2000</a>, he was allocated to the <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Claymores" title="Scottish Claymores">Scottish Claymores</a> of <a href="/wiki/NFL_Europe" title="NFL Europe">NFL Europe</a>. He recorded 20 tackles and 2.5 sacks for a defense that gave up only 76.3 rushing yards-per-game and helped the team reach <a href="/wiki/World_Bowl_2000" title="World Bowl 2000">World Bowl 2000</a>. He was released by the Patriots on August 1.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Miami_Dolphins">Miami Dolphins</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Miami Dolphins">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On August 3, <a href="/wiki/2000_NFL_season" title="2000 NFL season">2000</a>, he was signed as a <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agent</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Miami_Dolphins" title="Miami Dolphins">Miami Dolphins</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> He was cut on August 14.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Kansas_City_Chiefs">Kansas City Chiefs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Kansas City Chiefs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On October 25, he was signed by the <a href="/wiki/Kansas_City_Chiefs" title="Kansas City Chiefs">Kansas City Chiefs</a> to their <a href="/wiki/Practice_squad" title="Practice squad">practice squad</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Dallas_Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Dallas Cowboys">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On December 15, <a href="/wiki/2000_NFL_season" title="2000 NFL season">2000</a>, he was signed by the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Cowboys" title="Dallas Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</a> from the Chiefs' <a href="/wiki/Practice_squad" title="Practice squad">practice squad</a>, who were having injury problems on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defensive_line" title="Defensive line">defensive line</a>. He appeared as a backup in the season finale against the <a href="/wiki/Tennessee_Titans" title="Tennessee Titans">Tennessee Titans</a>. He was released on August 6, <a href="/wiki/2001_NFL_season" title="2001 NFL season">2001</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tulsa_Talons">Tulsa Talons</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Tulsa Talons">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/2002_AF2_season" title="2002 AF2 season">2002</a>, he signed with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tulsa_Talons" title="Tulsa Talons">Tulsa Talons</a> of the <a href="/wiki/AF2" title="AF2">AF2</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-afl_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-afl-1">[1]</a></sup> He appeared in 5 games, registering 8 tackles (6 solo), one tackle for loss, one sack, 2 forced fumbles and one fumble recovery.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="Minnesota_Vikings_.28second_stint.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Minnesota_Vikings_(second_stint)">Minnesota Vikings (second stint)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Minnesota Vikings (second stint)">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In July <a href="/wiki/2002_NFL_season" title="2002 NFL season">2002</a>, he was signed as a <a href="/wiki/Free_agent" title="Free agent">free agent</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Minnesota_Vikings" title="Minnesota Vikings">Minnesota Vikings</a>. He was released on August 17.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Calgary_Stampeders">Calgary Stampeders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Calgary Stampeders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/2003_CFL_season" title="2003 CFL season">2003</a>, he signed with the <a href="/wiki/Calgary_Stampeders" title="Calgary Stampeders">Calgary Stampeders</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Football_League" title="Canadian Football League">Canadian Football League</a>. He was released on June 5.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Coaching_career">Coaching career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Noel_Scarlett&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Coaching career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2016, he was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defensive_line" title="Defensive line">defensive line</a> coach at <a href="/wiki/Southwest_Christian_School_(Fort_Worth,_Texas)" title="Southwest Christian School (Fort Worth, Texas)">Southwest Christian School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Fort_Worth,_Texas" title="Fort Worth, Texas">Fort Worth, Texas</a>. In 2017, he was an intern <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defensive_line" title="Defensive line">defensive line</a> assistant with the <a href="/wiki/Minnesota_Vikings" title="Minnesota Vikings">Minnesota Vikings</a> during <a href="/wiki/Training_camp_(National_Football_League)" title="Training camp (National Football League)">training camp</a>. In 2017, he was named the <a href="/wiki/Defensive_coordinator" title="Defensive coordinator">defensive coordinator</a> at <a href="/wiki/Coram_Deo_Academy" title="Coram Deo Academy">Coram Deo Academy</a>.
</p>
