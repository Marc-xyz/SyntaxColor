<p><b>Yusuf Ibrahim</b> (born 10 June 1986 in <a href="/wiki/Lagos" title="Lagos">Lagos</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nigeria" title="Nigeria">Nigeria</a>) is a Nigerian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soccer" title="Soccer">footballer</a>, who currently plays for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Air_India_FC" title="Air India FC">Air India FC</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yusuf_Ibrahim_(footballer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ibrahim currently plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for Air India FC in the <a href="/wiki/I-League" title="I-League">I-League</a>, having signed a deal worth $500.000. Prior to that, he played for Mumbai United.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
