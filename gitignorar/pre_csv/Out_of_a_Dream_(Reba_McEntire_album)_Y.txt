<p><i><b>Out of a Dream</b></i> is American country music singer <a href="/wiki/Reba_McEntire" title="Reba McEntire">Reba McEntire</a>'s sophomore studio album, released on August 27, 1979. The first single from the album, <i>Last Night, Ev'ry Night</i>, was her first top 30 hit.  It was also the first to include a song written solely by McEntire.
</p><p>The album was released in CD format twice, the second time with alternate album artwork.
</p><p>Despite some success with the singles released, the album did not chart on Billboard.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
