<p><b>Nongdananlu station</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">农大南路站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">農大南路站</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Nóngdànánlù Zhàn</span></i>; literally: '<a href="/wiki/China_Agricultural_University" title="China Agricultural University">Nongda (Agricultural University)</a> South Road station') is a <a href="/wiki/Metro_station" title="Metro station">station</a> on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Line_16,_Beijing_Subway" title="Line 16, Beijing Subway">Line 16</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Beijing_Subway" title="Beijing Subway">Beijing Subway</a>, located southwest of the west campus of <a href="/wiki/China_Agricultural_University" title="China Agricultural University">China Agricultural University</a>. It was opened on 30 December 2017.
</p>
