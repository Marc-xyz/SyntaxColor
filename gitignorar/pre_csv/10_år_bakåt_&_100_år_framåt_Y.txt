<p><i><b>10 år bakåt &amp; 100 år framåt</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Music_album" title="Music album">music album</a> by the Swedish band <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bob_hund" title="Bob hund">bob hund</a>. The album comprises two discs – the first one with B-sides and other songs that cannot be found on the groups' studio albums, the second one with a live recording from London on 23 May 2001.
</p>
