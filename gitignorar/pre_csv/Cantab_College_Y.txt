<p><b>Cantab College</b> was founded in 1934 and led by L.S.N. Hoare, M.A. (<a href="/wiki/University_of_Cambridge" title="University of Cambridge">Cambridge</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto, Ontario</a>, Canada.  Its name is likely derived from the <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> name of the University of Cambridge, <i>Universitas <b>Cantab</b>rigiensis</i>, often abbreviated to Cantab.
