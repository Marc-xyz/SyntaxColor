<p><b>Mowtowr Pamp-e Shahidi Aqubi</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">موتورپم پشهيديعقوبي</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Mowtowr Pamp-e Shahīdī ʿAqūbī</b>) is a village in <a href="/wiki/Arzuiyeh_Rural_District" title="Arzuiyeh Rural District">Arzuiyeh Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Arzuiyeh_County)" title="Central District (Arzuiyeh County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Arzuiyeh_County" title="Arzuiyeh County">Arzuiyeh County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kerman_Province" title="Kerman Province">Kerman Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
