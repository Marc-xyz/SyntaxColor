<p><b>Miyoko Hirose</b> (広瀬 美代子 <i>Hirose Miyoko</i>, born March 5, 1959) is a former <a href="/wiki/Volleyball" title="Volleyball">volleyball</a> player from <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>, who was a member of the Japan Women's National Team that won the bronze medal at the <a href="/wiki/Volleyball_at_the_1984_Summer_Olympics" title="Volleyball at the 1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="National_team">National team</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Miyoko_Hirose&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: National team">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>1982: 4th place in the <a href="/wiki/FIVB_Volleyball_Women%27s_World_Championship" title="FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship">World Championship</a></li>
<li>1984: 3rd place in the <a href="/wiki/Volleyball_at_the_1984_Summer_Olympics" title="Volleyball at the 1984 Summer Olympics">Olympic Games of Los Angeles</a></li></ul>
