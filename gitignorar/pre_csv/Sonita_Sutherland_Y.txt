<p><b>Sonita Sutherland</b> (born 9 July 1987 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Manchester,_Jamaica" title="Manchester, Jamaica">Manchester</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a> <a href="/wiki/400_metres" title="400 metres">400 metres</a> runner.
</p><p>Her personal best is 51.13 seconds, achieved in April 2006.
