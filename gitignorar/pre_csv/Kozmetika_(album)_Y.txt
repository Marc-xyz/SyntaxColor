<p><i><b>Kozmetika</b></i> (trans. <i>Cosmetics</i>) is the only album by <a href="/wiki/Kozmetika" title="Kozmetika">the eponymous Yugoslav new wave band</a>. It was released in 1983.
</p><p>The album <i>Kozmetika</i> was polled in 1998 as the 98th on the list of 100 greatest Yugoslav popular music albums in the book <i><a href="/wiki/YU_100:_najbolji_albumi_jugoslovenske_rok_i_pop_muzike" title="YU 100: najbolji albumi jugoslovenske rok i pop muzike">YU 100: najbolji albumi jugoslovenske rok i pop muzike</a></i> (<i>YU 100: The Best albums of Yugoslav pop and rock music</i>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Legacy">Legacy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kozmetika_(album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Legacy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Kozmetika</i> was polled in 1998 as the 98th on the list of 100 greatest Yugoslav popular music albums in the book <i><a href="/wiki/YU_100:_najbolji_albumi_jugoslovenske_rok_i_pop_muzike" title="YU 100: najbolji albumi jugoslovenske rok i pop muzike">YU 100: najbolji albumi jugoslovenske rok i pop muzike</a></i> (<i>YU 100: The Best albums of Yugoslav pop and rock music</i>).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2006 the song "Utisci" was ranked #99 the <a href="/wiki/B92_Top_100_Domestic_Songs" title="B92 Top 100 Domestic Songs">B92 Top 100 Domestic Songs</a> List.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The song has also been used as the <a href="/wiki/Theme_music" title="Theme music">theme song</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Olja_Be%C4%87kovi%C4%87" title="Olja Bećković">Olja Bećković</a> political <a href="/wiki/Talk_show" title="Talk show">talk show</a> <i>Utisak nedelje</i> broadcast on <a href="/wiki/RTV_Studio_B" title="RTV Studio B">RTV Studio B</a> and later on <a href="/wiki/B92" title="B92">B92</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> For the 20th anniversary of the talk show the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Viva_Vox_Choir&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Viva Vox Choir (page does not exist)">Viva Vox Choir</a> from Belgrade covered "Utisci" and the version has become the new theme song for the show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
