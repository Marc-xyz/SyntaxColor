<p>The <b>Magdalena Formation</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Formation_(geology)" title="Formation (geology)">geologic formation</a> in <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a>. It preserves <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fossils" title="Fossils">fossils</a> dating back to the <a href="/wiki/Permian" title="Permian">Permian</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Period_(geology)" title="Period (geology)">period</a>.
</p>
