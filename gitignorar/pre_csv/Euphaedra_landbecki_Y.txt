<p><i><b>Euphaedra landbecki</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Butterfly" title="Butterfly">butterfly</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Nymphalidae" title="Nymphalidae">Nymphalidae</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Sankuru" title="Sankuru">Sankuru</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo" title="Democratic Republic of the Congo">Democratic Republic of the Congo</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-ATBUT_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-ATBUT-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
