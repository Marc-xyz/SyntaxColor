<p><b>Matthew Olawale Olosunde</b> (born March 7, 1998) is an American <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">soccer</a> player who plays for <a href="/wiki/Rotherham_United_F.C." title="Rotherham United F.C.">Rotherham United</a> as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)#Full-back" title="Defender (association football)">right-back</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Matthew_Olosunde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="New_York_Red_Bulls">New York Red Bulls</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Matthew_Olosunde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: New York Red Bulls">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Olosunde began his career at <a href="/wiki/New_York_Red_Bulls_Academy" title="New York Red Bulls Academy">New York Red Bulls Academy</a> as a right-winger, and represented the club's under-16 and under-18 academy teams.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>On August 16, 2014, he played 71 minutes for the New York Red Bulls Reserves in a 1–0 win over the Wilmington Hammerheads in the <a href="/wiki/United_Soccer_League" title="United Soccer League">United Soccer League</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>Having verbally committed to play college soccer at <a href="/wiki/Duke_Blue_Devils_men%27s_soccer" title="Duke Blue Devils men's soccer">Duke University</a>, Olosunde left the Red Bulls following the <a href="/wiki/2015_FIFA_U-17_World_Cup" title="2015 FIFA U-17 World Cup">2015 FIFA Under-17 World Cup</a> to further his education.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Manchester_United">Manchester United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Matthew_Olosunde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Manchester United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In January 2016, it was announced that Olosunde would join <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Manchester_United_F.C." title="Manchester United F.C.">Manchester United</a>. It was reported that the club had offered him online courses at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Oxford" title="University of Oxford">University of Oxford</a> to persuade him to join.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> He received international clearance and officially joined the team on March 11.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>He initially joined the Under-18 set-up, and was promoted to the Under-23 team ahead of the <a href="/wiki/2016%E2%80%9317_Manchester_United_F.C._season" title="2016–17 Manchester United F.C. season">2016–17 season</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> He made 18 appearances in his inaugural <a href="/wiki/Professional_Development_League" title="Professional Development League">Premier League 2</a> campaign.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2017, Olosunde was nominated for the club's Goal of the Month Award for his solo effort against <a href="/wiki/Real_Salt_Lake" title="Real Salt Lake">Real Salt Lake</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Cup" title="Dallas Cup">Dallas Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>The following month, he traveled with the first team squad to <a href="/wiki/Premier_League" title="Premier League">Premier League</a> games against <a href="/wiki/Arsenal_F.C." title="Arsenal F.C.">Arsenal</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Hotspur_F.C." title="Tottenham Hotspur F.C.">Tottenham Hotspur</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> In June 2019, Olosunde was released by Manchester United.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Rotherham_United">Rotherham United</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Matthew_Olosunde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Rotherham United">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On 5 July 2019, Olosunde signed a two-year contract with <a href="/wiki/Rotherham_United_F.C." title="Rotherham United F.C.">Rotherham United</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Matthew_Olosunde&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Olosunde has played for the United States at several youth levels. For the <a href="/wiki/United_States_men%27s_national_under-17_soccer_team" title="United States men's national under-17 soccer team">United States U17</a>, he played 22 matches and was part of the 21-player roster that represented United States at the <a href="/wiki/2015_FIFA_U-17_World_Cup" title="2015 FIFA U-17 World Cup">2015 FIFA U-17 World Cup</a> in <a href="/wiki/Chile" title="Chile">Chile</a>. He only appeared in the team's first match against <a href="/wiki/Nigeria_national_under-17_football_team" title="Nigeria national under-17 football team">Nigeria</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-15"><a href="#cite_note-15">[15]</a></sup> Due to his heritage, he is also eligible to represent <a href="/wiki/Nigeria_national_football_team" title="Nigeria national football team">Nigeria</a> at the international level.
</p><p>He made his senior debut for the <a href="/wiki/United_States_men%27s_national_soccer_team" title="United States men's national soccer team">United States</a> on May 28, 2018, coming on as a 74th-minute substitute against <a href="/wiki/Bolivia_national_football_team" title="Bolivia national football team">Bolivia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-16"><a href="#cite_note-16">[16]</a></sup>
</p>
