<p><b>Torres Airport</b> (<a href="/wiki/IATA_airport_code" title="IATA airport code">IATA</a>: <b>TOH</b>, <a href="/wiki/ICAO_airport_code" title="ICAO airport code">ICAO</a>: <b>NVSD</b>) is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Airfield" title="Airfield">airfield</a> serving the <a href="/wiki/Torres_Islands" title="Torres Islands">Torres Islands</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Torba_Province" title="Torba Province">Torba</a> <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_Vanuatu" title="Provinces of Vanuatu">province</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vanuatu" title="Vanuatu">Vanuatu</a>. It is located on <a href="/wiki/Linua" title="Linua">Linua</a> island, just north of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lo_(island)" title="Lo (island)">Lo</a> (or <i>Loh</i>) island.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GCM_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-GCM-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
