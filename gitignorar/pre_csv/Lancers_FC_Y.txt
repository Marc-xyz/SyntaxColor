<p><b>Lancers FC</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Saint_Lucia" title="Saint Lucia">Saint Lucian</a> football club based in <a href="/wiki/Castries" title="Castries">Castries</a>, competing in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Saint_Lucia_Gold_Division" title="Saint Lucia Gold Division">Saint Lucia Gold Division</a>, the top tier of <a href="/wiki/Football_in_Saint_Lucia" title="Football in Saint Lucia">Saint Lucian football</a>.
</p>
