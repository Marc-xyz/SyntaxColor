<p><b>George Hudson</b> (born November 10, 1976) is a former professional <a href="/wiki/Canadian_football" title="Canadian football">Canadian football</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Center_(American_football)" title="Center (American football)">centre</a>. He most recently played for the <a href="/wiki/Saskatchewan_Roughriders" title="Saskatchewan Roughriders">Saskatchewan Roughriders</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Football_League" title="Canadian Football League">Canadian Football League</a>. He was drafted by the <a href="/wiki/Edmonton_Eskimos" title="Edmonton Eskimos">Edmonton Eskimos</a> in the third round of the <a href="/wiki/2000_CFL_Draft" title="2000 CFL Draft">2000 CFL Draft</a>. He played <a href="/wiki/College_football" title="College football">college football</a> for the <a href="/wiki/New_Mexico_State_Aggies" title="New Mexico State Aggies">New Mexico State Aggies</a>.
</p><p>Hudson has also been a member of the <a href="/wiki/Dallas_Cowboys" title="Dallas Cowboys">Dallas Cowboys</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carolina_Panthers" title="Carolina Panthers">Carolina Panthers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ottawa_Renegades" title="Ottawa Renegades">Ottawa Renegades</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Hamilton_Tiger-Cats" title="Hamilton Tiger-Cats">Hamilton Tiger-Cats</a>.
</p>
