<p><b>Horonu</b> (also, <b>Orony</b>) is a village and municipality in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yardymli_Rayon" title="Yardymli Rayon">Yardymli Rayon</a> of <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>.  It has a population of 519.
