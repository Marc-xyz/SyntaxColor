<p><b>Losada River</b> (<small>Spanish pronunciation: </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Spanish" title="Help:IPA/Spanish">[loˈsaða]</a></span>) is a river of <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>. It is part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Orinoco_River" title="Orinoco River">Orinoco River</a> basin.
</p>
