<p><b>Ric Young</b> (born 1944) is a British <a href="/wiki/Character_actor" title="Character actor">character actor</a>. He is best known for his role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dr._Zhang_Lee" title="Dr. Zhang Lee">Dr. Zhang Lee</a> in the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Alias_(TV_series)" title="Alias (TV series)">Alias</a></i>, and as the henchman Kao Kan in the <a href="/wiki/Steven_Spielberg" title="Steven Spielberg">Steven Spielberg</a> film <i><a href="/wiki/Indiana_Jones_and_the_Temple_of_Doom" title="Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom">Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ric_Young&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Young was born in <a href="/wiki/Kuala_Lumpur" title="Kuala Lumpur">Kuala Lumpur</a> in 1944 as <b>Wing-Wah Yung</b>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> He trained as an actor first at the <a href="/wiki/Royal_Academy_of_Dramatic_Art" title="Royal Academy of Dramatic Art">Royal Academy of Dramatic Art</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and then moved to Los Angeles to study <a href="/wiki/Method_acting" title="Method acting">method acting</a> under <a href="/wiki/Shelley_Winters" title="Shelley Winters">Shelley Winters</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lee_Strasberg" title="Lee Strasberg">Lee Strasberg</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Examiner-Moy_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Examiner-Moy-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ric_Young&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Actor">Actor</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ric_Young&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Actor">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Originally, Young at 12 year old was taking part in radio sketches in Kuala Lumpur. He came to Britain in 1958 and got a break in London. An introduction to <a href="/wiki/Mike_Todd" title="Mike Todd">Mike Todd</a> led to a small part in film The Inn of the Sixth Happiness (1958). After he went to <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> and stayed on in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a> for five months to appear in Italian movie and television. Back to Britain he performed under name Eric Young appearing in TV shows in the 1960s and '70s such as <i><a href="/wiki/The_Saint_(TV_series)" title="The Saint (TV series)">The Saint</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Avengers_(TV_series)" title="The Avengers (TV series)">The Avengers</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Blake%27s_7" title="Blake's 7">Blake's 7</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Tomorrow_People" title="The Tomorrow People">The Tomorrow People</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Somerset_Maugham" title="Somerset Maugham">Somerset Maugham</a> Hour</i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Champions" title="The Champions">The Champions</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Danger_Man" title="Danger Man">Danger Man</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Chinese_Puzzle_(TV_series)" title="The Chinese Puzzle (TV series)">The Chinese Puzzle</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Are_You_Being_Served%3F" title="Are You Being Served?">Are You Being Served?</a></i> and <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Room_Service_(1979_TV_series)" title="Room Service (1979 TV series)">Room Service</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>Young has since appeared in many memorable roles, often as a villain. Some of the best-known are as Kao Kan in <i><a href="/wiki/Indiana_Jones_and_the_Temple_of_Doom" title="Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom">Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom</a></i>, as <a href="/wiki/Bruce_Lee" title="Bruce Lee">Bruce Lee</a>'s father in <i><a href="/wiki/Dragon:_The_Bruce_Lee_Story" title="Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story">Dragon: The Bruce Lee Story</a></i> (1993), as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mao_Tse-Tung" title="Mao Tse-Tung">Mao Tse-Tung</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Nixon_(film)" title="Nixon (film)">Nixon</a></i> (1995), as General Chang Jing Wu in <i><a href="/wiki/Seven_Years_in_Tibet" title="Seven Years in Tibet">Seven Years in Tibet</a></i> (1997), as Mr. Kwai in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Transporter" title="The Transporter">The Transporter</a></i> (2002) and as Henry Lee in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Corruptor" title="The Corruptor">The Corruptor</a></i> (1999).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IMDB_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-IMDB-7">[7]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>Young's prominent television roles include a recurring role as Dr. Zhang Lee on the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Alias_(TV_series)" title="Alias (TV series)">Alias</a></i> (2001–04).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IMDB_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-IMDB-7">[7]</a></sup> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>He is among the performers who can lay claim performing in both the original versions of the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Saint_(TV_series)" title="The Saint (TV series)">The Saint</a></i> (in 1964) and <i><a href="/wiki/Hawaii_Five-O_(1968_TV_series)" title="Hawaii Five-O (1968 TV series)">Hawaii Five-O</a></i> (in 1976) and their updated versions <i><a href="/wiki/Return_of_the_Saint" title="Return of the Saint">Return of the Saint</a></i> (in 1978) and <i><a href="/wiki/Hawaii_Five-0_(2010_TV_series)" title="Hawaii Five-0 (2010 TV series)">Hawaii Five-O</a></i> (in 2010).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IMDB_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-IMDB-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Producer">Producer</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ric_Young&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Producer">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Young has produced two projects, <i>Paranormal Whactivity</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Examiner-Moy_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-Examiner-Moy-4">[4]</a></sup> and the short, <i>Oy Vey!</i>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ric_Young&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Young currently teaches acting alongside his other projects.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup>
</p>
