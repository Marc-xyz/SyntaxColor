	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Ron Bottcher</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>

		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Ron Bottcher</b> (11 May 1940 – 12 April 1991) was an American <a href="/wiki/Opera" title="Opera">operatic</a> <a href="/wiki/Baritone" title="Baritone">baritone</a> who was actively performing with both the <a href="/wiki/New_York_City_Opera" title="New York City Opera">New York City Opera</a> (NYCO) and the <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_Opera" title="Metropolitan Opera">Metropolitan Opera</a> during the 1960s. A native of <a href="/wiki/Sandpoint,_Idaho" title="Sandpoint, Idaho">Sandpoint, Idaho</a>, he earned music degrees from the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Montana" title="University of Montana">University of Montana</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Curtis_Institute_of_Music" title="Curtis Institute of Music">Curtis Institute of Music</a>. He made his debut at the <a href="/wiki/Santa_Fe_Opera" title="Santa Fe Opera">Santa Fe Opera</a> in the summer of 1961, where he portrayed the roles of Leopold in <a href="/wiki/Richard_Strauss" title="Richard Strauss">Richard Strauss</a>' <i><a href="/wiki/Der_Rosenkavalier" title="Der Rosenkavalier">Der Rosenkavalier</a></i>, Marcello in <a href="/wiki/Giacomo_Puccini" title="Giacomo Puccini">Giacomo Puccini</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/La_boh%C3%A8me" title="La bohème">La bohème</a></i>, and the Head waiter in <a href="/wiki/Paul_Hindemith" title="Paul Hindemith">Paul Hindemith</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Neues_vom_Tage" title="Neues vom Tage">Neues vom Tage</a></i>. His roles at the NYCO included Escamillo in <a href="/wiki/Georges_Bizet" title="Georges Bizet">Georges Bizet</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Carmen" title="Carmen">Carmen</a></i> and Sharpless in Puccini's <i><a href="/wiki/Madama_Butterfly" title="Madama Butterfly">Madama Butterfly</a></i> among others. At the Met he created roles in the world premieres of <a href="/wiki/Samuel_Barber" title="Samuel Barber">Samuel Barber</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Antony_and_Cleopatra_(opera)" title="Antony and Cleopatra (opera)">Antony and Cleopatra</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Marvin_David_Levy" title="Marvin David Levy">Marvin David Levy</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Mourning_Becomes_Electra_(opera)" title="Mourning Becomes Electra (opera)">Mourning Becomes Electra</a></i>. He died at the age of 50 at <a href="/wiki/Lenox_Hill_Hospital" title="Lenox Hill Hospital">Lenox Hill Hospital</a> in Manhattan of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/AIDS" title="AIDS">AIDS</a> related illness.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
