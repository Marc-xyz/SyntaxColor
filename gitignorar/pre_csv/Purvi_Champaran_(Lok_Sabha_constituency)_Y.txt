	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Purvi Champaran (Lok Sabha constituency)</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Purvi Champaran Lok Sabha constituency</b> is one of the 40 <a href="/wiki/Lok_Sabha" title="Lok Sabha">Lok Sabha</a> (parliamentary) constituencies in <a href="/wiki/Bihar" title="Bihar">Bihar</a> state in eastern <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a>. This constituency came into existence in 2008 as a part of the implementation of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Delimitation" title="Delimitation">delimitation</a> of parliamentary constituencies based on the recommendations of the <a href="/wiki/Delimitation_Commission_of_India" title="Delimitation Commission of India">Delimitation Commission of India</a> constituted in 2002.
</p>
