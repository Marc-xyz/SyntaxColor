<p><b>Limotettigini</b> is a tribe of <a href="/wiki/Leafhopper" title="Leafhopper">leafhoppers</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cicadellidae" title="Cicadellidae">Cicadellidae</a>. There are at least 2 genera and about 11 described species in Limotettigini.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bugref_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-bugref-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
