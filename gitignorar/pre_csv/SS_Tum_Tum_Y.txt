</tbody></table><p><b>SS <i>Tum Tum</i></b> was a <a href="/wiki/Steamship" title="Steamship">Steamship</a> used to move building stone from the granite quarry on the lake shore south of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vernon,_BC" title="Vernon, BC">Vernon, BC</a>. She had a total of 8 horsepower. Some speculate she was the <i>Skookum</i> alias <i>Tut Tut</i> with a slight name change as they were similar in number of horsepower.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
