<p><b>Grič pri Trebnjem</b> (<small>pronounced </small><span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Slovene" title="Help:IPA/Slovene">[ˈɡɾiːtʃ pɾi ˈtɾeːbnjɛm]</a></span>) is a small settlement west of <a href="/wiki/Trebnje" title="Trebnje">Trebnje</a> in eastern <a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>. It lies below the northern slopes of Fat Hill (<a href="/wiki/Slovene_language" title="Slovene language">Slovene</a>: <i lang="sl">Debeli hrib</i>, 420 m). The <a href="/wiki/Municipality_of_Trebnje" title="Municipality of Trebnje">Municipality of Trebnje</a> is part of the historical region of <a href="/wiki/Lower_Carniola" title="Lower Carniola">Lower Carniola</a> and is now included in the <a href="/wiki/Southeast_Slovenia_Statistical_Region" title="Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region">Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Gri%C4%8D_pri_Trebnjem&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The name of the settlement was changed from <i>Grič</i> to <i>Grič pri Trebnjem</i> in 1955.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> 
</p>
