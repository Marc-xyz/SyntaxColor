<b>Karsanbhai Odedara</b> is an <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> politician and member of the <a href="/wiki/Bharatiya_Janata_Party" title="Bharatiya Janata Party">Bharatiya Janata Party</a>. Karsanbhai is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Gujarat_Legislative_Assembly" title="Gujarat Legislative Assembly">Gujarat Legislative Assembly</a> in 2007 from the <a href="/wiki/Kutiyana_(Vidhan_Sabha_constituency)" title="Kutiyana (Vidhan Sabha constituency)">Kutiyana constituency</a> assembly constituency in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Porbandar_District" title="Porbandar District">Porbandar District</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
