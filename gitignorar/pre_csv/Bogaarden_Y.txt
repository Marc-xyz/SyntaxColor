<p><b>Bogaarden</b> is a village in the municipality of <a href="/wiki/Pepingen" title="Pepingen">Pepingen</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgian</a> province of <a href="/wiki/Flemish_Brabant" title="Flemish Brabant">Flemish Brabant</a>.
</p><p>The name 'Bogaarden' means "<a href="/wiki/Orchard" title="Orchard">orchards</a>".<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Gertrude_of_Nivelles" title="Gertrude of Nivelles">Gertrude of Nivelles</a>, daughter of Pippin I, was due to the inherit the property.  But when she joined the Abbey of St. Gertrude, all her possessions were donated to the abbey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Bogaarden&amp;params=50.73992_N_4.13534_E_region:BE_type:city"><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">50°44′24″N</span> <span class="longitude">4°08′07″E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">50.73992°N 4.13534°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">50.73992; 4.13534</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>
</p>
