<p><b>Oueguedo-Yarce</b> is a village in the <a href="/wiki/Tenkodogo_Department" title="Tenkodogo Department">Tenkodogo Department</a> of <a href="/wiki/Boulgou_Province" title="Boulgou Province">Boulgou Province</a> in south-eastern <a href="/wiki/Burkina_Faso" title="Burkina Faso">Burkina Faso</a>. In 2005, the village had a population of 179.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
