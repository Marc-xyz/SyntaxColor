<p><b>Victor Ioniță</b> (born May 11, 1983)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a former <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romanian</a> <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> player. On 23 May 2005, he reached his highest <a href="/wiki/Association_of_Tennis_Professionals" title="Association of Tennis Professionals">ATP</a> singles ranking of no. 187. In 2015 he coached Romanian number 1 tennis player, who would later become World No 1, <a href="/wiki/Simona_Halep" title="Simona Halep">Simona Halep</a>. He formerly coached <a href="/wiki/Sorana_C%C3%AErstea" title="Sorana Cîrstea">Sorana Cîrstea</a>, former World No 21.
</p>
