<p><b>Ryne Robinson</b> (born November 4, 1984) is a former <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> <a href="/wiki/Wide_receiver" title="Wide receiver">wide receiver</a>. He was drafted by the <a href="/wiki/Carolina_Panthers" title="Carolina Panthers">Carolina Panthers</a> in the fourth round of the <a href="/wiki/2007_NFL_Draft" title="2007 NFL Draft">2007 NFL Draft</a>. He played <a href="/wiki/College_football" title="College football">college football</a> at <a href="/wiki/Miami_RedHawks_football" title="Miami RedHawks football">Miami University</a>. He is a graduate of <a href="/wiki/Central_Catholic_High_School_(Toledo,_Ohio)" title="Central Catholic High School (Toledo, Ohio)">Central Catholic High School (Toledo, Ohio)</a>.
</p><p>Robinson has also been a member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Florida_Tuskers" title="Florida Tuskers">Florida Tuskers</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="High_school">High school</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: High school">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Three-year letterwinner as a wider receiver and defensive back for <a href="/wiki/Central_Catholic_High_School_(Toledo,_Ohio)" title="Central Catholic High School (Toledo, Ohio)">Central Catholic High School (Toledo, Ohio)</a></li>
<li>Three-time all-<a href="/wiki/Toledo_City_League" title="Toledo City League">Toledo City League</a> selection</li>
<li>Two-time all-district honoree</li>
<li>2002 District Offensive Player of the Year</li>
<li>Totaled 62 receptions for 1,035 yards and 16 touchdowns as a senior</li>
<li>Averaged 25.3 yards per punt as a senior, returning two for touchdowns</li>
<li>Also grabbed five interceptions as a senior</li>
<li>Helped the Irish to a three-year record of 27-8</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="College_career">College career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: College career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Baseball">Baseball</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Baseball">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Robinson was a <a href="/wiki/Left_fielder" title="Left fielder">left fielder</a> for the RedHawks <a href="/wiki/Baseball" title="Baseball">baseball</a> team. He played in 157 games, and ended with a .284 <a href="/wiki/Batting_average_(baseball)" title="Batting average (baseball)">batting average</a>.  He holds the school single-season records for <a href="/wiki/Triple_(baseball)" title="Triple (baseball)">triples</a> (8) and <a href="/wiki/Stolen_base" title="Stolen base">stolen bases</a> (34).
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Football">Football</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Football">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Robinson played for the RedHawks' football team as a freshman, mostly on special teams. He finished his first year with a school record for most punt return yards in a season (674), and set a <a href="/wiki/Mid-American_Conference" title="Mid-American Conference">Mid-American Conference</a> record for yards in a game, when he returned 274 yards against the <a href="/wiki/University_at_Buffalo" title="University at Buffalo">University at Buffalo</a>. In his junior year, he became only the third player in school history to top 1,000 receiving yards in a single season, which he duplicated his senior year. In four years, he amassed a total of 3,697 receiving yards on 258 catches, both school records. His 1,677 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Punt_return" title="Punt return">punt return</a> yards are the third most in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NCAA" title="NCAA">NCAA</a> history.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional_career">Professional career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Professional career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Carolina_Panthers">Carolina Panthers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Carolina Panthers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Robinson was taken in the fourth round (118th overall) of the <a href="/wiki/2007_NFL_Draft" title="2007 NFL Draft">2007 NFL Draft</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Carolina_Panthers" title="Carolina Panthers">Carolina Panthers</a>.
</p><p>On October 8, 2008, Robinson was placed on season-ending <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Injured_reserve" title="Injured reserve">injured reserve</a> as the team signed wide receiver <a href="/wiki/Kenneth_Moore_(American_football)" title="Kenneth Moore (American football)">Kenneth Moore</a>.
</p><p>The Panthers waived Robinson on August 31, 2009.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Florida_Tuskers">Florida Tuskers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryne_Robinson&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Florida Tuskers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Robinson was signed by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Florida_Tuskers" title="Florida Tuskers">Florida Tuskers</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_Football_League_(2009)" title="United Football League (2009)">United Football League</a> on August 26, 2010.
Robinson is the second cousin of JoJuan Armour.
</p>
