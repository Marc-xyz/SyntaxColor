<p><b>Novi Christian Academy</b> (<b>NCA</b>), formerly <b>Franklin Road Christian School</b> (<b>FRCS</b>),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Novi,_Michigan" title="Novi, Michigan">Novi, Michigan</a>, is a <a href="/wiki/Private_school" title="Private school">private</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Coeducational" title="Coeducational">coeducational</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/College_preparatory" title="College preparatory">college preparatory</a> <a href="/wiki/Christian_school" title="Christian school">Christian</a> preK–12 <a href="/wiki/Day_school" title="Day school">day school</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Non-denominational" title="Non-denominational">non-denominational</a> Christian philosophy.
NCA<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> is affiliated with Brightmoor Christian Church, and was founded in 1986. The church and school moved from <a href="/wiki/Southfield,_Michigan" title="Southfield, Michigan">Southfield, Michigan</a>, to a new campus in Novi, Michigan, in 2000. The school mascot is the "Warriors".
</p><p>NCA competes in the Michigan Independent Athletic Conference (MIAC), and was red division champion for Varsity Men's Soccer, 2008; Varsity Men's Basketball, 2009; Varsity Women's Basketball, 2009; and Varsity Baseball, 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
