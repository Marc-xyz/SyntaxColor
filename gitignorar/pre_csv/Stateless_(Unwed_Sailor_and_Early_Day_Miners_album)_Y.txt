<p><i><b>Stateless</b></i> is a 2002 album on which the bands <a href="/wiki/Unwed_Sailor" title="Unwed Sailor">Unwed Sailor</a> and <a href="/wiki/Early_Day_Miners" title="Early Day Miners">Early Day Miners</a> collaborated to create a soundtrack for filmmaker Chris Bennett's film of the same name.  The film is included on the <a href="/wiki/Enhanced_CD" title="Enhanced CD">Enhanced CD</a> with the entire album playing seamlessly as its soundtrack.  The film features around 30 minutes of footage that Bennett shot on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Super_8_mm_film" title="Super 8 mm film">Super 8</a> film during his various travels.
</p>
