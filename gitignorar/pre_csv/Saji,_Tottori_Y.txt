Unsourced material may be challenged and <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Verifiability#Burden_of_evidence" title="Wikipedia:Verifiability">removed</a>.<br/><small><span class="plainlinks"><i>Find sources:</i> <a class="external text" href="//www.google.com/search?as_eq=wikipedia&amp;q=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22" rel="nofollow">"Saji, Tottori"</a> – <a class="external text" href="//www.google.com/search?tbm=nws&amp;q=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22+-wikipedia" rel="nofollow">news</a> <b>·</b> <a class="external text" href="//www.google.com/search?&amp;q=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22+site:news.google.com/newspapers&amp;source=newspapers" rel="nofollow">newspapers</a> <b>·</b> <a class="external text" href="//www.google.com/search?tbs=bks:1&amp;q=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22+-wikipedia" rel="nofollow">books</a> <b>·</b> <a class="external text" href="//scholar.google.com/scholar?q=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22" rel="nofollow">scholar</a> <b>·</b> <a class="external text" href="https://www.jstor.org/action/doBasicSearch?Query=%22Saji%2C+Tottori%22&amp;acc=on&amp;wc=on" rel="nofollow">JSTOR</a></span></small></span>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">April 2019</span>)</i></small><small class="hide-when-compact"><i> (<a href="/wiki/Help:Maintenance_template_removal" title="Help:Maintenance template removal">Learn how and when to remove this template message</a>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table><p><b>Saji</b><span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">佐治村</span><span style="display:none">,</span> <i>Saji-son</i>)</span> was a <a href="/wiki/List_of_villages_in_Japan" title="List of villages in Japan">village</a> located in <a href="/wiki/Yazu_District,_Tottori" title="Yazu District, Tottori">Yazu District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tottori_Prefecture" title="Tottori Prefecture">Tottori Prefecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japan</a>.
</p><p>As of 2003, the village had an estimated <a href="/wiki/Population" title="Population">population</a> of 2,689 and a <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">density</a> of 33.66 persons per km². The total area was 79.89 km².
</p><p>On November 1, 2004, Saji, along with the town of <a href="/wiki/Kokufu,_Tottori" title="Kokufu, Tottori">Kokufu</a>, the village of <a href="/wiki/Fukube,_Tottori" title="Fukube, Tottori">Fukube</a> (both from <a href="/wiki/Iwami_District,_Tottori" title="Iwami District, Tottori">Iwami District</a>), the towns of <a href="/wiki/Aoya,_Tottori" title="Aoya, Tottori">Aoya</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ketaka,_Tottori" title="Ketaka, Tottori">Ketaka</a> and <a href="/wiki/Shikano,_Tottori" title="Shikano, Tottori">Shikano</a> (all from <a href="/wiki/Ketaka_District,_Tottori" title="Ketaka District, Tottori">Ketaka District</a>), and the towns of <a href="/wiki/Kawahara,_Tottori" title="Kawahara, Tottori">Kawahara</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mochigase,_Tottori" title="Mochigase, Tottori">Mochigase</a> (all from <a href="/wiki/Yazu_District,_Tottori" title="Yazu District, Tottori">Yazu District</a>), was merged into the expanded city of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tottori,_Tottori" title="Tottori, Tottori">Tottori</a>.
</p>
