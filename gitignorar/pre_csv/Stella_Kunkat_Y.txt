<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Stella_Kunkat&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kunkat attended a <a href="/wiki/Montessori_education" title="Montessori education">Montessori school</a> in <a href="/wiki/Berlin" title="Berlin">Berlin</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mitte" title="Mitte">Mitte</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mopo_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mopo-2">[2]</a></sup> She sang in the children's choir of the <a href="/wiki/Komische_Oper_Berlin" title="Komische Oper Berlin">Komische Oper Berlin</a> where she was discovered in 2005 when she took part in the play <i><a href="/wiki/Madame_Butterfly_(play)" title="Madame Butterfly (play)">Madame Butterfly</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mopo_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Mopo-2">[2]</a></sup> At the age of eight, she played her first role in the film <i><a href="/wiki/March_of_Millions" title="March of Millions">March of Millions</a></i> as the daughter of the protagonist who was played by <a href="/wiki/Maria_Furtw%C3%A4ngler" title="Maria Furtwängler">Maria Furtwängler</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mopo_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-Mopo-2">[2]</a></sup> In 2009, she had the role of the young <a href="/wiki/Romy_Schneider" title="Romy Schneider">Romy Schneider</a> in the TV film <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Romy_(2009)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Romy (2009) (page does not exist)">Romy</a></i>. Two years later, Kunkat played the young <a href="/wiki/Sabine_Kuegler" title="Sabine Kuegler">Sabine Kuegler</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Jungle_Child_(film)" title="Jungle Child (film)">Jungle Child</a></i>. For that role, she was nominated as Leading Young Performer in an international feature film at the <a href="/wiki/33rd_Young_Artist_Awards" title="33rd Young Artist Awards">33rd Young Artist Awards</a>. In the German production <i><a href="/wiki/Das_Tagebuch_der_Anne_Frank" title="Das Tagebuch der Anne Frank">Das Tagebuch der Anne Frank</a></i>, a film about the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Holocaust" title="Holocaust">Holocaust</a> victim <a href="/wiki/Anne_Frank" title="Anne Frank">Anne Frank</a>, who wrote the famous <a href="/wiki/The_Diary_of_a_Young_Girl" title="The Diary of a Young Girl">diary</a>, Kunkat plays Anne's older sister <a href="/wiki/Margot_Frank" title="Margot Frank">Margot Frank</a>.
</p>
