	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">1936 Australian Tourist Trophy</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>1936 Australian Tourist Trophy</b> was a motor race staged at the <a href="/wiki/Phillip_Island_Grand_Prix_Circuit" title="Phillip Island Grand Prix Circuit">Phillip Island circuit</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Victoria,_Australia" title="Victoria, Australia">Victoria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a> on 30 March 1936.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Motor_Sport_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Motor_Sport-1">[1]</a></sup>
The race was held over 60 laps of the new 3.312 mile "triangular circuit", a total distance of 200 miles.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Motor_Sport_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Motor_Sport-1">[1]</a></sup>
It was open to factory built and catalogued racing cars and sports cars, irrespective of engine capacity.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Argus_11_February_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Argus_11_February-2">[2]</a></sup>
The race, which was organised by the Australian Racing Drivers Club, was contested on a handicap basis <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Argus_11_February_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-Argus_11_February-2">[2]</a></sup> with the 'Limit' starter, HR Reeves (<a href="/wiki/MG_P-type" title="MG P-type">MG P-type</a>), given a 38-minute start on the 'Scratch' starter, J McCutcheon (Day Special).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Argus_28_March_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Argus_28_March-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>The race was won by JH Fagan <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Motor_Sport_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Motor_Sport-1">[1]</a></sup> driving an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MG_K3_Magnette" title="MG K3 Magnette">MG K3 Magnette</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Argus_31_March_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Argus_31_March-4">[4]</a></sup>
Of the sixteen starters only six finished and of those, only two were officially placed as the other four failed to complete the course within the prescribed time limit, <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Argus_31_March_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-Argus_31_March-4">[4]</a></sup> which had been set at 15 minutes after the winner completed the race.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
