<p><b>Elpidio Coppa</b> (October 6, 1914 in <a href="/wiki/Milan" title="Milan">Milan</a> — August 31, 1978 in <a href="/wiki/Lucca" title="Lucca">Lucca</a>) was an <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player.
</p>
