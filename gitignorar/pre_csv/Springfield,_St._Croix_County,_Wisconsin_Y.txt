<p><b>Springfield</b> is a town in <a href="/wiki/St._Croix_County,_Wisconsin" title="St. Croix County, Wisconsin">St. Croix County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">Wisconsin</a>, United States. The population was 808 at the 2000 census. The unincorporated community of <a href="/wiki/Hersey,_Wisconsin" title="Hersey, Wisconsin">Hersey</a> is located in the town.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Springfield,_St._Croix_County,_Wisconsin&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the town has a total area of 34.4 square miles (89.0 km²), of which, 34.2 square miles (88.7 km²) of it is land and 0.2 square miles (0.4 km²) of it (0.44%) is water.
</p>

<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 808 people, 285 households, and 220 families residing in the town. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 23.6 people per square mile (9.1/km²). There were 299 housing units at an average density of 8.7 per square mile (3.4/km²). The racial makeup of the town was 95.17% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.12% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/African_American_(U.S._Census)" title="African American (U.S. Census)">African American</a>, 0.12% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Native_American_(U.S._Census)" title="Native American (U.S. Census)">Native American</a>, 2.97% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_(U.S._Census)" title="Asian (U.S. Census)">Asian</a>, 0.37% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(United_States_Census)" title="Race (United States Census)">other races</a>, and 1.24% from two or more races. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 0.50% of the population.
</p><p>There were 285 households out of which 34.4% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 67.0% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 6.0% had a female householder with no husband present, and 22.5% were non-families. 16.1% of all households were made up of individuals and 3.9% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.84 and the average family size was 3.20.
</p><p>In the town, the population was spread out with 27.2% under the age of 18, 8.3% from 18 to 24, 29.2% from 25 to 44, 24.8% from 45 to 64, and 10.5% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 38 years. For every 100 females, there were 109.3 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 110.8 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the town was $54,886, and the median income for a family was $56,154. Males had a median income of $36,932 versus $22,241 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the town was $21,303. About 0.9% of families and 2.4% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including none of those under age 18 and 3.4% of those age 65 or over.
</p>
