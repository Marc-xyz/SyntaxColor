<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=August_Sartorius_von_Waltershausen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was born in <a href="/wiki/G%C3%B6ttingen" title="Göttingen">Göttingen</a>, the son of <a href="/wiki/Wolfgang_Sartorius_von_Waltershausen" title="Wolfgang Sartorius von Waltershausen">Wolfgang Sartorius von Waltershausen</a>. He was educated in the <a href="/wiki/University_of_G%C3%B6ttingen" title="University of Göttingen">University of Göttingen</a>, became professor at Zurich in 1885, and in 1888 was appointed professor of economics (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i lang="de">Nationalökonomie</i>) in Strassburg, where he remained until 1918.  He is the father of <a href="/wiki/Hermann_Wolfgang_von_Waltershausen" title="Hermann Wolfgang von Waltershausen">Hermann Wolfgang von Waltershausen</a> (Göttingen, 12 October 1882 - Munich, 14 June 1954), a German composer, conductor, teacher and writer.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Works">Works</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=August_Sartorius_von_Waltershausen&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Works">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His principal works deal with American economic and industrial problems and include 
</p>
<ul><li><i>Die Zukunft des Deutschtums in den Vereinigten Staaten</i> (The future of German culture in the United States, 1885) (<a class="external text" href="http://www.recherche-portal.ch/ZAD:default_scope:ebi01_prod006535432" rel="nofollow">ZBZOnline</a>)</li>
<li><i>Nordamerikanische Gewerkschaften</i> (North American unions, 1886)</li>
<li><i>Moderner Sozialismus in den Vereinigten Staaten von Amerika</i> (Modern socialism in the United States of America, 1890)</li>
<li><i>Arbeitsverfassung der englischen Kolonien in Nordamerika</i> (Work contracts in the English colonies of North America, 1894)</li>
<li><i>Handelsbilanz der Vereinigten Staaten von Amerika</i> (Trade balance of the United States of America, 1901)</li></ul>
<p>He was one of the editors of the <i>Handwörterbuchs der Staatswirtschaften</i>.
</p>
