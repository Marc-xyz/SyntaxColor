<p><i><b>Ctenoplusia chillagoes</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> of the family <a href="/wiki/Noctuidae" title="Noctuidae">Noctuidae</a>. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Queensland" title="Queensland">Queensland</a>.
</p><p>Some authors consider it to be a subspecies of <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ctenoplusia_placida&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ctenoplusia placida (page does not exist)">Ctenoplusia placida</a></i>.
</p>
