<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a></b> competed at the <b><a href="/wiki/1976_Winter_Paralympics" title="1976 Winter Paralympics">1976 Winter Paralympics</a></b> in <a href="/wiki/%C3%96rnsk%C3%B6ldsvik" title="Örnsköldsvik">Örnsköldsvik</a>, Sweden. One competitor, Bill Hovanic, competed in four events in two sports. He did not win a medal.
</p><p>The country finished 10th in the <a href="/wiki/1976_Winter_Paralympics_medal_table" title="1976 Winter Paralympics medal table">medal table</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
