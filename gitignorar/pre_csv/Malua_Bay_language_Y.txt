<p><b>Malua Bay</b> (also called <b>Middle Nambas</b>) is an <a href="/wiki/Oceanic_languages" title="Oceanic languages">Oceanic language</a> spoken in northwest <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Malekula" title="Malekula">Malekula</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vanuatu" title="Vanuatu">Vanuatu</a>.
</p>
