<p><i><b>Graphium stratocles</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Butterfly" title="Butterfly">butterfly</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Papilionidae" title="Papilionidae">Papilionidae</a>. It is found in the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>.
</p>
