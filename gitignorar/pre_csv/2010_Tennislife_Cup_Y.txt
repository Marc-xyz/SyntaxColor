<p>The <b>2010 <a href="/wiki/Tennislife_Cup" title="Tennislife Cup">Tennislife Cup</a></b> was a professional <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> tournament played on <a href="/wiki/Clay_court" title="Clay court">outdoor red clay courts</a>. It was the fourth edition of the tournament which was part of the <a href="/wiki/2010_ATP_Challenger_Tour" title="2010 ATP Challenger Tour">2010 ATP Challenger Tour</a>. It took place in <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> between 27 September and 3 October 2010.
</p>
