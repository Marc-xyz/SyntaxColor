<p>The <b>1914 Rice Owls football team</b> was an <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> team that represented <a href="/wiki/Rice_University" title="Rice University">Rice University</a> as an independent during the <a href="/wiki/1914_college_football_season" title="1914 college football season">1914 college football season</a>. In its third season under head coach <a href="/wiki/Philip_Arbuckle" title="Philip Arbuckle">Philip Arbuckle</a>, the team compiled a 3–2–3 record and was outscored by a total of 113 to 59.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
