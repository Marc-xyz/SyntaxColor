<p><b>Nikita Kotlov</b> (born September 20, 1991) is an American <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">soccer</a> player who plays for Maltese club <a href="/wiki/Mosta_F.C." title="Mosta F.C.">Mosta FC</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nikita_Kotlov&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="College">College</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nikita_Kotlov&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: College">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Kotlov spent his entire college career at <a href="/wiki/Indiana_University" title="Indiana University">Indiana University</a>.  He made a total of 85 appearances for the Hoosiers, tallying 26 goals and 14 assists.  On December 9, 2012, he scored the game-winning goal in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2012_NCAA_Division_I_Men%27s_Soccer_Championship" title="2012 NCAA Division I Men's Soccer Championship">College Cup</a> final over <a href="/wiki/Georgetown_Hoyas_men%27s_soccer" title="Georgetown Hoyas men's soccer">Georgetown</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional">Professional</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nikita_Kotlov&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Professional">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On January 21, 2014, Kotlov was selected in the fourth round (73rd overall) of the <a href="/wiki/2014_MLS_SuperDraft" title="2014 MLS SuperDraft">2014 MLS SuperDraft</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Portland_Timbers" title="Portland Timbers">Portland Timbers</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> but was cut prior to the start of the season.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>On March 24, 2015, Kotlov signed a contract with <a href="/wiki/United_Soccer_League" title="United Soccer League">United Soccer League</a> club <a href="/wiki/Saint_Louis_FC" title="Saint Louis FC">Saint Louis FC</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>  He made his professional debut on April 2 in a 2–0 victory over the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Tulsa_(USL)" title="FC Tulsa (USL)">Tulsa Roughnecks</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>On August 24, 2019, Kotlov signed a contract with <a href="/wiki/Mosta_F.C." title="Mosta F.C.">Mosta FC</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Maltese_Premier_League" title="Maltese Premier League">Maltese Premier League</a>.
</p>
