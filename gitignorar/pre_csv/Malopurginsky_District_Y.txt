<p><b>Malopurginsky District</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Малопурги́нский райо́н</span>; <a href="/wiki/Udmurt_language" title="Udmurt language">Udmurt</a>: <span lang="udm">Пичи Пурга ёрос</span>) is an administrative<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Constitution_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Constitution-1">[1]</a></sup> and municipal<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UdmurtRMalopurginskyD_mun_7-2"><a href="#cite_note-UdmurtRMalopurginskyD_mun-7">[7]</a></sup> district (<a href="/wiki/Raion" title="Raion">raion</a>), one of the <a href="/wiki/Administrative_divisions_of_the_Udmurt_Republic" title="Administrative divisions of the Udmurt Republic">twenty-five</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Udmurt_Republic" title="Udmurt Republic">Udmurt Republic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>. It is located in the south of the republic. The area of the district is 1,223.2 square kilometers (472.3 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-sa_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-sa-3">[3]</a></sup> Its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Administrative_center" title="Administrative center">administrative center</a> is the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Types_of_inhabited_localities_in_Russia" title="Types of inhabited localities in Russia">rural locality</a> (a <i><a href="/wiki/Village#Russia" title="Village">selo</a></i>) of <a href="/wiki/Malaya_Purga" title="Malaya Purga">Malaya Purga</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-UdmurtR_admlaw_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-UdmurtR_admlaw-2">[2]</a></sup> Population: <span class="nowrap">33,058 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2010)" title="Russian Census (2010)">2010 Census</a>)</span>;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-4">[4]</a></sup> 31,558 (<a href="/wiki/Russian_Census_(2002)" title="Russian Census (2002)">2002 Census</a>);<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2002Census_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-2002Census-9">[9]</a></sup> <span class="nowrap">30,849 (<a href="/wiki/Soviet_Census_(1989)" title="Soviet Census (1989)">1989 Census</a>)</span>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census1989_10-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census1989-10">[10]</a></sup> The population of Malaya Purga accounts for 23.3% of the district's total population.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2010Census_4-2"><a href="#cite_note-2010Census-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
