<p><i><b>Pyrgomantis wellmanni</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Praying_mantis" title="Praying mantis">praying mantis</a> found in <a href="/wiki/Angola" title="Angola">Angola</a>. They have a green body with white &amp; green wings.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
