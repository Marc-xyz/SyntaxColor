<p><i><b>Aechmea tessmannii</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Species_(biology)" title="Species (biology)">species</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Genus_(biology)" title="Genus (biology)">genus</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Aechmea" title="Aechmea">Aechmea</a></i>. This species is <a href="/wiki/Native_plant" title="Native plant">native</a> to <a href="/wiki/Ecuador" title="Ecuador">Ecuador</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peru" title="Peru">Peru</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-t_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-t-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
