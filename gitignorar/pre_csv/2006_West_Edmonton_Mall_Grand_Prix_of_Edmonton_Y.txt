<p>The <b><a href="/wiki/Edmonton_Indy" title="Edmonton Indy">2006 West Edmonton Mall Grand Prix of Edmonton</a></b> was the eighth round of the <a href="/wiki/2006_Champ_Car_season" title="2006 Champ Car season">2006 Bridgestone Presents the Champ Car World Series Powered by Ford season</a>, held on July 23, 2006 at JAGFlo Speedway, a temporary track laid out at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Edmonton_City_Centre_(Blatchford_Field)_Airport" title="Edmonton City Centre (Blatchford Field) Airport">City Centre Airport</a> in <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton, Alberta, Canada</a>.  <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a> took the pole but the race was won by <a href="/wiki/Justin_Wilson_(racing_driver)" title="Justin Wilson (racing driver)">Justin Wilson</a>.
</p>
