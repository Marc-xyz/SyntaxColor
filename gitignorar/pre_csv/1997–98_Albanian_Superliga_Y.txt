<p>The <b>1997–98 Albanian National Championship</b> was the 59th season of the <a href="/wiki/Albanian_Superliga" title="Albanian Superliga">Albanian National Championship</a>, the top professional league for <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> clubs, since its establishment in 1930.
</p>
