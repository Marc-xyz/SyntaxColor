<p><i><b>Sin noticias de Holanda</b></i> (in <a href="/wiki/English_language" title="English language">English</a>: <i>No News From Holland</i>) is Spanish <a href="/wiki/Pop_music" title="Pop music">pop</a> singer <a href="/wiki/Melendi" title="Melendi">Melendi</a>'s début album. It sold over 400,000 albums, and was later reissued with three new songs.
</p>
