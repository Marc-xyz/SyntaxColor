<p><i><b>Polycyathus</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of small corals in the order <a href="/wiki/Scleractinia" title="Scleractinia">Scleractinia</a>, the stony corals. Most species occur in the Pacific Ocean.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-lin_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-lin-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
