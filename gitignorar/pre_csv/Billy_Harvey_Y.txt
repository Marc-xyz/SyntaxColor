<p><b>William George Harvey</b> (25 October 1892 — 14 September 1917) was an <a href="/wiki/Australian_rules_football" title="Australian rules football">Australian rules footballer</a> who played with <a href="/wiki/Essendon_Football_Club" title="Essendon Football Club">Essendon</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Australian_Football_League" title="Australian Football League">Victorian Football League</a> (VFL), and was killed in action in Belgium in World War I during the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Passchendaele" title="Battle of Passchendaele">Battle of Passchendaele</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Footballer">Footballer</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Billy_Harvey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Footballer">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Recruited from the Engineers, he played nine senior games for Essendon, and kicked two goals. His first match was against Geelong at the <a href="/wiki/East_Melbourne_Cricket_Ground" title="East Melbourne Cricket Ground">East Melbourne Cricket Ground</a>,  on 25 May 1912 (round 5), in which he played well.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> His last match was against St Kilda, at the <a href="/wiki/Junction_Oval" title="Junction Oval">Junction Oval</a>, on 24 August 1912.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Family">Family</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Billy_Harvey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Family">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The son of Edward and Annie Harvey, née Gilpin, he was born in <a href="/wiki/Canterbury,_Victoria" title="Canterbury, Victoria">Canterbury, Victoria</a> in 1892, and married Mona Madeleine Faragher, at <a href="/wiki/Wandsworth" title="Wandsworth">Wandsworth, London</a> on 18 October 1916.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Soldier">Soldier</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Billy_Harvey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Soldier">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He enlisted in the First AIF, giving his occupation as carpenter, on 24 August 1914.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Billy_Harvey&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Captain William George Harvey, M.C., 1st Australian Pioneers, was killed in action on 14 September 1917.
</p>
