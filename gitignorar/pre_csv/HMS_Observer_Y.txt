	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">HMS <i>Observer</i></h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>

Three ships of the British <a href="/wiki/Royal_Navy" title="Royal Navy">Royal Navy</a> have been named <b>HMS <i>Observer</i></b>:
</p>
<ul><li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=HMS_Observer_(1781)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="HMS Observer (1781) (page does not exist)">HMS <i>Observer</i> <span class="nowrap">(1781)</span></a>, a <a href="/wiki/Sloop-of-war" title="Sloop-of-war">sloop</a> of 10 guns, was the Massachusetts privateer <i>Amsterdam</i>, which <a href="/wiki/HMS_Amphitrite_(1778)" title="HMS Amphitrite (1778)">HMS <i>Amphitrite</i></a> captured on 19 October 1781.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <i>Observer</i> was sold on 21 October 1784.</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=HMS_Observer_(1916)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="HMS Observer (1916) (page does not exist)">HMS <i>Observer</i> <span class="nowrap">(1916)</span></a>, an <a href="/wiki/Admiralty_M-class_destroyer" title="Admiralty M-class destroyer">Admiralty M-class</a> <a href="/wiki/Destroyer" title="Destroyer">destroyer</a> launched in 1916 and sold in 1921.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/HMS_Oribi_(G66)" title="HMS Oribi (G66)">HMS <i>Observer</i></a>, an <a href="/wiki/O_and_P-class_destroyer" title="O and P-class destroyer">O-class destroyer</a> renamed whilst under construction to HMS <i>Oribi</i> (G66) and launched in 1941. The ship was transferred to Turkey in 1946 and broken up in 1965.</li></ul>

<ul><li><a href="/wiki/French_brig_Observateur_(1800)" title="French brig Observateur (1800)">HMS <i>Observateur</i></a>, a French brig launched at Le Havre in 1800, captured in 1806 and sold in 1814.</li></ul>
