<p><i><b>Phytoecia bohemani</b></i> is a species of <a href="/wiki/Beetle" title="Beetle">beetle</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cerambycidae" title="Cerambycidae">Cerambycidae</a>. It was described by <a href="/wiki/Francis_Polkinghorne_Pascoe" title="Francis Polkinghorne Pascoe">Francis Polkinghorne Pascoe</a> in 1858, originally under the genus <i><a href="/wiki/Saperda" title="Saperda">Saperda</a></i>. It is known from <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
