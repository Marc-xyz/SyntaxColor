<p><b>Richard Heinrich Ludwig Münch</b> (10 January 1916 – 6 June 1987), better known as <b>Richard Münch</b>, was a <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Actor" title="Actor">actor</a>, best known for portraying <a href="/wiki/Alfred_Jodl" title="Alfred Jodl">Alfred Jodl</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/Patton_(film)" title="Patton (film)">Patton</a></i> (1970). He also portrayed General <a href="/wiki/Erich_Marcks" title="Erich Marcks">Erich Marcks</a> in <i><a href="/wiki/The_Longest_Day_(film)" title="The Longest Day (film)">The Longest Day</a></i> (1962).
</p>
