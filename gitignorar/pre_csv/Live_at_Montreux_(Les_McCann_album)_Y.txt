<p><i><b>Live at Montreux</b></i> is an album by pianist <a href="/wiki/Les_McCann" title="Les McCann">Les McCann</a> recorded at the <a href="/wiki/Montreux_Jazz_Festival" title="Montreux Jazz Festival">Montreux Jazz Festival</a> in 1972 and released on the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_Records" title="Atlantic Records">Atlantic</a> label.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> 
</p>

</p>
