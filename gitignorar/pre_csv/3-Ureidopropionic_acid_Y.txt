<p><b>3-Ureidopropionic acid</b>, also called <b>N-carbamoyl-beta-alanine</b>, is an intermediate in the metabolism of <a href="/wiki/Uracil" title="Uracil">uracil</a>.  It is a <a href="/wiki/Urea" title="Urea">urea</a> derivative of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Beta-alanine" title="Beta-alanine">beta-alanine</a>.
</p>
