<p><b>Narciso Valentin Ticobay</b> (19 March 1932-21 July 2013) was a Filipino Episcopalian bishop.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-EAMC_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-EAMC-1">[1]</a></sup> He was the second Prime Bishop the <a href="/wiki/Episcopal_Church_in_the_Philippines" title="Episcopal Church in the Philippines">Episcopal Church in the Philippines</a> from 1993 to 1997.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-EDSP_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-EDSP-2">[2]</a></sup> He also served as the second Diocesan Bishop the EDSP from 1986-1993.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-EDSP_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-EDSP-2">[2]</a></sup> He died in 2013 at the age of 81.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-EAMC_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-EAMC-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
