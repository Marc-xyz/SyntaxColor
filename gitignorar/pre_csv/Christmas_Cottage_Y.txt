<p><i><b>Thomas Kinkade's Christmas Cottage,</b></i> is a 2008 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christmas_movie" title="Christmas movie">Christmas</a> <a href="/wiki/Biographical_film" title="Biographical film">biopic</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Michael_Campus" title="Michael Campus">Michael Campus</a> (the first film he had directed in over thirty years). It stars <a href="/wiki/Jared_Padalecki" title="Jared Padalecki">Jared Padalecki</a> as painter <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Kinkade" title="Thomas Kinkade">Thomas Kinkade</a>, and features <a href="/wiki/Peter_O%27Toole" title="Peter O'Toole">Peter O'Toole</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marcia_Gay_Harden" title="Marcia Gay Harden">Marcia Gay Harden</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Aaron_Ashmore" title="Aaron Ashmore">Aaron Ashmore</a>. The film was originally intended for a theatrical release in December 2007, but due to final edits and music rights, was delayed until November 2008. 
</p><p>Released in only a few overseas markets (and not in the United States), it earned only $41,724.
</p>
