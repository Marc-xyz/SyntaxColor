	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">1991 Brisbane Broncos season</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>

		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>

The <b>1991 <a href="/wiki/Brisbane_Broncos" title="Brisbane Broncos">Brisbane Broncos</a> season</b> was the fourth in the club's history. They competed in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NSWRL" title="NSWRL">NSWRL</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1991_Winfield_Cup" title="1991 Winfield Cup">1991 Winfield Cup</a> premiership and failed to reach the finals, finishing 7th (out of 16).
</p><p>Three Broncos players, <a href="/wiki/Paul_Hauff" title="Paul Hauff">Paul Hauff</a>, <a href="/wiki/Willie_Carne" title="Willie Carne">Willie Carne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andrew_Gee" title="Andrew Gee">Andrew Gee</a> were selected to make their international debuts for Australia in 1991.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Season_summary">Season summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_Brisbane_Broncos_season&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Season summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In the <a href="/wiki/1991_NSWRL_season" title="1991 NSWRL season">1991 NSWRL season</a> the Broncos again won the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Panasonic_Cup" title="Panasonic Cup">Panasonic Cup</a> competition. In round 16 they were kept scoreless by the Manly-Warringah Sea Eagles, the first team ever to do so. <a href="/wiki/Steve_Renouf" title="Steve Renouf">Steve Renouf</a> became the first Bronco to score four tries in a match in round 20. However they didn't perform consistently enough during the season, only gaining momentum in August, when they won their last five matches straight.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The club finished the season in seventh place, missing the finals despite winning their last 5 games consecutively and finishing with the second best points scored total in the League.
</p>
