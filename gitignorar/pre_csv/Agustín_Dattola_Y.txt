<p><b>Agustín Nicolás Dattola</b> (born 20 April 1999) is an Argentine professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> for <a href="/wiki/Club_Almirante_Brown" title="Club Almirante Brown">Almirante Brown</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Soccerway_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Soccerway-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Agust%C3%ADn_Dattola&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dattola began with <a href="/wiki/Club_Almirante_Brown" title="Club Almirante Brown">Almirante Brown</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Soccerway_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Soccerway-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mundo_Ascenso_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mundo_Ascenso-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-El1Digital_1_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-El1Digital_1-3">[3]</a></sup> He made the breakthrough into senior football under manager <a href="/wiki/Blas_Giunta" title="Blas Giunta">Blas Giunta</a> midway through <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2018%E2%80%9319_Primera_B_Metropolitana&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2018–19 Primera B Metropolitana (page does not exist)">2018–19</a>, appearing for his debut in <a href="/wiki/Primera_B_Metropolitana" title="Primera B Metropolitana">Primera B Metropolitana</a> on 16 February 2019 against <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_F%C3%A9nix" title="Club Atlético Fénix">Fénix</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Soccerway_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-Soccerway-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-El1Digital_2_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-El1Digital_2-4">[4]</a></sup> Dattola again played the full duration of the club's next nine fixtures, a streak that was ended after he received a red card versus <a href="/wiki/Club_Atl%C3%A9tico_Atlanta" title="Club Atlético Atlanta">Atlanta</a> on 14 April.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Soccerway_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-Soccerway-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Clarín_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Clarín-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
