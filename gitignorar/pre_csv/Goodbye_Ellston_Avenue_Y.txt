<p><i><b>Goodbye Ellston Avenue</b></i> is the debut studio album by the American <a href="/wiki/Punk_rock" title="Punk rock">punk rock</a> band <a href="/wiki/Pinhead_Gunpowder" title="Pinhead Gunpowder">Pinhead Gunpowder</a>. It was released on February 25, 1997 through <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Lookout!_Records" title="Lookout! Records">Lookout! Records</a>. The album was re-released on vinyl and CD through <a href="/wiki/Recess_Records" title="Recess Records">Recess Records</a> on February 12, 2010. This is also the only studio album by Pinhead Gunpowder as of 2018.
</p>
