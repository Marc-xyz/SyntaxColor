	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Shini</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Shini</b> (asomtavruli <style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r866748851">.mw-parser-output .script-khutsuri{font-family:"DejaVu Sans","DejaVu Serif","Kelvinch","Noto Sans Georgian","Noto Serif Georgian","Sylfean","Segoe WP","Segoe UI",Quivira,"MPH 2B Damase"}</style><span class="script-khutsuri">Ⴘ</span>, nuskhuri <link href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r866748851" rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style"/><span class="script-khutsuri">ⴘ</span>, mkhedruli შ) is the 28th letter of the three <a href="/wiki/Georgian_scripts" title="Georgian scripts">Georgian scripts</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>In the system of <a href="/wiki/Georgian_numerals" title="Georgian numerals">Georgian numerals</a> it has a value of 900.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Mched2_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Mched2-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Shini commonly represents the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Voiceless_palato-alveolar_sibilant" title="Voiceless palato-alveolar sibilant">voiceless palato-alveolar sibilant</a> <span class="IPA" title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)">/ʃ/</span>, like the pronunciation of ⟨sh⟩ in "<b>sh</b>oe".
</p>
