<p>The <b>Black Leopards</b> are a South African <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club based in <a href="/wiki/Thohoyandou" title="Thohoyandou">Thohoyandou</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Vhembe" title="Vhembe">Vhembe Region</a>, <a href="/wiki/Limpopo" title="Limpopo">Limpopo</a> that play in the <a href="/wiki/Premier_Soccer_League" title="Premier Soccer League">Premier Soccer League</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Black_Leopards_F.C.&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The club was founded in 1983 by business people in the Vhembe Region. In 1998 the club was taken over by the Thidiela family.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> In the beginning, the family received an un-hearty welcome due to the fact of the history the fans had towards positive racial equality, this was ended with a few of the 'fans' ending in jail for conspiracy of murder.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> After just two seasons of campaigning in the <a href="/wiki/National_First_Division" title="National First Division">National First Division</a>, Black Leopards were promoted to the elite division of the country.
</p><p>Leopards were relegated to the First Division in 2008 after seven seasons in the Premiership. In 2011 Leopards returned to the PSL.
</p>
