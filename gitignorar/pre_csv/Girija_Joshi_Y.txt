<p><b>Girija Joshi</b> (born 3 November 1988) is an <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> film actress who appears in <a href="/wiki/Marathi_language" title="Marathi language">Marathi</a> movies. She made her debut with <i>Govinda</i> in 2013 opposite <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swapnil_Joshi" title="Swapnil Joshi">Swapnil Joshi</a>.
</p>

<p>Girija was born in <a href="/wiki/Raigad_district" title="Raigad district">Raigad district</a>, <a href="/wiki/Maharashtra" title="Maharashtra">Maharashtra</a>. She completed her primary education in K.E.S English Medium School, Roha, Raigad then she moved to Vashi, Navi Mumbai. She has a completed an acting course through <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Joseph%27s_Academy,_Dehradun" title="St. Joseph's Academy, Dehradun">St. Joseph's Academy, Dehradun</a>. She is married to Chinmay Udgirkar.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Girija_Joshi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Girija started her career in Marathi film Govinda opposite <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Swapnil_Joshi" title="Swapnil Joshi">Swapnil Joshi</a>. She will be the co-star of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Siddharth_Jadhav" title="Siddharth Jadhav">Siddharth Jadhav</a> in Powder movie and <a href="/wiki/Priyatama" title="Priyatama">Priyatama</a> . In 2014 she appeared in 'Dhamak' opposite <a href="/wiki/Aniket_Vishwasrao" title="Aniket Vishwasrao">Aniket Vishwasrao</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
