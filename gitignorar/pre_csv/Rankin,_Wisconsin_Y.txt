<p><b>Rankin</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> in the town of <a href="/wiki/Ahnapee,_Wisconsin" title="Ahnapee, Wisconsin">Ahnapee</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Kewaunee_County,_Wisconsin" title="Kewaunee County, Wisconsin">Kewaunee County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin" title="Wisconsin">Wisconsin</a>, United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-gnis_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-gnis-1">[1]</a></sup> It is positioned along the intersection of <a href="/wiki/Wisconsin_Highway_54" title="Wisconsin Highway 54">Wisconsin Highway 54</a> and County Highway D, and it is the closest community to <a href="/wiki/Krohns_Lake" title="Krohns Lake">Krohns Lake</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Google_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Google-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
