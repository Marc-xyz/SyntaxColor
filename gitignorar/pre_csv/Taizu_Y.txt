	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Taizu</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Taizu</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chinese_language" title="Chinese language">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh"><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%A4%AA" title="wikt:太">太</a><a class="extiw" href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%A5%96" title="wikt:祖">祖</a></span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Tàizǔ</span></i>; <a href="/wiki/Wade%E2%80%93Giles" title="Wade–Giles">Wade–Giles</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-wadegile">T'ai<sup>4</sup>-tsu<sup>3</sup></span></i>; literally: 'grand progenitor') is an imperial <a href="/wiki/Temple_name" title="Temple name">temple name</a> typically used for <a href="/wiki/History_of_China" title="History of China">Chinese</a> <a href="/wiki/Emperor_of_China" title="Emperor of China">emperors</a> who founded a particular <a href="/wiki/Dynasty" title="Dynasty">dynasty</a>. It may refer to:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Gaozu_of_Han" title="Emperor Gaozu of Han">Emperor Gaozu of Han</a> (256 BC or 247 BC – 195 BC)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sun_Quan" title="Sun Quan">Sun Quan</a> (182–252) of Eastern Wu</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Liu_Yuan_(Han_Zhao)" title="Liu Yuan (Han Zhao)">Liu Yuan (Han Zhao)</a> (251–310) of Han Zhao</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Tuoba_Yul%C3%BC" title="Tuoba Yulü">Tuoba Yulü</a> (died in 321) of State of Dai</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Murong_Huang" title="Murong Huang">Murong Huang</a> (297–348) of Former Yan</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Shi_Hu" title="Shi Hu">Shi Hu</a> (295–349) of Later Zhao</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yao_Chang" title="Yao Chang">Yao Chang</a> (331–394) of Later Qin</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/L%C3%BC_Guang" title="Lü Guang">Lü Guang</a> (337–400) of Later Liang (Sixteen Kingdoms)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Daowu_of_Northern_Wei" title="Emperor Daowu of Northern Wei">Emperor Daowu of Northern Wei</a> (371–409)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Li_Gao" title="Li Gao">Li Gao</a> (351–417) of Western Liang (Sixteen Kingdoms)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Qifu_Chipan" title="Qifu Chipan">Qifu Chipan</a> (died in 428) of Western Qin</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Feng_Ba" title="Feng Ba">Feng Ba</a> (died in 430) of Northern Yan</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Juqu_Mengxun" title="Juqu Mengxun">Juqu Mengxun</a> (368–433) of Northern Liang</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Emperor_Wen_of_Liu_Song" title="Emperor Wen of Liu Song">Emperor Wen of Liu Song</a> (407–453)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Gao_of_Southern_Qi" title="Emperor Gao of Southern Qi">Emperor Gao of Southern Qi</a> (427–482) of Southern Qi</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Zhu_Wen" title="Zhu Wen">Zhu Wen</a> (852–912) of Later Liang (Five Dynasties)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wang_Shenzhi" title="Wang Shenzhi">Wang Shenzhi</a> (862–925) of Min (Ten Kingdoms)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Abaoji" title="Abaoji">Abaoji</a> (872–926) of the Liao dynasty</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Qian_Liu" title="Qian Liu">Qian Liu</a> (852–932) of Wuyue</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Duan_Siping" title="Duan Siping">Duan Siping</a> (893–944) of the Dali Kingdom</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Guo_Wei" title="Guo Wei">Guo Wei</a> (904–954) of Later Zhou</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Taizu_of_Song" title="Emperor Taizu of Song">Emperor Taizu of Song</a> (927–976)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Emperor_Taizu_of_Jin" title="Emperor Taizu of Jin">Emperor Taizu of Jin</a> (1068–1123)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hongwu_Emperor" title="Hongwu Emperor">Hongwu Emperor</a> (1328–1398) of the Ming dynasty</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ming_Yuzhen" title="Ming Yuzhen">Ming Yuzhen</a> (1331–1366) of Great Xia</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Wu_Sangui" title="Wu Sangui">Wu Sangui</a> (1612–1678) of Great Zhou</li></ul>
<p>It may also refer to those who never officially declared themselves as emperors, but were posthumously given the title by their imperial descendants:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Cao_Cao" title="Cao Cao">Cao Cao</a> (155-220), Emperor Taizu of Cao Wei (220–265)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Sima_Zhao" title="Sima Zhao">Sima Zhao</a> (211–265), Emperor Taizu of the Jin dynasty (265–420)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Zhang_Gui" title="Zhang Gui">Zhang Gui</a> (255–314), Emperor Taizu of Former Liang (320–376)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fu_Hong" title="Fu Hong">Fu Hong</a> (284–350), Emperor Taizu of Former Qin (351–394)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Huan_Wen" title="Huan Wen">Huan Wen</a> (312–373), Emperor Taizu of Huan Chu (403–404)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Liu_Weichen&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Liu Weichen (page does not exist)">Liu Weichen</a> (died in 391), Emperor Taizu of <a href="/wiki/Xia_(Sixteen_Kingdoms)" title="Xia (Sixteen Kingdoms)">Xia (Sixteen Kingdoms)</a> (407–431)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Xiao_Shunzhi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Xiao Shunzhi (page does not exist)">Xiao Shunzhi</a> (<a href="/wiki/Floruit" title="Floruit">fl.</a> 477–482), Emperor Taizu of the <a href="/wiki/Liang_dynasty" title="Liang dynasty">Liang dynasty</a> (502–557)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Gao_Huan" title="Gao Huan">Gao Huan</a> (496–547), Emperor Taizu of <a href="/wiki/Northern_Qi" title="Northern Qi">Northern Qi</a> (550–577)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yuwen_Tai" title="Yuwen Tai">Yuwen Tai</a> (507–556), Emperor Taizu of <a href="/wiki/Northern_Zhou" title="Northern Zhou">Northern Zhou</a> (557–581)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Chen_Wenzhan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Chen Wenzhan (page does not exist)">Chen Wenzhan</a> (died before 557), Emperor Taizu of the <a href="/wiki/Chen_dynasty" title="Chen dynasty">Chen dynasty</a> (557–589)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Yang_Zhong&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Yang Zhong (page does not exist)">Yang Zhong</a> (507–568), Emperor Taizu of the <a href="/wiki/Sui_dynasty" title="Sui dynasty">Sui dynasty</a> (581–618)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Li_Hu" title="Li Hu">Li Hu</a> (died in 551), Emperor Taizu of the Tang dynasty (618–907)</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wu_Shihuo" title="Wu Shihuo">Wu Shihuo</a> (559–635), Emperor Taizu of Southern Zhou (690–705)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Yang_Xingmi" title="Yang Xingmi">Yang Xingmi</a> (852–905), Emperor Taizu of Wu (Ten Kingdoms) (907–937)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Liu_Anren&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Liu Anren (page does not exist)">Liu Anren</a> (died before 917), Emperor Taizu of <a href="/wiki/Southern_Han" title="Southern Han">Southern Han</a> (917–971)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Li_Keyong" title="Li Keyong">Li Keyong</a> (856–908), Emperor Taizu of Later Tang (923–936)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Meng_Yi_(Tang_dynasty)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Meng Yi (Tang dynasty) (page does not exist)">Meng Yi (Tang dynasty)</a> (died before 934), Emperor Taizu of <a href="/wiki/Later_Shu" title="Later Shu">Later Shu</a> (934–965)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Xu_Wen" title="Xu Wen">Xu Wen</a> (862–927), Emperor Taizu of Southern Tang (937–975)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Li_Jiqian" title="Li Jiqian">Li Jiqian</a> (963–1004), Emperor Taizu of Western Xia (1038–1227)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Genghis_Khan" title="Genghis Khan">Genghis Khan</a> (1162?–1227), Emperor Taizu of the Yuan dynasty (1279–1368)</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nurhaci" title="Nurhaci">Nurhaci</a> (1559–1626), Emperor Taizu of the <a href="/wiki/Qing_dynasty" title="Qing dynasty">Qing dynasty</a> (1644–1912)</li></ul>
