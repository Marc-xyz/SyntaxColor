<p><b>Anna Jurčenková</b> (born July 26, 1985) is a Slovak female <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> player. She currently plays for <a href="/wiki/Olympiacos_Women%27s_Basketball" title="Olympiacos Women's Basketball">Olympiacos</a> in <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greece</a>.
</p>
