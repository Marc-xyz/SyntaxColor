<p><b>Abqad</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">ابقد</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Ābqad</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Golmakan_Rural_District" title="Golmakan Rural District">Golmakan Rural District</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Golbajar_District" title="Golbajar District">Golbajar District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chenaran_County" title="Chenaran County">Chenaran County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Razavi_Khorasan_Province" title="Razavi Khorasan Province">Razavi Khorasan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 480, in 132 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
