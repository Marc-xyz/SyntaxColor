<p><b>Rosny-sous-Bois</b> is a <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> railway station located in Rosny-sous-Bois, in <a href="/wiki/Seine-Saint-Denis" title="Seine-Saint-Denis">Seine-Saint-Denis</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">département</a></i>, in <a href="/wiki/%C3%8Ele-de-France" title="Île-de-France">Île-de-France</a> region.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station is situated at <a href="/wiki/Kilometric_point" title="Kilometric point">kilometric point</a> 12.631 of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paris-Mulhouse_railway" title="Paris-Mulhouse railway">Paris-Mulhouse railway</a>. Its altitude is 69 m.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Service">Service</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Service">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Facilities">Facilities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Facilities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The counter in the building is open every day. The station is equipped with automatic ticket machines, real time traffic information systems and facilities for disabled people.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Train_service">Train service</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Train service">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><b>Rosny-Bois-Perrier</b> is served by <a href="/wiki/RER_E" title="RER E">RER E</a> trains coming from or bound to <a href="/wiki/Villiers-sur-Marne_-_Le_Plessis-Tr%C3%A9vise_station" title="Villiers-sur-Marne - Le Plessis-Trévise station">Villiers-sur-Marne</a>. Trains from or bound to <a href="/wiki/Tournan_station" title="Tournan station">Tournan</a> call at the station only after 10 PM. The average waiting time in both directions is 15 minutes.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Connections">Connections</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Connections">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The station is served by:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/RATP_Group" title="RATP Group">RATP</a> bus lines 116, 118 and 143</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Noctilien" title="Noctilien">Noctilien</a> night bus line N142</li>
<li>Titus bus lines 1, 2, 3 and 4</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Museum">Museum</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rosny-sous-Bois_station&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Museum">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The station hosts <a class="extiw" href="https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rosny-Rail" title="fr:Rosny-Rail">Rosny-Rail</a>, a local railway museum.
</p>
