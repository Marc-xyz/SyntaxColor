<p>"<b>Wrap My Body Tight</b>" is the title of a number-one <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/R%26B" title="R&amp;B">R&amp;B</a> single by <a href="/wiki/Johnny_Gill" title="Johnny Gill">Johnny Gill</a> from his self-titled album, <i><a href="/wiki/Johnny_Gill_(1990_album)" title="Johnny Gill (1990 album)">Johnny Gill</a></i>. The song spent one week at number-one on the US R&amp;B chart.  A remix to the song features added vocals from singer <a href="/wiki/Karyn_White" title="Karyn White">Karyn White</a>.
</p>
