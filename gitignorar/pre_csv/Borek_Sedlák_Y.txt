<p><b>Borek Sedlák</b> (born <i>Vlástibor</i>, June 15, 1981) is a Czech former <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ski_jumper" title="Ski jumper">ski jumper</a> and current Assistant Race Director of the <a href="/wiki/FIS_Ski_Jumping_World_Cup" title="FIS Ski Jumping World Cup">FIS Ski Jumping World Cup</a>. He competed from 2001 to 2014. At the <a href="/wiki/2006_Winter_Olympics" title="2006 Winter Olympics">2006 Winter Olympics</a>, he finished ninth in the team large hill and 38th in the individual normal hill events.
</p><p>Sedlák's best individual World Cup finish was 17th in a large hill event in <a href="/wiki/Liberec" title="Liberec">Liberec</a> in 2008. His lone victory was in a Continental Cup normal event in <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a> in 2005.
</p>
