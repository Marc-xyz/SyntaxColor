<p><i><b>Nemacheilus saravacensis</b></i> is a species of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ray-finned_fish" title="Ray-finned fish">ray-finned fish</a> in the genus <i><a href="/wiki/Nemacheilus" title="Nemacheilus">Nemacheilus</a></i> from western <a href="/wiki/Borneo" title="Borneo">Borneo</a>.
</p>
