<p><b>Kim Ok-ju</b> (born 20 February 1988) is a South Korean <a href="/wiki/Field_hockey" title="Field hockey">field hockey</a> player.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> At the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Olympics" title="2012 Summer Olympics">2012 Summer Olympics</a> she competed with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Korea_women%27s_national_field_hockey_team" title="Korea women's national field hockey team">Korea women's national field hockey team</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Field_hockey_at_the_2012_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament" title="Field hockey at the 2012 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament">women's tournament</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> She competed at the 2010 and 2010 Asian Games.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>She won a gold medal as a member of the South Korean team at <a href="/wiki/2014_Asian_Games" title="2014 Asian Games">2014 Asian Games</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
