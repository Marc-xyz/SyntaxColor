<p>The <b>Roman Catholic Diocese of Coimbatore</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latin_language" title="Latin language">Latin</a>: <i lang="la">Coimbaturen(sis)</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Diocese" title="Diocese">diocese</a> located in the city of <a href="/wiki/Coimbatore" title="Coimbatore">Coimbatore</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Ecclesiastical_province" title="Ecclesiastical province">Ecclesiastical province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Roman_Catholic_Archdiocese_of_Madras_and_Mylapore" title="Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Madras and Mylapore">Madras and Mylapore</a> in India.
</p>

<ul><li>1845: Established as Apostolic Pro-Vicariate of Coimbatore from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Apostolic_Vicariate_of_Madura_and_Coromandel_Coast" title="Apostolic Vicariate of Madura and Coromandel Coast">Apostolic Vicariate of Madura and Coromandel Coast</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diocese_of_Madura" title="Diocese of Madura">Diocese of Madura</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></li>
<li>1850: Promoted as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Apostolic_Vicariate" title="Apostolic Vicariate">Apostolic Vicariate</a> of Coimbatore</li>
