<p><b>Michal Sýkora</b> (born July 5, 1973) is a retired Czech professional <a href="/wiki/Ice_hockey" title="Ice hockey">ice hockey</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Defenseman_(ice_hockey)" title="Defenseman (ice hockey)">defenseman</a> who played in the <a href="/wiki/National_Hockey_League" title="National Hockey League">National Hockey League</a> (NHL) for the <a href="/wiki/San_Jose_Sharks" title="San Jose Sharks">San Jose Sharks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chicago_Blackhawks" title="Chicago Blackhawks">Chicago Blackhawks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tampa_Bay_Lightning" title="Tampa Bay Lightning">Tampa Bay Lightning</a> and <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_Flyers" title="Philadelphia Flyers">Philadelphia Flyers</a>. He is not related to the NHL hockey player <a href="/wiki/Petr_S%C3%BDkora" title="Petr Sýkora">Petr Sýkora</a> who played with the <a href="/wiki/New_Jersey_Devils" title="New Jersey Devils">New Jersey Devils</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2011%E2%80%9312_NHL_season" title="2011–12 NHL season">2011-12 season</a>. He is the older brother to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Petr_S%C3%BDkora_(ice_hockey_b._1978)" title="Petr Sýkora (ice hockey b. 1978)">Petr Sýkora</a>, who played 12 NHL games.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Michal_S%C3%BDkora&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sýkora started his NHL career with the <a href="/wiki/San_Jose_Sharks" title="San Jose Sharks">San Jose Sharks</a> in <a href="/wiki/1993%E2%80%9394_NHL_season" title="1993–94 NHL season">1993–94</a>. He joined the <a href="/wiki/Chicago_Blackhawks" title="Chicago Blackhawks">Chicago Blackhawks</a>  for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1996-97_NHL_season" title="1996-97 NHL season">1996–97 season</a> and played in 28 games. He recorded a respectable +/- of 4 and scored 1 goal. Unfortunately Michal could not reprise these statistics with Blackhawks. He recorded a +/- of -10 in the same number of games in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1997-98_NHL_season" title="1997-98 NHL season">1997–98 season</a>. He was traded to the <a href="/wiki/Tampa_Bay_Lightning" title="Tampa Bay Lightning">Tampa Bay Lightning</a> for <a href="/wiki/Mark_Fitzpatrick" title="Mark Fitzpatrick">Goalie Mark Fitzpatrick</a> and a 4th round draft choice in 1999. Sýkora signed as a free agent with Philadelphia for the <a href="/wiki/2000%E2%80%9301_NHL_season" title="2000–01 NHL season">2000–01</a> season, leaving the NHL after that season.
</p>
