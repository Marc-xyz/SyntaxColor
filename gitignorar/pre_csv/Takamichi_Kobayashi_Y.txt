<p><b>Takamichi Kobayashi</b><span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">小林 高道</span><span style="display:none">,</span> <i>Kobayashi Takamichi</i>, born January 3, 1979)</span> is a former <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> player.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Takamichi_Kobayashi&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kobayashi was born in <a href="/wiki/Niigata_Prefecture" title="Niigata Prefecture">Niigata Prefecture</a> on January 3, 1979. After graduating from high school, he joined <a href="/wiki/Japanese_Regional_Leagues" title="Japanese Regional Leagues">Regional Leagues</a> club <a href="/wiki/Albirex_Niigata" title="Albirex Niigata">Albirex Niigata</a> based in his local in 1997. The club was promoted to <a href="/wiki/Japan_Football_League_(1992%E2%80%9398)" title="Japan Football League (1992–98)">Japan Football League</a> from 1998 and <a href="/wiki/J2_League" title="J2 League">J2 League</a> from 1999. Although he played several matches as midfielder every season, he could not play many matches. He retired end of 2001 season.
</p>
