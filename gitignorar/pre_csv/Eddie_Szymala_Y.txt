<p><b>Edmund "Eddie" Szymala</b> (second ¼ 1954<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Birth_details_at_freebmd.org.uk_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Birth_details_at_freebmd.org.uk-4">[4]</a></sup>), also known by the <a href="/wiki/Nickname" title="Nickname">nickname</a> of <b>"Smiler"</b>, is an English former professional <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league" title="Rugby league">rugby league</a> footballer who played in the 1970s and 1980s. He played at representative level for <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain_national_rugby_league_team" title="Great Britain national rugby league team">Great Britain</a> and <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a>, and at club level for <a href="/wiki/Barrow_Raiders" title="Barrow Raiders">Barrow</a>, as a <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Second-row_forward" title="Rugby league positions">second-row</a>, i.e. number 11 or 12.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Statistics_at_rugbyleagueproject.org-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eddie Szymala's birth was registered in <a href="/wiki/Barrow-in-Furness" title="Barrow-in-Furness">Barrow-in-Furness</a> district, <a href="/wiki/Lancashire" title="Lancashire">Lancashire</a>, England.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Playing_career">Playing career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Playing career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_honours">International honours</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: International honours">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Eddie Szymala won a <a href="/wiki/Cap_(sport)" title="Cap (sport)">cap</a> for <a href="/wiki/England_national_rugby_league_team" title="England national rugby league team">England</a> while at Barrow in 1979 against France (sub),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-England_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-England_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk-2">[2]</a></sup> and won <a href="/wiki/Cap_(sport)" title="Cap (sport)">caps</a> for <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain_national_rugby_league_team" title="Great Britain national rugby league team">Great Britain</a> while at Barrow in 1981 against France (sub), and France.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Great_Britain_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Great_Britain_Statistics_at_englandrl.co.uk-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="County_Cup_Final_appearances">County Cup Final appearances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: County Cup Final appearances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Eddie Szymala played right-<a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Second-row_forward" title="Rugby league positions">second-row</a>, i.e. number 12, in <a href="/wiki/Barrow_Raiders" title="Barrow Raiders">Barrow</a>'s 12–8 victory over <a href="/wiki/Widnes_Vikings" title="Widnes Vikings">Widnes</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1983_Lancashire_Cup" title="1983 Lancashire Cup">1983</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/RFL_Lancashire_Cup" title="RFL Lancashire Cup">Lancashire</a> <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_county_cups" title="Rugby league county cups">County Cup</a> Final during the <a href="/wiki/1983%E2%80%9384_Rugby_Football_League_season" title="1983–84 Rugby Football League season">1983–84 season</a> at <a href="/wiki/Central_Park_(Wigan)" title="Central Park (Wigan)">Central Park</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wigan" title="Wigan">Wigan</a> on Saturday 1 October 1983,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1983-1984_Lancashire_Cup_Final_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-1983-1984_Lancashire_Cup_Final-5">[5]</a></sup> the entire Barrow team was inducted into the Barrow Hall of Fame in 2003.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="John_Player_Trophy_Final_appearances">John Player Trophy Final appearances</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: John Player Trophy Final appearances">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Eddie Szymala played as an <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_positions#Interchange" title="Rugby league positions">interchange</a>/<a href="/wiki/Substitution_(sport)" title="Substitution (sport)">substitute</a>, i.e. number 15, (replacing <a href="/wiki/Hooker_(rugby_league)" title="Hooker (rugby league)">hooker</a> <a href="/wiki/Howard_Allen" title="Howard Allen">Howard Allen</a>) in <a href="/wiki/Barrow_Raiders" title="Barrow Raiders">Barrow</a>'s 5–12 defeat by <a href="/wiki/Warrington_Wolves" title="Warrington Wolves">Warrington</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1980%E2%80%9381_League_Cup_(rugby_league)" title="1980–81 League Cup (rugby league)">1980–81</a> <a href="/wiki/League_Cup_(rugby_league)" title="League Cup (rugby league)">John Player Trophy</a> Final during the <a href="/wiki/1980%E2%80%9381_Rugby_Football_League_season" title="1980–81 Rugby Football League season">1980–81</a> at <a href="/wiki/Central_Park_(Wigan)" title="Central Park (Wigan)">Central Park</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wigan" title="Wigan">Wigan</a>, on Saturday 24 January 1981.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Testimonial_match">Testimonial match</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Testimonial match">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Derek_Hadley" title="Derek Hadley">Derek Hadley</a>, and Eddie Szymala shared a <a href="/wiki/Testimonial_match" title="Testimonial match">Testimonial match</a>/<a href="/wiki/Benefit_season" title="Benefit season">Benefit season</a> at <a href="/wiki/Barrow_Raiders" title="Barrow Raiders">Barrow</a> during the <a href="/wiki/1983%E2%80%9384_Rugby_Football_League_season" title="1983–84 Rugby Football League season">1983–84 Rugby Football League season</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Honoured_at_Barrow_Raiders">Honoured at Barrow Raiders</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Eddie_Szymala&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Honoured at Barrow Raiders">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/David_Cairns_(rugby_league)" title="David Cairns (rugby league)">David Cairns</a>, and Eddie Szymala were both inducted into the Barrow Hall of Fame as individuals in 2010, having previously been inducted as part of <a href="/wiki/1983%E2%80%9384_Rugby_Football_League_season" title="1983–84 Rugby Football League season">1983–84</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/RFL_Lancashire_Cup" title="RFL Lancashire Cup">Lancashire</a> <a href="/wiki/Rugby_league_county_cups" title="Rugby league county cups">County Cup</a> winning team in 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cairns_and_Szymala_set_for_Barrow_RL_Hall_of_Fame_–_again_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cairns_and_Szymala_set_for_Barrow_RL_Hall_of_Fame_–_again-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
