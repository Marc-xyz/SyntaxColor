<p><b>Pasha Kola</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">پاشاكلا</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Pāshā Kolā</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Harazpey-ye_Jonubi_Rural_District" title="Harazpey-ye Jonubi Rural District">Harazpey-ye Jonubi Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Amol_County)" title="Central District (Amol County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Amol_County" title="Amol County">Amol County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mazandaran_Province" title="Mazandaran Province">Mazandaran Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 229, in 62 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
