<p><b>Shreepur </b> is a <a href="/wiki/Village_development_committee_(Nepal)" title="Village development committee (Nepal)">village development committee</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sarlahi_District" title="Sarlahi District">Sarlahi District</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Janakpur_Zone" title="Janakpur Zone">Janakpur Zone</a> of south-eastern <a href="/wiki/Nepal" title="Nepal">Nepal</a>. At the time of the <a href="/wiki/1991_Nepal_census" title="1991 Nepal census">1991 Nepal census</a> it had a population of 2925 people living in 487 individual households.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
