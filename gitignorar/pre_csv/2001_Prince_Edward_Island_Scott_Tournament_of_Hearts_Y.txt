<p>The 2001 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prince_Edward_Island_Scott_Tournament_of_Hearts" title="Prince Edward Island Scott Tournament of Hearts">Prince Edward Island Scott Tournament of Hearts</a> was held January 19–23  at the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Charlottetown_Curling_Club&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Charlottetown Curling Club (page does not exist)">Charlottetown Curling Club</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Charlottetown,_Prince_Edward_Island" title="Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island">Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island</a>. The winning team was Team <a href="/wiki/Shelly_Bradley" title="Shelly Bradley">Shelly Bradley</a> who represented <a href="/wiki/Prince_Edward_Island" title="Prince Edward Island">Prince Edward Island</a>, finished with a 7-4 round-robin record, losing the tiebreaker for a place in the semi-finals at the <a href="/wiki/2001_Scott_Tournament_of_Hearts" title="2001 Scott Tournament of Hearts">2001 Scott Tournament of Hearts</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sudbury,_Ontario" title="Sudbury, Ontario">Sudbury, Ontario</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
