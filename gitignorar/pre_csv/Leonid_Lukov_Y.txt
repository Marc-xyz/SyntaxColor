<p><b>Leonid Davydovich Lukov</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Леонид Давидович Луков</span>; 2 May 1909 – 24 April 1963) was a Ukrainian Soviet <a href="/wiki/Film_director" title="Film director">film director</a> and <a href="/wiki/Screenwriter" title="Screenwriter">screenwriter</a>. He directed 25 films between 1930 and 1963. Leonid Lukov was awarded the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/State_Stalin_Prize" title="State Stalin Prize">Stalin Prize</a> twice: in 1941 and 1952.
</p>
