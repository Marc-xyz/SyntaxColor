<p><i><b>Kaiju Ouji</b></i><span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">怪獣王子 <i>Kaijū Ōji</i></span>, also known as "The Monster Prince")</span> is a Japanese dinosaur-themed <a href="/wiki/Tokusatsu" title="Tokusatsu">tokusatsu</a>/<a href="/wiki/Kaiju" title="Kaiju">kaiju</a> television series, produced by <a href="/wiki/P_Productions" title="P Productions">P Productions</a> and ran on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fuji_Television" title="Fuji Television">Fuji Television Network</a> from October 2, 1967 to March 25, 1968, lasting a total of twenty-six episodes.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Characters">Characters</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaiju_ouji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Characters">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Heroes">Heroes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaiju_ouji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Heroes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Takeru Ibuki: The main character. A young boy who befriends a brontosaurus.
</p><p>Nessie: A young brontosaurus whose parents died and is now fighting the aliens who killed her parents, with the help of the Japanese military and her human friend. possesses great physical strength and much like her father, the ability to fire a steam of flames.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Villains">Villains</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaiju_ouji&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Villains">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Aliens (group 1): a group of bizarre gargoyle aliens serves as the show's antagonists. they were killed and defeated in episode 14. they possess various unnamed guns and missile-shooters, as well as the strange power to resurrect fossilized creatures.
</p><p>Aliens (group 2): a different, more powerful group of fly-like aliens appeared in episodes 15-26. These aliens possess more advanced weapons, being able to fire an unidentified yellow paste-like acid from their snouts, and being able to alter their revived creatures' genetic material and turn them into giants.
</p>
