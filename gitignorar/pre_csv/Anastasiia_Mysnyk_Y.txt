<p><b>Anastasiia Mysnyk</b> (born 19 February 1991) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Paralympian" title="Paralympian">Paralympian</a> <a href="/wiki/Track_and_field" title="Track and field">athlete</a> from <a href="/wiki/Ukraine" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a> competing mainly in category <a href="/wiki/T20_(classification)" title="T20 (classification)">T20</a> <a href="/wiki/Shot_put" title="Shot put">shot put</a> events.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> She won the silver medal in her event at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Summer_Paralympics" title="2012 Summer Paralympics">2012 Summer Paralympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>. As well as Paralympic success she has won medals at both World and European level.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IPC_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-IPC-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
