<p><b>Karjaküla</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Populated_places_in_Estonia" title="Populated places in Estonia">small borough</a> (<a href="/wiki/Estonian_language" title="Estonian language">Estonian</a>: <i lang="et">alevik</i>) in <a href="/wiki/L%C3%A4%C3%A4ne-Harju_Parish" title="Lääne-Harju Parish">Lääne-Harju Parish</a>, <a href="/wiki/Harju_County" title="Harju County">Harju County</a>, northern <a href="/wiki/Estonia" title="Estonia">Estonia</a>. It has a population of 387 (as of 1 January 2004).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Russian military leader <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Gannibal" title="Ivan Gannibal">Ivan Gannibal</a> (1735–1801), was born in Karjaküla Manor.
</p>
