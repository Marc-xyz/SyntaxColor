<p><b>Raikia</b> is a small town in <a href="/wiki/Kandhamal_district" title="Kandhamal district">Kandhamal district</a> in the <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">Indian</a> state of <a href="/wiki/Odisha" title="Odisha">Odisha</a>. There are <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hindu" title="Hindu">Hindu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christians" title="Christians">Christian</a> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Catholic" title="Catholic">Catholic</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Baptist" title="Baptist">Baptist</a>), and a good population of the tribe (<a href="/wiki/Khonds" title="Khonds">Kandha</a>). It is known as the business capital of Kandhamal district. The town has many educational institutions like <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Jivan_Jyoti_Mahavidyalaya&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Jivan Jyoti Mahavidyalaya (page does not exist)">Jivan Jyoti Mahavidyalaya</a> (where BA, BSc are the courses you can opt for), girls high school St. Catherine's Girls High School (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Co-education" title="Co-education">co-education</a> till std 5 and <a href="/wiki/Unisex" title="Unisex">unisex</a> education after words), Bijay High School (boys high school), Kanyashram (Govt. Residential Girls High School),  Saraswati sishu bidya mandir and many more. There are few very good hospitals like Govt. Hospital and Mission Hospital.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Raikia&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It is located 227 km from <a href="/wiki/Bhubaneswar" title="Bhubaneswar">Bhubaneshwar</a>, the capital of Odisha and 147 km from <a href="/wiki/Berhampur" title="Berhampur">Berhampur</a>, the major city of South Odisha. The nearest railway station is <a href="/wiki/Rairakhol" title="Rairakhol">Rairakhol</a> around 100 km from it. The region around <a href="/wiki/Phulbani" title="Phulbani">Phulbani</a> has a variety of flora and fauna. It is a place surrounded by hills and has a lot of small and big waterfalls around it. The most spectacular of the lot is situated at a distance of about 20 km Mondasur kuti among the local people is situated at a distance of about 18 km from Raikia.
</p>
