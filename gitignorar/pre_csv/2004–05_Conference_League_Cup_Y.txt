<p>The <b>2004–05 Conference League Cup</b>, known as the <b>Carthium Cup</b> for sponsorship reasons,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Season_2004–05_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Season_2004–05-1">[1]</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> tournament for clubs competing in that season's <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Football_Conference" title="Football Conference">Football Conference</a>. It was the first time the <a href="/wiki/Conference_League_Cup" title="Conference League Cup">Conference League Cup</a> had been held since the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=2000%E2%80%9301_Conference_League_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="2000–01 Conference League Cup (page does not exist)">2000–01 season</a>. The competition was won by <a href="/wiki/Woking_F.C." title="Woking F.C.">Woking</a>, who defeated <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stalybridge_Celtic" title="Stalybridge Celtic">Stalybridge Celtic</a> in the final.
</p>
