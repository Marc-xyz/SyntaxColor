<p><b>Waverly</b> is a city in <a href="/wiki/Morgan_County,_Illinois" title="Morgan County, Illinois">Morgan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Illinois" title="Illinois">Illinois</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>. The population was 1,346 at the 2000 census. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/Jacksonville,_Illinois" title="Jacksonville, Illinois">Jacksonville</a> <a href="/wiki/Jacksonville,_Illinois_micropolitan_area" title="Jacksonville, Illinois micropolitan area">Micropolitan Statistical Area</a>.  The town was named after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Waverley_novels" title="Waverley novels">Waverley novels</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sir_Walter_Scott" title="Sir Walter Scott">Sir Walter Scott</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Waverly,_Illinois&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Waverly is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Waverly,_Illinois&amp;params=39_35_34_N_89_57_9_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">39°35′34″N</span> <span class="longitude">89°57′9″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">39.59278°N 89.95250°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">39.59278; -89.95250</span></span></span></a></span> (39.592706, -89.952437).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>According to the 2010 census, Waverly has a total area of 1.03 square miles (2.67 km<sup>2</sup>), all land.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-census-g001_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-census-g001-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>Waverly is the geographical center of the Illinois Community College District #526, which is served by <a href="/wiki/Lincoln_Land_Community_College" title="Lincoln Land Community College">Lincoln Land Community College</a>.
</p>
