<p>The <b>Konawe Utara mine</b> is a large <a href="/wiki/Mining" title="Mining">mine</a> in the east of <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sulawesi" title="Sulawesi">Sulawesi</a>. Konawe Utara represents one of the largest <a href="/wiki/Nickel" title="Nickel">nickel</a> reserve in <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> having estimated reserves of 100 million tonnes of ore grading 2.86% <a href="/wiki/Nickel" title="Nickel">nickel</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Iacobeni_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Iacobeni-1">[1]</a></sup> The 100 million tonnes of ore contains 2.86 million tonnes of nickel metal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Iacobeni_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Iacobeni-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
