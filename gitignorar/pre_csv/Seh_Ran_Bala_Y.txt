<p><b>Seh Ran Bala</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">سه ران بالا</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Seh Rān Bālā</b>; also known as <b>Sarān-e Bālā</b>, <b>Sarān-e ‘Olyā</b>, and <b>Serān</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Mahru_Rural_District" title="Mahru Rural District">Mahru Rural District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Zaz_va_Mahru_District" title="Zaz va Mahru District">Zaz va Mahru District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Aligudarz_County" title="Aligudarz County">Aligudarz County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lorestan_Province" title="Lorestan Province">Lorestan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 35, in 5 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
