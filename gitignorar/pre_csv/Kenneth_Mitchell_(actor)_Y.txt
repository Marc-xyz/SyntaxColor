<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kenneth_Mitchell_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mitchell was born in <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>, Canada. He attended the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Guelph" title="University of Guelph">University of Guelph</a> where he studied <a href="/wiki/Landscape_architecture" title="Landscape architecture">landscape architecture</a> and played for the school soccer team as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Striker_(association_football)" title="Striker (association football)">striker</a>. He attended and worked at Kilcoo Camp in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Minden,_Ontario" title="Minden, Ontario">Minden, Ontario</a>, where he claims to have discovered his love for acting, as well as a passion for the art of interpretive dance.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kenneth_Mitchell_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2004 Mitchell was cast in the film <i><a href="/wiki/Miracle_(2004_film)" title="Miracle (2004 film)">Miracle</a></i> with Kurt Russell, in the role of hockey player <a href="/wiki/Ralph_Cox" title="Ralph Cox">Ralph Cox</a> who was the last player cut from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1980_Mens_Olympic_Hockey_Team" title="1980 Mens Olympic Hockey Team">1980 Mens Olympic Hockey Team</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Mitchell appeared as Eric Green in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">U.S.</a> <a href="/wiki/Television" title="Television">television</a> drama <i><a href="/wiki/Jericho_(2006_TV_series)" title="Jericho (2006 TV series)">Jericho</a></i>, which was cancelled by <a href="/wiki/CBS" title="CBS">CBS</a> in May 2007 but was brought back onto the 2007–08 schedule following a fan campaign to save the show.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>He has appeared in single episodes of <i><a href="/wiki/Flashpoint_(TV_series)" title="Flashpoint (TV series)">Flashpoint</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/CSI:_Miami" title="CSI: Miami">CSI: Miami</a></i>, <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Detroit_187" title="Detroit 187">Detroit 187</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Grey%27s_Anatomy" title="Grey's Anatomy">Grey's Anatomy</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Unit" title="The Unit">The Unit</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Law_%26_Order:_LA" title="Law &amp; Order: LA">Law &amp; Order: LA</a></i>.
</p><p>In 2014, Mitchell was cast in <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC's</a> <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Astronaut_Wives_Club" title="Astronaut Wives Club">Astronaut Wives Club</a></i> television drama series, playing the role of <a href="/wiki/Deke_Slayton" title="Deke Slayton">Deke Slayton</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>He stars in the television advertisements for the <a href="/wiki/Samsung_Galaxy_S4" title="Samsung Galaxy S4">Samsung Galaxy S4</a> and <a href="/wiki/Volkswagen" title="Volkswagen">Volkswagen</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kenneth_Mitchell_(actor)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In May 2006, Mitchell married actress <a href="/wiki/Susan_May_Pratt" title="Susan May Pratt">Susan May Pratt</a>. They have two children, a daughter (born 2007) and a son (born 2012).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
