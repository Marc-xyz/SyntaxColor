	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">2000 Hyndburn Borough Council election</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>

Elections to <a href="/wiki/Hyndburn" title="Hyndburn">Hyndburn</a> Borough Council were held on 4 May 2000.  One third of the council was up for election and the <a href="/wiki/Conservative_Party_(UK)" title="Conservative Party (UK)">Conservative</a> party gained overall control of the council from <a href="/wiki/No_overall_control" title="No overall control">no overall control</a>.
</p><p>After the election, the composition of the council was
</p>

<li><a href="/wiki/Labour_Party_(UK)" title="Labour Party (UK)">Labour</a> 15</li>
<li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Independent_(politician)" title="Independent (politician)">Independent</a> 1</li></ul>
