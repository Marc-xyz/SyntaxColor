<p><b>Jach'a Jawira</b> (<a href="/wiki/Aymara_language" title="Aymara language">Aymara</a> <i>jach'a</i> big, great, <i>jawira</i> river,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> "great river", hispanicized spelling <i>Jachcha Jahuira</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Bolivia" title="Bolivia">Bolivian</a> river east of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poop%C3%B3_Lake" title="Poopó Lake">Poopó</a> Lake in the <a href="/wiki/Potos%C3%AD_Department" title="Potosí Department">Potosí Department</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rafael_Bustillo_Province" title="Rafael Bustillo Province">Rafael Bustillo Province</a>,  <a href="/wiki/Chuquihuta_Municipality" title="Chuquihuta Municipality">Chuqi Uta Municipality</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
