<p><b>Pendle Vale College</b> is a mixed 11 to 16 <a href="/wiki/Comprehensive_school" title="Comprehensive school">comprehensive school</a> in <a href="/wiki/Nelson,_Lancashire" title="Nelson, Lancashire">Nelson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lancashire" title="Lancashire">Lancashire</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Pendle_Vale_College&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The school opened in September 2006, as part of the first wave of <a href="/wiki/Building_Schools_for_the_Future" title="Building Schools for the Future">Building Schools for the Future</a>. It occupied the site of the former <a href="/wiki/Walton_High_School,_Nelson" title="Walton High School, Nelson">Walton High School</a>. The new buildings were completed in September 2008.
</p><p>Pendle Vale College's facilities include facilities for ICT, a sporting centre, rock climbing wall, multi gym and all weather pitches, music studios, and dance and drama suites.
</p>
