<p><b>Mafunzo Football Club</b> is a Tanzanian / Zanzibarian <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> club.
</p><p>The team competes in the <a href="/wiki/Zanzibar_Premier_League" title="Zanzibar Premier League">Zanzibar Premier League</a>.
</p><p>They competed in the CAF Champions League for the first time in 2010.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
