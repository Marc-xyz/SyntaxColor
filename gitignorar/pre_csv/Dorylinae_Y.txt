<p><b>Dorylinae</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Ant" title="Ant">ant</a> <a href="/wiki/Subfamily" title="Subfamily">subfamily</a>, with distributions in both the <a href="/wiki/Old_World" title="Old World">Old World</a> and <a href="/wiki/New_World" title="New World">New World</a>. In 2014, Brady <i>et al.</i> <a href="/wiki/Synonym_(taxonomy)" title="Synonym (taxonomy)">synonymized</a> the previous dorylomorph subfamilies (Aenictinae, Aenictogitoninae, Cerapachyinae, Ecitoninae, and Leptanilloidinae) under  the Dorylinae.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Brady_et_al_2014_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Brady_et_al_2014-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
