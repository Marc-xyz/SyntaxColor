<p><b>Mandil Besar</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">منديل بسر</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Mandīl Besar</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Sara_Rural_District" title="Sara Rural District">Sara Rural District</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Central_District_(Saqqez_County)" title="Central District (Saqqez County)">Central District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Saqqez_County" title="Saqqez County">Saqqez County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kurdistan_Province" title="Kurdistan Province">Kurdistan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. As at the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 5 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
