<p>"<b>Talk to Me</b>" is a 1990 song by American recording artist <a href="/wiki/Anita_Baker" title="Anita Baker">Anita Baker</a>. The song was released as the lead single in support of her "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Platinum_album" title="Platinum album">platinum</a>" selling album, <i><a href="/wiki/Compositions_(album)" title="Compositions (album)">Compositions</a></i>. "Talk to Me" became a top five R&amp;B hit, peaking at number three on <i>Billboard's</i> <a href="/wiki/Hot_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Songs" title="Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Songs">Hot Black Singles</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and number four on Adult Contemporary Songs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
