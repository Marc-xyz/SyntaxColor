<p>It is notable that Jefferson County in Virginia became part of West Virginia as a result of the <a href="/wiki/American_Civil_War" title="American Civil War">American Civil War</a>. Virginia sued West Virginia to regain it, but lost the case before the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_Supreme_Court" title="United States Supreme Court">United States Supreme Court</a> when it was decided in 1871.
