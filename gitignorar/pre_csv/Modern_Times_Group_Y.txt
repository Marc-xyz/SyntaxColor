<p><b>Modern Times Group</b> (<b>MTG</b>) is a digital entertainment company based in <a href="/wiki/Stockholm" title="Stockholm">Stockholm</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sweden" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>. MTG was formed out of the media holdings of investment company <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Investment_AB_Kinnevik" title="Investment AB Kinnevik">Kinnevik</a>, which in 1997 was distributed to the company stockholders. Today MTG operates as a strategic and operational investment holding company managing a unique portfolio including eSports businesses <a href="/wiki/ESL_(company)" title="ESL (company)">ESL</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dreamhack" title="Dreamhack">DreamHack</a>, gaming companies <a href="/wiki/InnoGames" title="InnoGames">InnoGames</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kongregate" title="Kongregate">Kongregate</a> as well as digital network company <a href="/wiki/Zoomin.TV" title="Zoomin.TV">Zoomin.TV</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Kinnevik_AB" title="Kinnevik AB">Kinnevik</a> distributed the MTG shares to its shareholders in 2018<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and in 2019 MTG distributed shares in the newly founded <a href="/wiki/Nordic_Entertainment_Group" title="Nordic Entertainment Group">Nordic Entertainment Group</a> to its shareholders following the strategic transformation into a global digital entertainment company.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Company_structure">Company structure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Modern_Times_Group&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Company structure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Since July 2015, MTG's digital presence was expanded through a number of acquisitions. In 2015 MTG bought 74% of the shares in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Turtle_Entertainment" title="Turtle Entertainment">Turtle Entertainment</a>, a top-tier eSports tournament operator, owning brands such as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Electronic_Sports_League" title="Electronic Sports League">Electronic Sports League</a> (ESL). MTG then acquired 51% of the shares in <a href="/wiki/Zoomin.TV" title="Zoomin.TV">Zoomin.TV</a>, Europe's largest MCN and the 5th largest network on YouTube. Later the same year MTG made the acquisition of 100% of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dreamhack" title="Dreamhack">DreamHack</a>, one of the biggest eSports tournament organisers in the world. In 2016 and 2017 MTG continued its journey by acquiring 35% of a leading German games developing company <a href="/wiki/InnoGames" title="InnoGames">InnoGames</a> and U.S. based games publishing company <a href="/wiki/Kongregate" title="Kongregate">Kongregate</a>. Since the start of its investment operations MTG has made further share acquisitions in the before-mentioned companies.
</p>
