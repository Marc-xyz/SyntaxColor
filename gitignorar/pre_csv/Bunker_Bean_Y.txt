<p><i><b>Bunker Bean</b></i> is a 1936 American black-and-white comedy film adapted from a novel by <a href="/wiki/Harry_Leon_Wilson" title="Harry Leon Wilson">Harry Leon Wilson</a> and the subsequent play adapted by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lee_Wilson_Dodd&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lee Wilson Dodd (page does not exist)">Lee Wilson Dodd</a>. It was directed by William Hamilton and Edward Killy, produced by <a href="/wiki/William_Sistrom" title="William Sistrom">William Sistrom</a>, and starred <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Owen_Davis,_Jr." title="Owen Davis, Jr.">Owen Davis, Jr.</a> as the title character. The cast included <a href="/wiki/Lucille_Ball" title="Lucille Ball">Lucille Ball</a> as Mrs Kelly.
</p>
