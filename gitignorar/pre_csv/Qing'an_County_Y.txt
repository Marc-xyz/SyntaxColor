<p><b>Qing'an County</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">simplified Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">庆安县</span>; <a href="/wiki/Traditional_Chinese_characters" title="Traditional Chinese characters">traditional Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hant">慶安縣</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Qìng'ān Xiàn</span></i>) is a county of west-central <a href="/wiki/Heilongjiang" title="Heilongjiang">Heilongjiang</a> province, People's Republic of China. It is under the jurisdiction of the <a href="/wiki/Prefecture-level_city" title="Prefecture-level city">prefecture-level city</a> of <a href="/wiki/Suihua" title="Suihua">Suihua</a>.
</p>
