<p><b>Diqing Shangri-La Airport</b> (<a href="/wiki/IATA_airport_code" title="IATA airport code">IATA</a>: <b>DIG</b>, <a href="/wiki/ICAO_airport_code" title="ICAO airport code">ICAO</a>: <b>ZPDQ</b>) is an airport serving <a href="/wiki/Shangri-La_City" title="Shangri-La City">Shangri-La City</a>, <a href="/wiki/Diqing_Tibetan_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture">Diqing Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Yunnan_Province" title="Yunnan Province">Yunnan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. The airport does not have any taxiways(other than the one leading to the terminal building), requiring planes landing there to <a href="/wiki/Backtaxi" title="Backtaxi">backtaxi</a> to the terminal building.
</p>
