<p><i><b>Lecithocera omphacias</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Lecithoceridae" title="Lecithoceridae">Lecithoceridae</a>. It was described by Meyrick in 1910. It is found in <a href="/wiki/Sri_Lanka" title="Sri Lanka">Sri Lanka</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Wingspan" title="Wingspan">wingspan</a> is about 12 mm. The forewings are grey, irrorated with dark fuscous. The second discal stigma is represented by a cloudy dark fuscous transverse mark. The hindwings are grey.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
