<p>The <b>2015 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Desert_Diamond_Cup" title="Desert Diamond Cup">Desert Diamond Cup</a></b> was a soccer exhibition featuring five <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Soccer" title="Soccer">soccer</a> teams from <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Soccer" title="Major League Soccer">Major League Soccer</a> and one from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/USL_Premier_Development_League" title="USL Premier Development League">USL Premier Development League</a>, held from February 18 to February 28, 2015. It was the 5th annual <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Desert_Cup" title="Desert Cup">Desert Diamond Cup</a> and was won by <a href="/wiki/Real_Salt_Lake" title="Real Salt Lake">Real Salt Lake</a>.
</p>
