<p><i><b>Lygropia straminea</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Crambidae" title="Crambidae">Crambidae</a>. It was described by <a href="/wiki/George_Hampson" title="George Hampson">George Hampson</a> in 1912.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is found in <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
