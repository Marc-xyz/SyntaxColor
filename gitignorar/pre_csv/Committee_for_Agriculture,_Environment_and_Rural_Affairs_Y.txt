<p>The <b>Committee for Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs</b> was established to advise and assist the Minister for Agriculture and Rural Development, on matters within her responsibility as a Minister. The committee undertakes a scrutiny, policy development and consultation role with respect to the <a href="/wiki/Department_of_Agriculture,_Environment_and_Rural_Affairs" title="Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs">Department of Agriculture, Environment and Rural Affairs</a> and plays a key role in the consideration and development of legislation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Until 2016, the committee was called the Committee for Agriculture and Rural Development.
</p>
