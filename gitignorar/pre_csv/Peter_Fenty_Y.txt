	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Peter Fenty</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>

<div aria-label="Portals" class="noprint portal plainlist tright" role="navigation" style="margin:0.5em 0 0.5em 1em;border:solid #aaa 1px">
<ul style="display:table;box-sizing:border-box;padding:0.1em;max-width:175px;background:#f9f9f9;font-size:85%;line-height:110%;font-style:italic;font-weight:bold">
<li style="display:table-row"><span style="display:table-cell;padding:0.2em;vertical-align:middle;text-align:center"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:P_christianity.svg"><img alt="icon" class="noviewer" data-file-height="360" data-file-width="400" decoding="async" height="28" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/P_christianity.svg/31px-P_christianity.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/P_christianity.svg/47px-P_christianity.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/79/P_christianity.svg/62px-P_christianity.svg.png 2x" width="31"/></a></span><span style="display:table-cell;padding:0.2em 0.2em 0.2em 0.3em;vertical-align:middle"><a href="/wiki/Portal:Christianity" title="Portal:Christianity">Christianity portal</a></span></li></ul></div><p><b>Peter Decourcy Fenty</b> has been a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suffragan_Bishop" title="Suffragan Bishop">Suffragan Bishop</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Anglican_Diocese_of_Toronto" title="Anglican Diocese of Toronto">Anglican Diocese of Toronto</a>, Canada since 2013: he is in charge of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bishop_of_York-Simcoe" title="Bishop of York-Simcoe">York-Simcoe</a> area of the <a href="/wiki/Anglican_Diocese_of_Toronto" title="Anglican Diocese of Toronto">Diocese</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Fenty was born in 1951 in <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a>. He was educated at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_the_West_Indies" title="University of the West Indies">University of the West Indies</a> and ordained in 1975.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> After a <a href="/wiki/Curate" title="Curate">curacy</a> at St Matthias, <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a> he held <a href="/wiki/Incumbent_(ecclesiastical)" title="Incumbent (ecclesiastical)">incumbencies</a> in both <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a> and Canada and <a href="/wiki/Aurora,_Ontario" title="Aurora, Ontario">Aurora</a> until his election as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Suffragan_Bishop" title="Suffragan Bishop">Suffragan Bishop</a> in 2013.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
