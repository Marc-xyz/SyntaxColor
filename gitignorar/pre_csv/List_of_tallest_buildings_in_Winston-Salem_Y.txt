<p>Below is a list of the 10 tallest buildings in <a href="/wiki/Winston-Salem,_North_Carolina" title="Winston-Salem, North Carolina">Winston-Salem</a> above 200 feet. The tallest building in <a href="/wiki/Winston-Salem,_North_Carolina" title="Winston-Salem, North Carolina">Winston-Salem, North Carolina</a> is <a href="/wiki/100_North_Main_Street" title="100 North Main Street">100 North Main Street</a>. The 2nd tallest building in <a href="/wiki/Winston-Salem,_North_Carolina" title="Winston-Salem, North Carolina">Winston-Salem</a> the tallest in <a href="/wiki/Winston-Salem,_North_Carolina" title="Winston-Salem, North Carolina">Winston-Salem</a> from 1966 to 1995 and the tallest building in <a href="/wiki/North_Carolina" title="North Carolina">North Carolina</a> from 1966 to 1971 when <a href="/wiki/Two_Wells_Fargo_Center" title="Two Wells Fargo Center">Two Wells Fargo Center</a> in Charlotte was constructed. 
</p>
