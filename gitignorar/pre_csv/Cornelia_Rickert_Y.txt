<p><b>Cornelia Rickert</b> (born 30 July 1954) is a German <a href="/wiki/Volleyball" title="Volleyball">volleyball</a> player. She competed in the <a href="/wiki/Volleyball_at_the_1976_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_tournament" title="Volleyball at the 1976 Summer Olympics – Women's tournament">women's tournament</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1976_Summer_Olympics" title="1976 Summer Olympics">1976 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
