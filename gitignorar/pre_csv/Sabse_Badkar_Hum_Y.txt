<p><i><b>Sabse Badkar Hum</b></i> is a 2002 <a href="/wiki/Bollywood" title="Bollywood">Bollywood</a> action film directed by K. Ravi.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The film stars <a href="/wiki/Mithun_Chakraborty" title="Mithun Chakraborty">Mithun Chakraborty</a>, Manik Bedi,  <a href="/wiki/Samrat_Mukerji" title="Samrat Mukerji">Samrat Mukerji</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rituparna_Sengupta" title="Rituparna Sengupta">Rituparna Sengupta</a>.
</p>
