	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>1974 Nova Scotia general election</b> was held on 2 April 1974 to elect members of the <a href="/wiki/51st_General_Assembly_of_Nova_Scotia" title="51st General Assembly of Nova Scotia">51st House of Assembly</a> of the Province of <a href="/wiki/Nova_Scotia" title="Nova Scotia">Nova Scotia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. It was won by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Liberal_Party_of_Nova_Scotia" title="Liberal Party of Nova Scotia">Liberal party</a>.
