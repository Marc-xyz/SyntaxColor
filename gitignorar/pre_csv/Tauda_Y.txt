<p><b>Tauda</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar">تاودا</span>‎) is a town in western <a href="/wiki/Eritrea" title="Eritrea">Eritrea</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Location">Location</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Tauda&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Location">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The settlement is located in the <a href="/wiki/Gogne_Subregion" title="Gogne Subregion">Gogne</a> <a href="/wiki/Subregions_of_Eritrea" title="Subregions of Eritrea">subregion</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Gash-Barka_Region" title="Gash-Barka Region">Gash-Barka</a> <a href="/wiki/Regions_of_Eritrea" title="Regions of Eritrea">region</a>. It is situated 6.2 miles northeast of the district town of <a href="/wiki/Gogne" title="Gogne">Gogne</a>, and 8 miles northwest of <a href="/wiki/Barentu,_Eritrea" title="Barentu, Eritrea">Barentu</a> city.
</p><p>Nearby towns and villages include <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Alegada&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Alegada (page does not exist)">Alegada</a> (6.1 nm), Gogne (6.2 nm), <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ili,_Eritrea&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ili, Eritrea (page does not exist)">Ili</a> (6.3 nm), <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Gonye&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Gonye (page does not exist)">Gonye</a> (7.7 nm), <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Dedda&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Dedda (page does not exist)">Dedda</a> (7.8 nm), <a href="/wiki/Mescul" title="Mescul">Mescul</a> (8.2 nm) and <a href="/wiki/Markaughe" title="Markaughe">Markaughe</a> (12.4 nm).
</p><p><br/>
