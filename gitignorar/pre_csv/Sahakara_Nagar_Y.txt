<p><b>Sahakara Nagar</b> is a residential <a href="/wiki/Suburb" title="Suburb">suburb</a> near <a href="/wiki/Bangalore" title="Bangalore">Bangalore</a> , <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> . It is situated on the outskirts of Bangalore. Sahakanagar is formed by Ministry of Communication Employees housing society and has developed to this extent under the efforts of Sahakaranagar Residents welfare association. Ever since the inclusion of 45 new wards to Bangalore city in 2008, Sahakara Nagar comes under the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BBMP" title="BBMP">BBMP</a> (<a href="/wiki/Bruhat_Bengaluru_Mahanagara_Palike" title="Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike">Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike</a>) limits. The locality is accessible via <a href="/wiki/National_Highway_7_(India)" title="National Highway 7 (India)">NH 7</a> on the East.
<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> Sahakara Nagar was formed in the early 1980s as a co-operative society for Telecom and Post &amp; Telegraph employees of the <a href="/wiki/Government_of_India" title="Government of India">Government of India</a>. It has over 8 well maintained parks and two Playgrounds maintained by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BBMP" title="BBMP">BBMP</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Neighbourhood">Neighbourhood</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sahakara_Nagar&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Neighbourhood">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sahakara Nagar comes in parts, under both <a href="/wiki/Byatarayanapura" title="Byatarayanapura">Byatarayanapura</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kodigehalli" title="Kodigehalli">Kodigehalli</a> wards of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/BBMP" title="BBMP">BBMP</a>, and is flanked by <a href="/wiki/Hebbal" title="Hebbal">Hebbal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Thindlu" title="Thindlu">Thindlu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vidyaranyapura" title="Vidyaranyapura">Vidyaranyapura</a>, and Kempapura.
However, Sahakara Nagar is located outside the bounds of Bangalore City.
</p>
