<p>The <b>badminton men's doubles tournament</b> at the <a href="/wiki/2016_Summer_Olympics" title="2016 Summer Olympics">2016 Summer Olympics</a> took place from 11 to 20 August at Riocentro - Pavilion 4. The seeding was decided on 21 July 2016.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
