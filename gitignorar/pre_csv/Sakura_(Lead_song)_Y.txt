<p><i><b>Sakura</b></i> (サクラ) is the twenty-third single by the <a href="/wiki/Japan" title="Japan">Japanese</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hip-hop" title="Hip-hop">hip-hop</a> group <a href="/wiki/Lead_(band)" title="Lead (band)">Lead</a>, released five months after their previous single <i><a href="/wiki/Green_Days/Strings" title="Green Days/Strings">Green Days/Strings</a>,</i> on February 26, 2014. The single performed well on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon</a> charts, taking the #3 slot for the weekly ranking, remaining on the charts for two weeks.
</p><p>The single was released as a standard CD edition, along with three limited edition, two of which were CD+DVD combos, each carrying different content. While every edition contained the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B-side" title="B-side">b-side</a> "With U", each edition harbored a bonus track. The CD+DVD editions not only contained differing content on the CD portions, but also harbored different content on the DVDs.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Information">Information</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sakura_(Lead_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Information">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Sakura</i> <small>(サクラ)</small> is the twenty-third single by the <a href="/wiki/Japanese_people" title="Japanese people">Japanese</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hip-hop" title="Hip-hop">hip-hop</a> <a href="/wiki/Dance_troupe" title="Dance troupe">group</a> <a href="/wiki/Lead_(band)" title="Lead (band)">Lead</a> and first of 2014. The single charted well on the <a href="/wiki/Oricon_Singles_Chart" title="Oricon Singles Chart">Oricon Singles Charts</a>, taking the #3 spot for the week and remaining on the charts for two consecutive weeks.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> This became the group's fifth consecutive single to chart in the top five of the <a href="/wiki/Oricon" title="Oricon">Oricon charts</a>, beginning with <i>Wanna Be With You,</i> which was released two years prior in March of 2012.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>The single was released in four editions, a regular <a href="/wiki/Compact_disc" title="Compact disc">CD</a>, two limited edition CD+DVD combo packs, and a limited CD only edition. The standard CD contained the title track, the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/B-side" title="B-side">coupling track</a> "With U" - which was available on every edition - and the corresponding instrumentals.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The type A CD+DVD housed the two tracks, the bonus track "Akeneiro Somaru Koro Ni" <small>(茜色染まるころに / <i>Around Midnight</i>)</small> and their corresponding instrumentals, while the DVD portion contained the <a href="/wiki/Music_video" title="Music video">music video</a> for "Sakura" and the <a href="/wiki/Making-of" title="Making-of">making video</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The limited type B CD+DVD harbored the bonus track "Just Do It" (stylized as <i>Just do it</i>) on the CD, while carrying the dance version "Sakura" on the DVD, along with the recording document of the song "With U."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The limited CD only version carried the bonus track "Can't Stop Loving You," along with the corresponding instrumental.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p><p>The lyrics to "Sakura" were written by AnDisM, while the composition and arrangement was performed by <a href="/wiki/Composer" title="Composer">musical composer</a> YUKIYOSHI.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> AnDisM also wrote and composed the single's coupling tracks "With U", "Akeneiro Somaru Koro Ni" and "Just do it."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> AnDisM had worked with Lead for their previous song "<a href="/wiki/Green_Days/Strings" title="Green Days/Strings">Green Days</a>," where they wrote both the lyrics and the musical composition.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Packaging">Packaging</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sakura_(Lead_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Packaging">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Sakura</i> was released in four editions, three of which were <a href="/wiki/Special_edition" title="Special edition">limited editions</a>. Along with the standard <a href="/wiki/Compact_disc" title="Compact disc">CD</a>, the single was given two CD+DVD versions with different bonus tracks on the CD portion and differing content on the <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVD</a> portions. The limited CD contained one bonus track. 
</p><p>The regular edition contained the title track and the coupling track "With U," along with both songs' corresponding instrumentals. The single's cover was also the only one where each member was in a separate frame, while a white border on the bottom housed the title. 
</p><p>The type A limited edition harbored both songs, along with the track "Akeneiro Somaru Koro Ni" and the instrumentals. The DVD contained the <a href="/wiki/Music_video" title="Music video">music video</a> for "Sakura" and the video's <a href="/wiki/Making-of" title="Making-of">off-shot</a>. The type B limited editions contained the bonus track "Just do it" coupled with the other tracks. The DVD carried the dance version of "Sakura" and the recording document of "With U." While the type C limited edition did not carry a DVD, it did harbor the bonus track "Can't Stop Loving You."
</p>
