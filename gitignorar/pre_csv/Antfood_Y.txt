<p><b>Antfood</b> is an award-winning creative audio studio with offices in New York, São Paulo, and Amsterdam. Antfood is a collaborative of composers, engineers, producers, and sound designers that create audio for film, advertisements, and interactive installations.
</p><p>The studio is known for their work on the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Academy Awards</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> their multiple collaborations with <a href="/wiki/Buck_(design_company)" title="Buck (design company)">Buck</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and their sound design for <a href="/wiki/Virtual_reality" title="Virtual reality">virtual reality</a> platforms, including <a href="/wiki/Oculus_Rift" title="Oculus Rift">Oculus Rift</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p><b>History</b>
</p><p>Antfood was founded in 2007 in <a href="/wiki/Williamsburg,_Brooklyn" title="Williamsburg, Brooklyn">Williamsburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, by Wilson Brown an artist and creative entrepreneur, and Emmy-nominated songwriter,  composer and producer Polly Hall. 
</p><p>in 2008 Antfood moved from its original Williamsburg location to a 3000 sq. ft. loft in South Williamsburg, Brooklyn, New York City, near the <a href="/wiki/Domino_Sugar_Refinery" title="Domino Sugar Refinery">Domino Sugar Refinery</a> at 221 Jackson Street.
</p><p>In 2008 Sean McGovern joined Antfood as Executive Producer.
</p><p>In 2012, Antfood expanded opened its first overseas location in <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo" title="São Paulo">São Paulo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> at Rua Gonçalo Afonso, 71. Music producer, composer, sound designer and instrumentalist Pedro Botsaris heads up the Sao Paulo location as Executive Creative Director and Partner.
</p><p>In 2017, Antfood opened its second overseas location in <a href="/wiki/Amsterdam" title="Amsterdam">Amsterdam</a>, <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a> at Achtergracht 17-19, 1017 WL. Antfood’s Amsterdam location was launched under the guidance of Pedro Botsaris.  
</p><p><b>Operations</b>
</p><p>Antfood is a privately held company with 32 people on staff worldwide.
