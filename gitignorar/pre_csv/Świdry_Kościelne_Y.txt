<p><b>Świdry Kościelne</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[ˈɕfʲidrɨ kɔɕˈt͡ɕɛlnɛ]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Bia%C5%82a_Piska" title="Gmina Biała Piska">Gmina Biała Piska</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Pisz_County" title="Pisz County">Pisz County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Warmian-Masurian_Voivodeship" title="Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship">Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship</a>, in northern Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (<a href="/wiki/East_Prussia" title="East Prussia">East Prussia</a>).
</p>
