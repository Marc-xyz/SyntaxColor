<p>The <b>Complex of Silesian International Schools</b>, founded in 2007 in <a href="/wiki/Katowice" title="Katowice">Katowice</a>, is an International Centre of University of Cambridge International Examinations (CIE). Prywatne Liceum Ogolnoksztalcace im. Melchiora Wankowicza, which is a part of the Complex, offers the International Baccalaureate (IB) Diploma Programme.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Curriculum">Curriculum</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Complex_of_Silesian_International_Schools&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Curriculum">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Complex offers a curriculum for students aged 3–19 in Cambridge Primary, Checkpoint, IGCSE and IB Diploma Programmes. The language of instruction on all levels is English, but the school strongly emphasises other languages, such as Polish, Spanish and German.
</p>
