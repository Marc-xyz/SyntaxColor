<p><b>Jack Smith</b> (born December 4, 1947) is a former <a href="/wiki/American_football" title="American football">American football</a> <a href="/wiki/Defensive_back" title="Defensive back">defensive back</a> who played one season with the <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_Eagles" title="Philadelphia Eagles">Philadelphia Eagles</a> of the <a href="/wiki/National_Football_League" title="National Football League">National Football League</a>. He was drafted by the Philadelphia Eagles in the sixth round of the <a href="/wiki/1971_NFL_Draft" title="1971 NFL Draft">1971 NFL Draft</a>. He first enrolled at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Memphis" title="University of Memphis">University of Memphis</a> before transferring to <a href="/wiki/Troy_University" title="Troy University">Troy University</a>. Smith attended <a href="/wiki/Irwin_County_High_School" title="Irwin County High School">Irwin County High School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Ocilla,_Georgia" title="Ocilla, Georgia">Ocilla, Georgia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
