		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is an alphabetical list of terrestrial <a href="/wiki/Airport" title="Airport">airports</a>, water aerodromes and <a href="/wiki/Heliport" title="Heliport">heliports</a> in <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. They are listed in the format:
</p>

<p>Airports that are part of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_Airports_System_(Canada)" title="National Airports System (Canada)">National Airports System</a> are <b>emphasized</b>.
