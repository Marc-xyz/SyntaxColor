<p><i><b>Rubus kelloggii</b></i> is a rare North American species of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Brambles" title="Brambles">brambles</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Rosaceae" title="Rosaceae">rose family</a>. It has been found only in the state of <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a> in central United States.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pie_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-pie-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>The genetics of <i>Rubus</i> is extremely complex, making it difficult to decide on which groups should be recognized as species. There are many rare species with limited ranges such as this. Further study is suggested to clarify the taxonomy.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> 
</p>
