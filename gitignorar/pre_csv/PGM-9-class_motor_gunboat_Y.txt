<p>The <b><i>PGM-9</i>-class motor gunboats</b> were a class of 24 gunboats converted for the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Navy" title="United States Navy">United States Navy</a> from 1944-1945, succeeding the <a href="/wiki/PGM-1-class_motor_gunboat" title="PGM-1-class motor gunboat"><i>PGM-1</i>-class motor gunboats</a>.  All 24 <i>PGM-9</i>s were converted from <a href="/wiki/PC-461-class_submarine_chaser" title="PC-461-class submarine chaser"><i>PC-461</i>-class</a> <a href="/wiki/Submarine_chaser" title="Submarine chaser">submarine chasers</a> while still under construction.  The <i>PGM-9</i>s were created to support <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/PT_boats" title="PT boats">PT boats</a> in the Pacific, but were too slow to keep up, and were shifted to support <a href="/wiki/Minesweeping" title="Minesweeping">minesweeping</a> ships instead.
</p>
