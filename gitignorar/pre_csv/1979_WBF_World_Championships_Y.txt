	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">1979 WBF World Championships</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
		<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>1979 WBF World Championships</b> took place in June 1979 in <a href="/wiki/Hangzhou" title="Hangzhou">Hangzhou</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. It was one of the two editions of the world championships organised by the <a href="/wiki/World_Badminton_Federation" title="World Badminton Federation">WBF</a>, which was a rival body of the <a href="/wiki/Badminton_World_Federation" title="Badminton World Federation">Badminton World Federation</a>.
</p>
