		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Vohibato Tapa-kevitsa</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Madagascar" title="Madagascar">Madagascar</a>. In the 23 September 2007 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_Assembly_of_Madagascar" title="National Assembly of Madagascar">National Assembly</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Malagasy_parliamentary_election,_2007" title="Malagasy parliamentary election, 2007">elections</a>, the party won 1 out of 127 seats.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
