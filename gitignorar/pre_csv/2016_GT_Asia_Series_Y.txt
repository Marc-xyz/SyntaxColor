<p>The <b>2016 GT Asia Series</b> is the sixth season of the <a href="/wiki/GT_Asia_Series" title="GT Asia Series">GT Asia Series</a> championship. It began on 14 May at the <a href="/wiki/Korea_International_Circuit" title="Korea International Circuit">Korea International Circuit</a> and came to its conclusion at Shanghai International Circuit on 19-20 October, 2016 after 12 championship rounds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Calendar_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Calendar-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_calendar_and_results">Race calendar and results</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_GT_Asia_Series&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Race calendar and results">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The full calendar for the 2016 season was released on 12 January 2016. The series will consist of 12 championship rounds held in pairs on six circuits across Korea, Thailand, Japan and China. The 3-hour race at <a href="/wiki/Sepang_International_Circuit" title="Sepang International Circuit">Sepang International Circuit</a> will be discontinued after teams voted against it. Also, the non-championship round at the <a href="/wiki/Guia_Circuit" title="Guia Circuit">Circuito da Guia</a> in Macau does not feature in the calendar, now the main event of the <a href="/wiki/Macau_Grand_Prix" title="Macau Grand Prix">FIA GT World Cup</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Calendar_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Calendar-1">[1]</a></sup> The championship would have concluded at the new <a href="/wiki/Zhejiang_International_Circuit" title="Zhejiang International Circuit">Zhejiang International Circuit</a>, but delays in the final stages of construction had seen Motorsport Asia Limited make alternate plans. On September 9, it was decided to return to <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai</a> for the last two rounds, both run on Thursday 20 October.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[8]</a></sup>
