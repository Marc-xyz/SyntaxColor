<p><b>Mongmong-Toto-Maite</b> (<a href="/wiki/Chamorro_language" title="Chamorro language">Chamorro</a>: <i lang="ch"><b>Mong Mong-Totu-Maiti</b></i>) is a municipality in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> territory of <a href="/wiki/Guam" title="Guam">Guam</a> composed of three separate villages east of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hag%C3%A5t%C3%B1a" title="Hagåtña">Hagåtña</a> that experienced development after the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Second_World_War" title="Second World War">Second World War</a>.
</p><p>Mongmong is adjacent to the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Hag%C3%A5t%C3%B1a_Swamp&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Hagåtña Swamp (page does not exist)">Hagåtña Swamp</a>; Toto is situated to the north-east near <a href="/wiki/Barrigada" title="Barrigada">Barrigada</a>; Maite is located on the cliffs overlooking Agana Bay and the <a href="/wiki/Philippine_Sea" title="Philippine Sea">Philippine Sea</a>. The village's population has increased slightly following the island's 2000 census.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
