<p><b>Lipowy Las</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[liˈpɔvɨ ˈlas]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Baranowo" title="Gmina Baranowo">Gmina Baranowo</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Ostro%C5%82%C4%99ka_County" title="Ostrołęka County">Ostrołęka County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Masovian_Voivodeship" title="Masovian Voivodeship">Masovian Voivodeship</a>, in east-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 3 kilometres (2 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Baranowo,_Masovian_Voivodeship" title="Baranowo, Masovian Voivodeship">Baranowo</a>, 24 km (15 mi) north-west of <a href="/wiki/Ostro%C5%82%C4%99ka" title="Ostrołęka">Ostrołęka</a>, and 111 km (69 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Warsaw" title="Warsaw">Warsaw</a>.
</p>
