<p><b>Meike Freitag</b> (born February 7, 1979 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frankfurt_am_Main" title="Frankfurt am Main">Frankfurt am Main</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hessen" title="Hessen">Hessen</a>) is a retired female <a href="/wiki/Swimming_(sport)" title="Swimming (sport)">swimmer</a> from <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>, specialised in the <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_swimming" title="Freestyle swimming">freestyle</a>. A three-time Olympian (1996, 2000 and 2008) she won a total number of three medals (one silver and two bronze) as a member (heat swimmer) of the German women's relay teams. She is the daughter of two-time Olympian and butterfly swimmer <a href="/wiki/Werner_Freitag" title="Werner Freitag">Werner Freitag</a>.
</p>
