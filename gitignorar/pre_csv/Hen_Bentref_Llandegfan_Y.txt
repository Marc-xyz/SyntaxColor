<p><b>Hen Bentref Llandegfan</b>, often referred to by its shortened name <b>the old village</b> locally is a village in the  <a href="/wiki/Community_(Wales)" title="Community (Wales)">community</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cwm_Cadnant" title="Cwm Cadnant">Cwm Cadnant</a>, <a href="/wiki/Anglesey" title="Anglesey">Ynys Môn</a>, Wales, which is 128.9 miles (207.5 km) from <a href="/wiki/Cardiff" title="Cardiff">Cardiff</a> and 207.8 miles (334.3 km) from <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GM_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-GM-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>"Hen Bentref" in <a href="/wiki/Welsh_language" title="Welsh language">Welsh</a> means "the old village".
</p>
