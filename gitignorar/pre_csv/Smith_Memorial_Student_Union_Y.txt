<p><b>Smith Memorial Student Union</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Student_activity_center" title="Student activity center">student union</a> building on the <a href="/wiki/Portland_State_University" title="Portland State University">Portland State University</a> campus in <a href="/wiki/Portland,_Oregon" title="Portland, Oregon">Portland, Oregon</a>, in the United States. It is named after Michael Smith.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
