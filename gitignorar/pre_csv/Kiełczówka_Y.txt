<p><b>Kiełczówka</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[kʲɛu̯ˈt͡ʂufka]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Moszczenica,_%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA_Voivodeship" title="Gmina Moszczenica, Łódź Voivodeship">Gmina Moszczenica</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Piotrk%C3%B3w_County" title="Piotrków County">Piotrków County</a>, <a href="/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA_Voivodeship" title="Łódź Voivodeship">Łódź Voivodeship</a>, in central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Moszczenica,_Piotrk%C3%B3w_County" title="Moszczenica, Piotrków County">Moszczenica</a>, 17 km (11 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Piotrk%C3%B3w_Trybunalski" title="Piotrków Trybunalski">Piotrków Trybunalski</a>, and 32 km (20 mi) south-east of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/%C5%81%C3%B3d%C5%BA" title="Łódź">Łódź</a>.
</p>
