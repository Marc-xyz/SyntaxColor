<p>The <b>women's ski slopestyle</b> competition of the <a href="/wiki/FIS_Freestyle_Ski_and_Snowboarding_World_Championships_2015" title="FIS Freestyle Ski and Snowboarding World Championships 2015">FIS Freestyle Ski and Snowboarding World Championships 2015</a> was held at <a href="/wiki/Sch%C3%B6nberg-Lachtal" title="Schönberg-Lachtal">Kreischberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a> on January 20 (qualifying)  and January 21 (finals). 
15 athletes from 10 countries competed.
</p>
