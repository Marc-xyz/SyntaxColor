<div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">Region in Queensland, Australia</div><table class="infobox vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th class="adr" colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold;font-size: 110%; line-height: 1.3em; background-color:#e6c4fb;"><span class="fn org locality" style="font-size: 110%;">Western Queensland</span><br/><span class="region" style="font-weight:normal; font-size:95%"><a href="/wiki/Queensland" title="Queensland">Queensland</a></span></th></tr><tr><td colspan="2" style="text-align:center"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Qld_region_map_2.PNG"><img alt="Qld region map 2.PNG" data-file-height="1423" data-file-width="1302" decoding="async" height="295" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a2/Qld_region_map_2.PNG/270px-Qld_region_map_2.PNG" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a2/Qld_region_map_2.PNG/405px-Qld_region_map_2.PNG 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a2/Qld_region_map_2.PNG/540px-Qld_region_map_2.PNG 2x" width="270"/></a><div>Western Queensland comprises three regions</div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Western Queensland</b> is split into three regions:
</p>
<ul><li>North West Queensland, often known as <a href="/wiki/Gulf_Country" title="Gulf Country">Gulf Country</a>;</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Central_West_Queensland" title="Central West Queensland">Central West Queensland</a>; and</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/South_West_Queensland" title="South West Queensland">South West Queensland</a>.</li></ul>
<p>All three regions are serviced by the <a href="/wiki/ABC_Western_Queensland" title="ABC Western Queensland">ABC Western Queensland</a> radio station.
</p>
