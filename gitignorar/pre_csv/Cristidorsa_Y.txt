<p><i><b>Cristidorsa</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Lizard" title="Lizard">lizards</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Agamidae" title="Agamidae">Agamidae</a> native to <a href="/wiki/India" title="India">India</a> and <a href="/wiki/Myanmar" title="Myanmar">Myanmar</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-RDB_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-RDB-2">[2]</a></sup> The name <i>Cristidorsa</i> is <a href="/wiki/Latin" title="Latin">Latin</a> for "ridged dorsum", in reference to the ridges on the backs of lizards in this genus. The <a href="/wiki/Common_name" title="Common name">common name</a> <b>ridged dragons</b> was suggested by the authors of the 2018 genus description.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-wang2018_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-wang2018-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Species include:
</p>
<ul><li><i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cristidorsa_otai" title="Cristidorsa otai">Cristidorsa otai</a></i> <span style="font-size:85%;">(<a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Stephen_Mahony&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Stephen Mahony (page does not exist)">Mahony</a>, 2009)</span></li>
<li><i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Cristidorsa_planidorsata" title="Cristidorsa planidorsata">Cristidorsa planidorsata</a></i> <span style="font-size:85%;">(Jerdon, 1870)</span></li></ul>
<p><i><a href="/wiki/Nota_bene" title="Nota bene">Nota bene</a></i>: a <a href="/wiki/Binomial_nomenclature" title="Binomial nomenclature">binomial authority</a> in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than <i>Cristidorsa</i>.
</p>
