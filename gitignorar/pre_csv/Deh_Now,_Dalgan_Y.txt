<p><b>Deh Now</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">دهنو</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Deh-e Now</b>)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Jolgeh-ye_Chah_Hashem_Rural_District" title="Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem Rural District">Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem Rural District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jolgeh-ye_Chah_Hashem_District" title="Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem District">Jolgeh-ye Chah Hashem District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dalgan_County" title="Dalgan County">Dalgan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sistan_and_Baluchestan_Province" title="Sistan and Baluchestan Province">Sistan and Baluchestan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 866, in 143 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
