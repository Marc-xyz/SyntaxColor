<p><b>Predrag Pašić</b> (born 18 October 1958) is a <a href="/wiki/Bosnians" title="Bosnians">Bosnian</a> retired professional <a href="/wiki/Football_player" title="Football player">footballer</a> who played as an <a href="/wiki/Midfielder#Attacking_midfielder" title="Midfielder">attacking midfielder</a> or as a <a href="/wiki/Forward_(association_football)" title="Forward (association football)">Forward</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>During his club career, Pašić played for <a href="/wiki/FK_Sarajevo" title="FK Sarajevo">FK Sarajevo</a>, <a href="/wiki/VfB_Stuttgart" title="VfB Stuttgart">VfB Stuttgart</a> and <a href="/wiki/TSV_1860_Munich" title="TSV 1860 Munich">TSV 1860 Munich</a>. He earned 10 caps for the <a href="/wiki/Yugoslavia_national_football_team" title="Yugoslavia national football team">Yugoslavia national team</a>, scoring one goal, and was a non-playing squad member at the <a href="/wiki/1982_FIFA_World_Cup" title="1982 FIFA World Cup">1982 FIFA World Cup</a>. Pašić won the <a href="/wiki/1984%E2%80%9385_Yugoslav_First_League" title="1984–85 Yugoslav First League">1984–85 Yugoslav First League</a> with Sarajevo.
</p>
