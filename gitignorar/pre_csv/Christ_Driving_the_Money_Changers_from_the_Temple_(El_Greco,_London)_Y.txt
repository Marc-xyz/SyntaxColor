	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en"><i>Christ Driving the Money Changers from the Temple</i> (El Greco, London)</h1>
	
	<div class="mw-body-content" id="bodyContent">
		<div class="noprint" id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>
		<div id="contentSub"></div>
		
		
		
		<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><table class="infobox vevent" style="width:22em;width: 22em; line-height: 1.4em; text-align: left; float:right;"><tbody><tr><th class="summary" colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold;font-size: 125%; font-weight: bold; font-style: italic">Christ Driving the Money Changers from the Temple</th></tr><tr><td colspan="2" style="text-align:center">Spanish: <i>La expulsión de los mercaderes del templo</i></td></tr><tr><td colspan="2" style="text-align:center"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:El_Greco_016.jpg" title="Red-robed man preparing to strike a group of other men."><img alt="Red-robed man preparing to strike a group of other men." data-file-height="4226" data-file-width="5194" decoding="async" height="244" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/El_Greco_016.jpg/300px-El_Greco_016.jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/El_Greco_016.jpg/450px-El_Greco_016.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/El_Greco_016.jpg/600px-El_Greco_016.jpg 2x" width="300"/></a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" style="padding-right:0.65em;">Artist</th><td class="attendee"><a href="/wiki/El_Greco" title="El Greco">El Greco</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" style="padding-right:0.65em;">Year</th><td>1600</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" style="padding-right:0.65em;">Type</th><td class="category"><a href="/wiki/Oil_painting" title="Oil painting">oil painting</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" style="padding-right:0.65em;">Dimensions</th><td>106 cm × 130 cm (42 in × 51 in)</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" style="padding-right:0.65em;">Location</th><td><a href="/wiki/National_Gallery" title="National Gallery">National Gallery</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><i><b>Christ Driving the Money Changers from the Temple</b></i> is a 1600  painting by <a href="/wiki/El_Greco" title="El Greco">El Greco</a>, now in the <a href="/wiki/National_Gallery" title="National Gallery">National Gallery</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-nationalgallery_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-nationalgallery-1">[1]</a></sup> It depicts the <a href="/wiki/Cleansing_of_the_Temple" title="Cleansing of the Temple">Cleansing of the Temple</a>, an event in the <a href="/wiki/Life_of_Christ_in_art" title="Life of Christ in art">Life of Christ</a>.
</p><p>There exist four copies of the painting and also a faithful reproduction in the <a href="/wiki/National_Gallery" title="National Gallery">National Gallery</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, which has recently been considered as authentic by scholars in the field of visual arts.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Goshgarian-Genette_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Goshgarian-Genette-2">[2]</a></sup> Two versions and that other on loan from Madrid are titled <i>Purification of the Temple.</i> The one at the National Gallery in Washington is called <i>Christ Cleansing the Temple.</i>
</p>
