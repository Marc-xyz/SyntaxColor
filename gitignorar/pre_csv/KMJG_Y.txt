<p><b>KMJG</b> was a non-commercial <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Radio_station" title="Radio station">radio station</a> in <a href="/wiki/Homer,_Alaska" title="Homer, Alaska">Homer, Alaska</a>, broadcasting on 88.9 <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Frequency_Modulation" title="Frequency Modulation">FM</a>. KMJG was a <a href="/wiki/Simulcast" title="Simulcast">simulcast</a> of <a href="/wiki/KWJG" title="KWJG">KWJG</a>.
</p><p>The station's license was cancelled by the <a href="/wiki/Federal_Communications_Commission" title="Federal Communications Commission">Federal Communications Commission</a> on May 21, 2019, due to KMJG's extended period of silence.
</p>
