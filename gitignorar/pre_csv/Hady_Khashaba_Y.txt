<p><b>Hady Sherif Khashaba</b> (born 19 December 1972) is an Egyptian former <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who played at both professional and international levels as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hady_Khashaba&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Club_career">Club career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hady_Khashaba&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Club career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Khashaba spent his entire professional career with one club, <a href="/wiki/Al_Ahly_SC" title="Al Ahly SC">Al Ahly</a>, from 1991 to 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hady_Khashaba&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Khashaba made three appearances at the <a href="/wiki/1991_FIFA_World_Youth_Championship" title="1991 FIFA World Youth Championship">1991 FIFA World Youth Championship</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FIFA_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-FIFA-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Khashaba earned 84 caps for the <a href="/wiki/Egypt_national_football_team" title="Egypt national football team">Egyptian senior side</a> between 1992 and 2004, scoring twelve goals.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tarek_Said_profile_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Tarek_Said_profile-3">[3]</a></sup> Khashaba represented them at the <a href="/wiki/1992_Summer_Olympics" title="1992 Summer Olympics">1992 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a>.,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> the <a href="/wiki/1999_FIFA_Confederations_Cup" title="1999 FIFA Confederations Cup">1999 FIFA Confederations Cup</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FIFA_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FIFA-2">[2]</a></sup> and at the <a href="/wiki/Africa_Cup_of_Nations" title="Africa Cup of Nations">Africa Cup of Nations</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1998_African_Cup_of_Nations" title="1998 African Cup of Nations">1998</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2000_African_Cup_of_Nations" title="2000 African Cup of Nations">2000</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/2004_African_Cup_of_Nations" title="2004 African Cup of Nations">2004</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NFT_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-NFT-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Tarek_Said_profile_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Tarek_Said_profile-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
