<p><i><b>Hemibungarus</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Venomous_snake" title="Venomous snake">venomous</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Elapid" title="Elapid">elapid</a> <a href="/wiki/Snake" title="Snake">snakes</a> known commonly as <b>barred coral snakes</b>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution">Distribution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hemibungarus&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Distribution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Hemibungarus</i> species are  endemic to the <a href="/wiki/Philippines" title="Philippines">Philippines</a>.
</p>
