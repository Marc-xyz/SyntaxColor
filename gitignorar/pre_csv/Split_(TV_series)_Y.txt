<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Overview">Overview</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i>Split</i> is created by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ilan_Rozenfeld&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ilan Rozenfeld (page does not exist)">Ilan Rozenfeld</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Shira_Alon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Shira Alon (page does not exist)">Shira Alon</a>. <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Shai_Kapon&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Shai Kapon (page does not exist)">Shai Kapon</a> directs the television series, which is produced by the <a href="/wiki/Dori_Media_Group" title="Dori Media Group">Dori Media Group</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Hot_(Israel)" title="Hot (Israel)">HOT</a> VOD Young channel. <a href="/wiki/Amit_Farkash" title="Amit Farkash">Amit Farkash</a> plays the 15-year-old Ella Rosen, who finds out that she is half human and half vampire. <a href="/wiki/Yon_Tumarkin" title="Yon Tumarkin">Yon Tumarkin</a> supports her as the 600-year-old vampire Leo.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The series became the most popular show at the HOT VOD Young Channel.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Until January 2010 seven million viewers watched the series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <i>Split</i> became also popular outside of Israel. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Boomerang_(channel)" title="Boomerang (channel)">Boomerang</a> Channel bought the series and broadcast <i>Split</i> in <a href="/wiki/South_America" title="South America">South America</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> According to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Televiziyna_Sluzhba_Novyn#Programming_and_current_schedule" title="Televiziyna Sluzhba Novyn">TSN</a> <i>Split</i> was sold to 78 different countries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TSN_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-TSN-5">[5]</a></sup> The first season was filmed within 30 days. They started filming in the middle of February and ended at the beginning of April 2009.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> <i>Split</i> was influenced by the television series <a href="/wiki/Buffy_the_Vampire_Slayer" title="Buffy the Vampire Slayer">Buffy the Vampire Slayer</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/True_Blood" title="True Blood">True Blood</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Stephenie_Meyer" title="Stephenie Meyer">Stephenie Meyers</a> <a href="/wiki/The_Twilight_Saga_(film_series)" title="The Twilight Saga (film series)">Twilight</a> series.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Plot_summary">Plot summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Plot summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="First_Season">First Season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: First Season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The series centers around 15-year-old Ella Rosen who begins high school along with her best friend, Omer Teneh. The school is managed by the stone-hearted Amnon Green, who is secretly the head of "The Order of <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">Blood</a>" - An order which was founded 1000 years ago – its purpose is to cleanse the world of <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">Vampires</a>.
</p><p>During the series there are three major plot lines which are all connected:
</p>
<ul><li><b>First line of action</b> - Tells the story of Ella Rosen (who is involved with all three lines), a confused, introverted teenage girl who is sure that no one who understands her. Ella is a split - half <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">Vampire</a> and half <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">Human</a> and has a major part in a conflict between the two races. A 600-year-old vampire (but with the appearance of a high school student) named Leo is sent after her in intention to help her find out who and what she is and what she is destined to do. A love triangle is created between Ella, Leo and Omer, who is secretly in love with her.</li>
<li><b>Second line of action</b> - Reveals the story of Zohar, Sushi (Moshe) and Guy (Ella's Brother), who represent the school's <a href="/wiki/Fencing" title="Fencing">fencing</a> team. Guy fancies Zohar, daughter of the principal. Eventually she responds to his advances and they become a couple. Together, they try to help Sushi overcome his <a href="/wiki/Stage_fright" title="Stage fright">stage fright</a>, which leads to a love triangle between the three. When Zohar's father <a href="/wiki/Amnesia" title="Amnesia">loses his memory</a>, she has to take her father's place as the head of "The Order of <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">Blood</a>". Sushi stands by her when Guy is bitten and becomes a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">Vampire</a> which conflicts his relationship with Zohar.</li>
<li><b>Third line of action</b> - Reveals the story of <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">Vampires</a>. The Vampires' Prophet (also known as "The <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">Blood</a> Chosen who provides <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> replacement and has the authority to make the decision in arguments) is a split named Ardak, who realizes that his life is about to end and that he has to choose a successor. Phaton, his brother, is about to be crowned, but the coronation is interrupted because of Ardak's <a href="/wiki/Visual_perception" title="Visual perception">vision</a>, which reveals another split, their younger sister, who was believed to be dead. Ardak sends his guardian, Leo, to find her and tell her about her being a split. Phaton is angered for not being crowned and he undermines Ardak by a secret mutiny and does severe actions such as incitement against the Prophet, releases prisoners for his own benefit and murdering "The Vampire Council" members (a council of vampires with the authority to judge and define laws, which has to obey the <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">Blood</a> Chosen's rules).</li></ul>
<p>Nicky (Nurit), is a character who is involved with  all three plot lines. In the first one, Nicky befriends Ella, and is willing to listen to Ella's troubles and fears, which helps Ella deal with what she finds out about herself. In the second plot line, Nicky is the one who proves to Zohar that a <a href="/wiki/War" title="War">war</a> against the <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampires</a> is not the solution, and that it will not stop the vampires' attacks (a result of a shortage of the <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> replacement, following Ardak's death). Plus, Sushi and Nicky become a couple at the end of the season. In the third plot line, Carmel (Leo's past lover) is released by Phaton and possesses Nicky's body, in order to distract Leo from guarding Ella (to overcome Ella, and win the title of the Prophet). Carmel also bites Guy and turns him into a <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampire</a>. Later on Leo gets back on the right track and releases Nicky by vanquishing Carmel.
</p><p>At the end of the season, the <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampires</a> are exposed in front of the school's students and staff, and Ella, who is opened up and bolder changes during the season, is presented as the new prophet, in front of the <a href="/wiki/Vampire" title="Vampire">vampires</a> and <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">humans</a> both, during a <a href="/wiki/Concert" title="Concert">concert</a> in the school. Amnon thinks he won and wants to kill Ella and eliminate the <a href="/wiki/Blood" title="Blood">blood</a> replacement, but backs down after Ella shows him (in a <a href="/wiki/Visual_perception" title="Visual perception">vision</a>) that he should stop the <a href="/wiki/War" title="War">war</a>. According to the <a href="/wiki/Visual_perception" title="Visual perception">vision</a>, Zohar will die if he does not do so.

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Second_Season">Second Season</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Second Season">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The season begins in an event that occurred 1000 years prior season 2 in the Woods of Green. Ardak, is fighting against Lilth the queen of the demons. Lilth is stabbed in her heart by Ardak's Sword and is killed, so all of Lilth's demons vanish. Ardak is letting Lilth's Servant to run away and the Servant revealed to be Dima.
</p><p>1000 years later, a new School year is starting in green high, Ella decides not to move to Ardak's Tabernacle, and wants live her life like a normal teenager, the situation causes clashes between her and Leo. Meanwhile, Dima discovered the body of Lilth in the fountain's cave and discovers the cave is full with demons eggs. during an earthquake one of the eggs hatches and the demon who was inside of it flees away. the target of the demons is to draw vampires' souls create a stone out of it and bring it to their queen so she could create new demons. soon after the demon flees from the cave he arrives to Green high, there he draws a vampire's soul which enrolled as a student at the school (demons automatically draws vampires' souls when a vampire is near them) and takes over his identity. from now on the name of the demon is Adam. Adam is not aware of the fact that he is a demon and he doesn't know how to control his powers. Adam first meets Ella when he tries to attack her and he discovered he can not do so when she's around. Ella gets a premonition in which she brings Adam back to life, which later revealed to be an initiative by Dima, who realizes he could bring Lilith back to life with the help of Ella. Dima's soul is poisoned and Adam which draw it by Dima's demand, dies. Dima is calling Ella to the Cave and she brings Adam back to life. Ella feels there is something different in Adam and takes him under her wing, while trying to figure out what creature he actually is. Dima demands Adam to Ingratiate himself with Ella, so when the time comes he'll ask her to use her powers to revive Lilth- if this won't be done, Adam will die.
</p><p>Leo is heartbroken, because Ella chose to be with Omer instead of being with him, and is slowly dying. He is fired from his job as the prophet's guardian and is trying to make Ella realize he is her true love, not Omer. In the meantime Adam continues to draws vampires' soul and is creating stones out of them in a slow and painful process. Jamon who was a senior in the Vampires' council is now Ella's advisor and the manager of the new nightclub in town (a main location in the season) which is open exclusively to vampires, which prefer to stay away from humans. The humans and the vampires now have secret agreements. According to the agreement the humans will provide blood to the vampires and in exchange the vampires will provide their knowledge to humans.
</p><p>The human who signs on the agreements with the vampires is Amnon Green. Amnon asks Tamar (who was his assistant in the order of blood) to spy for him after Andrea Foox, and old friend of his and a hater of vampires, who own the box of blood, an ancient box which contains the powers of an ancient vampire, and can be opened only with the blood of 10 vampires and one split. Tamar proves Andre she is loyal to him by "shooting" Amnon, but in fact she warned him before that and Amnon is pretending to be in coma in the hospital.
</p><p>Ella and Omer break up during the season and Ella start dating with Leo. Adam Tells Ella he is a demon and she promise him she will protect him. When the vampires discover Adam's true nature they are trying to kill him but Ella saves him. Therefore, Leo opens the box of Blood and is trying to kill Adam with the powers he got. Ella brings Lilith back to life so she could help her to save Adam. Lilth kills Leo after she kissed him and is starting to produce a new species: new creatures which are half vampire, half demon. Although he is being loyal to Lilth, Dima brings Leo back to life. In the School's gym Ella fights against Lilth and is about to lose but then Adam draws Lilth's soul, killing her for good, and dies. the new offspring of Lilth hatched and the humans are shooting on them using archery, destroying all of them.
</p><p>Michael, Ella's foster father, manages to create a new blood replacement for the vampires, and Leo agrees that Ella does not have to move to the tabernacle.
</p><p>Just when everything seems perfect, the last scene of the season shows us one last offspring survived and is standing in the woods of Green, revealing her fangs and demon's nails to the viewers.

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Third_Season:_Split_-_The_True_Story">Third Season: <i>Split</i> - The True Story</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Third Season: Split - The True Story">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The third season of <i>Split</i> is a show within a show telling the story of a young group of actors and actresses which is starring in the successful youth-vampire series <i>Split</i>
</p><p>While filming the second season of <i>Split</i> Yon (who plays Leo in <i>Split</i>) and Amit (who plays Ella in <i>Split</i>) were a couple, Yon turned down an offer of playing the lead-role in a new movie abroad to stay with Amit, Amit thinks she's hunted by ghosts and is freaking out saying Yon is the only one who could save her from the ghosts. Yon, who's confused because of this, breaks up with Amit, Quit his role as Leo in <i>Split</i> and is taking the offer to star in the movie abroad.
</p><p>A year later the production team is already in the preparations process for the third season of <i>Split</i>. Meni, the producer of <i>Split</i> is casting a new guy named Ruby to play Leo instead of Yon. After Ruby is informed he got the part of Leo he is killed in a car accident and some kind of supernatural personality named "Echo" is taking over his body.  Amit who fell in love with Ruby start dating him not knowing he is "Echo".
</p><p>Meanwhile, Yon started to get signs from Ruby's Ghost who tries to warn him about "Echo". Yon Eventually gets fired from the movie and is returning to Israel, and is managed to get a minor role in <i>Split</i>. Yon is using the Help of Gabi, an actors' agent, who was in a relationship with Meni and also used to be the agent of Yon and all the others actors in <i>Split</i> before they left her, because she believes in mysticism and he is asking her to help him to vanish the ghost (not knowing it's Ruby).
</p><p>Meanwhile, Amit is not happy with the fact that Yon returned, thinking he did that only to win her back, but on the other hand,her roommate Maya (who plays Zohar in <i>Split</i>) is secretly in love with Yon and she trying to make him ask her out, but he sees her only as a good friend. Meanwhile, "Echo", in the body of Ruby, kills Gabi who tries to vanish him and uses her to vanish the ghost of Ruby before letting her go. Yon is asking Agam (who plays Carmel in <i>Split</i>) to pretend to be his girlfriend so Amit won't think he came back for he, Agam agrees but is slowly falling in love with him. After the ghost of Ruby vanishes Yon realizes he is still in love with Amit.
</p><p>Yedidia (who plays Omer in <i>Split</i>) is befriend with the new Actress in <i>Split</i>, Eleanor, despite Danny's (who plays Shahar in <i>Split</i>) anger who is in love with her, and Maya decides to take Eleanor under her wing and is moving in with her. Avi (who plays Guy in <i>Split</i>) and Idan (who plays Sushi in <i>Split</i>) are buying a bar and entitled it "Bracula" (bar+Dracula) [main location in the season]. Alma, Avi's sister, start to work in the bar as a bartender and also start dating with Idan. The three find out there is a homeless named Shaul in the basement of the Bar who is telling them he was a professor who did a research about ghosts and is later revealed to work in a police unit in his past.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Vampires">Vampires</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Vampires">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>There are two types of vampires: those who are born vampires and humans who were bitten by vampires. Vampires are described as handsome, very intelligent and talented. According to Amnon, being a vampire is against the laws of nature. Vampires have both strengths and weaknesses, however there are "frozen" vampires (those without special vampiric powers). Also, vampires must drink human blood or "Doll-Bar" to exist.
</p><p><b>Powers</b>
</p>
<ul><li><b>Memory erasure</b> - Vampires can "erase" several minutes of a person's memory. Multiple erasures can cause someone to lose their memory entirely. However, it is possible to regain memories that have been "erased"(e.g. Amnon Grin).</li>
<li><b>Super Speed</b> - Vampires can move at speeds many times faster than that of humans.</li>
<li><b>Telepathy</b> - Vampires can hear the minds of humans and vampires. However, wearing a hat makes a person immune to a vampire's telepathy. Vampires can also sense other vampires.</li>
<li><b>Strength</b> - Vampires possess inhuman strength.</li>
<li><b>Immortality</b> - Vampires can live much longer than humans. They can't grow old.</li></ul>
<p><b>Weaknesses</b>
</p>
<ul><li><b>Silver Daggers</b> - A silver dagger to the heart causes instantaneous death to vampires.</li>
<li><b>Garlic</b> - Suffocates vampires. A clove of garlic in the face of a vampire causes them to evaporate.</li>
<li><b>Rice</b> - Throwing rice at a vampire forces them to stop and count each grain in one breath.</li>
<li><b>Sea salt</b> - Sea salt burns vampire's skin.</li>
<li><b>Mirrors</b> - If a vampire were to look into a mirror, their soul would be imprisoned in it. Breaking the mirror causes the vampire's soul to "break", killing the vampire.</li>
<li><b>Heart break</b> - If vampires have their heart broken due to love, they fade and die.</li>
<li><b>Water</b> - Water weakens vampires, but does not hurt them.</li>
<li><b>Spells</b> - Special spells are designed to protect humans from vampires.</li>
<li><b>Blood lilies</b> - Not so much a weakness as it is a warning to humans. If a vampire is near a Blood Lily, the lily dies (the stem visibly curls), thus revealing vampire to humans (supposedly extinct, the Blood Lily is an endangered flower).</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Concepts">Concepts</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Concepts">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li><b>Split</b> - A being born from a human and a vampire. The Split have Powers as well. One Split is the product of the connection between vampires and humans.</li>
<li><b>Vampire</b> - A non-human creature with special powers and weaknesses.</li>
<li><b>Demon</b> - A non-human with special powers and weaknesses. Hates vampires.</li>
<li><b>The Vampire Council</b> - a group of vampires responsible for maintaining the world of vampires and decide on penalties for vampires who have defied them. They enforce the "Laws of Blood". The Council is shown meeting in a museum.</li>
<li><b>Prophet</b> - Also known as Blood Chosen. The prophet must be a split, and there can only be one at a time. They have the ability to communicate with humans and vampires at the same time. The prophet also has the ability to profess the ability to split. The Prophet is the spiritual leader of all vampires.</li>
<li><b>The Order of Blood</b> - A group of human beings whose sole aim is the destruction of all vampires in the world. The leader of the "Order of the Blood" is the principal of the School Grin, Amnon Grin. Orders must come from the Grin leader. "The Order" was founded 1000 years ago, when a vampire mercilessly attacked the Grin Dynasty, and the Head swore revenge on the vampires.</li>
<li><b>Doll-Bar</b> - A substitute for blood, like human blood. Tevel's father Aradak and Phaton, found the source of the blood substitute. "Doll-Bar" is the source of the existence of vampires, they are weak without it. The effect of the "Doll-Bar" is that it increases red blood cells, but destroys a few minutes. These can cause long term damage in the body of the "Doll-Bar."</li>
<li><b>Tabernacle</b> - The seat of the Prophet, a human person can not enter the tabernacle, in which case the Tabernacle causes an earthquake and  destroyed.</li>
<li><b>Laws of Blood</b> - Are the laws that vampires have to obey. For example, the rule states that if a vampire bites a human, this will result in punishment and imprisonment (usually imprisonment for hundreds of years), even in the killing was in self-defense. After the death of Aradak Chosen Blood, the Council, which among other things, is the legislative authority of the Vampire, adjourned. After the dispersal, the laws were enforced no more.  It was determined that "a vampire" can afford to bite because it is a necessity.</li>
<li><b>Blood Box</b> - A box with the image of power and the world's most powerful vampire. In order to  open the "Blood Box", ten vampires and a Split are required.</li></ul>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ukrainian_Remake">Ukrainian Remake</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Split_(TV_series)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=12" title="Edit section: Ukrainian Remake">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2011 a Ukrainian remake of the Israeli series was filmed. Split (Ukrainian: Спліт, Russian: Сплит) is the first  Ukrainian production about vampires. The series was filmed in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Petersburg" title="St. Petersburg">St. Petersburg</a>. Vlad Lanna is the director of Split. The main actors are Kristina Brodskaya, Artem Krylov, Maksim Salnikov, <a href="/wiki/Igor_Mirkurbanov" title="Igor Mirkurbanov">Igor Mirkurbanov</a> and Mariya Akhmetzyanova.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TSN_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-TSN-5">[5]</a></sup> On 17 October 2011 the first episode was broadcast on TET.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup> The series consists of 40 Episodes, which are 25 minutes long.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup>
</p><p>The Ukrainian series is about the war between humans and vampires which has been ongoing for centuries. The vampire prophet tries to mediate between the humans and vampires. Since the prophet Ardak has become very old he is looking for a successor. He findes the 16-year-old Split Leah. Leah is a shy girl. She is studying arts and doesn't know about the supernatural world. So Ardak sends the 428-year-old vampire Kai as a student to the art college. Kai wants to protect Leah and prepare her for her fate as the vampire prophet. Leah and Kai instantly fall in love with each other. However their love is forbidden by both the human and vampire laws. If they break these laws they will lose all their supernatural powers. So they have to make a difficult choice, should they either listen to their hearts or to their minds.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TSN_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-TSN-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup>
</p>
