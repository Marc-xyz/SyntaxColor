<p>The <b>Romanian Filmmakers Union</b> (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <i lang="ro"><b>Uniunea Cineaştilor din România</b></i>), known as <b>UCIN</b>, is the continuation since 1990 of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Communist_Romania" title="Communist Romania">Communist-era</a> <b>Asociaţia Cineaştilor din România</b> (The Romanian Filmmakers Association).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hist_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-hist-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-en-hist_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-en-hist-2">[2]</a></sup> It is an autonomous, <a href="/wiki/Non-governmental_organization" title="Non-governmental organization">non-governmental</a> body with an elected leadership, concerned with "the protection of the creative and professional activities, of the moral and material interests of the Romanian filmmakers." They award the <i>Premiile Uniunii Cineaştilor din România</i>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-en-hist_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-en-hist-2">[2]</a></sup> much as the U.S. <a href="/wiki/Academy_of_Motion_Picture_Arts_and_Sciences" title="Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences">Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences</a> gives the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Awards" title="Academy Awards">Academy Awards</a>. 
</p><p>There are 850 members (variously full, associated or honorary, Romanian or foreign). Its fourteen associations representing the basic film professions: film directors, directors of photography, film actors, set and costume designers, soundtrack creators, film critics, script-writers, film animators, film editors, film producers, film promoters, chemical engineers, make-up artists, and stuntmen.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-en-hist_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-en-hist-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>They also operate Cinema Studio,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-en-hist_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-en-hist-2">[2]</a></sup> a cinema at Bulevardul Magheru Gheorghe 29, Bucharest.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
