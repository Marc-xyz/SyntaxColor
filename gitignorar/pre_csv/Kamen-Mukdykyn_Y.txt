<p><b>Kamen-Mukdykyn</b> is a small uninhabited <a href="/wiki/Island" title="Island">island</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Sea_of_Okhotsk" title="Sea of Okhotsk">Sea of Okhotsk</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Far_East" title="Russian Far East">Russian Far East</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kamen-Mukdykyn&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Kamen-Mukdykyn lies in <a href="/wiki/Odyan_Bay" title="Odyan Bay">Odyan Bay</a>. It is 280 m south of the mainland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Administratively Kamen-Mukdykyn is within the <a href="/wiki/Magadan_Oblast" title="Magadan Oblast">Magadan Oblast</a>, in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Russian_Federation" title="Russian Federation">Russian Federation</a>.
</p>
