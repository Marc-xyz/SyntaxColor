<p><b>Palo Duro Records</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Independent_record_label" title="Independent record label">independent record label</a> established in <a href="/wiki/2001_in_music" title="2001 in music">2001</a> to focus primarily on <a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a>-based <a href="/wiki/Singer-songwriter" title="Singer-songwriter">singer-songwriters</a> and bands.  The label specializes in musical styles from the <a href="/wiki/Americana_(music)" title="Americana (music)">Americana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Country_music" title="Country music">country music</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alternative_country" title="Alternative country">alternative country</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Texas_music" title="Texas music">Texas music</a> genres.
</p><p>Artists featured on the roster include <a href="/wiki/The_Derailers" title="The Derailers">The Derailers</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jon_Christopher_Davis" title="Jon Christopher Davis">Jon Christopher Davis</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Trent_Summar_%26_The_New_Row_Mob" title="Trent Summar &amp; The New Row Mob">Trent Summar &amp; The New Row Mob</a>,  Ed Burleson, Brian Burns, <a href="/wiki/Eleven_Hundred_Springs" title="Eleven Hundred Springs">Eleven Hundred Springs</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Morrison-Williams&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Morrison-Williams (page does not exist)">Morrison-Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Two_Tons_of_Steel" title="Two Tons of Steel">Two Tons of Steel</a>, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Walt_Wilkins&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Walt Wilkins (page does not exist)">Walt Wilkins</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tommy_Alverson" title="Tommy Alverson">Tommy Alverson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dale_Watson_(singer)" title="Dale Watson (singer)">Dale Watson</a>. The label has also launched a music series, <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Texas_Unplugged&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Texas Unplugged (page does not exist)">Texas Unplugged</a>, which features original acoustic recordings by a variety of artists. 
</p>
