<p><b>Tighe Rock</b> (<span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Tighe_Rock&amp;params=74_26_S_100_4_W_"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">74°26′S</span> <span class="longitude">100°4′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">74.433°S 100.067°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-74.433; -100.067</span></span></span></a></span><span style="font-size: small;"><span id="coordinates"><a href="/wiki/Geographic_coordinate_system" title="Geographic coordinate system">Coordinates</a>: <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Tighe_Rock&amp;params=74_26_S_100_4_W_"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">74°26′S</span> <span class="longitude">100°4′W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">74.433°S 100.067°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-74.433; -100.067</span></span></span></a></span></span></span>) is a rock outcropping along the coastal slope at the west margin of the <a href="/wiki/Hudson_Mountains" title="Hudson Mountains">Hudson Mountains</a>, located 15 nautical miles (28 km) northwest of <a href="/wiki/Mount_Moses" title="Mount Moses">Mount Moses</a>. Mapped by <a href="/wiki/United_States_Geological_Survey" title="United States Geological Survey">United States Geological Survey</a> (USGS) from surveys and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/U.S._Navy" title="U.S. Navy">U.S. Navy</a> air photos, 1960-66. Named by <a href="/wiki/Advisory_Committee_on_Antarctic_Names" title="Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names">Advisory Committee on Antarctic Names</a> (US-ACAN) for <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Robert_F._Tighe&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Robert F. Tighe (page does not exist)">Robert F. Tighe</a>, electrical engineer at <a href="/wiki/Byrd_Station" title="Byrd Station">Byrd Station</a>, 1964-65.
