<p><b>Ang Junyang</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">洪俊扬</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Hóng Jùnyáng</span></i>) (born 17 August 1981) is a <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">Singaporean</a> singer and host.
</p><p>He was a contestant on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Project_SuperStar_2005" title="Project SuperStar 2005">Project SuperStar 2005</a></i>, organized by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MediaCorp" title="MediaCorp">MediaCorp</a>, and was runner-up to the eventual winner <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kelvin_Tan_Wei_Lian" title="Kelvin Tan Wei Lian">Kelvin Tan Wei Lian</a> in the final of the male category. After the competition, he signed a recording contract with <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Universal_Music" title="Universal Music">Universal Music</a>. He has also hosted television programmes such as <i>SuperFunkies</i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Campus_SuperStar" title="Campus SuperStar">Campus SuperStar</a></i> together with the rest of the superstars.
</p><p>His first album <i>《独角兽》</i> was officially released on 26 May 2006.
</p>
