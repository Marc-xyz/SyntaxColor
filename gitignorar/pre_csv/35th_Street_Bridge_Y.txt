<p>The <b>35th Street Bridge</b> crosses the <a href="/wiki/Kanawha_River" title="Kanawha River">Kanawha River</a> in <a href="/wiki/Charleston,_West_Virginia" title="Charleston, West Virginia">Charleston</a>, West Virginia, in the United States. It connects <a href="/wiki/Interstate_64" title="Interstate 64">Interstate 64</a>/<a href="/wiki/Interstate_77" title="Interstate 77">77</a> south
at Exit #98 with <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia_Route_61" title="West Virginia Route 61">West Virginia Route 61</a> in northern <a href="/wiki/Kanawha_City,_West_Virginia" title="Kanawha City, West Virginia">Kanawha City</a>.
</p>
