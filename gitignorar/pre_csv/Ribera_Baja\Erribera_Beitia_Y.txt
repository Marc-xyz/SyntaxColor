<p><b>Ribera Baja/Erribera Beitia</b> (also <a href="/wiki/Basque_language" title="Basque language">Basque</a> alternative <i>Erriberabeitia</i>) is a town and <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipality</a> located in the province of <a href="/wiki/%C3%81lava" title="Álava">Álava</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/Basque_Country_(autonomous_community)" title="Basque Country (autonomous community)">Basque Country</a>, northern <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. The coordinates given point to a location 37 km NW of Granada, not to the Basque country.
</p>
