<p>

<i><b>Flames</b></i> is a 1917 British silent <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Maurice_Elvey" title="Maurice Elvey">Maurice Elvey</a> and starring <a href="/wiki/Margaret_Bannerman" title="Margaret Bannerman">Margaret Bannerman</a>, <a href="/wiki/Owen_Nares" title="Owen Nares">Owen Nares</a> and <a href="/wiki/Edward_O%27Neill_(actor)" title="Edward O'Neill (actor)">Edward O'Neill</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It is based on a novel by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Robert_Smythe_Hichens" title="Robert Smythe Hichens">Robert Hichens</a>. It follows the experiments of a strange <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Occultist" title="Occultist">occultist</a>.
</p>
