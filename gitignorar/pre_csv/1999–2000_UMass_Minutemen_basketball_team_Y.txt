<p>The <b>1999–00 UMass Minutemen basketball team</b> represented the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Massachusetts_Amherst" title="University of Massachusetts Amherst">University of Massachusetts Amherst</a> during the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1999%E2%80%9300_NCAA_Division_I_men%27s_basketball_season" title="1999–00 NCAA Division I men's basketball season">1999–00 NCAA Division I men's basketball season</a>. The Minutemen, led by fourth year head coach <a href="/wiki/Bruiser_Flint" title="Bruiser Flint">Bruiser Flint</a>, played their home games at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/William_D._Mullins_Memorial_Center" title="William D. Mullins Memorial Center">William D. Mullins Memorial Center</a> and are members of the <a href="/wiki/Atlantic_10_Conference" title="Atlantic 10 Conference">Atlantic 10 Conference</a>. They finished the season 17-16, 9-7 in A-10 play to finish in third place.
</p>
