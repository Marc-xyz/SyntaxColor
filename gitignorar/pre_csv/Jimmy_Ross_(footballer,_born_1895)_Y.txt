<p><b>James Donaldson Ross</b> (born 7 March 1895, date of death unknown) was a Scottish professional <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who played for <a href="/wiki/Raith_Rovers_F.C." title="Raith Rovers F.C.">Raith Rovers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tottenham_Hotspur_F.C." title="Tottenham Hotspur F.C.">Tottenham Hotspur</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Joyce_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Joyce-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Football_career">Football career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Jimmy_Ross_(footballer,_born_1895)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Football career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ross joined Tottenham Hotspur from Raith Rovers in 1923. The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Right_back" title="Right back">right back</a> made seven appearances for the <a href="/wiki/White_Hart_Lane" title="White Hart Lane">White Hart Lane</a> club.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
