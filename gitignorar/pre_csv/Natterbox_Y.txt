<p><b>Natterbox</b> is a British telecommunications company, based in <a href="/wiki/Croydon" title="Croydon">Croydon</a>, <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>.  It delivers a global Cloud Telephony Platform which provides end to end telephony services embedded and managed within the <a href="/wiki/Salesforce.com" title="Salesforce.com">Salesforce</a> Platform.
</p><p>The company started its operations in 2009 in <a href="/wiki/Oxted" title="Oxted">Oxted</a>, <a href="/wiki/Surrey" title="Surrey">Surrey</a>. In 2012 <a href="/wiki/Groupon" title="Groupon">Groupon</a>, bought voice services and CRM integration from Natterbox.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  Natterbox was one of two <a href="/wiki/Small_and_medium-sized_enterprises" title="Small and medium-sized enterprises">SMBs</a> that received funding in <a href="/wiki/Croydon" title="Croydon">Croydon</a> from Coast to Capital, a <a href="/wiki/Local_enterprise_partnership" title="Local enterprise partnership">local enterprise partnership</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
