<p><b>Nikolai Yevgrafovich Sokolov</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Николай Евграфович Соколов</span>) (May 12, 1897 in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a> – December 15, 1988 in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Razliv" title="Razliv">Razliv</a>, <a href="/wiki/Leningrad_Oblast" title="Leningrad Oblast">Leningrad Oblast</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Soviet_Union" title="Soviet Union">Soviet</a> football player.
</p>

<ul><li>RSFSR champion: 1920, 1922.</li>
<li>USSR champion: 1920.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="International_career">International career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Nikolai_Sokolov_(footballer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: International career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
