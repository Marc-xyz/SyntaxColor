<p><b>Sutherland</b> is a town with about 2,841 inhabitants in the <a href="/wiki/Northern_Cape" title="Northern Cape">Northern Cape</a> province of <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> It lies in the western <a href="/wiki/Roggeveld_Mountains" title="Roggeveld Mountains">Roggeveld Mountains</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Karoo" title="Karoo">Karoo</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sutherland was founded in 1855 as a church and market town to serve the area's sheep farmers.  By 1872 the town had a population of 138 registered citizens living in 19 houses.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The large <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nederduits_Gereformeerde_Kerk" title="Nederduits Gereformeerde Kerk">Dutch Reformed church</a> in the centre of Sutherland was built in 1899.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p><p>During the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Anglo_Boer_War" title="Anglo Boer War">Anglo Boer War</a> the church was used as a fort by garrisoned British soldiers.  During the war a number of engagements between British and Boer forces occurred in the town.  In one such engagement a force of 250 Boer commandos attacked the local British garrison for 10 hours.  The ruins of a fort can be found on the outskirts of town on the hill called Rebelskop. This was named after this engagement.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Economy">Economy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Economy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Major economic activities include tourism and sheep farming.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The area includes at least twelve registered B&amp;B's, guest houses and guest farms.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> The nearby <a href="/wiki/South_African_Astronomical_Observatory" title="South African Astronomical Observatory">South African Astronomical Observatory</a> also plays a significant role in the town's economy and is a major driver of tourism to the area.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup> The town also has a number of bars, restaurants and an <a href="/wiki/Amateur_astronomy" title="Amateur astronomy">amateur astronomy</a> observatory that service the tourism sector.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>Sutherland has recently gained in popularity, with many <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Capetonian" title="Capetonian">Capetonians</a> buying property in the town and many more visiting on weekends and vacations.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2010)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sutherland's arid climate and remote location 1,450 metres (4,760 ft) above <a href="/wiki/Sea_level" title="Sea level">sea level</a> make its night skies among the world's clearest and darkest. The telescopes of the <a href="/wiki/South_African_Astronomical_Observatory" title="South African Astronomical Observatory">South African Astronomical Observatory</a> are nearby at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;params=32_22_46_S_20_48_38.5_E_type:landmark&amp;title=SAAO"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">32°22′46″S</span> <span class="longitude">20°48′38.5″E</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="vcard"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">32.37944°S 20.810694°E</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">-32.37944; 20.810694</span></span><span style="display:none">﻿ (<span class="fn org">SAAO</span>)</span></span></span></a></span>. These include the <a href="/wiki/Southern_African_Large_Telescope" title="Southern African Large Telescope">Southern African Large Telescope</a> (SALT), the largest single optical telescope in the southern hemisphere.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Climate">Climate</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Climate">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sutherland is the coldest town in South Africa, with an average yearly temperature of 11.3 °C and an average annual minimum temperature of 2.8 °C;<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-coldest_town_9-0"><a href="#cite_note-coldest_town-9">[9]</a></sup> although the farm <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Buffelsfontein&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Buffelsfontein (page does not exist)">Buffelsfontein</a> holds the official lowest temperature record in the country, of −20.1 °C (−4 °F).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> Snowfall is common in winter. <sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (February 2012)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> The coldest temperature recorded in Sutherland was −16.4 °C (2.5 °F) on 12 July 2003.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-coldest_town_9-1"><a href="#cite_note-coldest_town-9">[9]</a></sup> Sutherland has a <a href="/wiki/Semi-arid_climate" title="Semi-arid climate">semi-arid climate</a> (<a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6ppen_climate_classification" title="Köppen climate classification">Köppen climate classification</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Semi-arid_climate#Cold_Semi-arid_climates" title="Semi-arid climate"><i>BSk</i></a></i>).
</p><p><br/>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notable_residents">Notable residents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sutherland,_Northern_Cape&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Notable residents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
<ul><li><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/NP_van_Wyk_Louw" title="NP van Wyk Louw">NP van Wyk Louw</a>, famous Afrikaner poet and elder brother of WEG Louw was born in Sutherland.</li>

<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Dr_Henry_Olivier&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Dr Henry Olivier (page does not exist)">Dr Henry Olivier</a>, chief engineer of the Kariba Dam project and contributing inventor of the <a href="/wiki/Mulberry_harbour" title="Mulberry harbour">Mulberry Harbours</a> was born in Sutherland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Adriaan_Vlok" title="Adriaan Vlok">Adriaan Vlok</a>, national government minister of Law and Order from 1986–1991 was born in Sutherland.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_van_der_Merwe" title="André van der Merwe">André van der Merwe</a>, a famous South African urologist born in Sutherland.</li></ul>
