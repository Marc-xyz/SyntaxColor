<p><b>Kwasówka</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[kfaˈsufka]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Human_settlement" title="Human settlement">settlement</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Jan%C3%B3w,_Podlaskie_Voivodeship" title="Gmina Janów, Podlaskie Voivodeship">Gmina Janów</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Sok%C3%B3%C5%82ka_County" title="Sokółka County">Sokółka County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Podlaskie_Voivodeship" title="Podlaskie Voivodeship">Podlaskie Voivodeship</a>, in north-eastern Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 9 kilometres (6 mi) south of <a href="/wiki/Jan%C3%B3w,_Podlaskie_Voivodeship" title="Janów, Podlaskie Voivodeship">Janów</a>, 18 km (11 mi) west of <a href="/wiki/Sok%C3%B3%C5%82ka" title="Sokółka">Sokółka</a>, and 31 km (19 mi) north of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Bia%C5%82ystok" title="Białystok">Białystok</a>.
</p>
