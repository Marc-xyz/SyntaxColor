Blizzard</b> was a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pediatric_endocrinologist" title="Pediatric endocrinologist">pediatric endocrinologist</a> and a founding member of the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Lawson_Wilkins_Pediatric_Endocrine_Society&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Lawson Wilkins Pediatric Endocrine Society (page does not exist)">Lawson Wilkins Pediatric Endocrine Society</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
