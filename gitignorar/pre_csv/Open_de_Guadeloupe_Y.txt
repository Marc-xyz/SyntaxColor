<p>The <b>Open de Guadeloupe</b> (formerly known as <b>Orange Open Guadeloupe</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Tennis" title="Tennis">tennis</a> <a href="/wiki/List_of_tennis_tournaments" title="List of tennis tournaments">tournament</a> held in <a href="/wiki/Le_Gosier" title="Le Gosier">Le Gosier</a>, <a href="/wiki/Guadeloupe" title="Guadeloupe">Guadeloupe</a> since 2011. The event is part of the <a href="/wiki/ATP_Challenger_Tour" title="ATP Challenger Tour">ATP Challenger Tour</a> and is played on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hard_court" title="Hard court">hard courts</a>.
</p>
