<p><b>Lacarry-Arhan-Charritte-de-Haut</b> (<a href="/wiki/Basque_language" title="Basque language">Basque</a>: <i lang="eu">Lakarri-Arhane-Sarrikotagaine</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Communes_of_France" title="Communes of France">commune</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Pyr%C3%A9n%C3%A9es-Atlantiques" title="Pyrénées-Atlantiques">Pyrénées-Atlantiques</a> <a href="/wiki/Departments_of_France" title="Departments of France">department</a> in south-western <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a>.
</p><p>It is located in the <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_France" title="Provinces of France">former province</a> of <a href="/wiki/Soule" title="Soule">Soule</a>.
