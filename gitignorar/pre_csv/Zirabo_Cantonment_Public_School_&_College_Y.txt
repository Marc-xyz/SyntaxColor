<p><b>Zirabo Cantonment Public School and College</b> (<a href="/wiki/Bengali_language" title="Bengali language">Bengali</a>: <span lang="bn">জিরাবো ক্যান্টনমেন্ট পাবলিক স্কুল ও কলেজ</span>) is situated at <a href="/wiki/Savar_Cantonment" title="Savar Cantonment">Savar Cantonment</a> in Savar, Dhaka, <a href="/wiki/Bangladesh" title="Bangladesh">Bangladesh</a>. The institution is under the supervision of the director of <a href="/wiki/Bangladesh_Army" title="Bangladesh Army">Bangladesh Army</a> Education Corps.A Lieutenant Colonel is the Principal of this institution
