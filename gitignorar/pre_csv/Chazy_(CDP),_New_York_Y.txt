<p><b>Chazy</b> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hamlet_(New_York)" title="Hamlet (New York)">hamlet</a> and <a href="/wiki/Census-designated_place" title="Census-designated place">census-designated place</a> (CDP) in the <a href="/wiki/Chazy,_New_York" title="Chazy, New York">town of Chazy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Clinton_County,_New_York" title="Clinton County, New York">Clinton County</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">New York</a>, United States. The population was 565 at the 2010 census,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_2010_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Census_2010-1">[1]</a></sup> out of a total town population of 4,284.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Chazy_(CDP),_New_York&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The hamlet of Chazy is located in the northeastern part of the town of Chazy on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Little_Chazy_River" title="Little Chazy River">Little Chazy River</a>, 3 miles (5 km) west of where the river flows into <a href="/wiki/Lake_Champlain" title="Lake Champlain">Lake Champlain</a>. <a href="/wiki/U.S._Route_9_in_New_York" title="U.S. Route 9 in New York">U.S. Route 9</a> passes through the center of the hamlet, leading south 14 miles (23 km) to <a href="/wiki/Plattsburgh_(city),_New_York" title="Plattsburgh (city), New York">Plattsburgh</a>, the <a href="/wiki/County_seat" title="County seat">county seat</a>, and north 9 miles (14 km) to the <a href="/wiki/Canada%E2%80%93United_States_border" title="Canada–United States border">Canada–US border</a>. <a href="/wiki/New_York_State_Route_191" title="New York State Route 191">New York State Route 191</a> runs west from Chazy 1 mile (1.6 km) to Exit 41 of <a href="/wiki/Interstate_87_(New_York)" title="Interstate 87 (New York)">Interstate 87</a>, which leads south 173 miles (278 km) to <a href="/wiki/Albany,_New_York" title="Albany, New York">Albany</a>, New York's state capital, and north (via <a href="/wiki/Quebec_Autoroute_15" title="Quebec Autoroute 15">Quebec Autoroute 15</a>) 48 miles (77 km) to <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>.
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the Chazy CDP has a total area of 1.7 square miles (4.4 km<sup>2</sup>), of which 0.02 square miles (0.05 km<sup>2</sup>), or 1.04%, is water.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Census_2010_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Census_2010-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
