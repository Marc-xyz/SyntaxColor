<p>The <i><b>Journal of Dental Research</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Peer_review" title="Peer review">peer-reviewed</a> <a href="/wiki/Medical_journal" title="Medical journal">medical journal</a> that covers all aspects of <a href="/wiki/Dentistry" title="Dentistry">dentistry</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Editor-in-chief" title="Editor-in-chief">editor-in-chief</a> is William V. Giannobile (<a href="/wiki/University_of_Michigan" title="University of Michigan">University of Michigan</a>). It was established in 1919 and is published by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Sage_Publications" title="Sage Publications">Sage Publications</a> on behalf of the  <a href="/wiki/International_Association_for_Dental_Research" title="International Association for Dental Research">International</a> and <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=American_Association_for_Dental_Research&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="American Association for Dental Research (page does not exist)">American Association for Dental Research</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Abstracting_and_indexing">Abstracting and indexing</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Journal_of_Dental_Research&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Abstracting and indexing">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The journal is abstracted and indexed in <a href="/wiki/Scopus" title="Scopus">Scopus</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Science_Citation_Index" title="Science Citation Index">Science Citation Index</a>. According to the <i><a href="/wiki/Journal_Citation_Reports" title="Journal Citation Reports">Journal Citation Reports</a></i>, the journal has a 2015 <a href="/wiki/Impact_factor" title="Impact factor">impact factor</a> of 4.602.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-WoS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
