<p><i><b>Rhinoprora oribates</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> in the family <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Geometridae" title="Geometridae">Geometridae</a>. It is found on <a href="/wiki/Java" title="Java">Java</a>.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Wingspan" title="Wingspan">wingspan</a> is about 26 mm. The forewings are darker and more glossy than in <i><a href="/wiki/Rhinoprora_palpata" title="Rhinoprora palpata">Rhinoprora palpata</a></i>, the green shades almost suppressed except in the lines which bound the basal patch and the broad median band. The hindwings are more glossy and more smoky than in <i>palpata</i>. They are weakly marked.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
