<p><b>Ostaszewo</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[ɔstaˈʂɛvɔ]</a></span> is a village in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_%C5%81ysomice" title="Gmina Łysomice">Gmina Łysomice</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Toru%C5%84_County" title="Toruń County">Toruń County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kuyavian-Pomeranian_Voivodeship" title="Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship">Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship</a>, in north-central Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 9 km (6 mi) north of <a href="/wiki/Toru%C5%84" title="Toruń">Toruń</a>.
</p>
