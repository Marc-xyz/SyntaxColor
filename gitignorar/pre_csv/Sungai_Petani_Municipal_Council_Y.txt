<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Origins_of_the_establishment">Origins of the establishment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sungai_Petani_Municipal_Council&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Origins of the establishment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The early history of the establishment of the Local Government system in <a href="/wiki/Sungai_Petani" title="Sungai Petani">Sungai Petani</a> began in the 1950s, when the Local Councils Ordinance, 1952 was approved and adopted. Then, in 1953, the Lembaga Bandaran Sungai Petani (Sungai Petani Municipal Board) for the administration of the town area was established.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>On August 1, 1975, the Kuala Muda Local Government District Council (Majlis Daerah Kerajaan Tempatan Kuala Muda, MDKT. km) was established to cover the Sungai Lalang, Jeniang, Merbok, Bukit Selambau, Bedong and Gurun.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
On February 1, 1978, the Kuala Muda District Council (Majlis Daerah Kuala Muda, MDKM) was created to replace MDKT. km, the force of Law 124 of the Local Government (Temporary Provisions) Act which was gazetted in accordance with Section 3 of the Local Government Act 1976 (Act 171).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-2"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>This involves the establishment of Local Authorities of the merged Town Board as follows:<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-3"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Sungai Petani Town Council.
Kuala Muda Municipal Board , Kota Kuala Muda Municipal Board , Padang Lembu Municipal Board , Semeling Municipal Board , Tanjung Dawai Municipal Board , and Tanjung Batu Municipal Board .
Sungai Lalang Local Council , Jeniang Local Council , Merbok Local Council , Bukit Selambau Local Council , Bedong Local Council and Gurun Local Council .<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-4"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The Kuala Muda District Council (MDKM) is responsible for administering the whole area of Kuala Muda district in planning, building control, health, development and licensing projects. MDKM once again upgraded to Sungai Petani Municipal Council (Majlis Perbandaran Sungai Petani Kedah, MPSPK) on July 2, 1994 equal to 22 Muharram 1415H and this declaration was executed by the Honourable Tan Sri Dato 'Seri Haji Osman bin Haji Aroff, PSM, SSDK, DHMS, JMN, JP - Chief Minister of Kedah Darul Aman at the time.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-5"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>With this upgrade, MPSPK responsible for the entire development control, licensing, planning, and provision of facilities for the advancement of urban development and prosperity of the urban population living in Sungai Petani and Kuala Muda in general.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-auto_1-6"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
