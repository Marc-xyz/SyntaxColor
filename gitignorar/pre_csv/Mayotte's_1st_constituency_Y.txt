<p>The <b>1st constituency of Mayotte</b> is a French legislative <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Constituency_(France)" title="Constituency (France)">constituency</a> on the island of <a href="/wiki/Mayotte" title="Mayotte">Mayotte</a>.
</p><p>A <a href="/wiki/2018_Mayotte%27s_1st_constituency_by-election" title="2018 Mayotte's 1st constituency by-election">by-election</a> was held in the constituency after the invalidation of the election of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ramlati_Ali&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ramlati Ali (page does not exist)">Ramlati Ali</a> on 19 January 2018.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
It led to the election of <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ramlati_Ali&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ramlati Ali (page does not exist)">Ramlati Ali</a> in the second round.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
