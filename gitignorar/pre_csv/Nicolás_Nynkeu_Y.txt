<p><b>Nicolás Niverge Nynkeu</b> (born 14 December 1982, in <a href="/wiki/Douala" title="Douala">Douala</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Cameroon" title="Cameroon">Cameroonian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">footballer</a> who plays as a <a href="/wiki/Midfielder" title="Midfielder">midfielder</a> for the German club FC Germania Neureut.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
Nynkeu started his career with local club AS Babimbi in 2000, before signing for Bosnian side <a href="/wiki/NK_Kiseljak" title="NK Kiseljak">Kiseljak</a> in 2003. After spending a year as a player at Kiseljak, Nynkeu moved to <a href="/wiki/NK_%C5%BDep%C4%8De" title="NK Žepče">Žepče</a>, where he first started playing professionally. In July 2006 he left for Croatian lower level side <a href="/wiki/NK_Junak_Sinj" title="NK Junak Sinj">Junak Sinj</a>, where he stayed for six months and then had spells with <a href="/wiki/NK_Croatia_Sesvete" title="NK Croatia Sesvete">NK Croatia Sesvete</a> and <a href="/wiki/NK_Hrvatski_Dragovoljac" title="NK Hrvatski Dragovoljac">Hrvatski Dragovoljac</a> before joining <a href="/wiki/NK_Slaven_Belupo" title="NK Slaven Belupo">Slaven Belupo</a> in July 2008. In 2011 Nynkeu moved to <a href="/wiki/NK_Vinogradar" title="NK Vinogradar">Vinogradar</a>.
</p><p>Since early 2017, Nynkeu has been playing for FC Germania Neureut, achieving promotion with them to the eighth tier of <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> football in 2018.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
