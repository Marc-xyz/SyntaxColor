<p><b>John</b> "<b>Red</b>" <b>Beattie</b> (2 October 1906 – 7 January 1981) was a professional <a href="/wiki/Ice_hockey" title="Ice hockey">ice hockey</a> <a href="/wiki/Forward_(ice_hockey)" title="Forward (ice hockey)">forward</a>.
</p><p>Beattie was born in <a href="/wiki/Ibstock" title="Ibstock">Ibstock</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland" title="United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland">United Kingdom</a>, and grew up in <a href="/wiki/Edmonton" title="Edmonton">Edmonton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alberta" title="Alberta">Alberta</a>. He played 335 games in the <a href="/wiki/National_Hockey_League" title="National Hockey League">National Hockey League</a>, for the <a href="/wiki/Boston_Bruins" title="Boston Bruins">Boston Bruins</a>, <a href="/wiki/Detroit_Red_Wings" title="Detroit Red Wings">Detroit Red Wings</a> and <a href="/wiki/New_York_Americans" title="New York Americans">New York Americans</a>.  He played junior hockey in <a href="/wiki/Vancouver" title="Vancouver">Vancouver</a>.
</p>
