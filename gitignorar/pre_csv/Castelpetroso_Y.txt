<p><b>Castelpetroso</b> is a <i><a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">comune</a></i> (municipality) in the <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Isernia" title="Province of Isernia">Province of Isernia</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> region <a href="/wiki/Molise" title="Molise">Molise</a>, located about 25 kilometres (16 mi) west of <a href="/wiki/Campobasso" title="Campobasso">Campobasso</a> and about 10 kilometres (6 mi) southeast of <a href="/wiki/Isernia" title="Isernia">Isernia</a>. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 1,708 and an area of 22.6 square kilometres (8.7 sq mi).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-istat_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-istat-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>Castelpetroso borders the following municipalities: <a href="/wiki/Carpinone" title="Carpinone">Carpinone</a>, <a href="/wiki/Castelpizzuto" title="Castelpizzuto">Castelpizzuto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pettoranello_del_Molise" title="Pettoranello del Molise">Pettoranello del Molise</a>, <a href="/wiki/Santa_Maria_del_Molise" title="Santa Maria del Molise">Santa Maria del Molise</a>.
</p>
