<p><i><b>Heliophanus mirabilis</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Jumping_spider" title="Jumping spider">jumping spider</a> species in the genus <i><a href="/wiki/Heliophanus" title="Heliophanus">Heliophanus</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  It was first described by <a href="/wiki/Wanda_Weso%C5%82owska" title="Wanda Wesołowska">Wanda Wesołowska</a> in 1986 and lives in <a href="/wiki/South_Africa" title="South Africa">South Africa</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
