<p><i><b>Rissoina bruguieri</b></i>, common name <b>Bruguiere's risso</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of minute <a href="/wiki/Sea_snail" title="Sea snail">sea snail</a>, a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Marine_(ocean)" title="Marine (ocean)">marine</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusk" title="Mollusk">mollusk</a> or <a href="/wiki/Micromollusk" title="Micromollusk">micromollusk</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Rissoinidae" title="Rissoinidae">Rissoinidae</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoRMS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-WoRMS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
