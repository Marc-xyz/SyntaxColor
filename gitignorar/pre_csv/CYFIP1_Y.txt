<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Interactions">Interactions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=CYFIP1&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Interactions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>CYFIP1 has been shown to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Protein-protein_interaction" title="Protein-protein interaction">interact</a> with <a href="/wiki/FMR1" title="FMR1">FMR1</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid11438699_5-1"><a href="#cite_note-pmid11438699-5">[5]</a></sup> to the exclusion of <a href="/wiki/FXR1" title="FXR1">FXR1</a> and <a href="/wiki/FXR2" title="FXR2">FXR2</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid11438699_5-2"><a href="#cite_note-pmid11438699-5">[5]</a></sup> It also forms part of the <a href="/wiki/WAVE_regulatory_complex" title="WAVE regulatory complex">WAVE regulatory complex</a> (WRC),<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid21107423_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-pmid21107423-7">[7]</a></sup> and based on the crystal structure of the WRC it is believed CYFIP1 provides the binding site for the complex to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Rac1" title="Rac1">Rac1</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-pmid21844371_8-0"><a href="#cite_note-pmid21844371-8">[8]</a></sup>
