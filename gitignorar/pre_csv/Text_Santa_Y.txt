<p><i><b>Text Santa</b></i> was a charity initiative set up in 2011 by <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> to support UK-based charities during the Christmas period.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> The appeal to the public is to donate money mainly via text donation and profits from merchandise.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> The telethons have been hosted by popular <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_channel)" title="ITV (TV channel)">ITV</a> presenters including <a href="/wiki/Phillip_Schofield" title="Phillip Schofield">Phillip Schofield</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christine_Bleakley" title="Christine Bleakley">Christine Bleakley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ant_%26_Dec" title="Ant &amp; Dec">Ant &amp; Dec</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup> The 2015 appeal show was the last after <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> decided to axe the show to make way for an all-year appeal.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music">Music</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Text_Santa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <i>Text Santa</i> 2015 appeal theme music was created by London-based music production company A-MNEMONIC Music <a class="external text" href="http://www.a-mnemonic.com" rel="nofollow">Official Website</a>.
</p><p>In previous years the theme music that was used was created by British composer <a href="/wiki/Leigh_Haggerwood" title="Leigh Haggerwood">Leigh Haggerwood</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Florin_Street_Band" title="The Florin Street Band">The Florin Street Band</a>, who re-worked his Christmas song "<a href="/wiki/My_Favourite_Time_of_Year" title="My Favourite Time of Year">My Favourite Time of Year</a>" especially for the appeal, creating theme and background music along with various other original compositions. The song – with its sweeping string melodies and critically acclaimed Victorian-themed music video<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> has gained worldwide popularity and was recently performed by the United States Army Band in Washington D.C. Leigh Haggerwood is proud of the songs association with the <i>Text Santa</i> campaign and has pledged to support it each year.
</p><p>"My Favourite Time of Year"  was not used for the 2015 appeal.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Official_single">Official single</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Text_Santa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Official single">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In 2013, the cast of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Big_Reunion" title="The Big Reunion">The Big Reunion</a></i> released a cover of "<a href="/wiki/I_Wish_It_Could_Be_Christmas_Everyday#The_Big_Reunion_version" title="I Wish It Could Be Christmas Everyday">I Wish It Could Be Christmas Everyday</a>", with all proceeds going to the <i>Text Santa</i> charities. The single peaked at 21 in the UK singles charts.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Charities">Charities</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Text_Santa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Charities">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="2014_appeal">2014 appeal</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Text_Santa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: 2014 appeal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In May 2014, it was announced that <i>Text Santa</i> would return for a fourth year  in December 2014. The telethon aired live on 19 December 2014, a couple of weeks after the launch show, hosted by <a href="/wiki/Phillip_Schofield" title="Phillip Schofield">Phillip Schofield</a>.
</p><p>The presenting line-up changed slightly. With Holly Willoughby off on maternity leave, <a href="/wiki/Alesha_Dixon" title="Alesha Dixon">Alesha Dixon</a> joined <a href="/wiki/Paddy_McGuinness" title="Paddy McGuinness">Paddy McGuinness</a> for a segment of the show, while <a href="/wiki/Phillip_Schofield" title="Phillip Schofield">Phillip Schofield</a> was reunited with his former <i><a href="/wiki/Dancing_on_Ice" title="Dancing on Ice">Dancing on Ice</a></i> co-host <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christine_Bleakley" title="Christine Bleakley">Christine Bleakley</a>. <a href="/wiki/Ant_%26_Dec" title="Ant &amp; Dec">Ant &amp; Dec</a> returned to the show as well.
</p><p>On 19 December, the  first part of <i>Text Santa</i> was hosted by <a href="/wiki/Ant_%26_Dec" title="Ant &amp; Dec">Ant &amp; Dec</a>, the second by <a href="/wiki/Phillip_Schofield" title="Phillip Schofield">Phillip Schofield</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christine_Bleakley" title="Christine Bleakley">Christine Bleakley</a>, and the remainder of the show by <a href="/wiki/Paddy_McGuinness" title="Paddy McGuinness">Paddy McGuinness</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alesha_Dixon" title="Alesha Dixon">Alesha Dixon</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-25"><a href="#cite_note-25">[25]</a></sup> It was broadcast from <a href="/wiki/The_London_Studios" title="The London Studios">The London Studios</a>.

<dl><dt>Within other shows</dt></dl>
<ul><li>15p from each vote on <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/I%27m_a_Celebrity...Get_Me_Out_of_Here!_(UK_TV_series)" title="I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here! (UK TV series)">I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!</a></i>, went to the 2015 <i>Text Santa</i> charities.</li>
<li>A <i>Text Santa</i> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Jeremy_Kyle_Show" title="The Jeremy Kyle Show">The Jeremy Kyle Show</a></i> aired at 09:25 on 18 December 2015.</li>
<li>A <i>Text Santa</i> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Judge_Rinder" title="Judge Rinder">Judge Rinder</a></i> aired on 18 December 2015 at 14:00.</li>
<li>A celebrity <i>Text Santa</i> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/1000_Heartbeats" title="1000 Heartbeats">1000 Heartbeats</a></i> aired on 18 December 2015 at 15:00. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Keith_Lemon" title="Keith Lemon">Keith Lemon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tess_Daly" title="Tess Daly">Tess Daly</a> took part in the episode and collectively won £7,500 for <i>Text Santa</i>.</li>
<li>A celebrity <i>Text Santa</i> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Tipping_Point_(game_show)" title="Tipping Point (game show)">Tipping Point</a></i> aired on 18 December 2015 at 16:00 with <a href="/wiki/Mark_Foster_(swimmer)" title="Mark Foster (swimmer)">Mark Foster</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tanni_Grey-Thompson" title="Tanni Grey-Thompson">Tanni Grey-Thompson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chris_Kamara" title="Chris Kamara">Chris Kamara</a> and <a href="/wiki/Bobby_George" title="Bobby George">Bobby George</a>. Together, they raised a combined total of £4,850</li>
<li>A <i>Text Santa</i> celebrity edition of <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/The_Chase_(UK_game_show)" title="The Chase (UK game show)">The Chase</a></i> was recorded in October 2015. The celebrities who took part were <a href="/wiki/Robert_Rinder" title="Robert Rinder">Robert Rinder</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brian_McFadden" title="Brian McFadden">Brian McFadden</a>, <a href="/wiki/Denise_Robertson" title="Denise Robertson">Denise Robertson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andrea_McLean" title="Andrea McLean">Andrea McLean</a>. The chaser was <a href="/wiki/Mark_Labbett" title="Mark Labbett">Mark Labbett</a> who beat the contestants. They still took away £6,000 for the <i>Text Santa</i> charities.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Phillip_Schofield" title="Phillip Schofield">Phillip Schofield</a> appeared in <i>Pip Knit</i> on 1 December 2015. It saw him make guest appearances in every <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> show broadcast from 06:00 until 22:30.</li>
<li>The <i><a href="/wiki/Downton_Abbey" title="Downton Abbey">Downton Abbey</a></i> sketch for <i>Text Santa</i> guest starred <a href="/wiki/Bruce_Forsyth" title="Bruce Forsyth">Bruce Forsyth</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gordon_Ramsay" title="Gordon Ramsay">Gordon Ramsay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gok_Wan" title="Gok Wan">Gok Wan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Brenda_Blethyn" title="Brenda Blethyn">Brenda Blethyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jim_Broadbent" title="Jim Broadbent">Jim Broadbent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michelle_Keegan" title="Michelle Keegan">Michelle Keegan</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-35"><a href="#cite_note-35">[35]</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Warwick_Davis" title="Warwick Davis">Warwick Davis</a> and aired during the telethon on 18 December.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-36"><a href="#cite_note-36">[36]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-37"><a href="#cite_note-37">[37]</a></sup></li>
<li>A special edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Benidorm_(TV_series)" title="Benidorm (TV series)">Benidorm</a></i> for <i>Text Santa</i> starred <a href="/wiki/Joan_Collins" title="Joan Collins">Joan Collins</a> and aired during the <i>Update</i> show on 25 December.</li>
<li>A <i>Coronation Street</i> sketch aired during the live telethon on 18 December. The special edition guest starred <a href="/wiki/Richard_Branson" title="Richard Branson">Richard Branson</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-38"><a href="#cite_note-38">[38]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-39"><a href="#cite_note-39">[39]</a></sup></li>
<li>A celebrity edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Ninja_Warrior_UK" title="Ninja Warrior UK">Ninja Warrior UK</a></i> aired during the telethon on 18 December. The celebrities who took part were <a href="/wiki/Ryan_Thomas" title="Ryan Thomas">Ryan Thomas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimberly_Wyatt" title="Kimberly Wyatt">Kimberly Wyatt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Wright_(TV_personality)" title="Mark Wright (TV personality)">Mark Wright</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marvin_Humes" title="Marvin Humes">Marvin Humes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gemma_Atkinson" title="Gemma Atkinson">Gemma Atkinson</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jayne_Torvill" title="Jayne Torvill">Jayne Torvill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Dean" title="Christopher Dean">Christopher Dean</a>, Philip Olivier, <a href="/wiki/Louise_Hazel" title="Louise Hazel">Louise Hazel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ugo_Monye" title="Ugo Monye">Ugo Monye</a> and <a href="/wiki/Carl_Froch" title="Carl Froch">Carl Froch</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-40"><a href="#cite_note-40">[40]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-41"><a href="#cite_note-41">[41]</a></sup></li>
<li>A special <i>Text Santa</i> edition of <i><a href="/wiki/Big_Star%27s_Little_Star" title="Big Star's Little Star">Big Star's Little Star</a></i> called <i>Big Star's Bigger Star</i> aired during the telethon and was presented by <a href="/wiki/Stephen_Mulhern" title="Stephen Mulhern">Stephen Mulhern</a>. The celebrities who took part were <a href="/wiki/Emma_Willis" title="Emma Willis">Emma Willis</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Shelley_(singer)" title="George Shelley (singer)">George Shelley</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jennie_McAlpine" title="Jennie McAlpine">Jennie McAlpine</a>.</li></ul>

Items for sale included a <i><a href="/wiki/Ninja_Warrior_UK" title="Ninja Warrior UK">Ninja Warrior UK</a></i> foam finger, signed by the presenters and a VIP <i><a href="/wiki/Loose_Women" title="Loose Women">Loose Women</a></i> studio experience.</li></ul>
