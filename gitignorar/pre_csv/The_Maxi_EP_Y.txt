<p><i><b>The Maxi EP</b></i> is an EP released in Japan to introduce the upcoming album <i><a href="/wiki/Clown_in_the_Mirror" title="Clown in the Mirror">Clown in the Mirror</a></i> by the Danish <a href="/wiki/Progressive_metal" title="Progressive metal">progressive metal</a> band <a href="/wiki/Royal_Hunt" title="Royal Hunt">Royal Hunt</a>. The song "Bad Luck" appears only on this release.
</p>
