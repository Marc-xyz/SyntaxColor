<p>"<b>Saahore Baahubali</b>" (English: Glory be to Baahubali) is a <a href="/wiki/Telugu_language" title="Telugu language">Telugu</a> song from the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Telugu_films_of_2017" title="List of Telugu films of 2017">2017 film</a> <a href="/wiki/Baahubali_2:_The_Conclusion" title="Baahubali 2: The Conclusion">Baahubali 2: The Conclusion</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> Sung by <a href="/wiki/Daler_Mehndi" title="Daler Mehndi">Daler Mehndi</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Firstpost_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Firstpost-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> the song is composed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M._M._Keeravani" title="M. M. Keeravani">M. M. Keeravani</a>, with lyrics penned by his father Siva Shakti Datta and <a href="/wiki/Kodi_Ramakrishna" title="Kodi Ramakrishna">Kodi Ramakrishna</a>. Most of the lyrics were composed in <a href="/wiki/Sanskrit" title="Sanskrit">Sanskrit</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>The music video of the track features <a href="/wiki/Prabhas" title="Prabhas">Prabhas</a>, who plays <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Amarendra_Baahubali" title="Amarendra Baahubali">Amarendra Baahubali</a>, the protagonist of the film, in various action sequences.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Firstpost_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-Firstpost-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup> The song also shows the close bound between Amarendra Baahubali and the <i>Rajamatha</i> Sivagami.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NDTV_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-NDTV-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saahore_Baahubali&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The audio of the song was released on 24 March 2017 along with other tracks in the album. The music video of the song was officially released three weeks after the film's release through the YouTube channel of <a href="/wiki/T-Series_(company)" title="T-Series (company)">T-Series</a> Telugu.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-NDTV_7-1"><a href="#cite_note-NDTV-7">[7]</a></sup> The video of the song has received more than 102 million views on YouTube as of 12 March 2018.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p><p>The song was released in Tamil as <i>Bale Bale Bale</i> on 9 April 2017,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-9"><a href="#cite_note-9">[9]</a></sup> in Hindi as <i>Jiyo Re Baahubali on 5 April 2017</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-10"><a href="#cite_note-10">[10]</a></sup> and in Malayalam as <i>Bali Bali Baahubali</i> on 24 April 2017.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-11"><a href="#cite_note-11">[11]</a></sup>
</p><p>On 1 December 2017, Google announced the most streamed list and Saahore Baahubali topped the list of most streamed Indian songs.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-12"><a href="#cite_note-12">[12]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saahore_Baahubali&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><i><a href="/wiki/The_Indian_Express" title="The Indian Express">The Indian Express</a></i> writes, "The song is about people celebrating the legend of Amarendra Baahubali. And it shows Prabhas in a more intense avatar coupled with his matured performance and body language." It also writes, "The theme of SS Rajamouli's film resonates in this uplifting track."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-13"><a href="#cite_note-13">[13]</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Bollywood_Hungama" title="Bollywood Hungama">Bollywood Hungama</a></i> writes, "Watch Baahubali 2's promo Saahore Baaahubali now!"<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-14"><a href="#cite_note-14">[14]</a></sup>
</p>
