<p><b>Kazem-e Mohammad</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa">كاظم محمد</span>‎, also <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Romanize" title="Romanize">Romanized</a> as <b>Kāz̧em-e Moḩammad</b>) is a village in <a href="/wiki/Ahudasht_Rural_District" title="Ahudasht Rural District">Ahudasht Rural District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shavur_District" title="Shavur District">Shavur District</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shush_County" title="Shush County">Shush County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Khuzestan_Province" title="Khuzestan Province">Khuzestan Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. At the 2006 census, its population was 381, in 52 families.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
