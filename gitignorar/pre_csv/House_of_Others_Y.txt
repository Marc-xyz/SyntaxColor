<p><i><b>House of Others</b></i> (<a href="/wiki/Georgian_language" title="Georgian language">Georgian</a>: <span lang="ka" title="Georgian language text">სხვისი სახლი</span>) is a 2016 Georgian <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Drama_film" title="Drama film">drama film</a> directed by <a href="/wiki/Rusudan_Glurjidze" title="Rusudan Glurjidze">Rusudan Glurjidze</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Cineuropa_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Cineuropa-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-hollywoodreporter_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-hollywoodreporter-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
It was selected as the Georgian entry for the <a href="/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Foreign_Language_Film" title="Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film">Best Foreign Language Film</a> at the <a href="/wiki/89th_Academy_Awards" title="89th Academy Awards">89th Academy Awards</a> but it was not nominated.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Georgia_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Georgia-4">[4]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Variety_2_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-Variety_2-5">[5]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-thewrap_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-thewrap-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
