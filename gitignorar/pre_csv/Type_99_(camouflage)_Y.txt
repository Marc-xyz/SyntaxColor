<p><b>Type 99</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Camouflage" title="Camouflage">camouflage</a> pattern used by <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Liberation_Army" title="People's Liberation Army">People's Liberation Army</a> of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" title="People's Republic of China">People's Republic of China</a>.
</p><p>Type 99 has developed many variants throughout its service, each with different designations and colour variants. For example, there are <i>Type 87</i>, <i>Type 95</i>, and <i>Type 03</i> patterns.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang,_China..jpg"><img alt="" class="thumbimage" data-file-height="1500" data-file-width="2250" decoding="async" height="147" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg/220px-Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg/330px-Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/63/Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg/440px-Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang%2C_China..jpg 2x" width="220"/></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a class="internal" href="/wiki/File:Shooting_range_at_1st_Marine_Brigade_in_Zhanjiang,_China..jpg" title="Enlarge"></a></div>U.S. and Chinese Marines shooting Type-95 rifles in Zhanjiang, China</div></div></div>
<p>The main variant is a four-colour <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Woodland_pattern" title="Woodland pattern">woodland pattern</a> with olive, green, yellowish-green and black. Type 99, like many other woodland uniforms with black, has been criticized for its use of this color, but once faded, it becomes dull and subdued, and therefore virtually unnoticeable.
</p><p>Type 99's style imitates that of the older American <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/M81_Woodland" title="M81 Woodland">M81 Woodland</a> camouflage, in the sense it utilizes a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Macropattern" title="Macropattern">macropattern</a> of four colours for concealment, printed in interlocking, elongated and branching blobs. Type 99 uses a yellowish-green colour for its fourth colour, as opposed to the US pattern's khaki. Type 99 is the camouflage pattern for Type 87 Combat &amp; Training Uniform. The camouflage and uniform is replaced by the digital <a href="/wiki/Type_07" title="Type 07"> Type 07 Combat &amp; Training Uniform</a>.
</p>
