<p><i><b>Prionovolva brevis</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sea_snail" title="Sea snail">sea snail</a>, a marine <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusk" title="Mollusk">mollusk</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Ovulidae" title="Ovulidae">Ovulidae</a>, the ovulids, cowry allies or false cowries.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoRMS_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-WoRMS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Distribution">Distribution</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Prionovolva_brevis&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Distribution">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
