<p><b>Rushville</b> is a village in <a href="/wiki/Buchanan_County,_Missouri" title="Buchanan County, Missouri">Buchanan County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Missouri" title="Missouri">Missouri</a>, United States. The population was 303 at the <a href="/wiki/2010_United_States_Census" title="2010 United States Census">2010 census</a>. It is part of the <a href="/wiki/St._Joseph,_Missouri" title="St. Joseph, Missouri">St. Joseph</a>, MO–<a href="/wiki/Kansas" title="Kansas">KS</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/St._Joseph,_Missouri_Metropolitan_Area" title="St. Joseph, Missouri Metropolitan Area">Metropolitan Statistical Area</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rushville,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rushville was laid out in 1847. The village name was derived from the dense growth of rushes nearby.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rushville,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Rushville is located at <span class="plainlinks nourlexpansion"><a class="external text" href="//tools.wmflabs.org/geohack/geohack.php?pagename=Rushville,_Missouri&amp;params=39_35_14_N_95_1_30_W_type:city"><span class="geo-default"><span class="geo-dms" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location"><span class="latitude">39°35′14″N</span> <span class="longitude">95°1′30″W</span></span></span><span class="geo-multi-punct">﻿ / ﻿</span><span class="geo-nondefault"><span class="geo-dec" title="Maps, aerial photos, and other data for this location">39.58722°N 95.02500°W</span><span style="display:none">﻿ / <span class="geo">39.58722; -95.02500</span></span></span></a></span> (39.587324, -95.025131).<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR1_7-0"><a href="#cite_note-GR1-7">[7]</a></sup>
</p><p>According to the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Census_Bureau" title="United States Census Bureau">United States Census Bureau</a>, the village has a total area of 0.25 square miles (0.65 km<sup>2</sup>), all land.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Gazetteer_files_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-Gazetteer_files-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2010_census">2010 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rushville,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2010 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-FactFinder_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-FactFinder-2">[2]</a></sup> of 2010, there were 303 people, 112 households, and 81 families residing in the village. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 1,212.0 inhabitants per square mile (468.0/km<sup>2</sup>). There were 127 housing units at an average density of 508.0 per square mile (196.1/km<sup>2</sup>). The racial makeup of the village was 99.3% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a> and 0.7% from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Race_(U.S._Census)" title="Race (U.S. Census)">other races</a>. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Hispanic_(U.S._Census)" title="Hispanic (U.S. Census)">Hispanic</a> or <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Latino_(U.S._Census)" title="Latino (U.S. Census)">Latino</a> of any race were 0.7% of the population.
</p><p>There were 112 households of which 33.0% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 57.1% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 8.9% had a female householder with no husband present, 6.3% had a male householder with no wife present, and 27.7% were non-families. 21.4% of all households were made up of individuals and 12.5% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.71 and the average family size was 3.10.
</p><p>The median age in the village was 37.8 years. 27.7% of residents were under the age of 18; 6.7% were between the ages of 18 and 24; 24.3% were from 25 to 44; 26.7% were from 45 to 64; and 14.5% were 65 years of age or older. The gender makeup of the village was 50.2% male and 49.8% female.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2000_census">2000 census</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Rushville,_Missouri&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2000 census">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As of the <a href="/wiki/Census" title="Census">census</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-GR2_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-GR2-4">[4]</a></sup> of 2000, there were 280 people, 113 households, and 78 families residing in the town. The <a href="/wiki/Population_density" title="Population density">population density</a> was 1,236.4 people per square mile (470.0/km²). There were 121 housing units at an average density of 534.3 per square mile (203.1/km²). The racial makeup of the town was 99.29% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/White_(U.S._Census)" title="White (U.S. Census)">White</a>, 0.36% <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pacific_Islander_(U.S._Census)" title="Pacific Islander (U.S. Census)">Pacific Islander</a>, and 0.36% from two or more races.
</p><p>There were 113 households out of which 34.5% had children under the age of 18 living with them, 54.0% were <a href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">married couples</a> living together, 7.1% had a female householder with no husband present, and 30.1% were non-families. 25.7% of all households were made up of individuals and 12.4% had someone living alone who was 65 years of age or older. The average household size was 2.48 and the average family size was 2.99.
</p><p>In the town the population was spread out with 27.5% under the age of 18, 9.3% from 18 to 24, 27.5% from 25 to 44, 20.7% from 45 to 64, and 15.0% who were 65 years of age or older. The median age was 36 years. For every 100 females, there were 87.9 males. For every 100 females age 18 and over, there were 93.3 males.
</p><p>The median income for a household in the town was $31,875, and the median income for a family was $36,071. Males had a median income of $26,563 versus $18,839 for females. The <a href="/wiki/Per_capita_income" title="Per capita income">per capita income</a> for the town was $15,147. About 8.0% of families and 11.2% of the population were below the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poverty_line" title="Poverty line">poverty line</a>, including 16.1% of those under the age of eighteen and 4.4% of those sixty five or over.
</p>
