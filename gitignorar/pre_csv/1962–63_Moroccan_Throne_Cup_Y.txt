<p>The <b>1962–63 season</b> of the <a href="/wiki/Moroccan_Throne_Cup" title="Moroccan Throne Cup">Moroccan Throne Cup</a> was the 7th edition of the competition.
</p><p>The teams played one-legged matches. In case of a draw, there was either a penalty shoot-out, or a replay at the opponents' ground.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Kawkab_Marrakech" title="Kawkab Marrakech">Kawkab Marrakech</a> beat <a href="/wiki/Hassania_Agadir" title="Hassania Agadir">Hassania Agadir</a> 3–2 in the final, played at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stade_Mohamed_V" title="Stade Mohamed V">Stade d'honneur</a> in <a href="/wiki/Casablanca" title="Casablanca">Casablanca</a>. <a href="/wiki/Kawkab_Marrakech" title="Kawkab Marrakech">Kawkab Marrakech</a> won the title for the first time in their history.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Tournament">Tournament</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1962%E2%80%9363_Moroccan_Throne_Cup&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Tournament">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The final took place between the two winners of the semi-finals, <a href="/wiki/Kawkab_Marrakech" title="Kawkab Marrakech">Kawkab Marrakech</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hassania_Agadir" title="Hassania Agadir">Hassania Agadir</a>, on 9 June 1963 at the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Stade_Mohamed_V" title="Stade Mohamed V">Stade d'honneur</a> in <a href="/wiki/Casablanca" title="Casablanca">Casablanca</a>. The match was refereed by Mohamed Bellefkih. <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KAC_Marrakech" title="KAC Marrakech">KAC Marrakech</a> faced their southern neighbours, <a href="/wiki/Hassania_Agadir" title="Hassania Agadir">Hassania Agadir</a>. It was the first final for <a href="/wiki/Hassania_Agadir" title="Hassania Agadir">Hassania Agadir</a>, and the second for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/KAC_Marrakech" title="KAC Marrakech">KAC Marrakech</a>. The match featured a notable comeback. <a href="/wiki/Hassania_Agadir" title="Hassania Agadir">Hassania Agadir</a> opened the scoring through <a class="new" class="nowrap">'</span></small>), followed by a goal from Hama (<img alt="Goal" data-file-height="313" data-file-width="313" decoding="async" height="13" </p>
