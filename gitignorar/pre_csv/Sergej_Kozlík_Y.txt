<p><b>Sergej Kozlík</b> (born 27 July 1950 in <a href="/wiki/Bratislava" title="Bratislava">Bratislava</a>)
is a <a href="/wiki/Politics_of_Slovakia" title="Politics of Slovakia">Slovak</a> politician and
<a href="/wiki/Member_of_the_European_Parliament" title="Member of the European Parliament">Member of the European Parliament</a> (MEP)
with the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ludova_strana_-_Hnutie_za_demokraticke_Slovensko" title="Ludova strana - Hnutie za demokraticke Slovensko">Ludova strana - Hnutie za demokraticke Slovensko</a>, member of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Group_of_the_Alliance_of_Liberals_and_Democrats_for_Europe" title="Group of the Alliance of Liberals and Democrats for Europe">ALDE Group</a> in the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament" title="European Parliament">European Parliament</a>.
</p><p>He sits on its <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Committee_on_Budgets" title="Committee on Budgets">Committee on Budgets</a>, and is a substitute for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Committee_on_Economic_and_Monetary_Affairs" title="Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs">Committee on Economic and Monetary Affairs</a> and a member of the Delegation to the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/ACP-EU_Joint_Parliamentary_Assembly" title="ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly">ACP-EU Joint Parliamentary Assembly</a>.
<i>See also:</i> <a href="/wiki/2004_European_Parliament_election_in_Slovakia" title="2004 European Parliament election in Slovakia">2004 European Parliament election in Slovakia</a>
</p>
