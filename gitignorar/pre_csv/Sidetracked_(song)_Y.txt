<p>"<b>Sidetracked</b>" is a single by English <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grime_(music)" title="Grime (music)">grime</a> artist <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Jme_(MC)" title="Jme (MC)">Jme</a>, from his second studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Blam!_(Jme_album)" title="Blam! (Jme album)">Blam!</a></i> (2010). It was released on 21 February 2010 and has peaked to number 147 on the <a href="/wiki/UK_Singles_Chart" title="UK Singles Chart">UK Singles Chart</a>. The song features vocals from <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">British</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Grime_(music)" title="Grime (music)">grime</a> artist <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Wiley_(rapper)" title="Wiley (rapper)">Wiley</a> and was produced by D Solz.
</p>
