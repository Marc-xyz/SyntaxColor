		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Mohammed "Mo" Yasin</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Urdu_language" title="Urdu language">Urdu</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ur"><b>محمد یاسین</b></span>‎) is a Pakistani <a href="/wiki/Squash_(sport)" title="Squash (sport)">squash</a> coach and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Retired" title="Retired">retired</a> squash player from <a href="/wiki/Pakistan" title="Pakistan">Pakistan</a>. One of the leading players in the game in the 1970s, Yasin was a finalist in the 1974 <a href="/wiki/British_Open_Squash_Championships" title="British Open Squash Championships">British Open</a>.Yasin is very famous for beating World champion Jonah Barington and preventing him for equalling Hashim Khan's record of 7 British Open Titles at that time in 1974. He did not play the final due to his ankle injury while playing with Qamar Zaman in the semi-final.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
