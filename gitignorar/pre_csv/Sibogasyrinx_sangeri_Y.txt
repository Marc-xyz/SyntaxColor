<p><i><b>Sibogasyrinx sangeri</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Species" title="Species">species</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sea_snail" title="Sea snail">sea snail</a>, a marine <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Gastropod" title="Gastropod">gastropod</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Mollusc" title="Mollusc">mollusc</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Cochlespiridae" title="Cochlespiridae">Cochlespiridae</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-WoRMS_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-WoRMS-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
