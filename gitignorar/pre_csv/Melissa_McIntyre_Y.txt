<p><b>Melissa Adaleigh McIntyre</b> (born May 31, 1986)  is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Canadian" title="Canadian">Canadian</a> former actress. She is best known for her role as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ashley_Kerwin" title="Ashley Kerwin">Ashley Kerwin</a> on the long-running teen television series <i><a href="/wiki/Degrassi:_The_Next_Generation" title="Degrassi: The Next Generation">Degrassi: The Next Generation</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Melissa_McIntyre&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>McIntyre was a participant in many community theatre plays as a child, including <i><a href="/wiki/The_Wonderful_Wizard_of_Oz" title="The Wonderful Wizard of Oz">The Wizard of Oz</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Secret_Garden" title="The Secret Garden">The Secret Garden</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-www1_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-www1-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 1998, she was cast in another television show, <i>Real Kids, Real Adventures</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-www1_2-1"><a href="#cite_note-www1-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>McIntyre was also a radio actress, as she played the lead character in <i>Disco Does Not Suck.</i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-www1_2-2"><a href="#cite_note-www1-2">[2]</a></sup> She took some time off during 1999 to participate mainly in theatre, but in 2000 McIntyre, then thirteen years old, returned to television, voicing the character "<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/List_of_Redwall_characters#Cornflower" title="List of Redwall characters">Cornflower</a>" in the cartoon series <i><a href="/wiki/Redwall_(TV_series)" title="Redwall (TV series)">Mattimeo: A Tale of Redwall</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-www1_2-3"><a href="#cite_note-www1-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2001, she was cast in <i><a href="/wiki/Degrassi:_The_Next_Generation" title="Degrassi: The Next Generation">Degrassi: The Next Generation</a></i>, a sequel to the popular 1980s series <i><a href="/wiki/Degrassi_High" title="Degrassi High">Degrassi High</a></i>. McIntyre played <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ashley_Kerwin" title="Ashley Kerwin">Ashley Kerwin</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-:0_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-:0-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
