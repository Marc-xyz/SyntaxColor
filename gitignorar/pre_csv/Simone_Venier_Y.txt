<p><b>Simone Venier</b> (born 26 August 1984 in <a href="/wiki/Latina,_Lazio" title="Latina, Lazio">Latina</a>) is an <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Rowing_(sport)" title="Rowing (sport)">rower</a>. He won a silver medal in the <a href="/wiki/Rowing_at_the_2008_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_quadruple_sculls" title="Rowing at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Men's quadruple sculls">men's quadruple sculls</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2008_Summer_Olympics" title="2008 Summer Olympics">2008 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SR_profile_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SR_profile-1">[1]</a></sup> The Olympic rower <a href="/wiki/Annibale_Venier" title="Annibale Venier">Annibale Venier</a> is his father.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
