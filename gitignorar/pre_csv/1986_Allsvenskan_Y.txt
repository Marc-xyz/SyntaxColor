<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="1986_Allsvenskan_play-offs">1986 Allsvenskan play-offs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1986_Allsvenskan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 1986 Allsvenskan play-offs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <b>1986 Allsvenskan play-offs</b> was the fifth edition of the competition. The four best placed teams from Allsvenskan  qualified to the competition. Allsvenskan champions <a href="/wiki/Malm%C3%B6_FF" title="Malmö FF">Malmö FF</a> won the competition and the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Swedish_football_champions" title="List of Swedish football champions">Swedish championship</a> after defeating <a href="/wiki/AIK_Fotboll" title="AIK Fotboll">AIK</a> who finished third in the league.
</p>
