<p>The <b>1965 European Cup Winners' Cup Final</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> match between <a href="/wiki/West_Ham_United_F.C." title="West Ham United F.C.">West Ham United</a> of England and <a href="/wiki/TSV_1860_Munich" title="TSV 1860 Munich">1860 Munich</a> of West Germany. The final was held at <a href="/wiki/Wembley_Stadium_(1923)" title="Wembley Stadium (1923)">Wembley Stadium</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> on 19 May 1965. It was the final match of the <a href="/wiki/1964%E2%80%9365_European_Cup_Winners%27_Cup" title="1964–65 European Cup Winners' Cup">1964–65 European Cup Winners' Cup</a> and the fifth <a href="/wiki/UEFA_Cup_Winners%27_Cup" title="UEFA Cup Winners' Cup">European Cup Winners' Cup</a> final since the competition's inception.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Match">Match</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1965_European_Cup_Winners%27_Cup_Final&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Match">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Summary">Summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1965_European_Cup_Winners%27_Cup_Final&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>West Ham began the game brightly, but despite chances at both ends, there was no score at half-time. The breakthrough came in the 70th minute when <a href="/wiki/Ronnie_Boyce" title="Ronnie Boyce">Ronnie Boyce</a> threaded a pass between two defenders and <a href="/wiki/Alan_Sealey" title="Alan Sealey">Alan Sealey</a> scored from a difficult angle. Two minutes later, a free kick to West Ham was not cleared; <a href="/wiki/Bobby_Moore" title="Bobby Moore">Bobby Moore</a> crossed the ball, Radenkovic failed to collect the ball and Sealey scored a second goal to seal the match for West Ham.
</p>
