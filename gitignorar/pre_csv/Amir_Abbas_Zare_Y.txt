<p><b>Amir Abbas Zare</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="fa"><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C%D8%B1_%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B3_%D8%B2%D8%A7%D8%B1%D8%B9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="امیر عباس زارع (page does not exist)">امیر عباس زارع</a></span>‎) (born 24 December in <a href="/wiki/Tehran" title="Tehran">Tehran</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>) is an Iranian classical musician and composer. 
He is currently living in <a href="/wiki/Cologne" title="Cologne">Cologne</a>, Germany.
</p><p>At the age of 2 he left <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a> with his parents and immigrated to Germany. Later at the age of 6 he began studying the piano, his first instrument.
Since 1993 he began with lessons of the <a href="/wiki/Setar" title="Setar">Setar</a>, which became his main instrument later. He learned the <a href="/wiki/Setar" title="Setar">Setar</a> from the masters <a href="/wiki/Majid_Derakhshani" title="Majid Derakhshani">Majid Derakhshani</a> and <a href="/wiki/Hamid_Motebassem" title="Hamid Motebassem">Hamid Motebassem</a>.
</p><p>He also collected practical &amp; technical experiences from Ostad <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Mommand_Reza_Lotfi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Mommand Reza Lotfi (page does not exist)">Mommand Reza Lotfi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jalal_Zolfonun" title="Jalal Zolfonun">Jalal Zolfonun</a>. About three years later he began to learn the Percussion-Instrument (<a href="/wiki/Daf" title="Daf">DAF</a>) by studying on his own.
Here he collected some practical experiences from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bijan_Kamkar" title="Bijan Kamkar">Bijan Kamkar</a>, the <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Andalibi&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Andalibi (page does not exist)">Andalibi</a> Brothers and Sadegh Taarif.
</p><p>He also plays with the legendary Singer Maestra <a href="/wiki/Sima_Bina" title="Sima Bina">Sima Bina</a>.
</p><p>From 1997 he was professionally involved in music and began performing on various festivals like the well-known "Falun Folk Festival" in Sweden and many many others.
</p><p>Through numerous concerts in various countries, he became as a popular international artists.
</p><p>The Setarist and Composer has already passed through several master classes. 
In 2012, he was responsible for the Film Music of movie <a href="/wiki/The_Physician" title="The Physician">The Physician</a> as a supervisor.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Despite the fact that he grew up in a foreign country he has a strong bound to his roots and the traditional music of his home country.
</p>
