<p>"<b>Heartbreak Hill</b>" is a song co-written and recorded by American country music artist <a href="/wiki/Emmylou_Harris" title="Emmylou Harris">Emmylou Harris</a>.  It was released in December 1988 as the first single from the album <i><a href="/wiki/Bluebird_(Emmylou_Harris_album)" title="Bluebird (Emmylou Harris album)">Bluebird</a></i>.  The song reached number 8 on the <i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i> <a href="/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs" title="Hot Country Songs">Hot Country Singles &amp; Tracks</a> chart.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  The song was written by Harris and <a href="/wiki/Paul_Kennerley" title="Paul Kennerley">Paul Kennerley</a>.
</p>
