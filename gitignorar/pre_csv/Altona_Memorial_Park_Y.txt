<p><b>Altona Memorial Park</b> is a cemetery and crematorium serving the western suburbs of <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a>. It opened in January 1961.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The main gates to the memorial park originally stood at the <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Market,_Melbourne" title="Eastern Market, Melbourne">Eastern Market</a>, Exhibition St, Melbourne.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
