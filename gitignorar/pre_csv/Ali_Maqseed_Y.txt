<p><b>Ali Maqseed</b><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar"><b>علي مقصيد</b></span>‎) is a Kuwaiti football midfielder. He plays Left back or left wing with many other Positions for <a href="/wiki/Al-Arabi_SC_(Kuwait)" title="Al-Arabi SC (Kuwait)">Al Arabi SC</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Kuwaiti_Premier_League" title="Kuwaiti Premier League">VIVA Premier League</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_national_football_team" title="Kuwait national football team">Kuwait national football team</a>.
</p><p>Ali First joined the first-team when he was 18 years old.
</p><p>Maqseed is well known for his Talented Dribbling and speed, due to that he is nicknamed <b> "Al-Janah Al-Ta'ier" </b> (The Flyinf Wing). Ali Himself has rejected all offers from other clubs around the world staying loyal for his club.
</p><p>Ali started playing for the <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_national_football_team" title="Kuwait national football team">Kuwait national football team</a> in 2008, but initially he never got to play a lot.
</p><p>Ali played for Al-Arabi in the <a href="/wiki/2007_AFC_Champions_League" title="2007 AFC Champions League">2007 AFC Champions League</a> group stage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p><p>Maqseed has one son named Haidar.
</p><p>On October 29, 2015 Nominated for <a href="/wiki/Union_of_Arab_Football_Associations" title="Union of Arab Football Associations">UAFA Arabian Footballer of the Year 2015</a> with <a href="/wiki/Mohamed_Salah" title="Mohamed Salah">Mohamed Salah</a><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Asian_Player_of_the_Year" title="Asian Player of the Year">Asian Player of the Year 2015</a>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Professional_career">Professional career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Professional career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Al-Arabi">Al-Arabi</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Al-Arabi">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2005-06">2005-06</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2005-06">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Ali was called up to the first team while he was still young in age (18 Years).Later that season he made his debut for the club vs <a href="/wiki/Al-Sahel_SC" title="Al-Sahel SC">Al-Sahel SC</a> and played 8 other matches later that season. although the club lost <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Al_Kurafi_Cup" title="Al Kurafi Cup">Al-Kurafi Cup Final</a>. He later won the <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Emir_Cup" title="Kuwait Emir Cup">Kuwait Emir Cup</a> after beating <a href="/wiki/Qadsia_SC" title="Qadsia SC">Qadsia SC</a> 2-0,Ali Played his first final in his career giving Ali his First Professional Club title with Al-Arabi.  
</p>

<div class="quotebox-quote quoted left-aligned" style="">What A Debut Season i had with the Club even winning my first Professional title with the Club What a great Honour</div>
<p><cite class="right-aligned" style="">— Ali Maqseed to Club officials</cite>

<p>Maqseed was later named <a href="/wiki/Al-Arabi_SC_(Kuwait)" title="Al-Arabi SC (Kuwait)">Al-Arabi SC best U-23 player of the year</a>. Although he didn't play a lot with the first-team he played most of the season with the reserves in the reserves League. Many other Newspapers started naming him the best dribbler Kuwait would see in years.
</p><p>Before the final few weeks to the end of the season he was in Al-Arabi's <a href="/wiki/2006_AFC_Champions_League" title="2006 AFC Champions League">2006 AFC Champions League</a> Squad but didn't play due to injury.
</p><p>At the end of the season the club extended Maqaseed's contract for 6 more years with the club.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2006-07">2006-07</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2006-07">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After returning from injury Maqseed was the main option for the team as Cruical first-team player.
</p><p>Throughout the season Maqseed was selected for <a href="/wiki/Al-Arabi_SC_(Kuwait)" title="Al-Arabi SC (Kuwait)">Al-Arabi SC best U-23 player of the year</a> again.
</p><p>On February 22, 2007 he played his first <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Crown_Prince_Cup" title="Kuwait Crown Prince Cup">Kuwait Crown Prince Cup Final</a> which Al-Arabi won 1-0 vs <a href="/wiki/Kazma" title="Kazma">Kazma</a> gave him his first Crown Prince trophy and 2nd Trophy with the club.
</p><p>He was later called up for <a href="/wiki/2007_AFC_Champions_League" title="2007 AFC Champions League">2007 AFC Champions League</a> Squad he played his first match against Zawra.<a href="/wiki/2007_AFC_Champions_League" title="2007 AFC Champions League">2007 AFC Champions League</a> group stage.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2007-08">2007-08</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2007-08">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>He scored in 2007-08  <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Emir_Cup" title="Kuwait Emir Cup">Kuwait Emir Cup</a> Final at half time but the game was stopped due to the sheikh died between the game. The game was played again after 1 week Al-Arabi beat <a href="/wiki/Al-Salmiya_SC" title="Al-Salmiya SC">Al-Salmiya SC</a> 2-1 giving him his 3rd title.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="2008-09">2008-09</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ali_Maqseed&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: 2008-09">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the FA announced the new <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_Super_Cup" title="Kuwait Super Cup">Kuwait Super Cup</a> which was played by <a href="/wiki/Al-Arabi_SC_(Kuwait)" title="Al-Arabi SC (Kuwait)">Al-Arabi</a> vs <a href="/wiki/Kuwait_SC" title="Kuwait SC">Kuwait SC</a> the match ended 1-0 and he won his 4th Title in his career.
</p><p>VIVA Premier League team of the week 16 <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p><p>On October 29, 2015 Nominated for <a href="/wiki/Union_of_Arab_Football_Associations" title="Union of Arab Football Associations">UAFA Arabian Footballer of the Year 2015</a> <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-6"><a href="#cite_note-6">[6]</a></sup>
</p>
