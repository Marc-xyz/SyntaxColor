<p><i><b>Christmas...Our Gifts To You</b></i> is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Christmas_album" title="Christmas album">Christmas album</a> by <a href="/wiki/The_Winans" title="The Winans">The Winans</a>, released in 2000 and featuring many members of the <a href="/wiki/Winans_family" title="Winans family">Winans family</a>.
</p>
