<p><i><b>Hairy Bikers</b></i> is an American <a href="/wiki/Reality_television" title="Reality television">reality television</a> series which aired on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/History_(U.S._TV_channel)" title="History (U.S. TV channel)">History Channel</a>. The show was based <a href="/wiki/Hairy_Bikers" title="Hairy Bikers">on the British show of the same name</a> and featured chef Paul Patranella, and his friend Bill Allen, following the original's format as the two traverse the United States on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Motorcycles" title="Motorcycles">motorcycles</a>. Patranella was a former chef for <a href="/wiki/White_House" title="White House">The White House</a>, acting for a time as <a href="/wiki/George_W._Bush" title="George W. Bush">George W. Bush</a>'s personal chef.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>  Allen owns a  motorcycle repair shop in Texas.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
