	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Alina Sorescu</h1>

<p><b>Alina Sorescu</b> (born 14 July 1986 in Bucharest) is a Romanian singer and TV presenter. She is the host of <i>Tonomatul DP2</i> on <a href="/wiki/TVR2" title="TVR2">TVR2</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alina_Sorescu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sorescu won first prize at the Children's Music Festival in Bucharest in 1994. She won it again two years later, beating off nationwide competition. She released her debut album in 1997 at the age of 11, <i>Voi fi o stea</i> ("I'll be a star"), with tracks composed by <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Eugen_Mihaescu" title="Eugen Mihaescu">Eugen Mihaescu</a>. In 1999 she toured Europe, performing in concerts in France, Italy, Belgium and the Netherlands, and studied singing with Professor <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Viorela_Filip&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Viorela Filip (page does not exist)">Viorela Filip</a>. She later studied sociology at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Bucharest" title="University of Bucharest">University of Bucharest</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-agerpres_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-agerpres-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>Sorescu ventured into a career as a television presenter. She began presenting <i>Ploaia de stele</i>, a music competitive show on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/TVR_1" title="TVR 1">TVR 1</a>, which she appeared in along with <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Cosmin_Cernat&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Cosmin Cernat (page does not exist)">Cosmin Cernat</a>. She has since presented <i>Vacanță în stil mare</i>, <i>Ora fără catalog</i>, <i>Portativul piticilor</i>, <i>Miss Plaja</i>, and <i>Matinal de vacanță</i>, the latter with co-presenter <a href="/wiki/Leonard_Miron" title="Leonard Miron">Leonard Miron</a>. In 2013 she released the single "Numai tu". She now hosts <i>Tonomatul DP2</i> on <a href="/wiki/TVR2" title="TVR2">TVR2</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-agerpres_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-agerpres-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Alina_Sorescu&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sorescu is married to Alexandru Ciuc. The couple have a daughter named Elena.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
