<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Football">Football</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Egypt_at_the_1920_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Football">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Football_at_the_1920_Summer_Olympics" title="Football at the 1920 Summer Olympics">Football at the 1920 Summer Olympics</a></div>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Gymnastics">Gymnastics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Egypt_at_the_1920_Summer_Olympics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Gymnastics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable" role="note">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Gymnastics_at_the_1920_Summer_Olympics" title="Gymnastics at the 1920 Summer Olympics">Gymnastics at the 1920 Summer Olympics</a></div>
