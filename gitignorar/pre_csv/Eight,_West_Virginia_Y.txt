<p><b>Eight</b> was an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> located in <a href="/wiki/McDowell_County,_West_Virginia" title="McDowell County, West Virginia">McDowell County</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia" title="West Virginia">West Virginia</a>. The <b>Eight</b> post office <sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> closed in 1936.
</p>
