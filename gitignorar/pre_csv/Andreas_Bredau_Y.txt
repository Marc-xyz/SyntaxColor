<p><b>Andreas Bredau</b> (born 21 March 1984) is a German <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bobsled" title="Bobsled">bobsledder</a> who has competed since 2005. His best World Cup finish was second in the four-man event at <a href="/wiki/St._Moritz" title="St. Moritz">St. Moritz</a> in January 2010.
</p><p>Bredau finished seventh in the four-man event at the <a href="/wiki/2010_Winter_Olympics" title="2010 Winter Olympics">2010 Winter Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Vancouver" title="Vancouver">Vancouver</a>.
</p>
