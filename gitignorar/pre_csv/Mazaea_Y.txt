<p><i><b>Mazaea</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Flowering_plant" title="Flowering plant">flowering plants</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Family_(biology)" title="Family (biology)">family</a> <a href="/wiki/Rubiaceae" title="Rubiaceae">Rubiaceae</a>. It is <a href="/wiki/Endemism" title="Endemism">endemic</a> to <a href="/wiki/Cuba" title="Cuba">Cuba</a>.
</p>
