<p><i><b>Into the Shadows</b></i> is a 2009 <a href="/wiki/Documentary_film" title="Documentary film">documentary film</a> by co-directors Andrew Scarano and Phil Hignett about the state of <a href="/wiki/Cinema_of_Australia" title="Cinema of Australia">Australian cinema</a>. <i>Into the Shadows</i> placed 2nd in the <a href="/wiki/Audience_award" title="Audience award">Audience Award</a> for Documentary in the 2009 <a href="/wiki/Canberra_International_Film_Festival" title="Canberra International Film Festival">Canberra International Film Festival</a>.
</p>
