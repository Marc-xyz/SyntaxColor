<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Composition">Composition</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Composition">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="box-Expand_section plainlinks metadata ambox mbox-small-left ambox-content" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><a class="image" href="/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg"><img alt="[icon]" data-file-height="31" data-file-width="44" decoding="async" height="14" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/20px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/30px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/40px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 2x" width="20"/></a></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section <b>needs expansion</b>. <small>You can help by <a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=">adding to it</a>.</small>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">September 2015</span>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p>"Energy" contains samples from <i><a href="/wiki/Eazy-Duz-It_(song)" title="Eazy-Duz-It (song)">Eazy-Duz-It</a></i> performed by <a href="/wiki/Eazy-E" title="Eazy-E">Eazy-E</a> and <i>Ridin Spinners</i> performed by <a href="/wiki/Three_6_Mafia" title="Three 6 Mafia">Three 6 Mafia</a>. <i>Energy</i> was produced by <a href="/wiki/Boi-1da" title="Boi-1da">Boi-1da</a> and OB O'Brien. According to <a href="/wiki/WhoSampled" title="WhoSampled">WhoSampled</a> the song samples the score of an episode of <i><a href="/wiki/The_O.C." title="The O.C.">The O.C.</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Critical_reception">Critical reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Critical reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Ryan Dombal of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pitchfork_Media" title="Pitchfork Media">Pitchfork</a> gave the song a positive review and named it "Best New Track", stating "The song isn't aimed to any rappers or pop stars, not <a href="/wiki/Kendrick_Lamar" title="Kendrick Lamar">Kendrick Lamar</a>, <a href="/wiki/Big_Sean" title="Big Sean">Big Sean</a> or <a href="/wiki/Tyga" title="Tyga">Tyga</a>, but the song lines are dangerous."<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Release">Release</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Release">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The video of "Energy" was released on <a href="/wiki/Apple_Music" title="Apple Music">Apple Music</a> and users with <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a> and registered Apple Music account can watch this video.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-3"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Synopsis">Synopsis</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Synopsis">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The music video for "Energy" features Drake transformed into several notable celebrities, including: television personality <a href="/wiki/Oprah_Winfrey" title="Oprah Winfrey">Oprah Winfrey</a>, during her famous interview with <a href="/wiki/Tom_Cruise" title="Tom Cruise">Tom Cruise</a>; professional boxer <a href="/wiki/Floyd_Mayweather_Jr." title="Floyd Mayweather Jr.">Floyd Mayweather Jr.</a>; singer <a href="/wiki/Justin_Bieber" title="Justin Bieber">Justin Bieber</a>, from a <a href="/wiki/Calvin_Klein_(company)" title="Calvin Klein (company)">Calvin Klein</a> advertisement; singer <a href="/wiki/Miley_Cyrus" title="Miley Cyrus">Miley Cyrus</a>, from her own music video for "<a href="/wiki/Wrecking_Ball_(Miley_Cyrus_song)" title="Wrecking Ball (Miley Cyrus song)">Wrecking Ball</a>"; former mayor of <a href="/wiki/Toronto" title="Toronto">Toronto</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rob_Ford" title="Rob Ford">Rob Ford</a>, enjoying <a href="/wiki/Crack_cocaine" title="Crack cocaine">crack cocaine</a> in an alley; entertainer <a href="/wiki/Kanye_West" title="Kanye West">Kanye West</a>, during the filming of his own music video for "<a href="/wiki/Bound_2" title="Bound 2">Bound 2</a>"; <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/United_States_President" title="United States President">United States President</a> <a href="/wiki/Barack_Obama" title="Barack Obama">Barack Obama</a>, during a presidential address; NBA player <a href="/wiki/LeBron_James" title="LeBron James">LeBron James</a>; and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/O.J._Simpson" title="O.J. Simpson">O.J. Simpson</a>, during <a href="/wiki/O._J._Simpson_murder_case#Arrest_of_Simpson" title="O. J. Simpson murder case">the high speed car chase that led to his arrest</a>. In each of these iterations, Drake mimes along with the song. Interspersed with these clips is monochromatic footage of Drake and his entourage; along with portraits of other caricatures. Bookending the video, a child version of Drake is seen in a field with other children, who act not unlike bullies towards him. At the end of the video, the young Drake stands solitary in the field, while another child (who has now been revealed to have Drake's adult face) points a toy gun at him.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Watch_Drake's_'Energy'_Music_Video_4-0"><a href="#cite_note-Watch_Drake's_'Energy'_Music_Video-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Reception">Reception</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Reception">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Many critics stated that Drake uses this video to mock <a href="/wiki/Justin_Bieber" title="Justin Bieber">Justin Bieber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Miley_Cyrus" title="Miley Cyrus">Miley Cyrus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kanye_West" title="Kanye West">Kanye West</a> and many other celebrities in the video.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Watch_Drake's_'Energy'_Music_Video_4-1"><a href="#cite_note-Watch_Drake's_'Energy'_Music_Video-4">[4]</a></sup> However, each usage of a notable person is timed to emphasize a certain lyric within the song. None of the personalities parodied in the footage have publicly responded to the video for "Energy".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">[<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2015)">citation needed</span></a></i>]</sup> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pitchfork_Media" title="Pitchfork Media">Pitchfork Media</a> named "Energy" the 12th best music video of 2015.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-5"><a href="#cite_note-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Commercial_performance">Commercial performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Energy_(Drake_song)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Commercial performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In Canada, "Energy" peaked at number 65 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Billboard_Canadian_Hot_100" title="Billboard Canadian Hot 100">Billboard Canadian Hot 100</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-BB_TChistory_6-0"><a href="#cite_note-BB_TChistory-6">[6]</a></sup> In the United States, it is the highest-charting song from the album to date, reaching number 26 on <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>, number 9 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Songs" title="Billboard Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Songs">Billboard Hot R&amp;B/Hip Hop Songs</a> and number 5 on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Billboard_Rap_chart" title="Billboard Rap chart">Billboard Rap Chart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-7"><a href="#cite_note-7">[7]</a></sup> "Energy" also peaked at number 71 on <a href="/wiki/UK_Singles_Chart" title="UK Singles Chart">UK Singles Chart</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-8"><a href="#cite_note-8">[8]</a></sup>
</p>
