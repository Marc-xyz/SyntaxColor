<p><i><b>Anthracophyllum</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fungi" title="Fungi">fungi</a> in the family <a href="/wiki/Marasmiaceae" title="Marasmiaceae">Marasmiaceae</a> in the order <a href="/wiki/Agaricales" title="Agaricales">Agaricales</a>. The genus is widespread in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tropical" title="Tropical">tropical</a> regions, and contains 10 species.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Kirk2008_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Kirk2008-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
