<p><b>Oleh Dopilka</b> (<a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_language" title="Ukrainian language">Ukrainian</a>: <span lang="uk">Олег Васильович Допілка</span>; born 12 March 1986 in <a href="/wiki/Kiev" title="Kiev">Kiev</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Ukrainian_SSR" title="Ukrainian SSR">Ukrainian SSR</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Ukrainians" title="Ukrainians">Ukrainian</a> <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">football</a> <a href="/wiki/Defender_(association_football)" title="Defender (association football)">defender</a> who plays for <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/FC_Minaj" title="FC Minaj">FC Minaj</a> in <a href="/wiki/Zakarpattia_Oblast" title="Zakarpattia Oblast">Zakarpattia Oblast</a> championship.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Oleh_Dopilka&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>When he was younger, Dopilka played for Dynamo's reserve team <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dynamo-2_Kyiv" title="Dynamo-2 Kyiv">Dynamo-2 Kyiv</a>, which participates <a href="/wiki/Ukrainian_First_League" title="Ukrainian First League">Ukrainian First League</a>. Dopilka has also played for the <a href="/wiki/Ukraine_national_under-21_football_team" title="Ukraine national under-21 football team">Ukraine national under-21 football team</a> but was promoted in 2007 to the senior <a href="/wiki/Ukraine_national_football_team" title="Ukraine national football team">Ukraine national football team</a>.
</p>
