<p>The <b>2009 <a href="/wiki/Scottish_Challenge_Cup" title="Scottish Challenge Cup">Scottish Challenge Cup</a> Final</b> was played on 22 November 2009 at <a href="/wiki/McDiarmid_Park" title="McDiarmid Park">McDiarmid Park</a> in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Perth_(Scotland)" title="Perth (Scotland)">Perth</a> and was the 19th Scottish Challenge Cup Final. The final was contested by <a href="/wiki/Dundee_F.C." title="Dundee F.C.">Dundee</a> and <a href="/wiki/Inverness_Caledonian_Thistle_F.C." title="Inverness Caledonian Thistle F.C.">Inverness CT</a>. Dundee won the match 3–2.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
