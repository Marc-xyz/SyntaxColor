<p><b>Gorno Svilare</b> (<a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a>: <span lang="mk">Горно Свиларе</span>, <a href="/wiki/Albanian_language" title="Albanian language">Albanian</a>: <i lang="sq">Sullarë e Epërme</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_the_Republic_of_Macedonia" title="Municipalities of the Republic of Macedonia">municipality</a> of <a href="/wiki/Saraj_Municipality" title="Saraj Municipality">Saraj</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Republic_of_Macedonia" title="Republic of Macedonia">Republic of Macedonia</a>.
</p>
