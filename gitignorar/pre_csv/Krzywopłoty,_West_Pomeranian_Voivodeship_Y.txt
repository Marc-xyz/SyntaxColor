<p><b>Krzywopłoty</b> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish" title="Help:IPA/Polish">[kʂɨvɔˈpwɔtɨ]</a></span> is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the administrative district of <a href="/wiki/Gmina_Karlino" title="Gmina Karlino">Gmina Karlino</a>, within <a href="/wiki/Bia%C5%82ogard_County" title="Białogard County">Białogard County</a>, <a href="/wiki/West_Pomeranian_Voivodeship" title="West Pomeranian Voivodeship">West Pomeranian Voivodeship</a>, in north-western Poland.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-TERYT_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-TERYT-1">[1]</a></sup> It lies approximately 4 kilometres (2 mi) north-east of <a href="/wiki/Karlino" title="Karlino">Karlino</a>, 8 km (5 mi) north-west of <a href="/wiki/Bia%C5%82ogard" title="Białogard">Białogard</a>, and 114 km (71 mi) north-east of the regional capital <a href="/wiki/Szczecin" title="Szczecin">Szczecin</a>.
</p><p>Before 1945 the area was part of <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. For the history of the region, see <a href="/wiki/History_of_Pomerania" title="History of Pomerania">History of Pomerania</a>.
</p>
