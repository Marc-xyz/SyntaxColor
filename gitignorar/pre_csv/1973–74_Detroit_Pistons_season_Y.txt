<p>Following are the results of the <b>1973–74 season of the <a href="/wiki/Detroit_Pistons" title="Detroit Pistons">Detroit Pistons</a></b>, the franchise of the <a href="/wiki/National_Basketball_Association" title="National Basketball Association">National Basketball Association</a> based in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Detroit,_Michigan" title="Detroit, Michigan">Detroit, Michigan</a>.  The <b><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1973-74_NBA_season" title="1973-74 NBA season">1973-74 NBA season</a></b> was the Pistons' 26th season in the NBA and 17th season in the city of <a href="/wiki/Detroit" title="Detroit">Detroit</a>.
</p>
