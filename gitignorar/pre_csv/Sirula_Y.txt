<p><b>Sirula</b> (<a href="/wiki/Macedonian_language" title="Macedonian language">Macedonian</a>: <span lang="mk">Сирула</span>) is a <a href="/wiki/Village" title="Village">village</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Municipalities_of_North_Macedonia" title="Municipalities of North Macedonia">municipality</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ohrid_Municipality" title="Ohrid Municipality">Ohrid</a>, <a href="/wiki/North_Macedonia" title="North Macedonia">North Macedonia</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sirula&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The placename <i>Sirula</i>&lt;<i>Sirulja</i> and the possible suffix <i>-ja</i> stems from the name <i>Sirul</i> of which along with its suffix <i>-ul</i> is  derived from an <a href="/wiki/Aromanian_language" title="Aromanian language">Aromanian</a> foundation alongside <i>sir</i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Wlodzimierz96_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-Wlodzimierz96-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
