<p><b>Claudio Pollio</b> (born 27 May 1958 in <a href="/wiki/Naples" title="Naples">Naples</a>) is an <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> <a href="/wiki/Wrestling" title="Wrestling">wrestler</a> and Olympic champion in <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_wrestling" title="Freestyle wrestling">Freestyle wrestling</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Olympics">Olympics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Claudio_Pollio&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Olympics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Pollio competed at the <a href="/wiki/1980_Summer_Olympics" title="1980 Summer Olympics">1980 Summer Olympics</a> in <a href="/wiki/Moscow" title="Moscow">Moscow</a> where he received a gold medal in <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_wrestling" title="Freestyle wrestling">Freestyle wrestling</a>, the <i>light flyweight</i> class.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-db-ol-WRE1980_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-db-ol-WRE1980-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
