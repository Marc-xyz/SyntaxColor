<p><b>Jin Jian</b> (<a href="/wiki/Simplified_Chinese_characters" title="Simplified Chinese characters">Chinese</a>: <span lang="zh-Hans">金坚</span>; <a href="/wiki/Pinyin" title="Pinyin">pinyin</a>: <i><span lang="zh-Latn-pinyin">Jīn Jiān</span></i>; <span style="font-size:85%;">Mandarin pronunciation:</span> <span class="IPA" style="white-space:nowrap"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Mandarin" title="Help:IPA/Mandarin">[tɕín tɕjɛ́n]</a></span>; born 9 March 1994) is a Chinese <a href="/wiki/Bobsleigh" title="Bobsleigh">bobsledder</a>. He competed in the <a href="/wiki/Bobsleigh_at_the_2018_Winter_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Two-man" title="Bobsleigh at the 2018 Winter Olympics – Two-man">two-man event</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2018_Winter_Olympics" title="2018 Winter Olympics">2018 Winter Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-bio_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-bio-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
