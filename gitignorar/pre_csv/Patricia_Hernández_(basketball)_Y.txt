<p><b>Patricia Hernández</b> (born 8 September 1970) is a Spanish <a href="/wiki/Basketball" title="Basketball">basketball</a> player. She competed in the women's tournament at the <a href="/wiki/1992_Summer_Olympics" title="1992 Summer Olympics">1992 Summer Olympics</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-SportsRef_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-SportsRef-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
