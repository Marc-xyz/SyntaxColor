<p>The <b>2011 World Indoor Lacrosse Championship</b> was the third <a href="/wiki/World_Indoor_Lacrosse_Championship" title="World Indoor Lacrosse Championship">World Indoor Lacrosse Championship</a>, an international <a href="/wiki/Box_lacrosse" title="Box lacrosse">box lacrosse</a> tournament organized by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Federation_of_International_Lacrosse" title="Federation of International Lacrosse">Federation of International Lacrosse</a> every four years.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> It took place between 21 and 28 May 2011 in <a href="/wiki/Prague" title="Prague">Prague</a>, <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic" title="Czech Republic">Czech Republic</a> at the 4,900 seat Eden Arena, an Olympic-sized rink.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-yahoo_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-yahoo-3">[3]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Canada_national_indoor_lacrosse_team" title="Canada national indoor lacrosse team">Canadian</a> team was the defending champion and for the third time defeated the <a href="/wiki/Iroquois_national_indoor_lacrosse_team" title="Iroquois national indoor lacrosse team">Iroquois Nationals</a> in the finals, 13–6.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/United_States_national_indoor_lacrosse_team" title="United States national indoor lacrosse team">United States</a> defeated the host <a href="/wiki/Czech_Republic_national_indoor_lacrosse_team" title="Czech Republic national indoor lacrosse team">Czech Republic</a> 16–7 in the bronze medal game.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-laxallstars_5-0"><a href="#cite_note-laxallstars-5">[5]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pool_play">Pool play</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2011_World_Indoor_Lacrosse_Championship&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Pool play">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Eight participating teams were placed in two pools. After playing a <a href="/wiki/Round-robin_tournament" title="Round-robin tournament">round-robin</a>, the first place team in each pool advanced to the semi-finals, the second and third placed teams advanced to the quarter-finals, and the fourth place teams advanced to the 7th place games.
</p><p>Canada cruised through pool play with three easy victories. The Iroquois Nationals beat Ireland and the host Czech Republic by wide margins, but just edged the United States 11–10.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-yahoo_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-yahoo-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
