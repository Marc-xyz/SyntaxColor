	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Academy of Arts (Egypt)</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>Academy of Arts</b> (<a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Arabic_language" title="Arabic language">Arabic</a>: <span dir="rtl" lang="ar"><b>أكاديمية الفنون</b></span>‎; <i>Akādīmīya al-Finūn</i>) is a large educational complex in <a href="/wiki/Cairo" title="Cairo">Cairo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>, established by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Culture_(Egypt)" title="Ministry of Culture (Egypt)">Ministry of Culture</a> of the then-<a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Republic" title="United Arab Republic">United Arab Republic</a> in 1959.<a class="external autonumber" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20080606000657/http://www.egy-mhe.gov.eg/book/art_acadmic.htm" rel="nofollow">[1]</a>
</p><p>It began with three institutes (for film, music, and ballet), but today comprises several institutions of higher learning:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Cairo_Conservatoire" title="Cairo Conservatoire">Cairo Conservatoire</a> ("المعهد العالي للموسيقى "الكونســرفاتوار), established in 1959</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Higher_Institute_of_Artistic_Criticism&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Higher Institute of Artistic Criticism (page does not exist)">Higher Institute of Artistic Criticism</a> (المعهد العالي للنقد الفني)</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Higher_Institute_of_Arabic_Music&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Higher Institute of Arabic Music (page does not exist)">Higher Institute of Arabic Music</a> (المعهد العالي للموسيقى العربية), established in 1929; comprises two performing groups: the <a href="/wiki/Umm_Kulthum" title="Umm Kulthum">Umm Kulthum</a> Band for Arab Music and the Religious Recitation Band</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Higher_Institute_of_Cinema&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Higher Institute of Cinema (page does not exist)">Higher Institute of Cinema</a> (المعهد العالي للسينما), established in 1959</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Higher_Institute_of_Dramatic_Arts&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Higher Institute of Dramatic Arts (page does not exist)">Higher Institute of Dramatic Arts</a> (المعهد العالي للفنون المسرحية), established in 1944 and comprising programs in acting, dramatic arts, décor, criticism, make-up, and integrated artist</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Higher_Institute_of_Ballet" title="Higher Institute of Ballet">Higher Institute of Ballet</a> (المعهد العالي للباليه), established in 1959</li>
<li><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=High_Institute_of_Folk_Arts&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="High Institute of Folk Arts (page does not exist)">High Institute of Folk Arts</a> (المعهد العالي للفنون الشعبية), established in 1981</li></ul>
<p>Additional performing groups include:
</p>
<ul><li>Academic Troupes for Folk Music</li>
<li>Academy of Arts Symphonic Orchestra</li>
<li>Children &amp; Youth Chorale</li>
<li>Academic Troupe for Ballet</li>
<li>Academic Troupe for Theatre</li></ul>
<p>The Academy of Arts's former president right now is Ashraf Zaki.
The Academy of Arts's former president was <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Ahlam_younis&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Ahlam younis (page does not exist)">Ahlam younis</a>.
</p>
