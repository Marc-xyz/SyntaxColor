<p>The <b>2016 William &amp; Mary Tribe football team</b> represented the <a href="/wiki/College_of_William_%26_Mary" title="College of William &amp; Mary">College of William &amp; Mary</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2016_NCAA_Division_I_FCS_football_season" title="2016 NCAA Division I FCS football season">2016 NCAA Division I FCS football season</a>. The Tribe were coached by 37th year head coach <a href="/wiki/Jimmye_Laycock" title="Jimmye Laycock">Jimmye Laycock</a>, and the team played their home games at <a href="/wiki/Zable_Stadium" title="Zable Stadium">Zable Stadium</a>. They were members of the <a href="/wiki/Colonial_Athletic_Association" title="Colonial Athletic Association">Colonial Athletic Association</a>. They finished the season 5–6, 3–5 in CAA play to finish in a tie for eighth place.
</p>
