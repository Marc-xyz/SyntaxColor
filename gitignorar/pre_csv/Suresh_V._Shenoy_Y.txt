	<h1 class="firstHeading" id="firstHeading" lang="en">Suresh V. Shenoy</h1>

		<div class="mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Suresh V. Shenoy</b> is an <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian-American" title="Indian-American">Indian-American</a> <a href="/wiki/Engineer" title="Engineer">engineer</a>, senior <a href="/wiki/Business" title="Business">business</a> <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Executive_(management)" title="Executive (management)">executive</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Philanthropist" title="Philanthropist">philanthropist</a>.
</p><p>Suresh V. Shenoy is <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Executive_Vice_President" title="Executive Vice President">Executive Vice President</a> at Information Management Consultants, Inc., a <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a>-based consulting and technology services company that provides <a href="/wiki/Information_technology" title="Information technology">information technology</a> and <a href="/wiki/Software_development" title="Software development">software development</a> services.  He and his company, known for their philanthropic endeavours, received Northern Virginia Community Foundation's prestigious community leader award for 2009 from <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Congressman" title="US Congressman">US Congressman</a> Gerald E Connolly in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_House_of_Representatives" title="US House of Representatives">US House of Representatives</a>.
</p><p>Suresh Shenoy currently serves as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Chairman" title="Chairman">Chairman</a> of <a href="/wiki/American_Red_Cross" title="American Red Cross">American Red Cross</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/National_Capital_Region_(United_States)" title="National Capital Region (United States)">National Capital Region (United States)</a>.  He is on the boards of The Kevric Company, IMC Global Services, The <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fairfax_County" title="Fairfax County">Fairfax County</a> Information Technology Advisory Committee, the Capital IIT Alumni Association and the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fairfax_County_Chamber_of_Commerce" title="Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce">Fairfax County Chamber of Commerce</a>.  He was also the Program Committee Chairman of the PanIIT Global Conference held in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Washington,_DC" title="Washington, DC">Washington, DC</a> attended by more than 2,300 people.  He served as <a href="/wiki/President_(corporate_title)" title="President (corporate title)">co-President</a> of the PanIIT Alumni Association in <a href="/wiki/North_America" title="North America">North America</a> for the 2006-2008 Term.
</p><p>Suresh Shenoy is on the adjunct faculty of the School of Information Technology &amp; Engineering at <a href="/wiki/George_Mason_University" title="George Mason University">George Mason University</a>.  He has a <a href="/wiki/Bachelor_of_Technology" title="Bachelor of Technology">Bachelor of Technology</a> degree in <a href="/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">Engineering</a> from the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Indian_Institute_of_Technology" title="Indian Institute of Technology">Indian Institute of Technology</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Bombay" title="Bombay">Bombay</a> (1972), and a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/MBA" title="MBA">MBA</a> from the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Connecticut" title="University of Connecticut">University of Connecticut</a> (1975).
</p><p>Suresh Shenoy was a founding member of the Thomas Jefferson Partnership Fund and served on its board until 2002.  Thomas Jefferson Partnership Fund is a volunteer-driven nonprofit foundation for supporting the program and curriculum needs of Thomas Jefferson High School for Science &amp; Technology, Alexandria, <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a>, United States.
</p><p>Suresh Shenoy served on the Board of the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Fairfax_County" title="Fairfax County">Fairfax County</a> Library Foundation.  He has served as chairman of the Emerging Technologies Advisory Group (EMTAG) at the Association of Information and Image Management (AIIM) for 1996–1997, where he served on the International Board of Directors.  He was chairman of the International Society of Enterprise Engineers (ISEE), DC Chapter.  He has also served as co-chair of the BPR-SIG for the Industry Advisory Council to <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/US_Government" title="US Government">US Government</a> <a href="/wiki/Chief_information_officer" title="Chief information officer">CIOs</a>- Chief Information Officers.  Suresh has spoken at numerous industry events, including the annual AIIM International shows, Imaging Expo in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, <a href="/wiki/COMDEX" title="COMDEX">COMDEX</a>, the Information Management Congress in Europe and CENADEM in <a href="/wiki/Brazil" title="Brazil">Brazil</a> and his articles have been published in various industry publications.  Suresh Shenoy was inducted as a Fellow of the Information Management Congress (<a href="/wiki/Europe" title="Europe">Europe</a>) and AIIM International (United States) in 2000.
</p>
