<p>The <b>1982 Africa Cup of Nations</b> was the 13th edition of the <a href="/wiki/Africa_Cup_of_Nations" title="Africa Cup of Nations">Africa Cup of Nations</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">association football</a> championship of African nations run by the <a href="/wiki/Confederation_of_African_Football" title="Confederation of African Football">CAF</a>. It was hosted by <a href="/wiki/Libya" title="Libya">Libya</a>. Just like in <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/1980_African_Cup_of_Nations" title="1980 African Cup of Nations">1980</a>, there were eight teams which was split into two groups of four. <a href="/wiki/Ghana_national_football_team" title="Ghana national football team">Ghana</a> won its fourth championship, beating <a href="/wiki/Libya_national_football_team" title="Libya national football team">Libya</a> on <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Penalty_shootout_(football)" title="Penalty shootout (football)">penalty kicks</a> 7−6 after a 1−1 draw.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
