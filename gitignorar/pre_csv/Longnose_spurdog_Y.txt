<p>The <b>longnose spurdog</b> (<i>Squalus blainville</i>) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Dogfish_shark" title="Dogfish shark">dogfish shark</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Squalus" title="Squalus">Squalus</a></i>, found over <a href="/wiki/Continental_shelf" title="Continental shelf">continental shelves</a> in all oceans, at depths of between 15 and 800 metres.  They reach one metre in length.
</p>
