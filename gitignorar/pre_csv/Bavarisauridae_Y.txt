<p><b>Bavarisauridae</b> is an extinct family of <a href="/wiki/Basal_(phylogenetics)" title="Basal (phylogenetics)">basal</a> <a href="/wiki/Lizard" title="Lizard">lizards</a> found in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Solnhofen_limestone" title="Solnhofen limestone">Solnhofen limestone</a> near <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavaria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. Two genera are recognized: <i><a href="/wiki/Bavarisaurus" title="Bavarisaurus">Bavarisaurus</a></i><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> and <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Schoenesmahl&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Schoenesmahl (page does not exist)">Schoenesmahl</a></i>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
