<p><b>Fasangarten</b> is a suburban railway station on the <a href="/wiki/S3_(Munich)" title="S3 (Munich)">S3</a> line in <a href="/wiki/Munich" title="Munich">Munich</a>. Located just at the southeastern city limits, it serves areas including the former <i>Ami-Siedlung</i>, a neighborhood populated mostly U.S. military personnel and their dependents until the mid-1990s.
</p>
