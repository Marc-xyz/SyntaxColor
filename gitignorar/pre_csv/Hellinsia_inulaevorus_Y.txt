<p><i><b>Hellinsia inulaevorus</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> of the family <a href="/wiki/Pterophoridae" title="Pterophoridae">Pterophoridae</a> that can be found in <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> and <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
