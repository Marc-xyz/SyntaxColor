<p><b>Aimé Terme</b> (born <span style="display:none">(<span class="bday">1945-09-25</span>)</span>25 September 1945)<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IOC_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-1">[1]</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/Olympic_weightlifting" title="Olympic weightlifting">weightlifter</a>, who competed in the 75 kg category and represented France at international competitions. He participated at the <a href="/wiki/1972_Summer_Olympics" title="1972 Summer Olympics">1972 Summer Olympics</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Weightlifting_at_the_1972_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Men%27s_75_kg" title="Weightlifting at the 1972 Summer Olympics – Men's 75 kg">75 kg event</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-IOC_1-1"><a href="#cite_note-IOC-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
