<p><b>Pehlivanköy station</b> (<a href="/wiki/Turkish_language" title="Turkish language">Turkish</a>: <i lang="tr">Pehlivanköy garı</i>) is a station in <a href="/wiki/Pehlivank%C3%B6y" title="Pehlivanköy">Pehlivanköy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>. The station is located just before the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Pehlivank%C3%B6y-Svilengrad_railway" title="Pehlivanköy-Svilengrad railway">Edirne cut-off</a> splits off the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Istanbul-Pythio_railway" title="Istanbul-Pythio railway">Istanbul-Pythio railway</a>. <a href="/wiki/TCDD_Ta%C5%9F%C4%B1mac%C4%B1l%C4%B1k" title="TCDD Taşımacılık">TCDD Taşımacılık</a> operates a daily regional train from <a href="/wiki/Istanbul" title="Istanbul">Istanbul</a> to <a href="/wiki/Kap%C4%B1kule" title="Kapıkule">Kapıkule</a>, which stops at Pehlivanköy. 
</p><p>The station was opened on 4 April 1873, by the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Oriental_Railway" title="Oriental Railway">Oriental Railway</a>.
</p>
