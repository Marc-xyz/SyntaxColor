<p><i><b>Epargyreus</b></i> is the <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <b>flasher butterflies</b>, which belong to the <a href="/wiki/Eudaminae" title="Eudaminae">Eudaminae</a> <a href="/wiki/Skipper_(butterfly)" title="Skipper (butterfly)">skipper</a> <a href="/wiki/Subfamily" title="Subfamily">subfamily</a>. The members (species) occur in the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Nearctic" title="Nearctic">Nearctic</a> and <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Neotropical" title="Neotropical">Neotropical</a> ecozones.
</p><p>The genus name is derived from Greek argyros "silver",  referring to the white spot on the ventral hindwing.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p><p>The species in this genus are:
</p>

<li><i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Epargyreus_aspina&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Epargyreus aspina (page does not exist)">Epargyreus aspina</a></i> Evans, 1952 <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colombia" title="Colombia">Colombia</a></li>
