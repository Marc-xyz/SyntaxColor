<p><b>Marc Culwick</b> (sometimes credited as Mark Culwick) is a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/UK" title="UK">British</a>-born actor whose most famous role was as <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Prince_Albert_Victor" title="Prince Albert Victor">Prince Albert Victor</a> Christian Edward in the 1988 adaption of the <i><a href="/wiki/Jack_the_Ripper" title="Jack the Ripper">Jack the Ripper</a></i> legend starring <a href="/wiki/Michael_Caine" title="Michael Caine">Michael Caine</a>.
</p><p>He has also played parts in <a href="/wiki/Agatha_Christie" title="Agatha Christie">Agatha Christies</a>' <i><a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Poirot" title="Poirot">Poirot</a></i> and played Alec in <i><a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=A_Place_in_my_Heart&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="A Place in my Heart (page does not exist)">A Place in my Heart</a></i>, directed by <a class="new" href="/w/index.php?title=Dermot_Tynan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" title="Dermot Tynan (page does not exist)">Dermot Tynan</a>.
</p><p>Culwick is married with three children and currently works as a theatre studies teacher in <a href="/wiki/Honiton" title="Honiton">Honiton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Devon" title="Devon">Devon</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>.
As a teacher he is considered by his students to be truly inspirational, and has successfully directed several school musicals and co-directed several Shakespeare pieces.
</p>
