<p><i><b>Oedignatha</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Genus" title="Genus">genus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Asia" title="Asia">Asian</a> spiders first described by <a href="/wiki/Tamerlan_Thorell" title="Tamerlan Thorell">Tamerlan Thorell</a> in 1881 as a genus of <a href="/wiki/Corinnidae" title="Corinnidae">corrinid sac spiders</a>,<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Thor1881_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-Thor1881-2">[2]</a></sup> and moved to <a href="/wiki/Liocranidae" title="Liocranidae">Liocranidae</a> in 2014.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-Rami2014_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-Rami2014-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Species">Species</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Oedignatha&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Species">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
