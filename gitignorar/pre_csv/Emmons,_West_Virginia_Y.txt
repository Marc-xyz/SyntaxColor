<p><b>Emmons</b> is an <a href="/wiki/Unincorporated_area" title="Unincorporated area">unincorporated community</a> located on the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Big_Coal_River" title="Big Coal River">Big Coal River</a> in <a href="/wiki/Boone_County,_West_Virginia" title="Boone County, West Virginia">Boone</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kanawha_County,_West_Virginia" title="Kanawha County, West Virginia">Kanawha</a> counties in the <a href="/wiki/U.S._state" title="U.S. state">U.S. state</a> of <a href="/wiki/West_Virginia" title="West Virginia">West Virginia</a>.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jay_Rockefeller" title="Jay Rockefeller">Jay Rockefeller</a>'s political affiliation with West Virginia began in 1964-1965 while he served as a <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Volunteers_in_Service_to_America" title="Volunteers in Service to America">VISTA</a> volunteer in Emmons.
</p><p>The community bears the name of the Emmons family.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
