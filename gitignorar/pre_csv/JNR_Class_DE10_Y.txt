<p>The <b>Class DE10</b><span style="font-weight: normal"> (<span lang="ja">DE10形</span>)</span> is a class of Japanese <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/C-B" title="C-B">C-B</a> wheel arrangement <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Diesel-hydraulic_locomotive" title="Diesel-hydraulic locomotive">diesel-hydraulic locomotives</a>. 708 locomotives were built between 1966 and 1978.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-inoue1999_1-0"><a href="#cite_note-inoue1999-1">[1]</a></sup> As of 1 April 2016<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit">[update]</a></sup>, 138 locomotives remained in operation.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-kokutetsu_last_2-0"><a href="#cite_note-kokutetsu_last-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Variants">Variants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Variants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DE10-0_subclass">DE10-0 subclass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: DE10-0 subclass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>158 DE10-0 locomotives were built with <a href="/wiki/Steam_generator_(railroad)" title="Steam generator (railroad)">steam heating boilers</a> for passenger use.
</p><p>None of this subclass remains in use on JR, but several examples operate on private railways. DE10 1 is preserved at <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/JR_Shikoku" title="JR Shikoku">JR Shikoku</a>'s <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Tadotsu" title="Tadotsu">Tadotsu</a> depot.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DE10-500_subclass">DE10-500 subclass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: DE10-500 subclass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>74 DE10-500 locomotives were built from 1968 with concrete ballast in place of the steam heating boilers for freight use. None of this subclass remains in use on JR, but several examples operate on private railways.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DE10-900_subclass">DE10-900 subclass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: DE10-900 subclass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>One prototype locomotive, DE10 901, was built in 1967 as a heavy shunting locomotive with ballasting increasing the weight to 70 tonnes. This formed the basis for the <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/Class_DE11" title="Class DE11">Class DE11</a> design.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DE10-1000_subclass">DE10-1000 subclass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: DE10-1000 subclass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>210 DE10-1000 locomotives were built from 1969 with steam heating boilers and uprated DML61ZB engines offering 1,350 hp.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="DE10-1500_subclass">DE10-1500 subclass</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=JNR_Class_DE10&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: DE10-1500 subclass">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>265 DE10-1500 locomotives were built from 1969 with uprated DML61ZB engines and concrete ballast in place of the steam heating boilers for freight use.
</p>
