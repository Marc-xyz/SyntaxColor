<p><b>Şükran Albayrak</b> (born 12 May 1982 in <a href="/wiki/Kartal" title="Kartal">Kartal</a>, <a class="mw-redirect" href="/wiki/%C4%B0stanbul" title="İstanbul">İstanbul</a>) is a <a href="/wiki/Turkey" title="Turkey">Turkish</a> TV presenter, sport hostess, and retired professional female basketball player.
</p><p>1.86 m (6 ft 1 in) tall at 74 kg (163 lb), she played as forward for <a href="/wiki/Fenerbah%C3%A7e_Women%27s_Basketball" title="Fenerbahçe Women's Basketball">Fenerbahçe Istanbul</a> and Turkish National team between 1992 and 2006.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-1"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-2"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tbf_3-0"><a href="#cite_note-tbf-3">[3]</a></sup>
</p><p>Albayrak co-presents "Sport Center", a sports news program at the sportstv every weekday evening.<sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-tbf_3-1"><a href="#cite_note-tbf-3">[3]</a></sup><sup class="reference" id="cite_ref-4"><a href="#cite_note-4">[4]</a></sup>
</p>
